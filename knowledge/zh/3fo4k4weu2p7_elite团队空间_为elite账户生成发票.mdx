# 生成发票

1. **[Login](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)**您的coohom账户

2. 点击右上角的个人资料图标，选择**我的订阅**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695275093/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. 在订阅页面点击**购买记录**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695331952/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. 点击**创建发票**按钮，在列出的三个字段中输入相关信息并勾选**保存为发票模板**，然后点击**创建**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695452555/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695490783/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. 点击**下载发票**按钮，将发票保存到您的电脑上

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695543172/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1772682184914/F1233AF29A34A75E376985C42A1078AD.png)

如需进一步帮助，请随时通过以下方式联系酷家乐客服团队：support@[coohom.com](http://coohom.com)
