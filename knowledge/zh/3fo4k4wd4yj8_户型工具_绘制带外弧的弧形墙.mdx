# 绘制弧形墙

## 添加弧形墙

1. 点击弧墙工具后，选择弧形的起始点，设置**半径值**

2. 当角度为**45°**时，点击设置弧形的终点

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003382875/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757993686993/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. 此时墙体会变成弧形，当角度为**90°**时，再次点击即可

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003382887/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* 动图演示如下：

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757994489380/D203CD6805C93A1A82016D7A2D269EFB.gif)

## 分割并删除直墙

1. 添加弧形墙完成后，点击弧形墙相邻两边的墙体，在浮窗工具栏中点击**分割**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003382857/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757994834009/D71F20B876E2532EE3569B220C895344.gif)

2. 分割完成后，删除被分割出来的两段直墙便完成了，可以切换为**3D模式**查看弧形墙效果

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003382877/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757995029509/AA0F7CB9FE88CD7A94BD5016A8F28F8A.gif)
