在背景墙设计工具中，可使用矩形工具将墙面划分成多个区域，然后为不同区域分别涂刷各色墙漆，实现墙面多色个性化效果

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
进入背景墙设计页面，选择顶部**矩形工具**进行绘制。先确定矩形端点，再根据需求在长度、高度尺寸输入框中输入对应数值，然后点击Enter键确认即可

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776325883183/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776325883183/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
点击左侧栏的**贴图**，在顶部选择**墙漆&涂料**，在**色系**中选择**粉色**，然后选择合适的素材放置底部区域

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776326742192/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776326742192/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327165764/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327165764/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
接下来在**色系**中选择**白色**，然后选择合适的素材放置顶部区域

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327366811/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327366811/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
接下来你可以拖拽放大右上角的视窗查看效果，观察是否需要调整

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327629715/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327629715/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
若无需调整，点击右上角的**退出并保存**，渲染后便可以得到一张满意的效果图 * 最终效果图如下：
