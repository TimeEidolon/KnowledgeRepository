When you create a Floor plan, you want to change the wall into a Non-Load-bearing wall, Loadbearing Wall, or a Dwarf Wall.

How can this be done?

1. Left-click a wall under **2D** view.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851008865/8A3DA391C20D4333C0DE56C675BA6116.png)

2. Modify its properties in the right panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733851009528/CE09DF3DE257B9E7EEB0538347CBDDBA.jpeg)
