To facilitate smooth collaboration within the team, define the operational permission boundaries of each member, and avoid inconvenience caused by permission confusion, this document will clearly explain the operational scope of three roles—team owner/administrator/member—in the modules of scheme management, model management, settings, member management, and subscription and billing, providing references for daily collaboration.

# For Users

All Elite users 

# Role Description

In different modules, the detailed functionality corresponding to each role is as follows:

| Rights | | | **Owner** | **Admin** | **Member** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Project Management | My project | * Create, Edit, View, Copy Project | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Team projects | * Create, Edit, View, Copy Project | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Model Management | My Library (Models/Textures) | * My models (upload, edit) | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Team Library(Furniture, Furniture sets, Textures, Paving, Molding/Wall panels) | * Team model management (upload, edit) | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Settings | Customization | * Team profile and custom configuration | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Member management | * Invite members * Role assignment * Remove member | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Subscription & Billing | * Manage subscription * Seat Purchase * View and download invoice | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
