Enterprise can customize the attributes of their products at the product level. These attributes can be used for modeling, process editors, quotes, and other purposes, referencing the downstream business data defined by these attributes.

# Fou Users

Enterprise users with permissions for **Product Custom Attribute** configuration and **Enterprise Backend Configuration** can contact customer success consultants for inquiries if needed.

# Configure Product Custom Attributes

## New Custom Attribute

1. Click **New Custom Attribute** in the top right corner and fill in the relevant information for the custom attribute, including the attribute name, reference name, type, value type, etc. Click **Sure** after completing the form.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778838387898/6FBA1B18A0E84E35562C4156C3E0659D.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778838519375/7A0407A9BA7817D481F2766F807AE97C.png)

2. Click **Add Product Custom Attribute** in **Product Custom Attribute Management**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778838658546/9FBBC188D75A88C7A3075CD6FBADC7BD.png)

3. In the pop-up panel, select the products to add custom attributes to (multiple selections allowed), then click **Next Step**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778838776157/4C3B086C974A6CC5408A7E69545BA5B9.png)

4. Check the custom attributes to add (multiple selections allowed), then edit the **Attribute Value** as needed. Click **Sure** upon completion.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778838903532/CD8565A62CE9BBA47058C9B9EBDEBA7F.png)

## Delete Custom Attributes

**Method 1:** Click **Delete Custom Attribute** in **Product Custom Attribute Management**, then select the model list and custom attributes to be deleted.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778838947866/EAB40C092913BB21BB42A9F62E9882DC.png)

**Method 2:** Check the custom attributes to be deleted and click **Delete** directly.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778839049880/83E4A4CA046BDC0982FB49ED56DB2BB3.png)

## Modify Custom Attributes

1. Enter the **product details page** of a product and open the custom attributes panel (supports bulk addition and modification of custom attributes for a single product).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778839145460/0891092FA53F37C9F06AA12CB8F11B0E.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778839193277/1B82484A681A5055FB5D6DE7B539478F.png)

2. Click **Adding Properties** to batch add and edit product attributes.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778839231336/9C932DFB9BF367C1100CDE46997F26F3.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778839337078/E0B5E54231A8D059A2D48E5FE8752E57.png)

* Click **Editing Property Values** to modify the attribute values added to the product.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778839286764/01E6F28685F77059EF5296AECB56F171.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778839391111/856FC44D96286F57DDD27B0BC608F9B9.png)

# Notes

1. These attributes can be referenced in modeling and quoting rules, but custom attributes themselves cannot reference modeling variables.

2. For a single product, each custom attribute can only be added once.
