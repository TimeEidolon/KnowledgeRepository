**Noted:**  
-The Coohom Material Upload and Publishing Service is designed to assist customers who wish to publish their models or textures on Coohom by providing testing and uploading support.  
-If you have material publishing needs, you must provide the source files, naming conventions, and rendering categories for the materials.  
-You can choose the service based on their needs and prepare materials that meet the required standards.  Currently, the upload assistance service is only available for **B2B customers.**

### **1. Coohom Material Upload Assistance Process**

**Pricing for Upload Assistance**:  
a. Free testing for **models/textures**: Up to 5 items.  
b. Paid upload assistance:

* **Textures**: USD 1 per item
* **Models**: USD 1 per item

For upload assistance, please provide the **source files**, **naming conventions**, and **rendering categories** for the materials.

Refer to the following documents for details:

* **Models**:

  + How to Upload Models to Enterprise Library: [[Enterprise] How to Upload 3D Models?](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/enterprise-how-to-upload-3d-models?kjl_source=hc)
  + Model Upload Requirements: [Coohom 3DS MAX & SketchUp Model Upload Requirements](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/coohom-3ds-max-sketchup-model-upload-requirements?kjl_source=hc)
* **Textures**:

  + How to Upload Textures and Texture Requirements: [[Enterprise] How to Upload Textures?](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/enterprise-how-to-upload-textures?kjl_source=hc)

**Noted**: If modifications are needed, please refer to the **Model Pricing Sheet** for details.


### **2. Coohom Material Publishing Process**

For **B2B merchants** with material publishing needs, we offer two options: **Public Library** and **Brand Library**.

#### **1. Public Library Publishing**:

* Materials are published in the Coohom Public Library account.
* Users cannot edit the materials after publishing.

#### **2. Brand Library Publishing**:

* Materials are published in both the Public Library and the Brand Library.
* Materials are editable after publishing.

After users upload materials to the merchant backend, the operations team will handle the library setup.  
If users require assistance uploading materials to the merchant backend, please refer to the **Upload Assistance Process** above.
