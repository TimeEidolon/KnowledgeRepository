In the preferences, you can modify the brightness measurement unit, with options for % and lm. This article will explain in detail how to change the brightness measurement unit in the preferences.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HCSGJTbGvk)

# For Users

All users

# Set the Brightness Measurement Unit

1. Enter the interior design interface, move the mouse to the avatar, and select **Preferences.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761900077513/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. On the preferences page, select **Renderings - Brightness Measurement Unit**, and you can choose **% and lm.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761900171892/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1761900224566/CFDF376514C6C081592E5AA29B8E3860.gif)

3. After setting successfully, enter the render tool, choose to edit the lighting scheme, click on the light, and you can see that the brightness measurement unit in the right-side lighting parameter panel has changed to the unit just set.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761900284253/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761900343269/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1761900507577/9BF217586D33402B0399C64CC6923C77.gif)
