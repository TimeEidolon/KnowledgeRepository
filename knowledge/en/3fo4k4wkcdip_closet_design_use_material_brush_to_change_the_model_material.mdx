The material brush can copy the model material and assign it to another model to achieve the effect of quickly adjusting the cabinet material.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

### 🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oFyywXW8yV4)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Entrance Select the **Toolkit-Material brush** from the top toolbar

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1737615551293/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1737615551293/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
Material Brush Place the **Material brush** on the model to absorb the material style, find the model part that needs to replace the material, click the brush, and the material will be applied with one click.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737624331839/0E37999637DCC97C8FB3DBD228852C2C.gif)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737624331839/0E37999637DCC97C8FB3DBD228852C2C.gif" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
Material Brush Supports Cross-Tool Brushing In the design tool, common material styles can be brushed together. The following model materials can be brushed together: * custom furniture, custom cabinets, custom doors and windows, soft decoration, and other accessories (hardware decoration is not supported at the moment)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737622625184/E32810D1A655FB10F209C55E99CD9B66.gif)

# Material Brush

Place the **Material brush** on the model to absorb the material style, find the model part that needs to replace the material, click the brush, and the material will be applied with one click.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737624331839/0E37999637DCC97C8FB3DBD228852C2C.gif)

# Material Brush Supports Cross-Tool Brushing

In the design tool, common material styles can be brushed together. The following model materials can be brushed together:

* custom furniture, custom cabinets, custom doors and windows, soft decoration, and other accessories (hardware decoration is not supported at the moment)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737622625184/E32810D1A655FB10F209C55E99CD9B66.gif)
