After the cabinet design is completed, you can use the smart decor to place decors in one click, reducing the time to select and place decors, and improving the design efficiency.

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen & closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

1. Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **kitchen/Closet**.

2. Select the **Smart Decors** from the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744359703920/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Smart Decor

1. Select the cabinet where decors are needed.

2. Select the corresponding custom tool from the **Smart Decors** panel.

3. Select the appropriate decors template and click **Apply**.
