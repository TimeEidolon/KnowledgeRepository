In the ceiling editor tool, by utilizing the linear light tool and ready-made lighting materials, you can quickly complete the ceiling design without main lighting and achieve the desired design effect.

## For Users

All users

## Entrance

Select the ceiling surface and click to enter**Ceiling Editor.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775208957696/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Draw a Ceiling with No Main Light

1. In the ceiling editor tool, set the overall ceiling's **Extrusion** value as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775209165566/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Use the **Add Guides** tool to draw auxiliary lines for light strips, and then use the **Linear Light** tool to complete the shape drawing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775210627510/4CBF596BEBBAC672B982C639AE197B31.gif)

3. Click on the light strip and set the **Linear Light Setup** in the left panel, such as **Process Type, Brightness, Color, Width, Thickness**, etc.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775210700870/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Next, click the **Lighting** on the left panel, choose **Track Light** at the top, select the required lighting and place it on the ceiling, then adjust to the appropriate size on the left column and move to the target position.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775211369505/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775211292041/32B2A812872120A4F2AB471B939AC9E6.gif)

5. Click on **Downlight** at the top and select the desired lighting to place on the ceiling.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775211416842/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Click on **Array** in the floating window and choose **Rectangular Array** to quickly arrange downlights in multiples as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775211699016/97AA0A57E759FF244F7B2BADFDEACA78.gif)

7. Hold down **Shift or ⇧** key to select all the downlights, then click **Precise Movement** in the floating window to move the downlights to the desired position.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775212119876/BFC705D040BD0565FE6D706CB43EEA75.gif)

8. After setting, you can drag to enlarge the top-right viewing window to check the effect and see if further adjustments are necessary.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775212244582/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

9. If no adjustments are needed, click**Exit** **with Save** in the top-right corner, and after rendering, you will obtain a satisfactory effect image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775212263934/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
