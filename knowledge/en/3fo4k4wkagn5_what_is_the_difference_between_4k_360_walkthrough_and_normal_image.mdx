### Many users asked why the 4K 360° walkthrough is not as good as the 4K normal Images?

Many users asked why the 4K 360° walkthrough is not as good as the 4K normal Images?

First of all, The Images are made of multiple pixels, Theoretically, at the same settings, the more pixels, the clearer the final result will show.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849822625/C77BF1DD6891673A010946E09765D280.jpg)

Each cube in the picture above represents a pixel, thousands of pixels combine into a picture.

Coohom's normal 4K Images pixel is 3840\*2160 in 16:9. The Output shows below.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849823454/8A3CB14582B90F594CEFEDF19E7817D1.jpg)

I took a part of the picture above and shown below. The pixel of this picture is about 400\*400. The quality is not bad.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849824532/19A36FC83DD3CC6CA1634E5E77DB9A71.png)

The picture pixel of the 4K 360° walkthrough is 4000\*2000, and the picture below is a 360° panorama.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849825403/1D8B6870E9F8BA1C2AFE86C8F57DFBCB.jpg)

Then I took the same part from the 360° walkthrough, the pixel of the picture is only about 150\*150.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849826406/D6D5F8366F4B07FF809DBB12AC049B92.png)

Put these pictures together, we can see The 360° walkthrough is smaller than the normal Image.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849829082/90734C24D662DC0AA89A389A227EDA17.png)

When we zoom in, we can find that this part of the 360° walkthrough has blur/noise, etc.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849831274/A905F73D9A93334451102A91283C1E6C.png)
