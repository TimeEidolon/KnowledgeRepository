# Aspect Ratio Setting Tips

* **16:9 Aspect Ratio:** Most universal, suitable for wide living rooms or open spaces; pay attention to leaving blank spaces at the edges to avoid overly dispersed subjects.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761877878536/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **9:16 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for vertical lines, corridors, single-wall designs; use layered composition with the subject in the middle or upper third.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878050748/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **4:3 Aspect Ratio:** Visually stable, high information density; suitable for single room panoramas and mixed presentation of soft decoration details.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878163758/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **3:4 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for vertical space proportioned bedrooms or study corners.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878361548/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **1:1 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for symmetrical, centered compositions, and single furniture close-ups.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878462504/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
