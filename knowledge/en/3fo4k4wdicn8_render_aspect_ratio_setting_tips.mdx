Want to make every rendering more attractive? Start by choosing the right aspect ratio. This article will explain in detail how to set the aspect ratio and provide corresponding scenes and tips based on different aspect ratios to help you choose the best aspect ratio.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1YDvLAbO1kc)

### Step 2
Click **Aspect Ratios** in the **Basic Settings** panel on the left, and choose the appropriate composition ratio according to the design scene. Click on the **Composition Lines** in the lower right corner of the page, which will help you to better lay out your rendered scene using the rule of thirds or golden triangles composition. * Detailed knowledge of the techniques related to perspective composition can be found in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/perspective-composition-tips?kjl_source=hc) * **16:9 Aspect Ratio:** Most universal, suitable for wide living rooms or open spaces; pay attention to leaving blank spaces at the edges to avoid overly dispersed subjects. * **9:16 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for vertical lines, corridors, single-wall designs; use layered composition with the subject in the middle or upper third. * **4:3 Aspect Ratio:** Visually stable, high information density; suitable for single room panoramas and mixed presentation of soft decoration details. * **3:4 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for vertical space proportioned bedrooms or study corners. * **1:1 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for symmetrical, centered compositions, and single furniture close-ups.

# Turn on Composition Lines

Click on the **Composition Lines** in the lower right corner of the page, which will help you to better lay out your rendered scene using the rule of thirds or golden triangles composition.

* Detailed knowledge of the techniques related to perspective composition can be found in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/perspective-composition-tips?kjl_source=hc)

# Aspect Ratio Setting Tips

* **16:9 Aspect Ratio:** Most universal, suitable for wide living rooms or open spaces; pay attention to leaving blank spaces at the edges to avoid overly dispersed subjects.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761877878536/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **9:16 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for vertical lines, corridors, single-wall designs; use layered composition with the subject in the middle or upper third.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878050748/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **4:3 Aspect Ratio:** Visually stable, high information density; suitable for single room panoramas and mixed presentation of soft decoration details.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878163758/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **3:4 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for vertical space proportioned bedrooms or study corners.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878361548/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **1:1 Aspect Ratio:** Suitable for symmetrical, centered compositions, and single furniture close-ups.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761878462504/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
