Once you select tiles or floorings from Public Library, you can pave them piece by piece.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Once you select tiles or floorings from Public Library, you can pave them piece by piece.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907227117/DA7AC805A087039C4F7962F3A5D604AB.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907227117/DA7AC805A087039C4F7962F3A5D604AB.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
Select the tile or flooring *(in this tutorial, we select a piece of tile for example)*, move your mouse to the face, hold down left mouse button to pave.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733907228970/33D9790A9C4EF9060BFE0738A99E93B7.gif)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733907228970/33D9790A9C4EF9060BFE0738A99E93B7.gif" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
With the guide lines in light blue, you can start your paving wherever you like. Then drag it. Hold down **SHIFT** key, you can select the whole paving area.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733907232150/9294623A77278E324B8B1B4BD99A3FD2.gif)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733907232150/9294623A77278E324B8B1B4BD99A3FD2.gif" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
Once selected the whole area, you can do free paving settings on the left. Also you can customize the paving with float bar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907235237/C3DAE02604F9929CEFD57ACAC9CE772A.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907235237/C3DAE02604F9929CEFD57ACAC9CE772A.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
The default value of the gap is 1mm, you can customize as you need. *Remember, the maximum is 10mm.* Besides, the color of the gap can be customized, too.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907237200/D2076B1D1AEEA4256E7444F0F9A67A45.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907237200/D2076B1D1AEEA4256E7444F0F9A67A45.png" alt="Step 5" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 6
For the tiles, there are some more settings.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907238467/9E355781D7757CB1A8FD98473FA30747.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907238467/9E355781D7757CB1A8FD98473FA30747.png" alt="Step 6" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 7
**Vertical Slot, Horizontal Slot, Left Diagonal, Right Diagonal** are all allowed to be customized on the tiles, and also you can adjust the width of them. Notice that, *selecting multiple slots at a time to adjust the spacing, the diagonal defaults to equal division and cannot be moved.* The width of the slots depends on the sizes of the tiles.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907241881/E47575337DE9471F4FFE2169DB655A23.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907241881/E47575337DE9471F4FFE2169DB655A23.png" alt="Step 7" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 8
**Chamfer** is allowed, too. You can select the side of the tile and input the value of edge grinding.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907243578/443C7941476F32FA6F3D31656F603919.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907243578/443C7941476F32FA6F3D31656F603919.png" alt="Step 8" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 9
Once you add one piece of tiles or floorings onto the face, there are four types of paving, **grid, mixing, brickwork and checkerboard**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907244951/08911C7FF56FFBF3805B35CD2C79C7D0.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907244951/08911C7FF56FFBF3805B35CD2C79C7D0.png" alt="Step 9" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 10
**Grid** is the default kind of paving, the tiles in the whole area just look like copied ones.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907246298/7D1F4685B32367297114B07C7F369E35.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907246298/7D1F4685B32367297114B07C7F369E35.png" alt="Step 10" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 11
Once you select **mixing**, all the tiles paved mixed.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907248912/68DB1D67033D75D43D1CC558D41AA342.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907248912/68DB1D67033D75D43D1CC558D41AA342.png" alt="Step 11" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 12
**Brickwork** paves like this.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907251173/84C891A60E59CD78A5F5B10E95D74BDB.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907251173/84C891A60E59CD78A5F5B10E95D74BDB.png" alt="Step 12" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 13
**Checkerboard** is usually used with two different types of tiles, to mix them together.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907254193/6C828DFA8415CA70624A0576108A4066.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907254193/6C828DFA8415CA70624A0576108A4066.png" alt="Step 13" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 14
**Free paving settings** are also allowed when selected one piece of tiles or floorings. You can edit only one of them, then to pave the whole face.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907255169/46F72B97295E8549D0739EB4873D8409.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907255169/46F72B97295E8549D0739EB4873D8409.png" alt="Step 14" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 15
**Replace the material** is allowed when choosing from public library. You can replace one or replace all as you like.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907257957/563FCBC49E53C0C367605F5A2390789C.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907257957/563FCBC49E53C0C367605F5A2390789C.png" alt="Step 15" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 16
