Once you select tiles or floorings from Public Library, you can pave them piece by piece.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907227117/DA7AC805A087039C4F7962F3A5D604AB.png)

Select the tile or flooring *(in this tutorial, we select a piece of tile for example)*, move your mouse to the face, hold down left mouse button to pave.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733907228970/33D9790A9C4EF9060BFE0738A99E93B7.gif)

With the guide lines in light blue, you can start your paving wherever you like. Then drag it.

Hold down **SHIFT** key, you can select the whole paving area.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733907232150/9294623A77278E324B8B1B4BD99A3FD2.gif)

Once selected the whole area, you can do free paving settings on the left. Also you can customize the paving with float bar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907235237/C3DAE02604F9929CEFD57ACAC9CE772A.png)

The default value of the gap is 1mm, you can customize as you need. *Remember, the maximum is 10mm.* Besides, the color of the gap can be customized, too.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907237200/D2076B1D1AEEA4256E7444F0F9A67A45.png)

For the tiles, there are some more settings.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907238467/9E355781D7757CB1A8FD98473FA30747.png)

**Vertical Slot, Horizontal Slot, Left Diagonal, Right Diagonal** are all allowed to be customized on the tiles, and also you can adjust the width of them.

Notice that, *selecting multiple slots at a time to adjust the spacing, the diagonal defaults to equal division and cannot be moved.*

The width of the slots depends on the sizes of the tiles.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907241881/E47575337DE9471F4FFE2169DB655A23.png)

**Chamfer** is allowed, too. You can select the side of the tile and input the value of edge grinding.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907243578/443C7941476F32FA6F3D31656F603919.png)

Once you add one piece of tiles or floorings onto the face, there are four types of paving, **grid, mixing, brickwork and checkerboard**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907244951/08911C7FF56FFBF3805B35CD2C79C7D0.png)

**Grid** is the default kind of paving, the tiles in the whole area just look like copied ones.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907246298/7D1F4685B32367297114B07C7F369E35.png)

Once you select **mixing**, all the tiles paved mixed.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907248912/68DB1D67033D75D43D1CC558D41AA342.png)

**Brickwork** paves like this.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907251173/84C891A60E59CD78A5F5B10E95D74BDB.png)

**Checkerboard** is usually used with two different types of tiles, to mix them together.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907254193/6C828DFA8415CA70624A0576108A4066.png)

**Free paving settings** are also allowed when selected one piece of tiles or floorings. You can edit only one of them, then to pave the whole face.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907255169/46F72B97295E8549D0739EB4873D8409.png)

**Replace the material** is allowed when choosing from public library. You can replace one or replace all as you like.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907257957/563FCBC49E53C0C367605F5A2390789C.png)

Once you cut the size of the tile, you can pave the face with the new one. But the width and height cannot exceed the size of the tile itself.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907260413/AC434DD8DCB57BE35F6D5D8A75C484B5.png)

**Chamfer and slotting settings** are the same with what we talked about above.

This is all for the paving.
