### Step 1
**Description** Customers selling custom furniture and products can create their items in a modular 3D viewer. **Before:** Previously, the viewer was only compatible with desktops and did not support mobile browsing. **After：** Now, these customers can showcase their products made in the modular 3D viewer on mobile devices. **【Workspace】Supports merchants in comprehensively monitoring the activity of their accounts**

**Description**

Companies can now download Excel data to fully understand the overall activity of their accounts over a period of time, including for each account:

* the number of tool uses
* the duration of tool usage
* the most recent plan creation time
* the number of plan creations
* the number of new rendering plans
* the number of new rendered panoramas.

**Before:**

Previously, a company's manager could not understand the activity status of all accounts under Coohom's workbench.

**After：**

Now, as Admin and Manager users of the company, they can enter Coohom-Data Analysis-["Overall Activity"], choose a time span for data download, and understand the usage of each account during the corresponding time period (supports daily or weekly statistics).
