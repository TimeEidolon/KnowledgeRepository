How to solve weak reflection effects of materials.

# Solution

Adjust the Ambient light reflection in the Environment settings. The higher the value, the stronger and brighter the material reflects the environment, but if the value is too high, it can cause localized overexposure.

**![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762134156723/7E15E67F74628C9FAB22C8496CAD61DC.png)**

From left to right, the effects are with values of "2, 6, 12". Next time you encounter a situation with low reflection, just appropriately increase the ambient light reflection value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762134290486/5179608ADC35AAB585DBCD31C70C1CD6.png)
