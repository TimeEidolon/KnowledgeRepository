In the custom tools, using the rotate texture brush tool can help you rotate the panel texture direction more quickly, whether it is a cabinet or a sliding door, you can adjust the texture direction with one click. This article will explain in detail how to rotate the panel texture direction.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UfcpK9xh6zA)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Enter the design tool and select **"Advanced Tool - Kitchen＆Bath / Closet".**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756868503448/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Rotate Cabinet Texture Direction

## Method One

Double-click the cabinet, select **"Parameters - Advanced - Texture"** on the right parameter panel, **adjust the material angle to 90°.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756871098212/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1756868269104/9E81CC7ABEE5621394202EBE57E19A6E.gif)

## Method Two

1. Select **"Toolkit - Rotate Texture Brush"** from the upper toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756871098159/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. When the texture brush symbol appears, click on the panel where you want to rotate the texture direction, and the panel texture will rotate 90°.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1756868753688/AA6B06C9956A20E70C08A2148CA6CE84.gif)

# Rotate Sliding Door Texture Direction

1. Select **"Toolkit - Rotate Texture Brush"** from the upper toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756871098137/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. When the texture brush symbol appears, click on the sliding door you want to rotate the texture direction, and the panel texture will rotate 90°.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1756870135785/9ECC27491B93866EA92A85BD29B4BE63.gif)
