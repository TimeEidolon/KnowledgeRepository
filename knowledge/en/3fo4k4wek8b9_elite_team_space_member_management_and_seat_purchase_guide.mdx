The team owner may invite members to join the team and assign appropriate permissions according to actual work needs. Upon completion of the relevant design projects, the team owner and administrators may remove members as needed. If there are insufficient seats when inviting new members, additional seats can be purchased to meet the demand.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

Click the ... button next to a member, then click **Remove** (Note: Team administrators also have permission to remove members).

### Step 1
When upgrading to a team space, the original plan data will be migrated to the team space, but some historical editing process data cannot be migrated.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777277284423/2DF7230ED8DF626FB493544A37E0310C.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777277284423/2DF7230ED8DF626FB493544A37E0310C.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
After a team member leaves, the team owner can remove the member from the team. The seat will become vacant and can be reassigned to a new member. However, there are limits on the number of reassignments and cooldown periods within the current subscription cycle(Specific rules on the maximum number of assignments and the cooling-off period for operations will be separately planned and synchronized subsequently).

### Step 3
Once the team space expires, all Elite privileges will be revoked, team features will become unavailable, and team members will lose access to the space (including personal assets within the space). The team owner can reactivate the space within 30 days to restore all team functionalities. After this period, data such as the team library, member management, and team settings will be permanently inaccessible.

# Member Management

## Invite Members

**Entrance**

On the workspace page, click **Members** in the left panel, then click **Invite Member**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772699231595/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Invite by Email**

In the pop-up panel, select **Invite by Email**, then enter the invited member's email address and click **Send Invitation**.

* Note that before inviting members, you need to check if there are enough seats, if not, you need to add seats first.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772704604161/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Invite by Link**

In the pop-up panel, select **Invite by Link**, then copy the link and send it to the members who need to join.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772699323720/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Permission Assignment

You can go to the **Members** page, click the "..." button next to a member, and **Grant/Revoke** **Admin Access**.

* After becoming an **Admin**, you also have the right to invite members to join the team and assign permissions.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772699364570/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772699394464/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Remove Members

1. Click the ... button next to a member, then click **Remove** (Note: Team administrators also have permission to remove members).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772699438500/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. The plans created by removed members will still be retained in the **Team Plans** folder, with the creator displayed as **Deleted User**.

* Note: If the removed member is re-invited to the team, the creator’s name will be restored to the current name.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772699488318/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
