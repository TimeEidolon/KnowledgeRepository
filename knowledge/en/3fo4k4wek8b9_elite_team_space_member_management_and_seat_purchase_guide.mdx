The team owner may invite members to join the team and assign appropriate permissions according to actual work needs. Upon completion of the relevant design projects, the team owner and administrators may remove members as needed. If there are insufficient seats when inviting new members, additional seats can be purchased to meet the demand.

# Notes

1. When upgrading to a team space, the original plan data will be migrated to the team space, but some historical editing process data cannot be migrated.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777277284423/2DF7230ED8DF626FB493544A37E0310C.png)

2. After a team member leaves, the team owner can remove the member from the team. The seat will become vacant and can be reassigned to a new member. However, there are limits on the number of reassignments and cooldown periods within the current subscription cycle(Specific rules on the maximum number of assignments and the cooling-off period for operations will be separately planned and synchronized subsequently).

3. Once the team space expires, all Elite privileges will be revoked, team features will become unavailable, and team members will lose access to the space (including personal assets within the space). The team owner can reactivate the space within 30 days to restore all team functionalities. After this period, data such as the team library, member management, and team settings will be permanently inaccessible.
