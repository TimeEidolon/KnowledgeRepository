# Problem

When rendering materials with reflective effects such as wood grain or paint, unknown spots of light often appear on the material, as shown in the white parts of the image below.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1703059097048/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/resize,m_mfit,w_880,h_490/format,webp)

# Reason

This is due to the reflective effect of the material. The spots of light in the image are caused by reflections from the exterior view outside the window behind the camera. If the wood grain material in the image is replaced with a mirror surface material, the spots of light can be seen more clearly, as shown in the image below.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135152985/C6E7E1500FA37712765D69E791D81228.jpg)
