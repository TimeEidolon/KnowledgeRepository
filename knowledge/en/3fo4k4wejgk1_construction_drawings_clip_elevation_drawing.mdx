Using the elevation index , you can easily clip and adjust the range of the elevation drawing to make the generated elevation drawing better meet design needs. This article will guide you on how to clip elevation drawings.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=akaYZQKNvCs)

### Step 3

### Step 4
In the bottom left corner, you can choose to either **Open or Close the View Clipping Area**. When view cropping is displayed, you can directly drag the cropping box to adjust the elevation view size. * You can also click **Adjust Elevation Region** at the bottom, then manually drag the clipping frame to change its size. After completing, click **View Elevations** again to see the adjusted results.

# Entrance

1. After entering the design tool, click **Drawings—Construction Drawings** on the left sidebar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768375026227/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Elevation Index** in the **Edit Views**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768383813237/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Clip Elevation Diagram

1. Select the elevation index marker, then click **View Elevations** in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768383894685/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the bottom left corner, you can choose to either **Open or Close the View Clipping Area**. When view cropping is displayed, you can directly drag the cropping box to adjust the elevation view size.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1768384035853/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)

* You can also click **Adjust Elevation Region** at the bottom, then manually drag the clipping frame to change its size. After completing, click **View Elevations** again to see the adjusted results.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1768384651410/BDD03DBD7B143CFD44CBE3156FF860F8.gif)

3. After adjustments are completed, click the **Back to Elevation Index** button in the top left corner to return.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768384906379/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
