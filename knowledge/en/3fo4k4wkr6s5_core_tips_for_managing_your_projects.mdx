Mastering the basic management and sharing skills of design projects can make design work more efficient. Next, we will guide you step by step to understand how to share your carefully completed projects to social communities, pass them to others via links, and perform common operations such as renaming, duplicating, and deleting projects in daily management. Each step comes with a detailed tutorial document that you can click to view~

# For Users

All Users

# Enter the Coohom Design Project

1. **[Log in](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin?kjl_source=hc)** to your Coohom account.

2. Click on the upper right corner's **Workspace.**

3. On the **[Projects](https://www.coohom.com/pub/saas/apps/project/list?kjl_source=hc)** page, hover your mouse over the project you want to edit, click **Enter the Project.**

* **Detailed operations can be seen in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/quickly-enter-the-coohom-design-project?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752655438155/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1752827675050/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Share Your Projects to Social Community

1. Go to **Workspace - Projects**, select the project you wish to share, and click **Share**.

2. In the popup window, select the social community you want to share with.

* **Detailed operations can be seen in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/2-steps-to-share-your-projects-to-social-community?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752826263812/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1752827675089/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Share Your Project Links with Others

1. Go to **Workspace - Projects**, select the project you want to share with others, and click **Collaborate.**

2. You can share the project links with others and set viewing permissions, valid period, and access passwords in the pop-up window

* **Detailed operations can be seen in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/share-project-links-with-others-set-validity-and-password?kjl_source=hc)**

# Rename Your Projects

Go to **Workspace - Projects**, select the project you want to rename, click **Rename**, and you can rename the project.

* **Detailed operations can be seen in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/rename-your-projects?kjl_source=hc)**
