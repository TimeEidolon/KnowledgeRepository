No manual drawing needed, unlock realistic scene restoration and creative design with one click! This feature supports uploading realistic scene images, panoramic images, 3dgs file, automatically recognizing and generating floor plans. Subsequent design modifications can be freely done within the tool, and new renderings can be completed with one click. Moreover, when generating a floor plan, users can choose whether to remove soft furnishings and custom furniture: if not removed, the layout of the real scene from the uploaded image can be accurately restored; if removed, a clean floor plan base will be generated, flexibly accommodating various design transformation needs, helping users efficiently start design work.

# For Users

Users who have trial access to this feature~

# Notes

Point clouds displayed in the design tool may have holes, which is a normal phenomenon. These point clouds are only for design reference, and the final rendered effect will present high-definition realism.

# Entrance

1. In the design tool, click on the left panel **Room—3DGS Import.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770715605631/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. There are two modes of **3DGS Import**: **Upload Image** and **Upload 3D Gaussian File.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775630925698/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Upload Image/Panorama

## Generate Point Cloud

1. On the real scene import page, you can **Upload Images/Panorama** by clickupload and selecting the images you want to upload from the pop-up panel, then clicking **Open** (you can also directly drag images here to upload).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775630340383/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* If there are no suitable images/panoramas, you can choose recommended samples below

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770715665707/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Requirements for uploading regular images and panoramas are listed below:

**a. Regular Images:**

| Criteria | Range | Recommended |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pixel Range | 480\*480 and above | 2K |
| File Size | No requirement | 1~5MB |
| Format | jpg, jpeg, png, webp | All are fine |
| Shooting Requirements | Camera Height: 1.3 meters~1.6 meters  Camera Orientation: Position the camera with its back against one wall of the space, perpendicular to the opposite wall, shoot horizontally. | Camera Height: 1.4 meters (adjust flexibly according to the content captured at different heights on the site) |

**b. Panoramic images:**

| Criteria | Range | Recommended |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pixel Range | 1440\*720 and above | 3000\*1500 |
| Aspect Ratio | 2:1 | 2:1 |
| File Size | No requirement | 1~5MB |
| Format | jpg, jpeg, png, webp | All are fine |
| Shooting Requirements | **Must be fisheye panoramic images (using professional panoramic image shooting devices, not smartphone panoramic images)**  Camera Height: 1.3 meters~1.6 meters  Camera Orientation: Position the camera near the center of the space, perpendicular to any wall, shoot horizontally. | Camera Height: 1.4 meters  (adjust flexibly based on the content captured at different heights and camera positions) |

2. After uploading successfully, images will be automatically identified as panoramas or single images (manual modifications are allowed if errors occur). Then choose whether to clean soft furnishings and custom furniture (example shows disabled). After setup, click on **Start**.

* If the option to clean soft furnishings and custom furniture is disabled, soft furnishings and custom cabinets in the generated panoramic images will remain based on the original ones.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770716686625/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Floor plans and point clouds will be generated as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1770720366538/6B3D8A0BC48B0B54AA4504EF77BF8392.gif)

4. After generating point clouds, the top panel allows configuration of **Room Height**. Additionally, verify the orientation of the floor plan on the **XY Plane**. If incorrect, adjust using lower-right rotating axes and the top **Rotate** tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770717271234/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1770720460491/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)

5. Once adjustments are completed, click **Start Design**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770717306283/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Place Model Materials

Within the design tool, you will see the generated floor plan. Select the required soft furnishing models and custom furniture to place them within the plan.

* Point clouds might have holes, which is normal. Final rendering effects will showcase high-definition realism.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770722220577/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770779361048/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Rendering Output

1. After placing materials, click to enter **Rendering**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770779436392/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. During rendering, **Perspective View** and **360° Walkthrough** will automatically apply the optimal viewing angle. Then, on the left template panel, select your desired **Resolutions** and exclusive lighting template. Click **Generate** (AI enhancement will be applied to guarantee quality. Ensure **Perspective View** don't exceed 4K, and **360° Walkthrough** don't exceed 5K resolution).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770779602808/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770779707568/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Note: When **Perspective View** or **360° Walkthrough**, the 2D view displays a green elliptical optimal shooting area. The camera can freely adjust within this area and still produce high-quality renderings.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770779470380/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After rendering is complete, click on the top-right **Gallery** to view results.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770779749913/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1770792831952/BDD03DBD7B143CFD44CBE3156FF860F8.gif)

# Upload 3D Gaussian File

## Generate Point Cloud

1. On the real scene import page, click **Upload 3D Gaussian File**.Then select the required file in the pop-up panel and click **Open**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775628458074/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Recognize the layout to generate a point cloud. After generating the point cloud layout, you can use the rotation axis in the bottom right corner of the interface, along with tools like **Rotate, Section, Ceiling & Floor** in the top toolbar, to precisely adjust the position, angle, and display range of the point cloud layout.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1775630422703/6FB51597E71C9383060A677E01A2CC95.gif)

3. Once the adjustments are complete, click **Start Design**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775619573994/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Place Model Materials

Place appropriate furniture materials (e.g., decorative paintings, curtains, windows, custom cabinets, etc.) in the layout as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1775630422582/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

## Rendering Output

1. After placing the materials, click to enter **Rendering**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775616378112/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. On the rendering page, first select the rendering type at the top (supports **Perspective View**, **360° Walkthrough**), then adjust to the appropriate perspective. Next, choose the desired **Resolutions** and exclusive lighting template from the left-side templates, and click **Generate**.

* Note: AI enhancement will be applied during rendering. To ensure rendering quality, do not exceed 4K for regular images or 6K for panoramic images.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1775630422603/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. Once rendering is complete, view the rendered image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775627971960/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
