In Coohom, the design tool's interface is in **Light** mode by default.  However, some Coohom users will prefer the dark mode/theme. Here’s a simple guide to help you make the change quickly.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Step 1: Access the Profile Icon Start by locating the **Profile icon** in the upper right corner of the 3D design page. This icon represents your account settings and preferences.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741679007984/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 2
Step 2: Open Preferences Click on the Profile icon, and from the dropdown menu, select **Preferences**. This will take you to the settings where you can customize your workspace.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741679068578/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741679068578/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
Step 3: Enable Dark Mode In the **General** Settings section, look for the **Dark** Mode button. Click on it to enable dark mode immediately. Your interface will switch to the darker theme, providing a more comfortable viewing experience.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741679193097/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
