Al kitchen auto-layout provides efficient and intelligent layout design capabilities for cabinet customization. You can freely draw cabinet bodies, place appliances, and generate professional layout plans with one click to efficiently create a practical kitchen space.

# For Users

Individual users are gradually opening, and enterprise users can contact the CSC to apply for activation~

# Entrance

1. In the design tool, select the left sidebar's **Advanced Tool** and click to enter **Kitchen & Bath**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776739184817/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the customization tool page, and click the **Al Kitchen Auto-Layout** option at the top.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776739526342/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Al Kitchen Auto-Layout

1. Draw cabinets within the popup room, and after completing the drawing, right-click the mouse to exit this mode.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776739620911/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

2. Select the required **Appliances** from the left toolbar and place them in the target position.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776739762269/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

* Select an appliance, and you can adjust its position by inputting values in the input box or directly dragging it. If deletion is needed, simply click the **Delete** button in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776739880359/8B92EAD58843012015ACE668614C773A.gif)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776739910052/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. If an operation is incorrect, you can click the **Undo** or **Redo** buttons in the bottom-right corner. If you need to redraw, click **Clear**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776739958474/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. After completing the layout, click the **Generate** button in the bottom-right corner. Then, in the popup window labeled **Replace Current Design with the New Layout?**, click **Confirm**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776740023047/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. After generation is complete, you can switch to the **3D** view in the bottom toolbar to check the effect.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776741395044/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776741413707/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
