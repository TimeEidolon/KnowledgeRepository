Al kitchen auto-layout provides efficient and intelligent layout design capabilities for cabinet customization. You can freely draw cabinet bodies, place appliances, and generate professional layout plans with one click to efficiently create a practical kitchen space.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

### Step 1
In the design tool, select the left sidebar's **Advanced Tool** and click to enter **Kitchen & Bath**.
