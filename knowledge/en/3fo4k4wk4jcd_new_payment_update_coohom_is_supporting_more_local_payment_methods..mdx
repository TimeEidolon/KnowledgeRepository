Dear Coohom Users,

We have an important announcement regarding payments.

If you are experiencing any payment issues or difficulties previously with Stripe or Paypal, we have great news for you! We have introduced a new and improved payment channel that ensures a smooth and hassle-free transaction process.

To make your payment seamlessly, simply follow these steps:

Visit our website/app and log in to your Coohom account.

Navigate to the Pricing section:[https://www.coohom.com/pricing](https://www.coohom.com/pricing)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764069602861/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Select the plan you want to purchase to upgrade. If you cannot pay by Stripe or paypal, please select the new payment channel: **other payment methods** option.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851783587/67E86B9614EF173A576F33790098F9BB.png)

Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your payment securely.

With this new payment channel, we aim to provide you with a more convenient and reliable experience, ensuring that your transactions are processed efficiently.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851784887/720A79FF22E11E8709781CEC8430ECBE.png)

If you have any questions or require further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our dedicated customer support team: support@coohom.com. We're here to help!

Thank you for choosing Coohom as your preferred platform. We appreciate your continued support.
