The new kitchen & bath design Tool is a professional design tool tailored for custom designers. In comparison to the original 3D design tools, the tool simplifies the design process and expands the range of kitchen, bathroom and closet models. This enables designers to accomplish their kitchen & bath design more efficiently and swiftly~


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
In **kitchen design**, you can combine base cabinets, wall cabinets, countertop cabinets and add them to the design plan. They can adjust the size, material and style of the cabinet model, and generate countertop and crown molding with one click~ * **⭐It is recommended that you read before designing your kitchen: [[Kitchen & Closet] Kitchen Design Basics](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-kitchen-design-basics?kjl_source=hc)**

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen＆closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

Enter the **workspace** and select the **Kitchen ＆ Closet Design**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779701668973/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Enter the **Start Design** interface

* **Method One:** Click **"Start Design"** directly and use wall tools to draw the floor plan by yourself.
* **Method Two:** You can select the **Design Templates** and click **Apply**, the kitchen＆closet design project including floor plan, interior finish and decoration can be generated with one click.
* **Method Three:** You can select the **Floor Plan Template** and click **Apply**, the tool interface will generate the corresponding floor plan.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745752773003/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Tool Interface

Enter the kitchen＆closet design interface, you'll notice that it's divided into three modules: the left navigation bar, the top toolbar, and the right panel.

* **①** **Left Navigation Bar:**According to the design order, it is divided into five tool modules: **room, kitchen, closet, decors, images and drawing**.
* **②** **Top Toolbar:**Provides quick operation functions such as [Save], [Undo], [Toolkit], etc.
* **③** **Right Panel:**You can preview different viewing angles of the room, and you can set parameters after selecting the modified object.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745753199138/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Draw Floor Plan

In **room design**, you can use therect wallto quickly draw closed rooms, add columns, beams, flues, doors and windows to the floor plan, and name different rooms.

* **⭐It is recommended that you read before designing your floor plan: [[Kitchen & Closet] Room Design Basics](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-room-design-basics?hl=en&kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745753477967/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Design Kitchen and Closet

1. In **kitchen design**, you can combine base cabinets, wall cabinets, countertop cabinets and add them to the design plan. They can adjust the size, material and style of the cabinet model, and generate countertop and crown molding with one click~

* **⭐It is recommended that you read before designing your kitchen: [[Kitchen & Closet] Kitchen Design Basics](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-kitchen-design-basics?kjl_source=hc)**

2. In **closet design**, you can select the required Closet materials and add them to the design plan. They can adjust the size, material and style of the cabinet model and freely design the internal structure of the cabinet~

* **⭐It is recommended that you read before designing your closet: [[Kitchen & Closet] Closet Design Basics](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-closet-design-basics?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745754024330/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Add Decorations

Coohom public library collects millions of model resources to build diverse projects for users to protect them, and can quickly obtain models, collect them with one click or use them directly into the project.~

* **⭐It is recommended that you read before adding your decorations: [[Kitchen & Closet] Public Library Usage Guide](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-public-library-usage-guide?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745754355542/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Render Images

After building the design scene, the images tools can help us quickly generate good images. Start by selecting the rendering mode, add lights and environments, adjust the camera position, set image resolutions and advanced parameters, and finally complete rendering of the images~

* **⭐It is recommended that you read before rendering your images: [[Kitchen & Closet] Images Tools Basics](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-images-tools-basics?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1743153164757/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Download Construction Drawings

In the kitchen ＆ closet design process, drawing design is a key link connecting creativity with actual construction. You need to master the basic operations from views editing to drawings output~

* **⭐It is recommended that you read before downloading your construction drawings: [[Kitchen & Closet] Drawing Design Basics](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-drawing-design-basics?kjl_source=hc)**
