In the interior design environment, selecting the cabinet allows you to modify the cabinet's material in the style panel. This article will provide you with a detailed explanation of how to modify the cabinet material.

# For Users

All Users

# Entrance

1. Select **"Advanced tool - Kitchen ＆ Bath"**, to enter the interior design tool page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762487366786/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Modify Cabinet Material

1. Select one or more cabinets, click the **Style** option in the parameter bar on the right, then click the replacement button for **Body Material**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1762502511610/36A7EEB304196EFAEAB0EEF7579653AA.png)

2. In the popped-up **Body Material** panel, click the **Public Catalog** at the top, then select the desired material from the resources below and click to apply the modification successfully.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762497641827/153B34DC5080F27B4DE852C6AB783D6A.png)

3. Click the replacement button for **Door Material**, then in the popped-up **Door Material** panel, click the **Public  Catalog** at the top, select the desired material from the resources below, and click to modify. At this point, the cabinet's material modification is complete.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1762502511717/AE8A2347CF2ED8055181D9BA7BC2CCDA.png)
