In this article, we will explain in detail how to set up the material parameters of a customized model.

# Entrance

1. Enter the **workspace**, select **"Enterprise Catalog - Custom Products - Product Catalog"**, and click **Create Asset** in the upper right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756886166278/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Select the **Material** under the corresponding tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756887829611/C709A4E4F6A47B36A86D49088F73D677.png)

3. Select **"Create One Material"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756886601087/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Parameter Settings

* 1. Upload diffuse map. (Size not exceed 5M, resolution not exceed 5000pixels)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930301382/9DB39A83FB8161B1F9F3E68F6C40FBA2.png)
  2. Adjust Reflection and Reflection Glossiness.

     ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930302436/967CF2C2FD3BAEBB1E1CC9949EF5A38D.png)  
     ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930304367/8229ACC30D9CA2CFFCD151D168A8DAF7.png)
  3. Adjust Bump Scale. (Bump map should be correspond with Diffuse map)  
     ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930306600/AD7A05E3CB9437580DFEBE00BC98B193.png)
  4. Adjust Replacement.  
     ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930309288/3201551F661AAA1C181C29CE1187514E.png)
  5. Adjust Fresnel.  
     ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930310361/932AC925E5BF1A5D5333C91ACE76E4A4.png)
