Welcome to start the kitchen＆closet design journey! Whether you are a newcomer in decoration or a designer, mastering the foundation of building a house is the first step in creating a personalized space. This article will take you to quickly get started: from creating a new project, drawing a room to adding door and window structures, helping you easily build a floor plan frame. You will learn two methods to quickly generate rooms, master space naming techniques, and learn how to make the floor plan closer to the real apartment by adding elements such as columns, doors and windows, etc.

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen＆closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

Enter the **workspace** and select the **Kitchen ＆ Closet Design**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779701668973/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Entrance One:** In the **Start Design** interface, you can select the **Floor Plan Template** and click **Apply**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741318182455/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)**Entrance Two:** Enter the kitchen＆closet design and choose the **Room**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742540238302/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Draw Rooms

* **Specific operations related to drawing rooms can be found in the tutorial documents: [Draw Rooms](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/draw-rooms?hl=en&kjl_source=hc)**

**Method One: Use Floor Plan Templates to Generate**

After the floor plan template is applied, the tool interface will generate the corresponding floor plan.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741333416403/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Method Two: Use Wall Tools to Draw**

Enter the kitchen＆closet design, select the **Room-Wall**, and use the **rect wall** to quickly draw closed rooms. **The wall drawing must be closed to generate rooms**.

* **How to draw a wall, you can see the relevant tutorial documents: [Draw Walls](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/draw-walls?kjl_source=hc)****、[Adjust a Wall Size](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/adjust-a-wall-size?kjl_source=hc)、[Split a Wall](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/split-a-wall?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741334662364/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Name the Room

* **Specific operations related to naming rooms can be found in the tutorial documents: [Draw Rooms](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/draw-rooms?hl=en&kjl_source=hc)**

1. Switch to **2D mode**.

2. Select the **room**.

3. In the **basic** parameters of the right panel, set the **room type** and **room name**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741335337118/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Add Columns、Beams and Flues

Select a **structure**, there are three structures: **columns, beams, and flues**.

* **How to add columns, beams and flues to the floor plan, you can see the relevant tutorial documents: [Add Columns and Flues to the Floor Plan](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/add-columns-and-flues-to-the-floor-plan?kjl_source=hc)、[Add Beams to the Floor Plan](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/add-beams-to-the-floor-plan?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741779870348/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1742539797074/15961A372F8EC1FFD2815283B9790B8C.gif)

# Add Doors & Windows

Select a **door/window**, there are three types of doors: **single door, double door, sliding door**, as well as three types of windows: **linear window, linear bay window, french window**.

* **How to add doors &** **windows to the floor plan, you can see the relevant tutorial documents: [Add Doors & Windows to the Floor Plan](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/add-doors-windows-to-the-floor-plan?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741844312864/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1742540153074/3981B34739E92458C95964014D572913.gif)

# Notice

 In the kitchen & closet design, **the material /wall /ceiling /floor editor is not supported**. If you need to use this function, you can choose **Create Blank Project** tools to create a project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779703833180/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
