### Overview

The **Photo Studio** feature is based on model processing, material processing, light processing, and rendering technologies. It provides a virtual studio in which you can conveniently shoot photos and high-resolution videos for your products from various camera angles and in different scenes.

*The feature is currently a beta version, which is available to only some random users.*

### **Access 1.1 Photo studio >> Products**

In the left-side navigation pane, click the Photo Studio, click on Products.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911009549/CA0D4F96F2E2DC7AC2D62A90943F9B28.png)

1. Add Product

In the page that appears, click the + Add Product blue button on the top-right corner of the page. Or you can simply click on Add Product on the left side of the page.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911011745/275139C764E1FB53D6245D8E088AE496.png)

When you add products for shooting in the pop-up window, you can select one or multiple products at a time for shooting.

The products that you can select are all from your Personal Library. Therefore, you can also upload the products that you want to shoot to your Personal Library so that you can add them to your studio.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911012743/6F501384B9450A6698DD5C09AAAE6AAB.png)

2. Studio Shooting

Move the cursor over the product that you have added or shot photos/videos for. Then, click the Studio button.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911013824/53A6F10E9F12BE682B84B43064F1745E.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911015819/47D217F21F3F53007A62CF32D451C400.png)

### **Access 1.2 Photo Studio >> Studio Collection**

In the left-side navigation pane, click the Photo Studio icon, click on Studio Collection.

1. You can filter studios from Public Studio based on style and space. You can also filter them by tags such as default, New and Hottest. The camera icon that appears means the studio comes with the video shooting feature.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911017210/595684C227E965732910AC84D6853BD4.png)

2. Then, you can click the studio to preview a photo or video album.

Click the Photo button if you want to do a photo. For video, click Video.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911019537/018BE64D261804027A709A8775FF33AF.png)

### **2.1 Approaches to Photo Shooting**

Click the Photo button if you want to do a photo. For video, click Video.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911021816/971F41CB640B544CF21D16ABF46C5C2A.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911022638/5B3DDF37CAA05B89A6E652CEB6CCEA91.png)

There are three ways for photo shooting.

* Direct shooting:

Direct shooting:

You can click the Shoot Now button to shoot photos for the product in pre-configured camera angles and settings. This way, no change will be made to the camera or studio.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911025525/50AEE6EAD6B04A563146CE9D0A3E7C39.png)

* Adjust the lens for shooting:

Adjust the lens for shooting:

You can also click on Camera settings to configure camera angles and settings for your photo shooting. However, no change will be made to the studio itself.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911027257/4F8DDCD0C46E02B5702B5BE23281BE47.png)

* Edit the studio for shooting:

Edit the studio for shooting:

If you want to edit the studio before shooting, click on Edit the studio button near the top-right corner of the page. Then, you can replace and add products, substitute materials and textures of products as well as change product positions. Besides, you can edit interior finishes such as the wall, floor and ceiling.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911028578/A0A166951CFCAF2FAC019B4CAD7A62F1.png)

For details about Photo Shooting, you can check out the video below.

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Hk2ma246naU )

### **2.2 Approaches to Video Shooting**

There are two ways for video shooting.

* Auto Generation:

Auto Generation:

This option allows you to shoot the video automatically in one click based on the pre-set camera paths. Click on it, and you will see the preview of the video playing.

* Make It Manually:

Make It Manually:

If you choose this option, you can edit the camera path for your video by adding 4 types of lenses.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911029423/CB56A736D852F4EF4E2ADA1F3118B8D6.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911030676/524B9919C883C6E0ADDD5186B2C5E224.png)

In either way, you are allowed to change camera position and path as well as rearrange the sequence of video clips.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911032957/BD814B9825152B9E20D613D753916C66.png)

After you shoot the video, you can make more modifications such as adjusting natural lighting, adding background music, changing the sequence of clips and editing effects.

Note: The duration of the video you shoot is up to 30 seconds.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911034791/723D9E8C750F78FA2450FC176D8CA741.png)

For details about Video Shooting, you can check out the video below.

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkWs3d98lI4 )

At last, when the rendering gets done, you can view the photo or video that you have shot in the Product List.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911037647/0303263B6458452D81952195C780905F.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911040650/2D527BB95D6B03C8DAACD8DEF72A00C5.png)
