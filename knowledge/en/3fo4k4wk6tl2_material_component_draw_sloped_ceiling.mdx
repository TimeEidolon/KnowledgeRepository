Users can use the tools at the top of the material & component tool to draw the desired sloped roof shape, then select suitable materials to place on the shaped surface, achieving personalized design. This article will explain in detail how to draw a sloped ceiling.

# Drawing a Sloped Ceiling

1. After completing the room design, set the **Room** **Height** in the basic panel on the right (Example: 5000mm).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1762915184595/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. Click **Advanced Tool** on the left sidebar, then enter the **Material & Component** tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762856852196/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Select the wall surface and click **Wall Editor** in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1762915184618/66768BB59C3CA242507CADCD42F2CE2D.png)

4. After entering **Wall Editor**, click the **Straight Line** in the top toolbar to draw the outline of the sloped ceiling on the wall surface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762857110747/23202A30C151BB93F586399F01896FA1.png)

5. Click the outline surface and input a value in the **Extrusion** floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1762915184602/ACBB3F0C75F0DA960A75094E8EBAAFDD.png)

6. After completing the settings, click **Exit with Save** at the top right.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762917047800/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

7. Drag and drop materials from the left sidebar onto the shaped surface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1762915184638/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1762859291825/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

 8. Once the materials are placed, click **Back to Floor Planner** at the top right.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1762915184744/91D4BF7DE5B6EF9CB57A8C84224E9874.png)

9. Switch to **3D** mode to view the results.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762858045417/ECC273D366A0C8CF647118CA741B5D89.png)
