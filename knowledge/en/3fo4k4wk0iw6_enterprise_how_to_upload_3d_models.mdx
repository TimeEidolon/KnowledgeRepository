This article is recommended for [Enterprise] account.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Enterprise Library** ＞**Products**＞ **Upload Model**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853281327/69D367350261C10FD640CE1B88252D76.png)

### Step 2
Drop files or upload models from your computer. **Note**: Each 3D model file should not exceed 150MB. MAX, RAR, ZIP, and SKP formats are allowed. see more [Coohom 3DS MAX & SketchUp Model Upload Requirements](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/coohom-3ds-max-sketchup-model-upload-requirements?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853281874/B75F60265D88D81AC641B8C519F7189A.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853281874/B75F60265D88D81AC641B8C519F7189A.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
Modify the single model or click **Edit All** to manage multiple ones.

### Step 4
Click **Submit** to confirm.

### Step 5
Choose to **Continue Uploading** to repeat this process or tap **Check Models** to go to the **New Product** folder under the 3D model library for viewing your newly-uploaded models. ***It may take some time to render the model you just uploaded.***

<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853282675/BB68FBC9BAC35083C14F103B05111C60.png" alt="Step 5" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />


1. Select **Enterprise Library** ＞**Products**＞ **Upload Model**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853281327/69D367350261C10FD640CE1B88252D76.png)

2. Drop files or upload models from your computer.

**Note**: Each 3D model file should not exceed 150MB. MAX, RAR, ZIP, and SKP formats are allowed. see more [Coohom 3DS MAX & SketchUp Model Upload Requirements](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/coohom-3ds-max-sketchup-model-upload-requirements?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853281874/B75F60265D88D81AC641B8C519F7189A.png)

3. Modify the single model or click **Edit All** to manage multiple ones.

4. Click **Submit** to confirm.

5. Choose to **Continue Uploading** to repeat this process or tap **Check Models** to go to the **New Product** folder under the 3D model library for viewing your newly-uploaded models.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853282675/BB68FBC9BAC35083C14F103B05111C60.png)

***It may take some time to render the model you just uploaded.***
