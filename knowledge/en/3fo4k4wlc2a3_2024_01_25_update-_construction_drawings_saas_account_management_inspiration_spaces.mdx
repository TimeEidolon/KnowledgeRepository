### 【Advanced Tool】Support to Import Combined Custom Models

#### **Overall**

Users can freely combine custom models from the enterprise catalog, add them to their favorites, and import them into the enterprise catalog. This allows for direct drag-and-drop composition, increasing the potential of products and making design solutions more creative. With the support of SaaS, users can easily import their combined custom models from the favorites folder, enabling them to explore more possibilities and enhance the creativity of their design solutions.

### 【Inspiration Spaces】Support to Custom Show Shadow Configuration

#### **Overall**

This switch controls whether the inspiration space displays a shadow image. Shadow images are suitable for showing loose furniture in a spacious environment, as they can enhance the realism of the models (such as bathtubs and sofas).

#### **Aim Users**

Enterprise

#### **Steps**

Step 1: Click to enter the editing mode of the Showroom

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747808288879/4647C452E780D77566F7BFFF7FD53F82.png)

Step 2: Choose whether to display shadow effects.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747808310553/F70555FC1E70EBA4175EB3CCF5E5A973.png)

**Tips**

* If the project is too complex, shadows may overlap with each other. So please use them reasonably
