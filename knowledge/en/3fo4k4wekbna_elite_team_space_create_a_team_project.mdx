When receiving an invitation to join a team, registered coohom users will have access to a new team space and can freely switch between personal and team spaces. New users need to register first, and after registration and login, they can directly join the team space. Any project created within the team space can be viewed and edited by all team members, enhancing collaboration efficiency.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Click **Accept Invitation** in the received email. * Alternatively, open the link sent by the other party and click Agree. Place the mouse over the dropdown arrow icon behind the avatar in the upper left corner to switch betweenpersonal and team spaces as needed.

# Create a Team Project

## Join the Team

1. Click **Accept Invitation** in the received email.

* Alternatively, open the link sent by the other party and click Agree.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768977671100/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Switch Spaces

Place the mouse over the dropdown arrow icon behind the avatar in the upper left corner to switch betweenpersonal and team spaces as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772693245198/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Create Projects

1. Projects created in **Team Space—My Projects/Team Projects** can be viewed and edited by all team members. After other members edit and save, the project will be updated synchronously.

* In **My Projects**, you can see the team projects that you created for easy searching. In the **Team** **Projects**, you can see the team plan created by all members.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772696659715/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772696701775/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. When creating a team projects, You can also create folders to categorize different projects to improve search and management efficiency.

* Select a **New Category**, then name the new folder and click **Create**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772696782116/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Next, you can click the**...** button after the **Project Details**, select **Move to**, select the folder in the pop-up panel, and click **Confirm.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772696938778/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1772698444972/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* Then you can see the Project in the corresponding folder.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772697452899/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After switching to personal space, the projects created in personal space can only be seen by yourself.

* If removed from the team, you will only have access to your personal space, and previously created team files will no longer be accessible.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773021723148/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
