When generating elevation drawings in construction plans, you can choose to automatically or manually place elevation markers, automatically identify spaces and quickly generate elevation drawings. You can also drag to adjust the elevation range directly or clear and regenerate with one click, significantly improving drawing efficiency.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBiRSf8It50)

# For Users

All Users

# Entrance

After entering the design tool, click **Drawings - Construction Drawings** in the left sidebar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768375026227/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Entrance 1: In the **Edit Views**, click **Elevation Index** to enter the elevation tool page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768369908252/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Entrance 2: In the **Edit Views**, click **Generate Views - Elevation** at the top to enter the elevation tool page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768369821254/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Generating Elevation Drawings

## Automatic Placement

1. Click **Auto-Plac** at the top, then click **Reposition** in the panel that pops up.

* After clicking **Reposition**, the original index markers and elevation drawings in the left-side **View List** will be cleared together.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768370213678/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After new markers are generated, click **Generate**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768370361111/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. The new elevation drawings will then be generated in the left-side **View List**, which you can click to view.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768370787253/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Manual Placement

1. Click **Manu-Place** at the top, move the mouse to the desired location, and the system will automatically identify and generate the elevation box. Click to place, and right-click to exit after placement is complete.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1768371599820/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

2. Move the mouse over a specific elevation marker and click the small circle that appears. You can quickly generate elevation markers for the other three directions based on this marker.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1768552425587/5BE693C2A037151FD75670ED2B8C2A1C.gif)

3. After all placements are completed, click **Generate** at the top.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768373122467/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Adjusting Elevation Box Range

Click the index marker, and you can drag the edges to adjust the range size. After adjustment, click **View Elevations** to see the result.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1768374100581/CD8FC63C91BC49078853BAFD719BE561.gif)

## Clear Markers

If you are dissatisfied with the current elevation drawings, click **Clear** to delete all elevation markers in the current plan and the corresponding elevation drawings on the left.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768374564437/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Elevation Setting

Click **Elevation Setting** at the top, and make adjustments according to your needs in the pop-up settings panel. Once settings are complete, click **Save**.

* Note: It is enabled by default, but you can choose to disable it. When disabled, newly generated elevation drawings will not display the structural walls on both sides, nor will elevation construction drawings be automatically generated.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768373204677/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
