# Create Multiple Floors

## Create a New Floor

1. After the first floor plan is drawn, click **new upper floor** in the **floor** of the right panel.

* **Note: You need to determine the height of the first floor before building the second floor.**

2. Set the **floor name** and **generate location**, and you can choose whether to **copy the first floor plan structure**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747810913500/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Draw the Floor Opening

1. On the second floor, **set the** **slab thickness** **to 1mm** in the right panel, select the **floor plan**, click the **floor opening**, and enter the drawing interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747811243156/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the floor opening drawing interface, select the tool to draw the floor opening in the top toolbar, and click **Finish** after drawing.

* **Note: The drawn floor opening shape must be closed.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747811452275/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Add Railings and Stairs

1. Switch to **3D mode**.

2. Click the **floor navigation**, select **show all floors**, and set the **other floors opacity** to view effect.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747811666547/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Search for a **railing** model from the **models library**, drag it to a suitable location on the second floor, and the right panel can resize the railing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747812564457/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Switch to the **first floor** in the **floor navigation**, search for the **stair** model from the **models library**, drag it to the appropriate location on the first floor, and the right panel can be used to resize the stair.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747813812740/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
