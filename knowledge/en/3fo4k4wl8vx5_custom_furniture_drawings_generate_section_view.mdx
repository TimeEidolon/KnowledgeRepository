In construction drawings, users can manually place section start and end points as needed, then click to generate the corresponding sectional view.

# For Users

Enterprise users, while each Elite user will also be provided with 5 free trial opportunities~

# Entrance

1. Enter the design tool and click the left sidebar **Advanced Tool—Kitchen & Bath/Closet**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770012724806/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Hover the mouse over the top **Drawings & Schedules**, then click **Custom Furniture Drawings**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770018402042/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. In the popup panel, select **Drawing Target**, **Drawing Method**, then click **Generate Drawings**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770012840506/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Generate Section Drawings

1. Select the cabinet, then click the bottom-right **Get Construction Drawing**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770018402238/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* Note: After selection, the selected cabinet will be displayed in the right panel; holding Shift allows multiple selections as a group.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1770013296919/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

2. After entering the drawing page, click the top **Generate Views—Sectional View**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770018402039/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. At the top, select the cut method, then click **Place the Section** and place the section start/end points in an appropriate position on the drawing. Once placement is complete, click **Build the View**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1770014406547/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

4. The generated sectional view can be viewed in the left view list.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770018402068/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)
