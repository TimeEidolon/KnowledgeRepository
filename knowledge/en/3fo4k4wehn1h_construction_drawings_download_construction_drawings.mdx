All elite users and enterprise users

# Entrance

In the design tool, click on the left sidebar **Drawings—Construction Drawings**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769423216193/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Download Construction Drawings

1. In **Edit Views/Drawings Layout**, click **Download** in the upper right corner.

* Detailed view of **Edit Views** is shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769423640775/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Detailed view of **Drawings Layout** is shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769423733593/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. On the download interface, you can select the required drawings and file formats as needed, then click **Download** to download the files.

* Select the required drawings from the left drawing directory.
* You can choose the download format as CAD, JPG, or PDF. For CAD format, you can choose whether to retain color drawings; for JPG format, you can choose color or black and white and resolution; for PDF format, you can choose color or black and white.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1769423854054/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

Note: On the **Download** page, when selecting the CAD format, you can further select whether or not you want to use the **Layout Space** for the drawing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769425429691/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* When layout output is selected, the drawings will be displayed in the layout space (as shown below). Through layout output, you can achieve the linkage of partial view modification, modifying the views in model space will link and modify drawings applied with that viewport in layout space. Currently, the default linked views are divided into the following groups:

1. * Group 1: Wall Positioning Drawing, Floor Plan Layout, Furniture Dimension Drawing, Floor Layout Drawing.
   * Group 2: Comprehensive Ceiling Drawing, Ceiling Dimension Drawing, Lighting Positioning Drawing.
   * Group 3: Strong Electrical Position Drawing, Weak Electrical Position Drawing, Water Route Construction Drawing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769422059241/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After clicking **Download**, a progress bar will pop up, allowing you to view the download progress (the pop-up window will feature random operation tips).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769423890443/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. After the download is complete, you can view the result of the last download in the project and proceed with further downloads as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769423951393/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Set Default Export Drawings (Enterprise Feature)

When performing **Download/Archive** in the construction drawing environment, if the drawing folder has a default export configuration, the drawings in the folder will be automatically selected in the **Download/Archive** pop-up window without the need for manual selection.

* Note:  If enterprise users need this feature, they can contact a CSC for consultation.

1) When archiving drawings, as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769422939307/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2) When downloading drawings, as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769422842762/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
