## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Click **Render** in the header of our 3D floor planner.

### Step 2
Click **Customize** under **Ambient Lights** to go to the **New Scheme** window.

### Step 3
Save the new lighting scheme and go to **Customize Light** page.

### Step 4
Left-click the lights in the target room.

### Step 5
Modify light properties in the right panel to make the room brighter or dimmer. * Adjust the **brightness** of your light. (***Spotlight and downlight with 300-400 brightness; area light with 300 brightness.***) * **Height** decides the position of the light. * **Length**, **Width,** and **Radius** determine the size of the light.
