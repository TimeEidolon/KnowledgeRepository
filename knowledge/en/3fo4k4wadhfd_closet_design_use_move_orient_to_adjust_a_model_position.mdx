The cabinet can be moved quickly and accurately through 3D Move, Precision Move, Dimension Modification, Six Sides Orientation, and Four Corner Orientation.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xAKSFazQKPQ)

# For Users

All users

# 3D Move

1、Switch to **Select cabinet**.

2、Select the cabinet, select **3D Move ＆ Rotate** in theswitch control, or press the **shortcut key R** to switch.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1736908359038/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3、After switching, the cabinet can **3D move** (up/down, left/right, forward/backward)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1736908672442/7F93F91250AFECFD49DCC86367FC25E7.gif)

# Precision Move

1、Click on the cabinet and select **Move Mode** in the parameter panel.

2、According to the **right positive and left negative, forward positive and backward negative, top positive and bottom negative**, input the corresponding **Move Value**, the cabinet can be moved accurately

**Note: The direction of the arrow is positive!**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1736913930473/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1736920338266/84BC1ACDC398175622C22D1E78C7F516.gif)

# **Dimension Modification**

1、When a cabinet is selected, display dimensions. **Click to modify the value**, and accurately modify the distance between the cabinet and the wall or between cabinets.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1736924850833/FC4713AF26670A0581C28C9A0AD543ED.gif)

2、When a component is selected, display dimensions. **Click to modify the value**, and accurately modify the distance between the shelves.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1736923930669/686DEEE15DDAB68D8F2DB175B735E2EB.gif)

# **Six Sides Orientation/Four Corner Orientation**

1、Switch to **Select cabinet**.

2、Select the cabinet, select **Orient** in the floating toolbar, or press the **shortcut key P**.

3、Select  the **Six Sides Orientation/Four Corner Orientation**.

**Note: Only cabinet units support Orient!**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1736927420316/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1736927458965/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## **Six Sides Orientation**

The selected cabinet A can be placed on the six sides of cabinet B. Select the arrow on the corresponding side of cabinet B to complete the placement.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1736927806417/6464D0FA3C1B20E2B3AEE8ED1929B0D7.gif)

## **Four Corner Orientation**

The selected cabinet A can be placed at the four corners of cabinet B. Select the arrow on the corresponding side of cabinet B to complete the placement.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1736928113102/EFF41F3C5BC3AE08E51159E76ABD93CF.gif)
