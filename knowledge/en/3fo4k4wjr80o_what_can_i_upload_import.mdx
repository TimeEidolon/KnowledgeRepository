* **Floor Plan**

**Floor Plan**

Coohom supports uploading a floorplan in DWG, JPG, PNG.

Please note the requirements and restrictions of the CAD： [How to Upload/Import AutoCAD file?](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/article/how-to-upload-import-autocad-file?kjl_source=hc)

And the requirements of the image, see： [How to Upload CAD Floor Plan as Image?](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/article/how-to-upload-cad-floor-plan-as-image?kjl_source=hc)

* **Texture**

**Texture**

- Textures will be uploaded to New Product.

- Upload up to 20 textures at a time.

- Each file should not exceed 5MB. JPG and PNG formats are allowed.

- The length and width of the image should not exceed 8,000 pixels.

Texture Upload Requirement in detail, please see: [How to Upload My Own Textures?](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/article/how-to-upload-my-own-textures-material?kjl_source=hc)
