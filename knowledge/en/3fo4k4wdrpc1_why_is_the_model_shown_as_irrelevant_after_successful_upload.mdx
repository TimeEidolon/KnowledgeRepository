# Problem

What to do if an unrelated model is displayed after uploading the model

# Reason

1. Used a higher version of 3D Max modeling, the current model upload supports up to 3D Max 2021 version.

2. Uploaded an encrypted model file, or improperly decrypted model file, causing the online machine's 3D Max software to be unable to open the file.
