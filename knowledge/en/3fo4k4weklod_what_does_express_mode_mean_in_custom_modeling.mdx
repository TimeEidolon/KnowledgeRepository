Some Coohom users will meet this alert message " *The design is too large and has entered the express mode, and some edits are limited*" while doing the modeling in custom modeling tool. This article will give a full explanation regarding the reason and resolution.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1767947049656/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Users: All Users


## Reason

While using the tool, some users may create one or more large and complex modelings with quite a large number of faces. Once they stop working on a certain complex modeling, this message of **express mode** will appear. But it's not an error but an alert to notify that the tool has entered a restricting mode where some functions like bending, bezier curving function etc. cannot be used temporarily. This is for better performance of the whole custom modeling tool to prevent any glitching or lagging issues.

## Resolution

To resolve this, it's quite simple. Please click the model you want to edit so that a toolbar will show. And you can click the button of "**Exit Quick Mode**" to resume full capabilities.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1767947535266/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

If you have multiple models that are having this same issue, please click them one by one to solve this.

For any further concerns, please feel free to contact **Coohom Customer Service** team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
