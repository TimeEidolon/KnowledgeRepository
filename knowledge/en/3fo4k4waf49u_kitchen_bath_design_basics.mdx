The Kitchen＆Bathmaterial library includes **Product Catalogue, Parts, Combinations and Materials**. Users can combine base cabinets, wall cabinets, countertop cabinets and add them to the design plan.You can adjust the size, material and style of the cabinet model, and generate countertop and crown molding with one click.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uiR4cb8BXP4)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Enter the **Workspace** and select **Create** **New Project** in the upper right corner.

### Step 2
After entering the interior design page, click **Advanced Tool**.

### Step 3
Select **Kitchen＆Bath** to enter the customization interface. After selecting the cabinet model in the **Public Catalog** and dragging it out, **the parameter panel** can adjust the model parameters. # For Users

All users

# Entrance

1. Enter the **Workspace** and select **Create** **New Project** in the upper right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779343695360/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After entering the interior design page, click **Advanced Tool**.

3. Select **Kitchen＆Bath** to enter the customization interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763015775986/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Filter Materials

In the **Public Catalog**, you can choose Kitchen＆Bath cabinets, wall cabinets, tall cabinets and other furniture materials for modular design and combination.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763005322550/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Set up Cabinets and Sizing

After selecting the cabinet model in the **Public Catalog** and dragging it out, **the parameter panel** can adjust the model parameters.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763005448895/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1763005518728/DA75470F1080D9EA6AB7FA4F57D9903F.gif)

# Click Generate

Select **Generate** in the toolbar to generate cabinet countertop, crown molding, toekick molding, sink, stove, handle, furniture leg, etc. with one click.

* The tutorial document can be seen in the detailed operation: [Generate Countertop](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/generate-countertop?kjl_source=hc)、[Generate Crown Molding](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/generate-crown-molding?kjl_source=hc)、[Generate Toekick Molding](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-design-generate-toekick-molding?kjl_source=hc)、[Generate Sink](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/generate-sink?kjl_source=hc)、[Generate Stove and Chimney Hood](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/generate-stove-and-chimney-hood?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763005573071/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Appearance Modification

## Style

1. Click on the cabinet whose style needs to be changed.

2. Select **Style** in the Parameters panel to replace the material style of the cabinet, door and handles.

* The tutorial document can be seen in the detailed operation: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-design-modify-cabinet-material-in-style-panel?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763005651918/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Global Style

1. In the top toolbar, switch to **Select Cabinet**.

2. Select **Global Style** in **Global Edit**. 

3. Click on the cabinet, and select the material, hardware, etc. that need to be replaced in the parameter panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763005809995/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

After completing the above basic operations, the next step is to imaging, drawing and quotating~
