This Kitchen & Bath, Render & Light and other tools bring new functions, welcome to experience and give feedback!

Function update reading instructions:

* Some functions are gradually being opened, and will be marked in the article as "Available to some users, gradually opening up".
* Some functions can only be used by users with relevant permissions, and will be marked in the article as "Available to some users".
* For enterprise functions, please contact the corresponding CSC for consultation.

# 1. Functions for All Users

## Key Update Details

**[Render & Light] The split screen preview function added to the real-time rendering, [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/real-time-rendering-split-screen-preview-function-guide?kjl_source=hc)**

The split screen preview function enables bi-directional collaboration —— you can focus on adjusting rendering parameters, moving the canvas, or optimizing materials and lighting on one computer screen, while another screen displays the rendering effect in real-time, allowing design adjustments and effect preview without interference. 

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1754373961990/175FD20C7E46514376DFC22D0C36AD7F.gif)

**[Kitchen & Closet] The Kitchen & Closet advanced 2D design tool (Available to some users), [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-advanced-2d-design-tool-operation-guide?kjl_source=hc)**

The key functions of the Kitchen & Closet advanced 2D design tool —— Auto-refresh annotate, convenient multi-view layout, and one-click generation of elevation and callout views.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754473743765/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
