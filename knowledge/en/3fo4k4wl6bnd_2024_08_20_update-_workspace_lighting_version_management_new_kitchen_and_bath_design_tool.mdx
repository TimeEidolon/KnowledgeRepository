**Target users**

All enterprise clients

**Before**

Previously, when enterprise users entered the Coohom design tool and selected products from the enterprise library, they could only see fixed product information within the product cards. Even if the enterprise had maintained other dimensional fields for its products (such as "SKU," "Description," etc.), these could not be displayed within the tool.

**After**

Now, enterprises can control which product fields to display or hide within the Coohom workspace, enabling them to showcase these fields within the design tool.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245078686/71425868C8F1D8313ADD6C1539EF9DB1.png)

**How to use**

First, navigate to the enterprise product library, select "Product infor management," then "Product Information Management," and choose "System product fields" Next, select the relevant product information, and click on "Edit."

Then, set the desired display environment for that field. For example, if you choose to display it in the "Furniture Product Enterprise Library," it means that the product field will be shown in that field within the Coohom design tool's enterprise library.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245080832/D97ECD03D391B29ABCFDA74319F3844F.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245082788/5AF85E40D8298AE4761BA90B48EE4575.png)

**【New Kitchen and Bath Design Tool】Allows users to add models such as closets and decorations within scenes.**

**Target users**

Kitchen and Bath Designer

**Before**

Previously, there was no closet function.

**After**

Now, the new closet function has been added, allowing you to add models such as wardrobes and decorations within the scene. The usage is the same as the previous kitchen and bath tools.

**How to use**

When the user clicks on the "closet" button in the left navigation list, the closet catalog library will be displayed.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245094221/4460259700ECFE05B74456B927982AD8.png)

Currently, models from "selected closets" are provided for the user to choose from.

After opening the library, the user can select models, and the subsequent process is the same as the kb tool.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245096517/149975296DA2A761DEF2BBECE721DB2C.png)

**【New Kitchen and Bath Design Tool】Support adding decorations in the scenes**

**Target users**

Kitchen and Bath Designer

**Before**

Previously, to add decorations, users needed to manually enter the decor function and add them.

**After**

One-click to add decorations within the scene, supporting multiple kitchen, bathroom, and closet templates.

**How to use**

In the secondary menu bar at the top, a new "Smart Decor" function has been added.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245097783/A8E6F11BEBCC2A832D03A7D0B1495AE1.png)

When the user clicks on it, they enter the smart accessory addition state.

After building the desired kitchen, bathroom, or closet scene, users can choose to add decorations with one click through the smart accessories feature.

Five closet templates and three kitchen and bathroom templates are supported.

Users can switch between them using the dropdown option at the top of the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245102714/8466F25E68145E1B1E87E9DB53BACDE4.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245107622/A92B8E3BF7DD1166CFF291EBE8F554F6.png)

**【New Kitchen and Bath Design Tool】Supports closet list export**

**Target users**

Kitchen and Bath Designer

**Before**

Previously, there were no closet-related functions.

**After**

After adding the new closet feature, the export list function has been optimized simultaneously. Currently, it supports users to export kitchen, bath, and closet lists.

**How to use**

In the top function bar, the list function allows users to select between exporting kitchen or closet lists.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245110241/7DC9A3391E1D4DA5F5505D1A757C102E.png)
