Some of our Coohom users have the request of changing the cover of loading interface of 360/720 walkthrough tour as they don't like the black cover.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392835240/ED22647B5C1D65F6ACCCCF97E73206F6.png)

Yes, this is possible by **Enterprise account**. (Users who want to upgrade to Enterprise account can fill in this [**business form**](https://www.coohom.com/b2b/schedule-a-demo?utm_source=Inbound+Digital+Marketing&utm_medium=Customer+Service&kjl_source=hc) so that sales person can contact you within 1-2 days. )

Step one: Go to **Settings** in the Workspace  
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392837684/EA261CA1A8A0B9E6F21A2A52B2287BC2.png)

Step two: Access **Panorama**>**For Mobile**

**![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392841034/66E90FD0132647A1418ED92D4CD375EB.png)**

Step three: Click **Edit** button to upload the new cover to change

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392843793/C039F348B2BB10231DFF1BEA833A6297.png)
