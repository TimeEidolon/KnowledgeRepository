The enterprise projects feature allows any member to share outstanding projects to the enterprise projects library. Once shared successfully, any member within the enterprise can view and copy them, thereby improving the usage rate of excellent design projects within the enterprise.

# For Users

Enterprise users with relevant permissions. If you have any needs, please contact CSC for consultation.

# Entrance

On the Workspace page, click **Enterprise Projects** in the left sidebar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773287858095/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Submitting Solutions

1. For Enterprise Admin/Manager, you can choose required solutions from **My Projects/Team Projects/Department Projects**, click the **「…」** button, and select **Add to Enterprise Projects**. After setup, the solution will be directly added to the enterprise projects library.

* **My Projects**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773287995070/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Team Projects**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773288490069/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Department Projects**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773288089089/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. For Head designer/Designer/Designer manager, you can choose target solutions from **My Projects**, enter the **Projects****Details** page, click the 「…」 button at the top, and select **Submit to Enterprise Projects** to contribute.

* Note: Submitted solutions need to be approved by Enterprise Admin/Manager before officially entering the enterprise projects library.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773288138926/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772002566224/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Review Submitted Solutions

1. Enterprise Admin/Manager can click into **Enterprise Projects**, then click **Review Projects** in the upper-right corner to review submitted solutions. They can click **Approve** or **Reject**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773288207578/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773288230018/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Approved solutions will enter the  enterprise projects library, where any member of the enterprise can view or copy them.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773288270799/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Prevent Sub-account Submissions

If you do not want enterprise sub-accounts to submit solutions autonomously, you can disable it. For Enterprise Admin/Manager, enter **Enterprise Projects**, click the **Settings** button in the upper-right corner, **Turn off sub-account submission**, and click **Confirm** to complete the setup.
