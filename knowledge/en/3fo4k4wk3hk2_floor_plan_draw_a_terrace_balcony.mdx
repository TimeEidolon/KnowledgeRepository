## Drawing a Terrace/Balcony

1. In the **Floor plan**, use the wall tool from the left sidebar to draw a room.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772187696433/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Select the area, then change the **Room Type** in the **Room** properties section on the right to **Terrace/Balcony** (selecting terrace defaults to no ceiling).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1772188927138/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. Select the walls, and then adjust the required **Height** in the properties section on the right.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772187880480/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Switch to a 3D view, select the floor/walls, and replace them with desired materials from the **Models**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1772188927049/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772188116252/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Place the required model assets.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1772188927299/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

6. Use the **Rendering** tools to render, and display the effect as shown in the figure below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1772187001830/1052C5FBAC4E51F37D6AED190B942E71.jpg)
