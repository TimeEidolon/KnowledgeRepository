**I. Definition**

The Light Rail Molding is the basic molding that forms any lighting shape.

**II. Operation Steps**

Go to Custom Cabinet - Upload Material.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853763640/4E55502943EF22CFDB82D6B5098A53ED.png)

Select the option to upload the profile molding.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853765980/936388B0AFFC3D75EC6F9292FEA1DFD2.png)

Click on "Add File" and choose the corresponding profile or drag and drop the profile directly.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853766856/007C33024486E9CD75311DB71E6B378A.png)

Modify the name and select "Light Rail Molding" as the molding type.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853768759/9F5FEAB7FE3DC2AE09AD833AFBB6D1A5.png)

Adjust the profile height to a whole number, select the appropriate category for saving, leave the extended parameters blank, and click "Complete Upload."

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853769606/4AE863A624BFC496C03B570FED98372E.png)

On the Edit Information page, you can modify the preview image of the profile molding and adjust the height and extended parameters of the profile molding.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853770450/C5C850C8D99D9F9493C3EA0418FECA69.png)

Click on "Modify Preview Image," choose the desired preview image, and click "Save". (Note: Modifying the preview image is for better identification of the profile information but it is optional.)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853771223/FE674FD9C69043C7FB9B467FAAC5D788.png)

Click "Save" to complete the model upload.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853772012/D5FD0556659079A67BBA6CAF0BF2619D.png)

View the uploaded profiles in the corresponding category.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853772781/DF41447690869CA3B0E8D9FFE2466740.png)

Application of the Light Rail Molding:

In the front-end tool, under Custom Cabinet / Cabinet Mode?, click on "Generate - Light Rail Molding," select the appropriate generation method, cabinet, lighting style, and parameter settings, then click "Confirm."

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853773741/A37E1B35462126C47781CC1F88171ABC.png)
