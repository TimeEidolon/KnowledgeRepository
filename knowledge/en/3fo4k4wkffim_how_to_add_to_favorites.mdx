1. Enter Tool and click a product. Choose to add this product to your favorites.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886001387/47FC2D315ED0B87FE66E80AEB16BA265.png)

2. Select or create a folder for the product, and it will be added to your favorites.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886004089/1CCA4464F21825FEF67DC3826A31152B.png)

​

### Where to find my favorite products?

Enter Personal Catalog to see your favorite products. If you want to use it, drag it into the drawing.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886006655/9440B4560657D7C9C8FF1B90CDFBFA36.png)
