Enterprises can choose their own quick templates through the quick material configuration. After selecting enterprise templates, they can set commonly used models in the tool interface. This article will explain in detail how to configure enterprise templates for quick materials.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

Enter the **Workspace**,select **"Enterprise Catalog ＞ Custom Products ＞Product Catalog ＞Advanced".**

If a product is hidden, assigned permissions, or set as a brand series, it will affect the availability of the corresponding shortcut to ensure the correct logic of product availability.** **1. # For Users

**Enterprise users** with relevant permissions. If you need it, please contact CSC for consultation.

# Configuration Entrance

1. Enter the **Workspace**,select **"Enterprise Catalog ＞ Custom Products ＞Product Catalog ＞Advanced".**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753955000660/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Select the appropriate tool and click **Quick Material Configuration.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753955060166/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

 3. After entering the quick material configuration interface, select the templates available in the 3D design tool. After checking the enterprise template, the configuration entrance for the corresponding enterprise template will expand below, and it can be configured as needed.

**Note:**

* **The selected template will be effective for all sub-accounts under the merchant account!**
* **The setting of product availability permissions will affect related shortcuts. If a product is hidden, assigned permissions, or set as a brand series, it will affect the availability of the corresponding shortcut to ensure the correct logic of product availability.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753955496978/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Enterprise Multi-templates Configuration

**1. Add a New Template**

 The multi-templates of the enterprise catalog can be configured in the workspace by the management account. By clicking to **Add a New Template**, up to five templates can be added.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753955576008/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**2. Manage Templates**

Templates can be set to **Delete** and **Go Live/Offline** on the right side of the template. Offline templates will not be deleted but will not be available on the tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753955685026/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**3. Modify Template Name**

 By clicking on the edit button next to the template name, the template name can be edited and customized.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753955893385/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Design Tool Interface

After configuration, how to use the quick material function in the design tool can be found in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/use-quick-material-panel-to-quickly-access-models?hl=en&kjl_source=hc)
