We are sorry to inform you that **Lighting Design** tool has been officially offline since July,2025 due to strategic adjustment on other development processes of Coohom company.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753082670120/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Target user: All Coohom users**


For new users, you will not gain this function any more whether by purchase or trial.

For existing users who have had this feature by Enterpise account, you can continue to use it until the given period is over.

**Alternative Solution**


You can take full advantage of [**real-time lighting**](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/real-time-rendering-is-launched-in-coohom?kjl_source=hc) to customize your lighting and design to the best.
