Team members can copy their personal space projects and model assets to the team space. Once a project is copied to the team space, all team members can view and edit it. However, model assets copied to the team space are only visible and usable by the individual who copied them, and other members cannot view them.

# For Users

All Elite users, gradually opening up~

# Copying Projects to the Team Space

1. Click the **...** button in the upper-right corner of the project, and select **Move to.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773818601669/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the panel that appears, select **Space** and then click **Confirm.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773818786091/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After copying successfully, click on the upper-left corner to switch toteam space , and you will see the copied project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773818975689/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773819098930/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Batch Copying Models to the Team Space

1. Click **Models** in the left sidebar, where you can see all the **Models** and **Textures** you uploaded, then click the **Batch Copy** button in the upper-right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773821015337/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773821110888/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the panel that appears, select **Space** and then click **Ok.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773819867083/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After copying successfully, click on the upper-left corner to switch toteam space.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773821148661/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. In the **Personal Library** of the team space, you will find all the models and textures copied from the personal space.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773820418470/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773820462906/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Notes

1. When copying projects, onlysingle copyis supported; batch operations are not yet supported.

2. Model and texture copying defaults tobatch copying; all models and textures in the model library will be copied at once without manual selection.

3. You can only copy from personal space to team space, not from team space to personal space.
