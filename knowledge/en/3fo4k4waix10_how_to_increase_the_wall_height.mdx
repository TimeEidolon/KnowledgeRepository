When designing a floor plan, adjusting the wall height can significantly impact the overall aesthetics and functionality of your space. Whether you are remodeling an existing structure or creating a new design from scratch, understanding how to properly increase wall height is essential. Here’s a simple guide to help you through the process.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Step 1: Adjust the Room Height Before you can increase the wall height, it’s important to first adjust the **Room Height** in the Floor Plan interface. This step is crucial because the wall height must always be lower than the designated room height. To do this: * Navigate to the **Floor Plan** section of your design tool. * Locate the option for **Room Height** and input your desired measurement.

### Step 2
Step 2: Increase Wall Height Once you have successfully increased the Room Height, you can proceed to adjust the wall heights. To do this: * Click on the **Custom** button located in the right panel of your design interface. This will unlock additional options specific to customizations related to wall dimensions. * With this feature activated, look for the option that allows you to modify **Wall Height** and enter your preferred measurement.

#### Step 1: Adjust the Room Height

Before you can increase the wall height, it’s important to first adjust the **Room Height** in the Floor Plan interface. This step is crucial because the wall height must always be lower than the designated room height. To do this:

* Navigate to the **Floor Plan** section of your design tool.
* Locate the option for **Room Height** and input your desired measurement.  
  ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742386670452/7D45A0667561EC7C4920639277F09F85.png)

By ensuring that your room height is set appropriately, you create a framework within which your walls can be adjusted accordingly.

#### Step 2: Increase Wall Height

Once you have successfully increased the Room Height, you can proceed to adjust the wall heights. To do this:

* Click on the **Custom** button located in the right panel of your design interface. This will unlock additional options specific to customizations related to wall dimensions.
* With this feature activated, look for the option that allows you to modify **Wall Height** and enter your preferred measurement.  
  ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742386676595/E10E643E7B5FA8638E91FB625BC9E7D9.png)

This two-step approach ensures that you maintain compliance with design rules while achieving your desired aesthetic for both rooms and walls.

### Conclusion

Adjusting wall heights may seem like a straightforward task, but it requires careful consideration of room dimensions. By first increasing the Room Height and then customizing your wall heights through accessible features in your design tool, you can create spaces that are both functional and visually appealing.
