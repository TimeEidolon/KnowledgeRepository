# Problem

The rendered effect image does not show sunlight effects at the door and window positions.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1666956285159/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/resize,m_mfit,w_800/format,webp)

# Solution

1. Activate sunlight and render again.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762136136013/D06E825CD0DD01A2372D219348410AC4.png)

2. Select the door and window model, enter material replacement, and replace the glass position with ordinary glass/clear glass material from the public library.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762136136167/67B39D8A85EED9F77A1BF638AF8EB84B.png)

3. Adjust the elevation angle and azimuth of the sunlight so that the sunlight can be projected to the position of the windows.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762136136004/DA88F3016CB72D96F7D525B0CD8909FB.png)

4. Choose Custom sunlight position, adjust the elevation angle and azimuth of the sunlight to control where the sunlight appears, operation is the same as the above point 3.
