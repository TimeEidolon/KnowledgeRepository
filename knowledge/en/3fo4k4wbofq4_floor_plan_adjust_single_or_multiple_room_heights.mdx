In the floor plan tool, you can modify the top height of the room to achieve the effect of adjusting the height of a single room, or you can modify the room height to adjust the height of all rooms. This article explains in detail how to adjust the height of a single and multiple rooms.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H3nZmv2nOSY)

# Adjust Single Room Heights

1. Enter the floor plan tool and switch to **"Ceiling"** in **2D mode**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747738526184/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Select a single room and modify the **top height** in the right panel.

* **Note: The top height entered cannot be greater than the room height.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747738745771/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Adjust Multiple Room Heights

## Method One: Modify the Room Height

If you select any room and modify the room height in **"Floor - Room Height"** on the right (assuming that it is 3000mm), the height of all rooms will be changed to 3000mm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747740810944/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Method Two: Select Multiple Rooms to Modify the Top Height

Hold down **Shift/⇧** Multi-select rooms to modify the **top height** in the right panel to modify multiple room heights at the same time.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747741936380/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1747742202149/6B925265637480545AB8F15CB5738BFB.gif)
