If you'd like to upgrade your subscription,  you can upgradeyour subscription with one of the following two methods:

# Option 1:

1. **[Login](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)**with your Coohom account.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773023542497/86E1A01A9854AC2601F47F37512E7C59.png)

* If you're an existing user, just **Sign In**. If you're new, please register first, then **Sign In**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773023611062/1512F7B47BF7ECB7C582D5C0CAB358EC.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773023642644/84341AF1326016A13AF763A1AA38BFEC.png)

2. On the workspace page, click **Upgrade Now** in the top-left corner, then choose the plan you want to upgrade to or renew.

Note: When upgrading to Elite,  you can choose a single subscription or continuous subscription, and then select seats according to your needs on the right (each seat corresponds to one team member you can invite in Team Member Management).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773023768902/1955984AA2E83646412BDC404E6EF2EE.png)

* You can also click the avatar in the upper right corner to select **My Subscription**, click **Upgrade Now**, and then select the desired package upgrade/Renew your subscription.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773024025792/D701041D39AB785DEAF964C886982733.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773024031063/2C1F4D4E4B60F19F0CCF0D8EAE1ADD48.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773024061771/AF20F3DC7917E5B826085812A8F0BB15.png)

3. Fill in all required fields on the checkout page below.

4. Click **Complete Order** to confirm.

# Option 2:

1. You can click on **Pricing** at the top of the homepage, then select the package you need.

![image2026-3-9_11-37-46.png](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773027671583/B9AC83543C4C83ACC5D0C30E2EB95603.png)

* You can also click on your avatar in the top right corner of the workbench page to select My Subscription, and click on Plans & Pricing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773024332127/4686885AEA188E365FD013A456F92740.png)

2. Click on the desired plan to upgrade/renew your subscription.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1773051997323/AF20F3DC7917E5B826085812A8F0BB15.png)

3. Fill in all required fields on the checkout page below.

4. Click **Complete Order** to confirm.

5. When purchased successfully, your order information will be displayed in a new web page and a PDF invoice will be sent to your login email address.

6. To upgrade to Coohom Enterprise, please fill up **[this form](https://www.coohom.com/b2b/book-a-consultation?kpm=qkWL.10444e360755181f.3a6a173.1655351096784)** and our salesperson will get in touch with soon (usually 1-2 business days).

**Please take note** that once the subscription fees is deducted, it is non-refundable. More details **[No-Refund Policy](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/no-refund-policy?kjl_source=hc).**
