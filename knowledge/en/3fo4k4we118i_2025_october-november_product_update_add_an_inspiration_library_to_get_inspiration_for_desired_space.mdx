This Floor Plan Design, Kitchen & Closet and other tools bring new functions, welcome to experience and give feedback!

Function update reading instructions:

* Some functions are gradually being opened, and will be marked in the article as "Available to some users, gradually opening up".
* Some functions can only be used by users with relevant permissions, and will be marked in the article as "Available to some users".
* For enterprise functions, please contact the corresponding CSC for consultation.

# 1. Functions for All Users

## Key Update Details

**[Floor Plan Design] Wall height is supported for modification directly in the property bar, [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/floor-plan-adjust-individual-wall-height?kjl_source=hc)**

When modifying wall height, you can directly modify it in the right property bar without switching to a low wall first. When the input wall height ＞ floor height, you can choose whether to adjust the floor height directly.

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BxDwfyasWoE)

**[Inspiration Library] Add inspiration library to efficiently get inspiration for desired space, [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/inspiration-library-usage-guide?kjl_source=hc)**

From filtering inspiration spaces to various ways of flexibly picking and dragging out models, and then to one-click favorites of preferred designs, the inspiration library helps you efficiently obtain space inspiration you want, easily reuse favorite models and interior finish.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763361404916/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# 2. Functions for Enterprise Users

For details on enabling and using enterprise functions, please consult your corresponding CSC.

## Key Update Details

**[Account Management] Added VIP management and pre-binding functions (Available to some users), [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/enterprise-guide-for-using-vip-management-and-pre-binding-functions?kjl_source=hc)**

VIP account management functions can help enterprises better manage accounts and VIPs, supporting VIP viewing and binding, including active and expired VIPs.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762307823292/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762308160686/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**[Al Templates] Submitted the AI templates support calling in inspiration library / AI style****, [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/enterprise-submission-guide-for-ai-templates?kjl_source=hc)**

Enterprise users can submit AI templates in the workspace. After uploading, you can be accessed in the design tool’s inspiration library/AI style, making it convenient to directly use them for future designs and reduce repetitive work.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1765367490620/55193BEFA826B4A4E6E375D78EF09C05.gif)
