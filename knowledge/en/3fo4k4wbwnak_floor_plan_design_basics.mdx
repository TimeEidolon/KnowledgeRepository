This article will take you to quickly get started: from creating a new project, drawing a room to adding door and window structures, helping you easily build a floor plan frame. You will learn two methods to quickly generate rooms, master space naming techniques, and learn how to make the floor plan closer to the real floor plan by adding elements such as columns, doors and windows, etc.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vm2RwiBdSxI)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

**Entrance One:** Enter the **workspace**, click **upload floor plan** and select the required drawings to import, you can choose to import a single or multiple CAD files, or select a CAD file containing multiple drawings. Uploading floor plan supports**importing CAD floor plan files and images in pdf/ png/ jpg format. The specific operation can be seen in the tutorial document:****[Click here for Tutorial 1](https://www.coohom.com/zh_CN/helpcenter/%E5%A6%82%E4%BD%95%E4%B8%8A%E4%BC%A0-%E5%AF%BC%E5%85%A5cad%E6%88%B7%E5%9E%8B%E6%96%87%E4%BB%B6?kjl_source=hc)****, [Click here for Tutorial 2](https://www.coohom.com/zh_CN/helpcenter/%E5%A6%82%E4%BD%95%E4%B8%8A%E4%BC%A0-%E5%AF%BC%E5%85%A5cad%E6%88%B7%E5%9E%8B%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87?kjl_source=hc)**

# Draw Room

Enter the 3D design, select the **Floor Plan****-Wall**, and use the**rect wall**to quickly draw closed rooms. **The wall drawing must be closed to generate rooms**.

* **How to draw a wall, you can see the relevant tutorial documents:  [Click here](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/coohom-cloud-design-5-0-wall-drawing-function-summary-quickly-draw-floor-plan?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1779356103930/C709A4E4F6A47B36A86D49088F73D677.png)

# Name the Room

* **Specific operations related to naming rooms can be found in the tutorial documents: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/floor-plan-divide-the-floor-plan-area-and-name-the-room?kjl_source=hc)**

1. Switch to **2D mode**.

2. Select the **room**.

3. In the **basic** parameters of the right panel, set the **room type** and **room name**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779352655892/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
