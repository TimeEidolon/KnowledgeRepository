We have Coohom users reaching support team to ask why their uploaded textures/materials in the personal library are not found in the project library while designing countertop in advanced tool. This article will explain the reason and solution regarding this.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769508023884/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769508300310/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Users: Individual users


## Reason

In Coohom, while uploading the textures, the category matters a lot. If the category is selected wrongly, the texture may not show in the library where users want to use it. Hence, the reason why the uploaded textures are not shown in project for countertop's personal catalog is that the selected categories are wrong.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769510271593/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Resolution

To resolve this, users can only select these categories.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769510638285/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

For any further concerns, please feel free to contact Coohom Customer Service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
