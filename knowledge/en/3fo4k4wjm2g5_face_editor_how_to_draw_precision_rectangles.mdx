1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

4. Select **Rect (R)** ＞ **Precision Rect** in the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849110563/E76E1FFAB2A346BEBC9389128BCBC74C.jpeg)

5. Set **Paving** and **Angle** parameters in pop-up **Area Settings**.

6. Click **Confirm**.

7. Place the generated rectangle in your floor plan.

8. Click the rectangle to do more operations.

9. Click **Done**.
