1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

### Step 2
Click the face you want to edit.

### Step 3
Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

### Step 4
Select **Rect (R)** ＞ **Precision Rect** in the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849110563/E76E1FFAB2A346BEBC9389128BCBC74C.jpeg)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849110563/E76E1FFAB2A346BEBC9389128BCBC74C.jpeg" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
Set **Paving** and **Angle** parameters in pop-up **Area Settings**.

### Step 6
Click **Confirm**.

### Step 7
Place the generated rectangle in your floor plan.

### Step 8
Click the rectangle to do more operations.

### Step 9
Click **Done**. Did this answer your question?
