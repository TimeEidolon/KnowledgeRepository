# Change the Model Material

1. Switch to **3D** in the footer.

2. Drag the target model into your floor plan.  
​  
3. Select your model and click **Material Editor** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768359570242/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Select the relevant part of the model by clicking it, or choose the part associated with the material in the **Location** panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768359606234/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. The left material panel shows broad material categories, with more detailed subcategories available after selection.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768360553450/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768360636926/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Choose one material in the left panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768359977004/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

7. Edit its texture in the right panel.

8. Click Complete to confirm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768360093942/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
