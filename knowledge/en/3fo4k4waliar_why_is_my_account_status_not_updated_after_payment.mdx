Some Coohom users will come to service team to complain that the account status is not changed or they can still get pop-up to upgrade even if they have made the payment successfully. This can be really frustrating. 

## Affected Users: Individual Users

## **Possible Reasons:**

* User left the payment page too early before the successful payment notice is shown so that the status is not refreshed.  ![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741769630874/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
* User has the lower level’s Pro plan in effect so that the higher level’s Elite plan will take effect later.![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741769695561/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## **Solutions:**

* Refresh the broswer or log out to log in gain to check the status.
* Go to **User Center**>**Subscription** to check the effective lower level’s PRO plan to click the Ratio button to freeze that to make higher leve’s Elite plan take effect.![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741769970236/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

If you still have any concerns, please contact Coohom Customer Service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
