In the process of using daily accounts, protecting personal information security is particularly important. Whether it is due to the need for security protection or forgetting the password, it is necessary to change and reset the account password in a timely manner. For this reason, we have organized in detail how to change your personal account password and the specific steps to quickly reset your password if you forget it. This article will guide you step by step through the operation process to help you easily manage and protect your account security~

# For Users

All users

# Navigate to Your Workspace

Start by logging into your Coohom account. Once you're in, head over to your **Workspace** where all of your projects are displayed. This is the central hub for managing and accessing everything related to your designs.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751623486358/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Change Your Account Password

1. In the upper right corner of the Workspace interface, you will find the **Profile Icon**. This icon typically resembles a person or an avatar and serves as a gateway to access your account settings.

2. Click the profile icon to open a dropdown menu. Select **Account Settings** from the menu.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1752822087738/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. Select **Security Settings** and click **Reset Password**. Here, you can reset your account password.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752821130888/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Fill in the fields for **Old Password**, **New Password,** and **Confirm Password**. After completing the entries, click **Save**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1752822087785/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

5. If you forget your old password, you can click **Forgot Old Password** to help you quickly reset your password by **verifying your email**. Detailed instructions can be found in the tutorial documentation: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/how-to-reset-password-when-forgotten?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752821326279/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
