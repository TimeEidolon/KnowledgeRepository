Updating your profile in Coohom is a straightforward process that allows you to keep your information current and personalized. Whether you want to change your username, update your address, or modify other details, here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process.

# For Users

All users

# Navigate to Your Workspace

Start by logging into your Coohom account. Once you're in, head over to your **Workspace** where all of your projects are displayed. This is the central hub for managing and accessing everything related to your designs.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751623486358/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Access Account Settings

1. In the upper right corner of the Workspace interface, you will find the **Profile Icon**. This icon typically resembles a person or an avatar and serves as a gateway to access your account settings.

2. Click on the Profile Icon, which will open a dropdown menu. From this menu, select **Account Settings**. This section allows you to make various changes to your profile.

Once you're in Account Settings, you'll be able to:

* Change your profile image
* Change your user name
* Modify personal details such as your physical address

Simply enter the new information you wish to save and ensure everything is accurate before finalizing any changes.
