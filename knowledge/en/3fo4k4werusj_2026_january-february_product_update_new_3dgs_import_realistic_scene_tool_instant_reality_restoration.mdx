# 2. Functions for Enterprise Users

For details on enabling and using enterprise functions, please consult your corresponding CSC.

## Key Update Details

**[Workspace] Added UGC multi-language translation function to the enterprise workspace (Available to some users), [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/enterprise-ugc-multi-language-translation-function-usage-guide?kjl_source=hc)**

UGC multi-language translation is primarily used for translating UGC texts in corporate databases, such as product descriptions, parametric models, and other data. These UGC texts are customized by various enterprise users, characterized by their individuality and professionalism, and adhere to respective industry standards.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773284658788/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**[Workspace] Added the enterprise projects function to the enterprise workspace (Available to some users), [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/enterprise-enterprise-projects-library-usage-guide?kjl_source=hc)**

The enterprise projects feature allows any member to share outstanding projects to the enterprise projects library. Once shared successfully, any member within the enterprise can view and copy them, thereby improving the usage rate of excellent design projects within the enterprise.
