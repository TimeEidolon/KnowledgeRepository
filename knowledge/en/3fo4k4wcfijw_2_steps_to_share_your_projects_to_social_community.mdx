Want your meticulously designed projects to gain attention in social communities but don't know how to share them? Just follow these two key steps to successfully share your design projects in social communities~

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

**Entrance One:** Go to **Workspace - Projects**, select the project you wish to share, and click **Share**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777271302509/D32765A5D402158A0E4C9CE243AAD5B4.png)

**Entrance Two:** Go to **Workspace - Projects**, click the **Share to Community** in the upper right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777271361232/2311F87FC6575BCACC971284F28303D8.png)

**Entrance Three:** After entering the project details, click the **Share** icon in the top right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777271388504/6EBF6FD704F4587FA21B70A9F350C9F5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777271419015/5A3669C138663267A8BC698532366E41.png)

# Share Design Projects

In the popup window, select the social community you want to share with.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777271481284/D33068050EF733B83A98CCB5400D6634.png)

Check the plan you want to share and click **Next.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777271803971/28E04516BCE3A8B04A6CABD123F45360.png)

After setting the **Cover** and **Design Work Information**, click **Publish.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751618360637/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
