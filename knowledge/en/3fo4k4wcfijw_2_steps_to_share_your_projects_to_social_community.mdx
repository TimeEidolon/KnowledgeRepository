Want your meticulously designed projects to gain attention in social communities but don't know how to share them? Just follow these two key steps to successfully share your design projects in social communities~

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

Entrance **Entrance One:** Go to **Workspace - Projects**, select the project you wish to share, and click **Share**.
