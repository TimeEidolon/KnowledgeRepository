You may want to remove the ceiling of floor plan just like that in the **3D design project** while you are using the **Coohom Architect** tool in the **House Project**.

This article will show you how to make this change precisely.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754047797825/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**For Usesr: All Users**


Step 1: Proceed to **2D** **view** in the tool

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754046690774/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 2: Click the **room name area** to trigger the ceiling parameter in the right panel

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754046747619/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Please pay attention that you need to select exactly the room name to trigger that. If you just click the room space instead of that name , this may trigger the panel for slab parameter as the hotkey zone is set differently.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754046871283/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 3: Select **No** for **Has Ceiling** button to remove the ceiling

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754047470964/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
