**Furniture Sets** help designers to use combined furniture in the floor planner directly. Users can combine similar styles of different furniture as a furniture set.

1. Go to the **3D floor planner**.

2. Drag multiple furniture to your project.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908632972/137204311F69381D192E14A8AB57EB05.png)

3. Hold down **Shift** to select them.

4. Click **Group** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908635115/6378DC848690051041C90E9DAE73395D.png)

5. Next, click **Add to Favorites**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908636704/FCF1A260FD54BB53CBFC6718BC3FBCD7.png)

The liked group will show up in the **Enterprise Library > Furniture Sets > New Product**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908639078/AA864564AD94E882341D3F74B7CA9740.png)

6. **Edit** the information of the new furniture set. Name, status, preview image, tag, the price can all be edited.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908642136/5538BFB0ABD438DC144FC2E076D6FC6D.png)
