In Coohom, there are two ways to duplicate and delete design projects. This article provides a detailed explanation on how to duplicate and delete design projects.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Enter **Workspace-Projects**, select the project you want to copy, and click **Duplicate.**

# Duplicate Your Projects

## Method One

1. Enter **Workspace-Projects**, select the project you want to copy, and click **Duplicate.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574228934/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. The copied project needs to be renamed, after renaming, click **Confirm.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574271397/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. The new copied project will appear in the project list. Click edit to redesign the copied project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574309826/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Method Two

1. Select the project you want to copy, and click **Project Details.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574361109/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click the ... icon at the top right, select **Duplicate Project**, rename the new project, and click **Confirm** to successfully copy.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574401685/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574505786/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Delete Your Projects

## Method One

Enter **Workspace-Projects**, select the project you want to delete, and click **Delete.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574820481/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Method Two

After entering **Project Details**, click the ... icon at the top right, select **Delete Project**, and click **Delete** to successfully delete.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752574877191/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
