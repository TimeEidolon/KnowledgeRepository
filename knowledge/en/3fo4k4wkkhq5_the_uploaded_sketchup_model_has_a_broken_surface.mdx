If the model looks perfect in the SketchUp. But a broken surface occurs after uploading like below. We need to modify the model.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
If the model looks perfect in the SketchUp. But a broken surface occurs after uploading like below. We need to modify the model.

### Step 2
Model in Coohom Reason: The model will be reduced surface optimization in Coohom to ensure the operation performance of the tool. lf the poly of the model itself is less, it will lead to local broken surface. Solution：To add some poly to the structure of the broken surface in SketchUp to ensure the integrity of the model.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854765049/C6C4C6AAC620BE80E8205FEE4D6425F4.png)

### Step 3
In this case, a surface on the border has too few frames. We split this surface to make more poly face. Then error was resolved.
