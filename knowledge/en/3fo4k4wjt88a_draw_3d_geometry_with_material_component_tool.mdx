**Previously called Construction in Advanced tool, now its name has been changed to Material&Component tool.**

1. Draw a geometry with AutoCAD and download it.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842544938/FD52E57F5E41642F192AF993CCBF63C4.png)

2. Enter the Coohom tool and go to the Material&Component tool.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842547783/4ED52B17D88A4FEB439F7BFA241B738E.png)

3. Import CAD file.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842551703/C2DC48545493EB1AE035FB7D44826277.png)

4. Put the geometry on the desired area in 3D plan.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842554200/0C829DD0A836574668F1B560832259DC.png)

5. Ungroup the geometry.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842556828/875E9B6845EC2629B5ADEDC44FC1D1B0.png)

6. Using the Push/Pull tool, create the thickness.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842560135/291674E8D8D0D367D1E89EB82427138F.png)

7. Press on the shift button and click on left on mouse to select all geometry face, then Group.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842562817/6D5F0FFCBE97FEDC8E674D291D66FC0F.png)

8. Drag the texture onto Geometry.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842566771/49BDAFDC385A732216171AAB231B6E16.png)

9. Then can duplicate more Geometrys

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842570227/4215B230C41654E4FB3891C9E7B987B9.png)

10. That's it. You can render it and see what it looks like.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842576320/386131F497A3444CD74A8EDE6A44009E.png)
