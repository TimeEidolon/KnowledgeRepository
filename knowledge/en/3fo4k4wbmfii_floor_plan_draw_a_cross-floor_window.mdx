### Step 5
Adjust the height of the window opening and the distance from the ground. **Note: The position of the window opening on the second floor should be lower than the height of the second floor, if the thickness of the floor is 120mm, the height of the window from the ground should be -121mm. For example, the thickness of the floor is 0mm, and the height of the window from the ground is -1mm.**

# Draw a Cross-Floor Window

1. Enter the floor plan tool and drag the window to a suitable wall on the first floor.

* **How to draw a multi-floor villa, see the tutorial document: [[Floor Plan] Draw a Multi-Floor Villa](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/floor-plan-draw-a-multi-storey-villa?kjl_source=hc)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748339047028/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Switch to **3D mode**, and set the **height** of the cross-floor window in the parameters panel on the right.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748340313768/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Switch to the **second floor**, and use the wall **split** tool to cut out the location of the opening that needs to be drawn next according to the position of the cross-floor window in the lower floor.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748341189111/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1748342373084/8BE987E4EC875FFBA0DA8E228F6A9BCC.gif)

4. According to the position of the opening cut in the previous step, place the **window opening**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748342559665/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Adjust the height of the window opening and the distance from the ground.

**Note: The position of the window opening on the second floor should be lower than the height of the second floor, if the thickness of the floor is 120mm, the height of the window from the ground should be -121mm. For example, the thickness of the floor is 0mm, and the height of the window from the ground is -1mm.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748344915041/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Click **show all floors** to see the overall effect.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748344959151/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The cross-floor window effect is as follows：

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748334832488/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
