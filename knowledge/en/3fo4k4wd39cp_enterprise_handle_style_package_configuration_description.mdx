In interior design, if the combination of doors and handles is too free, it often leads to inconsistent styles or installation conflicts, affecting design efficiency. To make the design more efficient and accurate, this article will teach you how to configure handle style packages for doors, thus implementing installation restrictions so that a certain door can only match specific handles. This not only avoids inappropriate combinations but also allows you to quickly call the correct combinations in the design tool.

# Configuration Entrance

1. Enter the **workspace** and choose **"Enterprise Catalog - Custom Products - Product Catalog".**

2. In the product catalog, select **"Kitchen & Bath or Custom Furniture"**, click on the **door** to enter the product details page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758614921790/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. In the product details page, choose **Edit Attributes** under **Configuration****.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758615070861/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)4. In the pop-up panel, **add the handle style package** and click **Submit.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758615151870/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758615224147/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Application in the Design Tool

1. After configuration is completed, enter the design tool and click to enter **Kitchen & Bath or Closet Tools.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758616125921/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Double-click the cabinet door, click on the **Door Style** in the right parameter panel, **select the door where the handle style package was previously configured.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758616582236/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After replacing with the configured door, when adding a handle in the right panel, **you can only add handles from the handle style package, and cannot select handles outside the style package.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758616869899/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
