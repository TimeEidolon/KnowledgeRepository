The Closetmaterial library includes **Product Catalogue, Parts, Combinations and Materials**. Users can select the required Closet materials and add them to the design plan. You can adjust the size, material and style of the cabinet model and freely design the internal structure of the cabinet.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CQAwuDuCdQU)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

1. Enter the **Workspace** and select **Create** **New Project** in the upper right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779343695360/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After entering the interior design page, click **Advanced Tool**.

3. Select **Closet** to enter the customization interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763001679397/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Filter Materials

In the **Public Catalog**, you can choose unit cabinets, wardrobes, tatami, wall panels and other furniture materials for modular design and combination.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763001803270/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Set up Cabinets and Sizing

After selecting the cabinet model in the **Public Catalog** and dragging it out, **the parameter panel** can adjust the model parameters.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763001945249/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1763002043095/86584353388FEAEE807150ADD7F270CE.gif)

# Design the Internal Structure

## Add Panels

1. Select a panel from the **Parts**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763002111398/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click and drag the panel, long press and drag it into the cabinet, and the panel is dragged in.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763002193883/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1763002243775/DBD229A2618F64F7133FC63D4FCF4E67.gif)

## Add Doors

1. Select a door from the **Parts**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763002307208/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Drag the door onto the cabinet.

3. Select the four adjustment points on the door and drag them to the appropriate size. The door will automatically adsorb when it is close to the edge of the cabinet.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763002917059/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1763003005093/E8CB51DD282E8F91C375C5CC12BF339C.gif)

# Appearance Modification

## Style

1. Click on the cabinet whose style needs to be changed.

2. Select **Style** in the Parameters panel to replace the material style of the cabinet, door and handles.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763003916138/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Global Style

1. In the top toolbar, switch to **Select Cabinet**.

2. Select **Global Style** in **Global Edit**. 

3. Click on the cabinet, and select the material, hardware, etc. that need to be replaced in the parameter panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763004027530/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
