title: "how-to-adjust-brightness-of-tiles"
description: "1. Find the Tile Paving Texture in your Enterprise Libray."
---

1. Find the Tile Paving Texture in your Enterprise Libray.  
   Enterprise Catalog - Paving - Libray - Tile Texture  
   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930892875/ED378EA011737DE4EAE38249F4E22028.png)
2. Open the texture, find Properties Edit, use Advanced Settings.  
   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930895357/4B7F062E24183178722A3D6B0E5EB0EC.png)  
   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930897561/C0CA38D33C3726E94E33784E5B04A96F.png)
3. Change the Value of Advanced Brightness.  
   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930899467/0D1DB20D29240883F6E7FB80E15BE522.png)
4. Render with the right scene to test the brightness, and the render test could be save.  
   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930902483/5478F4D8ADE9D9CD902E7A169864E1A9.png)  
   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930905066/49921E8A0CB66B7BFD42D48D4B55B98F.png)
