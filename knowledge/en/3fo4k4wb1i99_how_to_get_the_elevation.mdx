Some users may wonder whether Coohom is having the elevation funtion. The answer is Yes. We will introduce how to get the elevation step by step in this article.

**Target User: All Users**


Step 1: Go to design tool to create a new project or access an existing project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743579948875/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 2: Select 2D>Elevation

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743580007076/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 3: Select a wall to show the elevation

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743580208571/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
