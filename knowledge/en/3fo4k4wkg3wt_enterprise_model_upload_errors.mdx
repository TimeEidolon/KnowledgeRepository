### Reasons & Solutions:

1. **Mapping is missing** (very common)

*Solution: Reset material editor slots.*  
​  
​**Reset Material Editor Slots** to prevent unnecessary materials from affecting the **Asset Tracking**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885772566/C3492C5A7B55E9D3F2560CC7D26333C8.png)

Press Shift+T to open **Asset Tracking** and check which files are missing.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885773538/88FB976203E3683C3FBC325A3F513FF1.png)

Select one blank multi-layer. Then, click **Eyedropper** tool to copy the material of the model. We find that the multi-layer does not display any materials.

Click **M** button after **Diffuse** and open the target mapping in **Bitmap**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885774558/E4BD4D744B9D87A81349494934949469.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885775530/5447CA18EE253BDE45B2A5639AA3CF19.png)

If you only want to adjust the path or the material name, you may change them in **Asset Tracking** directly.

Shift+T to open **Asset Tracking** ＞ **Set Path...** ＞ find your mapping ＞ **Use Path** ＞ **Convert to Editable Mesh** ＞ **Archive**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885778278/8FBB55E37278D97C7655072CB55639FD.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885779162/7524301F4463E32C2C594CAA39943CE5.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885781144/68DF043B595B4FC7A282FD6856E93FA8.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885782099/FC65D80DC7B1FE6AD1357E20BBA0A9CF.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885784122/8CE5D4E724C799A5EACD7FD982D3867B.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885786125/A7A77E828173C9C26279EE468C3C3E11.png)

**Archive** all required files concerning this model to a compressed package. If you copy this package to and use it on any devices, these mappings will not be missing.

​

### Notes:

* Coohom supports compress package uploading. You can use Archive function in 3ds Max to upload your file.
* Before archiving, you should toggle off Gamma and LUT ( **Customize**＞ **Preferences...**＞**Gamma and LUT**＞toggle off options).

Coohom supports compress package uploading. You can use Archive function in 3ds Max to upload your file.

Before archiving, you should toggle off Gamma and LUT ( **Customize**＞ **Preferences...**＞**Gamma and LUT**＞toggle off options).

​

### 2. Uploaded .max file without mapping

If the model does not need the mapping, you can just ignore it. But, if the mapping is needed, you should archive and upload the mapping with .max file together.

Open .max file to review your model. If the mapping is missing or path goes wrong, there will be no material mappings.

Find the target mapping and save it in the same folder with the .max file.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885786942/F661FE5019B2C85103AB31A3E635C49D.png)

Archive your file.

​  
​

### 3. Model’s normals have been flipped

We may encounter the preview problem as below after uploading the model successfully. This is caused by the normals in the model.  
​

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885787800/843A312203E34363474A8269E4DC3E4F.png)

*Solution*: select the model ＞ **Convert to Editable Poly** ＞ **Polygon** ＞ choose the normals in the model ＞ **Flip Normals**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885789543/09FCDD84D50D5E634D6B3CED5DFDF03B.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885791893/94A0646185D1407001DE943BA715DDAA.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885794337/B28D51C70CAFAD0C63D24ED28653A5F3.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885796696/8F536B5A5EA0C508919EDCD05B8E221B.png)

Finally, select your model and right click to **Convert to Editable Mesh**. Then, archive your file.

​

### 4. Diffuse ratio

*Solution: Readjust the diffuse ratio.*

Models with this problem may be demonstrated correctly during the preview step in the 3ds Max.

Under this circumstance, we can check that whether the multi-layer displays the material. Double click the multi-layer to preview. If the diffuse value is 10, just as shown below, the material might not be shown.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885797758/1EE21FBDDB6F33C87111AA48C08C3318.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885799836/E8009A680AA14B24284E6881358875E8.png)

In most cases, the diffuse ratio should be 100.

### ​**5. Render settings**

In this case, there are no problems with the model and mapping. You need to check your render settings.

**Render** ＞ **V-Ray** ＞ **Global switches** ＞ **Override mtl** ＞ toggle off.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885801652/A6B72717125B703337EA937212692F1A.png)

**Render** ＞ **V-Ray** ＞ **Color mapping** ＞ **Gamma** (adjust Gamma to 2.2).

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733885802495/DC118729AD14EA2BDB07F5967D781ADC.png)

When you finish, archive your file.
