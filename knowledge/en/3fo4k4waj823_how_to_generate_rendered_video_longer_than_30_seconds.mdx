Currently for manually generated roaming video, the length is less than 30Seconds. But for the video generated by video template, it can be over 30 seconds, so you can use the video template  to do it.

1- you can find video template option when entering rendering page

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742387487276/73C365C037EBFC7E3B2BBC4930172F42.png)

2- you can choose what you like from the three templates

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742387497154/5CA9561149CA6E437A9C27814215F678.png)

3- take a look at the preview![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245336313/E3F43CFE0F2B90D16566EF875C5384DA.png)

4- click confirm to start rendering the video![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742387575454/6001D8AB894E441B066577672132F3BD.png)
