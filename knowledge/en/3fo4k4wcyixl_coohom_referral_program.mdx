Dear Coohomers,

It's excited to annouce that **Coohom Referral Program** is online! By this program, users not only can get some free days of paid plan or free rendering coupons but also can gain some gift cards to purchase Coohom plan with quite favorable a price.

This article will introduce the process and some painful points users may meet.


## Users: All Individual Users

## Operation Steps

First, Go to Workspace page to find the **Refer&Earn** Button in the left corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1759053086550/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Then, Click the **Share** button to copy the link to send to your friend to create Coohom account via this exact link. That's all.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1759053187959/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Once your friends create the Coohom account, their emails will show here in the dashboard and your gift cards/rendering coupons will also show here. The gift cards will pop up while you pay the plan in the pricing page:

[https://www.coohom.com/pricing](https://www.coohom.com/pricing).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1759053372948/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## **FAQ**:

**Question 1**: Why do I only see 3 user emails in the dashboard while I invited over 5 users?

**Answer** 1: This is because some users may not create the Coohom account via the shared link. Hence, their emails will not show.

**Question 2**: Why does the dashboad only show 5 user emails while I invited more users to create account by the link?

**Answer 2**: This is because only 5 users can be invited within one month. Kindly control the invitation amount.

If you still have any further concerns, please feel free to contact Coohom Customer Service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
