**Background**

Currently, the official website of Coohom does not include a page that displays the projects designed by Coohom's users.  
​  
Coohom shares designers' projects on platforms such as Facebook and Instagram.

**Prerequisites**

Make sure that you have a Facebook account or an Instagram account.

**Procedures**

Facebook:

1. Log on to Facebook, enter [facebook.com/coohom](http://facebook.com/coohom) in the address bar of your browser, and press the Enter key to go to the Coohom page.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896797552/B092BCF194300E8455DEE94D88290064.png)

2. On the Coohom page, Click Follow to follow the Coohom page.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896800196/5410566C56DEACD70C8E6C3B276734CE.png)

3. Click **Photos** in the left-side navigation pane to view the designers' projects that we have shared.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896803603/9130AE9F1B94FF0090BA17B9FD3403D5.png)

Instagram:

1. Log on to Instagram, enter [instagram.com/coohom/](https://www.instagram.com/coohom/) in the address bar of your browser, and press the Enter key to go to the Coohom page.

Log on to Instagram, enter [instagram.com/coohom/](https://www.instagram.com/coohom/) in the address bar of your browser, and press the Enter key to go to the Coohom page.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896804284/08A40D489B5F31C2402694416CC91612.png)

2. On the Coohom page, Click Follow to follow the Coohom page.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896805102/736B4986B6E5639B3D05F78BCC222165.png)

3. Click the **POSTS** tab to view the designers' projects that we have shared.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896806629/2D5BE892FC9B29F7FD8E766A45409649.png)
