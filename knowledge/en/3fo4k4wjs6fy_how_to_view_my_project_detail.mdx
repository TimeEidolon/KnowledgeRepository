1. **[Log in](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)**with your Coohom account.

2. Click **Hi, [your name]** at the top of the homepage to go to the merchant platform.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843407858/92DC3271074646AD08AA969EAB01A808.png)

3. Select **[My Projects](https://www.coohom.com/pub/saas/apps/project/list)**in the left panel.

4. Hover your mouse over one project.

5. Click **View Project** to check your project detail.
