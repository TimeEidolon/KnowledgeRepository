### 【Inspiration Space】Support to Select Rendering Resolution

### **Overall**

The rendering speed of the Inspiration Spaces is determined by the resolution of the original image/panorama and the complexity of the models within the design. Therefore, sometimes users must wait a long time when rendering, making it difficult to view and adjust the effects in time.

Currently, we provide users to select the resolution of the rooms in the Inspiration Space. This allows users to quickly preview the display situation within the rooms in a short time, enabling them to make adjustments and evaluate the results quickly.

### **Aim User**

Enterprise User

### **Steps**

After users finish configuring their Inspiration Space and click 【Submit】， they can choose the rendering quality of the design. If they want to preview the effects quickly, they can select a lower-resolution

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855103624/67CC7B1E5A8BD5FE5BCD8602DCBC8DF6.png)

When users have finished previewing or want to upgrade the resolution, they can find the showroom in 【Not Published】, and select the resolution for rendering.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855104889/C4AD45F03E890EACDB498D8852269603.png)

### 【Inspiration Spaces】Support to Modify Model Size in Adjust Location Editor

### **Overall**

Users can adjust the size and position of the model location editor, this breakthrough in overcoming the limitations caused by model size allows customers to have more flexibility in their choice of replacement options.

### **Aim User**

Enterprise User

### **Steps**

Accessing the 【Adjust Location】 in the Inspiration Space, users can modify the length, width, and height of the models (currently only available for furniture).
