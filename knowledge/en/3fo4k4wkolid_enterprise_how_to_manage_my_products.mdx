1. Select **Enterprise Library** ＞**Products**＞ **3D Models** / **Finishes** in the left panel.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Enterprise Library** ＞**Products**＞ **3D Models** / **Finishes** in the left panel.

### Step 2
Hover your mouse over one product in the library.

### Step 3
Click **...** at the top right corner of the selected item.

### Step 4
Choose **Move to**, **Add to Photo Studio** or **Delete Permanently** in the drop-down menu.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733921409416/EE9488B4B380133CF1FCDED3EFF16C0B.jpeg)

### Step 5
Or tick the item you want to manage and edit in the toolbar. **Note**: You can also choose several items for bulk editing.
