1. Select **Enterprise Library** ＞**Products**＞ **3D Models** / **Finishes** in the left panel.

2. Hover your mouse over one product in the library.

3. Click **...** at the top right corner of the selected item.

4. Choose **Move to**, **Add to Photo Studio** or **Delete Permanently** in the drop-down menu.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733921409416/EE9488B4B380133CF1FCDED3EFF16C0B.jpeg)

5. Or tick the item you want to manage and edit in the toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733921412696/10E7A468B7F8FE8964F6CE5C3B3B39D9.png)

**Note**: You can also choose several items for bulk editing.
