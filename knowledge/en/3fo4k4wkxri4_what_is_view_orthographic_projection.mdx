* What is View Orthographic Projection?

### What is View Orthographic Projection?

The supported viewing angles are a front view, back view, left view, right view, top view, bottom view.

After clicking the model material, in the small triangle on the far right of the pop-up window, select [View Orthographic Projection] to switch the viewing angle of our selected model.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733898342244/D0FD4D69238B1521AA0F32F2D9B4866F.png)

Note: The default display is the perspective view effect, you can refer to the top view example in the figure below. The top view is shown as follows:

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733898343936/E2668E29EE345A8650C1C8168150CB6E.png)
