### Step 1
What is View Orthographic Projection? The supported viewing angles are a front view, back view, left view, right view, top view, bottom view. After clicking the model material, in the small triangle on the far right of the pop-up window, select [View Orthographic Projection] to switch the viewing angle of our selected model.
