## Why is the Layout Function Going Offline?

The decision to take the Layout function offline was not made lightly. After careful consideration, it has become clear that continuing support for this tool is no longer feasible due to limited research and development (R&D) resources. Here are a couple of key points that led to this decision:

**1. Lack of Updates for New Features**

As technology evolves rapidly, user needs change, and new functionalities are regularly introduced in various tools across our platform. Unfortunately, we have been unable to keep the Layout tool updated with these advancements. As a result, many users may find it lacking essential features that could enhance their overall experience.

**2. Unresolved Issues**

In addition to not being able to roll out new features, we recognize that there have been ongoing issues within the Layout function that have remained unresolved for some time. With our limited R&D resources diverted towards other priority projects and tools with more immediate user demand, fixing these issues has not been possible.

## What this Means for Users？

We understand that many users rely on the Layout tool for their projects and workflows. While we regret any inconvenience this may cause, we encourage you to explore alternative options available within our platform or consider other tools that can meet your layout needs effectively.

## Transitioning Away from the Layout Tool

* **Explore Other Tools**: We recommend checking out other layout or design tools available in our suite that may offer enhanced functionality and ongoing support.
* **Feedback Welcome**: Your feedback is invaluable to us as we continue improving our offerings. If you have suggestions for alternative tools or features you wish to see in future updates, please don’t hesitate to reach out.
