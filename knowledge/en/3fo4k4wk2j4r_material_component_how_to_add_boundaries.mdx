1. Select Material&Component in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853252993/F455D09F27978B747280D5238C04C950.png)

​  
2. Click the face you want to edit.  
​  
3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853255183/540FA909941AC3D00E1D37110FE9C0CD.png)

​  
4. Click the face.  
​  
5. Choose **Multi-Boundary** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853255966/1108290A11BC82404AC884DFBC4E68A2.png)

6. Hold down Ctrl to select only one boundary.

7. Edit in the floating toolbar or under **Boundary Settings** in the left panel.

8. Click **Done** to confirm.
