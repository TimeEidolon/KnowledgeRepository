This new feature would help to rename each of the Panorama in Gallery and the changes would be happen in real-time on 720 tour included generating, editing and broadcasting process.

1. Rename rendering pictures in Gallery. Click on the pointed column and rename it. Only 30 characters are allowed for each of the column.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733844178967/14A4F3B2D24A50378642FED6F0896D66.png)

2. After rename, it is easier to differentiate the pictures.
