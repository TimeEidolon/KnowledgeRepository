This new feature would help to rename each of the Panorama in Gallery and the changes would be happen in real-time on 720 tour included generating, editing and broadcasting process.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Rename rendering pictures in Gallery. Click on the pointed column and rename it. Only 30 characters are allowed for each of the column.
