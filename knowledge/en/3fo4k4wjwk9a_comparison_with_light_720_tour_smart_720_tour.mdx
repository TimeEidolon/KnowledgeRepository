Both Light 720tour and Smart 720tour are in Coohom now, users can generate either of them as needed.

**Floor plan navigator, enterprise logo, sharing options** are in both of the 720tour, but there are still some differences as the table attached below.

**DIFFERENT FEATURES**

**Light 720tour**

Jump to different rooms

Support multi-floor tours

Faster speed

Lower Computer Performance Requirements

More compatible with the browsers

**Smart 720tour**

Walkthrough different rooms

Support location lines

Better transition effects

You can generate different 720tours according to your preference.
