In Face Editor, we can use **Scheme** directly to pave tiles or floorings.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849954894/91FEF9E6C11E5F07E62082CED62DB827.png)

Also, we can map different finishes from Public Library, Enterprise Library, or My Account - Uploads. Just drag and drop the finishes to the area. The whole area will be filled with the same finishes. (Read More)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849956322/DEF98F73356E675F418977939865EE5B.png)

**Line** and **Rect** can help to divide the entire face to different parts.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849957859/532E92CEB6B34B289BECA72E0E8B8D27.png)

You can input the accurate value while drawing lines, then add different finishes to each area.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849959231/278A79BCB799CBEAEF1E910DC2253512.png)

**Measure** helps us to know the distance between two lines.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849961538/2802C2EA7F75731929D5129FFF3DD4DE.png)

**View** option is to select **Line Length** or **Line Spacing** shown or hidden in Face Editor.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849963112/7D495FB5D6A2E3B57DC9A18AEAB28473.png)
