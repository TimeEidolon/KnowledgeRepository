# Problem

When adjusting the rendering effect, it was found that after adjusting the bump parameters, the graininess became too strong.

# Solution

1. Reduce bump scale: You can try further fine-tuning in material editing during real-time rendering, customizing the adjustment of the bump scale of the normal map.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762134893640/8F3BDF68BFFEDA2569C8E146AD480476.png)

2. Adjust materials: Sometimes, the graininess may be related to the material itself. Try changing the material or adjusting other material properties, such as reflection glossiness or Reflection.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762134893531/3A64130960B7E4E7C452D6827CA04E17.png)  
3. Light source adjustment: Properly adjusting the number and position of light sources may affect the material's performance, alleviating graininess.  
  
4. Rendering settings: Check the rendering settings to ensure that there is no excessive sharpening or other post-processing affecting the material performance.
