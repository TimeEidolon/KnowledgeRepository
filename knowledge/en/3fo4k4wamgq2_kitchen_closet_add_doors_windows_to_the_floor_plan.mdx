In the room tools, there are a variety of doors & windows styles to choose from, which supports the adjustment of the size and position of doors & windows, and replace the doors & windows styles with one click to meet the needs of different doors & windows types in the floor plan.

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen & closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

1. Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **room**.

2. Select a **door/window**, there are three types of doors: **single door, double door, sliding door**, as well as three types of windows: **linear window, linear bay window, french window**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741844312864/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Add Doors & Windows

Select the **door/window** and drag it on the **wall**. The tutorial demonstration takes the window as an example:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741845609268/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Adjust the Doors & Windows Size

1. You can modify the **width, height, thickness and distance above the ground** in the **size parameters** in the right panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741846153181/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741846267530/9463A88B6C047FDFD8CE14C2D76D6D86.gif)

2. Click to **restore default** to restore the size before the modification.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741846373408/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741846592739/91E45DF27C58CA0FBAEE0D17AD9719C9.gif)

# Replace the Doors & Windows Styles

## Method One

In**2D mode**, switch different door/window styles in the right panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741846842000/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741847062654/2167EF7C379238A84BE64288BEAC7D20.gif)

## Method Two

1. Select the model and select **Replace** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741847180785/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Select the door/window style in the left panel to replace.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741847350672/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741847399191/58E97FD179135554518E2FE9A4E83B02.gif)

# Flip the Doors & Windows Position

1. Select the model and select **Flip** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741847652241/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. The flip function supports **left/right flip and front/back flip**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741847681999/C79A0AA898A441228FBF96005DACCA01.gif)
