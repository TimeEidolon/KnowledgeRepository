Flexibly controlling the show and hide of walls allows us to view the internal structure of the floor plan more clearly. Whether setting the show and hide settings for exterior walls individually or controlling the display of all walls as a whole, there are simple and easy-to-understand methods of operation. This article will explain in detail how to hide and show walls.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IZAfz88vi3o)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Hide and Show Exterior Walls Enter the design tool, switch to **3D Mode**, and click on the **eye icon** to choose whether to check or uncheck exterior walls. When exterior walls are checked, they will be showed; when unchecked, they will be hidden.

### Step 3
Hide and Show All Walls Enter the design tool, either in **2D/3D Mode** (demonstrated in 3D mode in the text), and click the **eye icon** to choose whether to check or uncheck wall in the structure. When wall is checked, all walls will be showed; when unchecked, all walls will be hidden.
