# Design Corner Bay Windows

1. Click on the **Floor Plan**.

2. Select **L-shaped/U-shaped bay windows** from **Custom** **Doors＆Windows** and place them on the wall.

* Tutorial demonstration takes L-shaped bay window as an example.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740735025516/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click on the bay window model to enter the **Doors＆Windows**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740736294454/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Select the bay window model, and the parameter panel on the right can adjust the specific parameters of the bay window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740736472133/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. In the **Style**, you can replace **windows, casing, jamb, window sill and material styles**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740736827128/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
