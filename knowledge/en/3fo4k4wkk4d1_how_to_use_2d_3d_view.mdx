### How to Use 2D View?

1. Click **3D** in the footer.

2. Drag the left mouse to rotate. Right mouse to pan. Use the scroll wheel to zoom in or zoom out your camera.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854925112/DB039C62DCB1FF7FD4CD0E887FB85702.png)

* How to Use 3D Walk

### How to Use 3D Walk

1. Click **Walk** under **3D** in the footer to enter the walkthrough mode.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854926654/A4024607C62164D2B9A1E5CC2D0573B0.png)

2. Hold down **W，A，S，D** in the keyboard to move the camera.

3. Drag to rotate and check details from the first-person perspective.

4. Use **Q** and **E** to adjust the camera height.
