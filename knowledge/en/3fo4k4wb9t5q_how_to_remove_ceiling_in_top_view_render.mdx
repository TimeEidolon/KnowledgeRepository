Normally, users can see the whole furnitures in **Top View** as the ceiling structure will be invisible by default. However, some users will meet issue when doing the **Top View** render as they can still see the ceiling and cannot see the funitures inside.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745309721811/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Target Users：All Users


The reason for the display of ceiling in the **Top View** render can be as below:

* Users may have added some ceiling furniture models from public library
* Users may have done ceiling modeling in the material&component or custom modeling tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745319542407/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

To remove the ceiling in **Top View**, users need to hide the ceiling furniture or modeling before rendering.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745319819966/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
