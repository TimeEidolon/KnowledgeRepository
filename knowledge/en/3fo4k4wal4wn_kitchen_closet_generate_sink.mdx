# Entrance

Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **kitchen**.

**Entrance One:** Select the **Generate-Sink** from the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742279647509/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Entrance Two:** Select the cabinet, and select **Generate-Sink** molding in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742279691605/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Generate Sink

1. After entering the generate sink interface, select the cabinet that needs to be generated. In the basic parameters on the right panel, select add **Sink Style and Tap Style**. Determine the **Installation Type**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742279812511/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742279919187/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After selecting the appropriate sink style, modify the sink margin at the **Spacing Mode**, and then click **Generate**.

**Note: If the generate button turns gray, you need to check whether the cabinet that needs to be generated is selected!**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742280008714/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After generating the sink, click **Finish** to exit the generate sink interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742280067067/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Generate Double Sinks

1. Click **Add Sink**, select the **Sink Style and Tap Style** of Sink 2 to determine the **Installation Type**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742280107892/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742280142018/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Modify the sink margin at the **Spacing Mode**, and then click **Generate**.

**Note: If the spacing input box turns red, you need to determine whether the cabinet can lower two sinks, and the distance cannot be negative!**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742280250306/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
