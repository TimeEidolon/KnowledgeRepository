# Problem

Abnormal light strips or bright edges appear at corners, floor seams, or ceiling edges.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762136031450/43CA081BC2833BA37D008814AE7F1E1C.png)

# Solution

1. Check if there are openings or gaps between walls in the imported model.

2. Open the light leakage repair in the lighting version.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762136031416/DAA19F2E6CD423718B5AFF13A6B650A3.png)
