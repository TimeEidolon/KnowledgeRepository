**Target Users**

All enterprise users

**Before**

For enterprise users, in order to use the same texture across materials such as furniture, flooring, K&B, and Closet, it is necessary to upload and create this texture separately for each of the furniture, flooring, and parametric models, which can be very time-consuming.

**After**

Now, you only need to upload the texture image to the Original Material Catalog, and you can simultaneously publish a single texture across various industry lines including furniture, flooring, K&B, and Closet. This greatly reduces the time and effort required for material maintenance.

**How to use**

**Enable Functionality**

You need to contact your Customer Success Advisor to try or purchase services related to the Original Material Catalog.

**Usage Steps**

1. Create a new material and publish:

Once the functionality is enabled, you can directly create a new original material.

1.1 Create Original Material:

Enter the Original Material Catalog and click on [Create Original Material] to access the material creation tool.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392661303/05128472D79DEB58B5DEEBA9C26CBC24.png)

1.2 Replace Material Texture:

First, select the type of material from the left panel. Then adjust the material’s texture and color properties based on specific requirements in the right panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392663339/5D9606CBF208A91AF7035DEDE9EC0E74.png)

1.3 Save Original Material:

After completing property editing, you can click save and fill in any remaining material information.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392667012/16B8F52E016E2E3C4766A3B76D32D5EF.png)

1.4 Publish Original Material:

Once the original material is created, it cannot be used directly in tools; you need to select these original materials and publish them to the required industry lines.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392670988/77352CA578BA5723956260A3ED4CA8DC.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392672536/2EC8C1B353009A2C2C40C2645928C12B.png)

2. Extract from Existing Materials:

You can also extract materials from existing ones into the Original Material Catalog and send them to other industry lines.

2.1 Select a Non-Original Material:

From any industry line in your enterprise library, choose one or more existing materials. Click on bulk operations and select “Material Extraction.”

The following images show how materials are extracted within furniture, flooring, and K&B.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392674378/A7113253E291C27DBEB859B24DF55A68.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392677573/1B212D41BF4885C08769B8BDE1A22F7A.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392680530/4ABE10AFA1F7E8A942D228CDCB59E03B.png)2.2 Publish Original Material:

After extraction is complete, you can enter the Original Material Catalog, find the corresponding original material, and publish it to the required industry lines.

For detailed steps refer to “1.4 Publish Original Material.”
