To help you manage and utilize your design results more efficiently, please read this guide for using the gallery of images. Whether you need to download or delete single or multiple images, share your images, or edit and restore accidentally deleted images, this guide will show you step by step the key operations.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UiVtmgZ0q7o)

# Entrance

**Entrance One:** Enter the interior design tool page, click the **Gallery** on the top toolbar to enter.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760612713750/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Entrance 2:** Enter the **Render** tool, click the **Gallery** at the top right to enter

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765175744294/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Download or Delete Images

1. After entering the gallery, you can click on the top left to select **"List"** or **"Large View".**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760667734682/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1765175744306/57D6B239C4AF39F2F9130DD3F1DB30AC.gif)

2. Click the toolbar in the rendering to download or delete a single image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760668277027/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click **List** to select multiple images for download or deletion.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765175744133/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Share Images

Click the toolbar in the rendering to share your images with others in the form of a QR code or link.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760668416261/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1765175744100/476F1822775EB51DE5A67AE4F26004C1.gif)

# Favorite Images

Outstanding images can be favorited by clicking the favorite button in the rendering, which can be viewed in the left **Favorite** sidebar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760668016728/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1765175744155/3AB74445393B3E184177DF62E8947710.gif)

# Edit Images

Select **Large View**, and on the toolbar below, you can save the perspective, beautify the image, and upgrade image clarity.

* **Note:** Upgrading image clarity cannot be undone.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760669319992/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1765175744282/3AD3F3106D2924AC75ADE92878865E6C.gif)

# Restore Deleted Images

Click the **Trash** on the left sidebar, select the images you want to restore, and click **Restore.**

* **Note:** Renderings in the trash are only kept for 15 days and will be automatically removed after 15 days.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760670606502/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1765175744450/3426BCE174D892DB19A0FF8712B95472.gif)
