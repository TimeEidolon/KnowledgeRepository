#### 【Photo Studio2.0】Support to Generate AI Video

**Overall**

Users can directly choose their favorite style template, and AI will replace the models in the video to quickly generate beautiful videos!

**Aim User**

Enterprise

**Steps**

Enter 【AI Video】, choose a video template you want to generate, and click on 【Capture the same style】

Preview of the AI video
