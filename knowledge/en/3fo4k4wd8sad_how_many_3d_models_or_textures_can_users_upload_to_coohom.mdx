Coohom is a powerful online interior design and visualization platform that enables designers, architects, and hobbyists to bring their ideas to life using 3D models, textures, and smart tools. One of the most frequently asked questions among new users is: **How many 3D models or textures can I upload to Coohom?**

The answer depends on your account type. Coohom offers different upload limits based on whether you are using the **free version**, a **Pro/Elite plan**, or an **Enterprise account**. Below is a breakdown of the upload allowances:

### 1. Free Basic Users

If you are just getting started with Coohom, the free basic account allows only a limited number of uploads.

* **Maximum Uploads:** 10 models and 10 textures.

This restriction is designed to give new users a chance to explore Coohom’s features while encouraging them to upgrade if they need more creative flexibility.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778312000505/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### 2. Pro or Elite Users

For professional designers or anyone who requires a wider range of customization, the Pro or Elite plans offer significantly higher limits.

* **Maximum Uploads:** 100 models and 100 textures.

This upgrade is ideal for freelancers and design enthusiasts who want to expand their library with custom 3D assets and textures without the constraints of the free version.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778228309814/EF5880D5C0AA4C53059F5BF78726F9FF.png)

### 3. Enterprise Account Users

For large teams, agencies, or enterprises working on multiple projects, Coohom’s Enterprise account offers the most generous capacity.

* **Maximum Uploads:** 2,500 models and 2,500 textures in the personal library.

This level of access ensures that design professionals can manage extensive projects, maintain a large collection of assets, and collaborate effectively without worrying about upload limits.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755859461028/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

For any further concerns, please feel free to send email to [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
