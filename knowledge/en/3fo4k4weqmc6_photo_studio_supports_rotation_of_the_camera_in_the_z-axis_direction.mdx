# **Feature Introduction**

## **1. Feature Access**

* Access 3D Photo Studio - Right-side resource panel ‘Camera’ - Select the camera to adjust the Z-axis - Scroll down to ‘Rotation’ - Enable Z-axis

* + The Z-axis of the camera is disabled by default and needs to be manually activated.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773395402168/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Z-axisAdjustment Method:Z-axiscan only be adjusted by entering specific values in the operation panel on the right side (cannot be directly adjusted with the mouse on the canvas).

* Z-axis animation case operations:

[![
](//qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/newt/1144/image/png/1549879747859/B0556275535F7BF4FEF2DCC4F752A054.png)](https://qhstaticva-cos.kujiale.com/media/yun/help/video/UID_16001f97_a67b_4fa9_1773397424498.mp4)

## 2. Special Notes

* Both free cameras and target cameras support rotation along the Z-axis.

* Free cameras supportcontrolling Z-axis rotation through constraint relationships.

* + Applicable Scenario: The Z-axis of the cameraobject will rotate according to the corresponding constraint rules and the operations of the target object.
