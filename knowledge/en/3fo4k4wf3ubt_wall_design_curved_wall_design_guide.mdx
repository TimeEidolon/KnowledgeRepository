In the wall editor tool, you can use tools such as rectangles, chamfers, and texture mapping to quickly create an curved feature wall and achieve the desired design effect.

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Select the wall surface and click to enter **Wall Editor**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096681/58442DE83EB56F138DDD40E2FC991698.png)

# Curved Wall Design

1. Enter the **Wall Editor** page, use the **Rectangle** tool in the top toolbar to draw the base shape of the feature wall according to the design requirements.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096244/E6DFC698C9EF4DFB4788F9581F64EFCE.png)

2. Then hold down the **Shift** key, select the wall surface that needs to be set in an arc, and click on the **Chamfer** option in the floating window. Choose the **Outer Fillet**, input the **Radius** as required, and set the **Extrusion** value for the designated wall surface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096305/F4F1F0930D78DE09B50BE1A875DADF7A.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096507/21C56E0CEF2C22596F240EF58BC9855C.png)

3. Click **Wall Decor** from the left sidebar, select **Paints & Coatings** from the top, choose **Green** from the **Color...**, and select the appropriate material to place in the target area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096348/7DD38F84D0B9FF7B61E126F825CC4DAF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096510/A025D2ABFE4246368AEAB553631289B3.png)

4. Continue within the **Paints & Coatings** module, select **White** from the **Color...**, and select the material you want to place in the remaining area of the wall.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096401/D488D8064F7A1018B293DD1810DA6786.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096401/A7D0436FDD37F00EF5E8D6582E0F33D3.png)

5. Next, select modeling line, enter **Edit** **Elevation**, and choose **Wall Decor** from the left sidebar. Select **Paints & Coatings** from the top, choose **White** in the **Color...**, and apply the required materials to the elevation areas. Then click **Done**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096560/F899420504F4AC89D5CEF4AE2CC6A1C3.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096499/3909E2980041186C8923CDA4BC805C4A.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096417/726C911E3845457F41694E8C5F7A4105.png)

6. After the setting is completed, select the set modeling line, click **Duplicate** in the floating window, and **Paste into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096546/F63129366A66B5B6D24CC4D5CD452F09.png)

7. Once the setup is complete, you can drag and enlarge the upper-right view window to check the design outcome and see if adjustments are needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096703/4AC746A66F28EF8660B6E71C2CA8706F.png)

8. If no adjustments are necessary, you can click **Exit with Save** at the top-right corner, and after rendering, you will obtain a satisfactory result image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096675/507D0203A7A14B6D691AC4D9948CC3FB.png)

* The final result is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776739096713/24617BAD4C0318B722B51D57017EFEAA.png)
