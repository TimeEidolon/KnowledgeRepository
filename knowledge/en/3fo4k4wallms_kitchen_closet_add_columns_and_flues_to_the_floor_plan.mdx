In the room tools, there are three structures: columns, beams, and flues. After adding columns and flues, the size and position can be adjusted, which is convenient for designers to restore the floor plan structure.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Enter the kitchen＆closet design and choose the **room**.

### Step 2
Select a **structure**, there are three structures: **columns, beams, and flues**.

### Step 1
Move the mouse to the lower right corner of the column, and the selection bar of **square column and circle column** will appear. You can choose by yourself.

### Step 2
After clicking the **column**, drag it into the floor plan and place it in the corresponding position.

### Step 3
You can directly drag and drop the adjustment points on the **column** to modify the size.

### Step 4
You can also modify the **length and width** of the column in the **basic** parameters in the right panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741781472014/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 5
Adjust the column **height**, you need to switch to the **custom** in the parameter panel, and then modify the value.

### Step 1

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen＆closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

1. Enter the kitchen＆closet design and choose the **room**.

2. Select a **structure**, there are three structures: **columns, beams, and flues**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741779870348/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Add Columns

1. Move the mouse to the lower right corner of the column, and the selection bar of **square column and circle column** will appear. You can choose by yourself.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741781075042/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After clicking the **column**, drag it into the floor plan and place it in the corresponding position.

3. You can directly drag and drop the adjustment points on the **column** to modify the size.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741781348738/4A2B66F955E5E0CBD74AFE7F1E6459E9.gif)

4. You can also modify the **length and width** of the column in the **basic** parameters in the right panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741781472014/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Adjust the column **height**, you need to switch to the **custom** in the parameter panel, and then modify the value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741781617232/6FA4E8D396F51AC7A536AFC219CEFE14.gif)

# Add Flues

1. After clicking the **flues**, drag it into the floor plan and place it in the corresponding position.

2. You can directly drag and drop the adjustment points on the **flue** to modify the size. You can also modify the **length and width** of the flue in the **basic** parameters in the right panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741781811876/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Adjust the flue **height**, you need to switch to the **custom** in the parameter panel, and then modify the value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741781896484/292D1CB6F9C0651EB96A4AD690F3F6BA.gif)
