If you forget your password and can no longer log in to your account, follow these steps to help you quickly reset your password.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Visit the page: [Click to Visit](https://www.coohom.com/user/forgetpassword?kjl_source=hc) and enter your email, then click the **Continue** button.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1752822087673/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

### Step 2
You will receive a **4-digit verification code** in your email. Enter the verification code and click **Verify**. * **Note:** If the verification code expires and shows **Incorrect Verification Code**, you can click **Resend Code** below, and a new code will be sent to your email.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761215915183/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

### Step 3
After entering the new password you want to set, click **Reset Password** to complete the password reset.

# Main steps

1. Visit the page: [Click to Visit](https://www.coohom.com/user/forgetpassword?kjl_source=hc) and enter your email, then click the **Continue** button.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1752822087673/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. You will receive a **4-digit verification code** in your email. Enter the verification code and click **Verify**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761215915098/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752822446581/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Note:** If the verification code expires and shows **Incorrect Verification Code**, you can click **Resend Code** below, and a new code will be sent to your email.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761215915183/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. After entering the new password you want to set, click **Reset Password** to complete the password reset.
