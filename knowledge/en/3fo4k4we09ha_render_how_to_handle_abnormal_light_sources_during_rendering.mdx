# Problem

The wall has light and shadow, the ground has light and shadow, or there is a blue light source at a certain position, and the surface light and ceiling overlap causing shadows and other issues.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135272598/BCCC5B64072D0F821B4822BE71DFCA13.png)

# Solution

Enter Custom lighting, check if there is a light source at the corresponding position, and delete or adjust it as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135272610/150BB9B2F596979081B2932B95D1688E.png)
