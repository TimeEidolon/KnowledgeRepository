Do you want to say goodbye to the monotony of bathroom walls and create a sophisticated sense of layering? We can add a ceramic tile molding in the edit paving of the background wall design tool to enhance the design of the wall and effectively improve the spatial texture.

# For Users

All Users

# Entrance

Select the wall and click to enter **Wall Editor.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887711/A0251F9BA9D191327FA156F3733079CA.png)

# Ceramic Tile Wall Design

1. After entering the **Wall Editor** page, click on the wall, and in the floating window that pops up,select**Edit Paving.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887514/EB1B8D710321692C215818424D1833E2.png)

2. After entering **Edit Paving**, select a wall and click the **Chair Rail Molding** button in the floating window to add it with a single click.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887476/870DBA9DD26F6A510208D2CDD7434F12.png)

* You can also choose the **Common Accessories** section in the left panel to add a **Chair Rail Molding**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887532/A5014716E31ACBC063728B34B315E3AB.png)

3. After selecting the **Chair Rail Molding**, adjust the **Height Above Floor** and **Pattern** as needed in the left panel. Once done, click the replace button for the **Brown...**, and then select suitable material in the panel that pops up.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887537/957D0CFBDEB90F78E4E35F10EBF0C5D8.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777271292806/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. After configuring the **Chair Rail Molding**, click **Duplicate** in the floating window, move the mouse to the desired position, and click the left mouse button to complete the duplication.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887563/55BBAE65DC9FFC22843E17FD700F1610.png)

5. Select the newly copied **Chair Rail Molding** and modify the **Height Above Floor** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887512/F95D2D9330A449BA70BD8C5F3412DDAD.png)

6. After configuration, click on the **Model Catalog** in the left panel, select **Tiles**, click **White** under the **Color...** tab, and choose suitable material to place in the target area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887647/46E115A15B4030513A29FBA048EB4566.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887699/92120615114332FD38169E01E2D370BD.png)

7. Then select the tiles that have been placed, click **Set as Paving Scheme** in the floating window, choose **Grid**, and modify the**Gap** and **Pattern** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887523/A5B6EEAF469CC71F9588C7F124380567.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887544/8008615DF947913E5C1C45393AB2EFD8.png)

8. Next, select the tile area, click **Add Start Point** in the floating window, and configure the starting point based on design requirements.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887719/22B893042FB39ED28DAE7E94998313F5.png)

9. Click the **Model Catalog** in the left panel, select **Tiles**, and place suitable material in the desired area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887565/C28359A75BABFBDBB8467AA99F14B1CD.png)

10. Select the material, click **Set as Paving Scheme** in the floating window, choose **Grid**, and then use the **Rotate** function in the floating window to adjust to a suitable angle. After setting, click **Add Start Point** in the floating window to make adjustments.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887557/FA0231C8BD1216582A6EBE18D945CB2E.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887668/43E0B8C28BE73992987264538C908173.png)

11. After tiling is completed, modify the**Gap** value in the left panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887522/926A0108468A746ECD557B44D710426D.png)

12. After all settings are completed, click **Exit with Save** in the top right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887694/DE8DC2DF2DB75BE113D163A7CE8BA9B5.png)

13. Then you can enlarge the top-right view window by dragging it to observe whether adjustments are needed. If no adjustments are needed, you can click **Exit with Save** in the top right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255887656/9337D8F7F5FF1A3323D55CEC4C37477B.png)

14. Finally, select the entire wall you have designed, click **Copy** in the floating window, move the mouse to other walls, and when **Click Copy to the Face** is displayed, click to complete the wall design for the entire space. After rendering, you can achieve a satisfactory effect diagram.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1777255888052/AC4E02550946979BC5AACF14763570DA.png)
