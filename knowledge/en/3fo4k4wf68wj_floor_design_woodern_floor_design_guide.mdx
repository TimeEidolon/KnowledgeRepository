In the floor editor tool, by selecting wooden floor materials and flexibly adjusting the paving method and various parameters, you can quickly complete floor designs and achieve ideal results.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8MYEZptqM5Q)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
On the floor editor page, drag the upper right corner window to enlarge it as needed to allow real-time preview during subsequent operations.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775716184457/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 2
Click **Flooring** in the left sidebar, select the required floor type at the top, and then click the desired material to apply it to the floor.

### Step 3
Click the floor, and select the desired laying method in the **Paving** section of the left panel.

### Step 4
In the **Control** section, you can adjust the laying position,angle, andgap value attributes of the floor.

### Step 5
In the **Pattern** section, you can directly modify dimensions. If you need to change the floor style, click the replace button to select suitable materials for replacement.

# Woodern Floor Design

1. On the floor editor page, drag the upper right corner window to enlarge it as needed to allow real-time preview during subsequent operations.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775716184457/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Flooring** in the left sidebar, select the required floor type at the top, and then click the desired material to apply it to the floor.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775716691862/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click the floor, and select the desired laying method in the **Paving** section of the left panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775717305271/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. In the **Control** section, you can adjust the laying position,angle, andgap value attributes of the floor.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775717853209/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775717999022/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

5. In the **Pattern** section, you can directly modify dimensions. If you need to change the floor style, click the replace button to select suitable materials for replacement.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775718210537/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. After completing the setup, click **Exit with Save** in the upper right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory render image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775718468640/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The final effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1775724727122/3CCDB03F5351A5CA9FAFDD8EA44AA2CB.jpg)
