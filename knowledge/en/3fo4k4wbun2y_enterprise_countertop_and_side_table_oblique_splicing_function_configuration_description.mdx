The splicing methods of the countertop and the side table are mainly divided into two types: direct splicing and bevel splicing. After adding the edge type, it can be further subdivided into three ways: the edged straight splicing, the edged oblique splicing and only the edged oblique splicing. This function supports the effect and docking ability of the edged oblique splicing, so it is called oblique splicing, and there are the following design scenarios that may be encountered:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Scenario One** | **Scenario Two** |
| For different high and low cabinet scenarios, there may be a situation of high and low oblique or high oblique and low straight splicing, as shown in the section view of high oblique splicing and high and low oblique splicing below. | The oblique splicing is more beautiful in appearance, after fine polishing, the side seams are basically not visible, and the shape of the front edge is also more coherent, as shown in the following edged oblique. Due to the mature cutting process combined with the better visual effect of oblique splicing, many enterprises tend to use oblique splicing when splicing platforms and side tables, and the application of straight splicing is relatively rare. |

**⭐By using the oblique splicing function, the following effects can be achieved:：**

* Countertop styling function to cover more design scenarios that are in line with the actual countertop process.
* Optimize the data structure to make it more in line with the production and processing logic of the industry, and improve the integrity and ability of the output data to production.
* Through the service countertop, the customer is connected to achieve more freedom in modeling design.

# FAQ

**Q1:**After oblique splicing, does the quotation and JSON output size of the side table include the beveled part?

**A1:**The dimensions of the flat side countertop (customsize field) will include the beveled part, and the front edgesize (lengthWithAllowance field) of the flat side countertop will also include the beveled part.

**Q2:**After oblique splicing, how to express the outline of the countertop and the path of thefront edge in JSON?

**A2:**We have added the countertop projection area, the projection path of the front edge, to show the outline and path of the beveled part, and added the installAngle marker, which is marked as 45 for the inward oblique cut and -45 for the outward oblique cut.
