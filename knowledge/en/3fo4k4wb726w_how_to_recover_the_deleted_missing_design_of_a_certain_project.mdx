Have you ever faced the frustrating experience of losing a design element in your Coohom project? Whether it’s an accidental deletion or a sudden disappearance, there’s no need to panic! Coohom has a built-in feature that allows you to recover previous versions of your project easily.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
**Navigate to Your Project**: Open the specific project where the design went missing.

### Step 2
**Access Restore History**: Click on the **File** menu located at the top of the interface.

### Step 3
**Select Restore History Versions**: From the dropdown menu, choose the option to view your restore history. This will display all previous versions of your project. By selecting an earlier version, you can effortlessly recover any lost designs and restore your project to its former state. With Coohom’s intuitive restore feature, recovering missing designs is quick and straightforward. Don’t let minor setbacks derail your creativity—take advantage of this handy tool and keep designing with confidence!

#### Recovering Your Design

If you notice that certain design elements are missing from your project, simply follow these steps:

1. **Navigate to Your Project**: Open the specific project where the design went missing.
2. **Access Restore History**: Click on the **File** menu located at the top of the interface.
3. **Select Restore History Versions**: From the dropdown menu, choose the option to view your restore history. This will display all previous versions of your project.

By selecting an earlier version, you can effortlessly recover any lost designs and restore your project to its former state.
