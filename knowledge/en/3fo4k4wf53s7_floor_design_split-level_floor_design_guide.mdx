In the floor editor tool, users can divide floor areas as needed. By setting height differences, applying multi-material paving, and adding ambient light strips, they can achieve personalized customization of floors for staircases, platforms, tatami mats, etc., easily creating layered and texture-coordinated floor effects.

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Select the floor and click to enter **Floor Editor**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776156752489/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Split-level Floor Design

1. After entering floor editor page, use the **Add Guides** tool to draw auxiliary lines, then use the **Rectangle** tool at the top to draw a rectangle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776157712345/5BE693C2A037151FD75670ED2B8C2A1C.gif)

2. Select the target area and set the **Extrusion** value in the bottom right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776157810751/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click the **Flooring** on the left sidebar, select the desired type at the top, and then choose suitable materials to place in the inner circle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776157938180/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Click the **Tiles** on the left sidebar, select the desired type at the top, and then choose suitable materials to place in the outer circle. Add the material to **Add to Favorites** for easy reuse.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776158071927/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776158212504/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Next, select the contour line and click to enter **Edit Elevation**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776158154426/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Click **My Account** on the left sidebar, select **Favorites**, drag the material to place it on the elevation, and then click **Done**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776158620844/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

7. After setting is complete, select the configured contour line, click **Duplicate** on the floating window, and then **Paste Into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776158855214/E621DA89292319CD0682934CAE911ACF.gif)

8. Select the contour line again and check **The Indented Lamp Belt** on the left sidebar. Adjust the **Brightness** and **Color** of the light strip as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776159613424/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

9. After setting is complete, select the configured contour line, click **Duplicate** on the floating window, and then **Paste Into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776160048721/224070BBD0FEA231C555DBA16061712F.gif)

10. Next, drag to enlarge the view window in the top-right corner to see the effect and check whether adjustments are needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776160464224/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

11. If no adjustments are needed, click **Exit with Save** in the top right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory effect image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776160553521/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
