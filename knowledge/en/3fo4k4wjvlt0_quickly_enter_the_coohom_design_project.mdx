# Open Your Design Projects

## Method One

1. **[Log in](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin?kjl_source=hc)** to your Coohom account.

2. Click on the upper right corner's **Workspace.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752655438155/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. On the **[Projects](https://www.coohom.com/pub/saas/apps/project/list?kjl_source=hc)** page, hover your mouse over the project you want to edit.

4. Click **Enter the Project.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752655544924/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Enter the selected design project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752656212566/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Method Two

1. After entering the **Workspace**, on the **[Projects](https://www.coohom.com/pub/saas/apps/project/list?kjl_source=hc)** page, select the design project you want to open and click **Project Details.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752656000233/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click the upper right corner's **Enter Tool.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752656068469/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Enter the selected design project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752656206834/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
