Want to create a closet that is both practical and elegant? This guide will take you to build your ideal storage space from scratch! Users can select the required Closet materials and add them to the design plan. They can adjust the size, material and style of the cabinet model and freely design the internal structure of the cabinet.

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen＆closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

1. Enter the **workspace** and select the**Kitchen＆Closet Design**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779701668973/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the **Start Design** interface, you can select the **Design Templates** and click **Apply**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745224754585/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Enter the kitchen＆closet design and choose the **Closet**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742809493140/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Filter Materials

In the **Public**, you can choose unit cabinets, wardrobes, tatami, wall panels and other furniture materials for modular design and combination.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742548850190/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Set up Cabinets and Sizing

After selecting the cabinet model in the **Public** and dragging it out, **the parameter panel** can adjust the model parameters.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742809764366/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1742809883382/D24AF99A36DFF3213DFC9D7A229B3883.gif)

# Design the Internal Structure

## Add Panels

Click and drag the panel, long press and drag it into the cabinet, and the panel is dragged in.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742810445922/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1742810386955/836D5DB4A18C3AB6D181DF82CD04AFA9.gif)

## Add Doors

1. Drag the door onto the cabinet.

2. Select the four adjustment points on the door and drag them to the appropriate size. The door will automatically adsorb when it is close to the edge of the cabinet.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742810716545/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1742810737517/1616C05C8C878F40AF6DF7A8BABBD51B.gif)

# Appearance Modification

## Style

1. Click on the cabinet whose BodyStyle needs to be changed.

2. Select **Style** in the Parameters panel to replace the material style of the cabinet, door and handles.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742810826597/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Global Style

1. In the top toolbar, switch to **Select cabinet**.

2. Select **Global style** in **Global edit**. 

3. Click on the cabinet, and select the material, hardware, etc. that need to be replaced in the parameter panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742810901893/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

After completing the above basic operations, the next step is to imaging, drawing and quotating~

# **Notice**

 In the kitchen & closet design, **the material /wall /ceiling /floor editor is not supported**. If you need to use this function, you can choose **Create Blank Project** tools to create a project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779703833180/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
