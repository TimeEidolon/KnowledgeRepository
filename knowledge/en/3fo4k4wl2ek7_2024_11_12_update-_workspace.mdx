**Target Users**

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
**Target Users** All Coohom enterprise users (Limited-time free offer) **Before** Previously, sub-accounts in the enterprise were unable to submit their favorite models to the main enterprise account, which hindered the dissemination of high-quality models within the organization. **After** Now, as long as you have enabled the relevant features, sub-accounts can submit models to the enterprise model library. **How to use** **Main Account - Enable Features** You need to contact your Customer Success Consultant to obtain the relevant permissions for the “Favorite and Submit” feature (limited-time free offer). **Main Account - Grant Sub-account Permissions** You need to go to “Account Management,” select the target sub-account, and assign it the roles of “Contributor to the Enterprise Catalog models” or “Automatic approval of models submission”

### Step 3
**Sub-account - Submit Models** As long as the sub-account has the relevant role permissions, they can find the related features under [My Favorites] in Coohom. By clicking on it, they will see all their historical favorites, then select the corresponding model and submit it to the enterprise library.

### Step 4
**Main Account - Review Model Submissions** The main account can review submissions in the enterprise library under Material Submission Management by selecting the corresponding model. If a sub-account has the role of “Submission Auto-Approved,” then no review is needed from the main account, and their submitted models will go directly into the enterprise library.

All Coohom enterprise users (Limited-time free offer)

**Before**

Previously, sub-accounts in the enterprise were unable to submit their favorite models to the main enterprise account, which hindered the dissemination of high-quality models within the organization.

**After**

Now, as long as you have enabled the relevant features, sub-accounts can submit models to the enterprise model library.

**How to use**

**Main Account - Enable Features**

You need to contact your Customer Success Consultant to obtain the relevant permissions for the “Favorite and Submit” feature (limited-time free offer).

**Main Account - Grant Sub-account Permissions**

You need to go to “Account Management,” select the target sub-account, and assign it the roles of “Contributor to the Enterprise Catalog models” or “Automatic approval of models submission”

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392552189/D9CA4484A5E4EB27F709ED8F5CB26FFC.png)

**Sub-account - Save Models**

Sub-accounts can select corresponding models to save in the 3D design tool.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392558159/74C9D3F70468D3E7D00B3D1B8DD62125.png)

**Sub-account - Submit Models**

As long as the sub-account has the relevant role permissions, they can find the related features under [My Favorites] in Coohom. By clicking on it, they will see all their historical favorites, then select the corresponding model and submit it to the enterprise library.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392560005/50B24CD1FE09BB2B52D580686168272F.png)

**Main Account - Review Model Submissions**

The main account can review submissions in the enterprise library under Material Submission Management by selecting the corresponding model. If a sub-account has the role of “Submission Auto-Approved,” then no review is needed from the main account, and their submitted models will go directly into the enterprise library.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392562101/14FFD09569627D9F3CDEA7DEA0635091.png)
