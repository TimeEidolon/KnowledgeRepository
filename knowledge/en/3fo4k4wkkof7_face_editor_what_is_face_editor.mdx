Face Editor is essentially a series of editing and 'paving' on the face. Functionally, it can divide the area, parametric paving, tiling, free paving, and making boundaries and waistlines.

# How to go to Face Editor?

**There are three ways to use face editor:**

1. Once you click on a wall/floor/ceiling in 3D model, you will see Face Editor in the floating bar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749456992587/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. When you go to Material&Component.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457057503/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Click on a wall/floor/ceiling in 3D model, you will see Face Edit in the floating bar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457075937/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3.Click on a wall/floor/ceiling in 3D model, you will see Wall Editor/Floor Editor/ Ceiling Editor in the floating bar. Click on it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457091909/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Then you will see Face Editor.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457107880/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

These are three different ways, but to do the same edit: Face Editor.

We can modify different faces of the room here.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457122717/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Note: To go back to Face Editor from Floor Plan, follow Step Two and Step Three mentioned above.**  
​![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457137962/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
