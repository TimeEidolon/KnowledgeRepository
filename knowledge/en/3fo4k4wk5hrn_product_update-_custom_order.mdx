### Step 2
You can find all the operation records of customer orders, including the content of the operation, the time of the operation, and the operator. In addition, the Design ID will be marked in Remarks. Custom orders provide the ability for merchants to configure custom fields for order review, and some custom fields may be used by merchants for system docking and feedback writing. If the value of a field that is written back is modified when resubmitted, it may cause data errors. Therefore, this update adds a configuration item that allows custom fields to be configured to not allow manual modification when resubmitted. For part of Enterprise customers. Enter the custom order management background → order settings → custom information, click "Edit" or "Edit Field", and you can find a new configuration item 【Allow Modification】.
