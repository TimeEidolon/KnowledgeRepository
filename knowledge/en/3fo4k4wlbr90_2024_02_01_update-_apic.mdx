### **【APIC】Supports Configuring Multilingual Onboarding Guides**

#### **Overall**

Since each business user may have different usage scenarios and language requirements, Coohom offers customizable configuration options for onboarding content and language to meet the specific needs of users. The goal is to help businesses provide new user onboarding guides for APIC, assisting users in quickly understanding the basic functionalities of the tool within a short period. Additionally, Coohom allows for displaying different onboarding content in various languages.

#### **Aim User**

Enterprise

#### **How to configure?**

1. Specify the desired language
2. Provide the desired onboarding content to be displayed
3. Submit your requirements to CSC, and the IT from Coohom will contact you to assist with the configuration
