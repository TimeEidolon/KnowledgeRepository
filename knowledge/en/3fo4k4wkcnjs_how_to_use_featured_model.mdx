### How to use

1.[Click here](https://www.coohom.com/model-library) to go to the [Featured Model Page.](https://www.coohom.com/model-library)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733888617726/C03038B20A7A0581D5F93442BE59FB37.png)

2.View and select one volume that you need.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733888621898/2001E83665D05D42198581208F368FC6.gif)

3.View the models in the volume. When you find models you need, just **add them to favourite**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733888623510/3846C2079B70DA54C4C4DC9F613F6DEE.gif)

4.After you add the models to favourite, you can **get back to design tool** to use them. Click **My Account** on the left side bar. Click **View All** to view the models you have. **Drag them** to where you need them in your design.
