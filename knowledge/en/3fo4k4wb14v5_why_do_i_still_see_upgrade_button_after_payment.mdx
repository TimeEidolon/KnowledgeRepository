Some paid users will come to service team to complain that their accounts are not upgraded after payment or they still see the upgrade button although their account is of Pro or Elite status. This artilce will clear the confusion for most users. 

## Target User: All paid individual users


**Type 1: PRO account**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743589655484/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)


**What is the reason for this?**

In fact, Coohom offers business plan(Enterprise account) and individual plan(Pro and Elite). Whatever individual plan a user purchases, he can still upgrade until the highest level of Enterprise. That's why the Upgrade button always shows.

Hence, users do not need to worry as long as the purchased plan is well updated. If the purchased plan is not updated, please reach Customer Service team to gain further help via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
