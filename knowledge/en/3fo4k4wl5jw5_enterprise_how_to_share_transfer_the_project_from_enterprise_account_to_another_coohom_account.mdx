Dear Coohomer,  
  
The Enterprise account will enable to share your project to another coohom account with copy/edit access. Here are steps for your reference.   
  
Step 1,  Proceed to **My Projects** page to find the project you want to share.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733931796094/1CEF41314A6EC5FDC8B9FC8681F3F480.png)

Step 2, Set the project's access type, valid period and password before copying the link.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733931800990/D5D37B336D679D45BFA154938C95E077.png)

Step3, Paste the link to the broswer that is logged in with another coohom account.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733931802064/7C497238624CC1795F68DC406FF40CC0.png)

Step4, Open the link and then click **Copy to my projects** button.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733931805565/090D845D5F7A7740FBE516EDC87BB792.png)

Step5, Click **Go to Projects** button so that you can see the project in another coohom account.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733931806427/B025985C563607AC99B7883524129F6C.png)
