In the floor plan tool, users can use the flip function to mirror the floor plan symmetrically, supporting floor plan and model following flipping, helping users quickly generate symmetrical floor plan decoration schemes, reducing the time cost of repetitive design. This article will provide a detailed explanation of how to use the floor plan flip tool.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

# Restore to the Previous Design

If you need to restore to the previous floor plan effect, there are two ways to restore:

**Method One:** After successfully flipping the floor plan, click **Restore** in the prompt window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1754645987179/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

**Method Two:** Select **"File - Restore History Versions"**, and you can restore to the previous floor plan effect.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754645270469/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
