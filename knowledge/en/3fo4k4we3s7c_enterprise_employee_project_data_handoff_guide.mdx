After an employee leaves, the enterprise administrator can use the employee project data handoff feature to batch transfer and copy the projects of employee A to employee B. This document will explain in detail how to use the employee project data handoff feature.

# For Users

Coohom enterprise account main administrator

# Prerequisites

Only the main administrator of the enterprise account can use this feature, and they need to switch to the new version of the workbench page to operate.

# Entrance

Log in to the merchant account, select **"Account Management - Employee Management"**, enter the **Employee Management** page, click **Employee Data Handoff,** you can choose **Initiate the Handover** or **Handover****Records**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763617906671/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1763617839007/F51E2A6946908AE7A4DFA5E37F6705FF.png)

# **Initiate the Handover**

1. Click **Initiate the Handover**, in the pop-up **Initiate Handover** panel, select the account to handover and the account to receive the projects. After completing the settings, click **Sure**. The system will automatically calculate the number of projects to be copied and transferred, as shown below:

* The system limits the maximum number of projects to transfer in a single session to 2,000.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1763618092755/800BA7DC7B28AA7954D71141591C0F7C.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763618730402/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763618826841/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After clicking sure, the system will ask you to confirm again. Once confirmed, the pop-up will close, and the transfer process will begin.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763618950430/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1763619015938/10CCD7C0945CF1F9BCD7D96619A8A776.png)

# View Handover Records

Click **Handover Records** to view the transfer progress. When the progress reaches 100%, it means the project transfer is complete.

* The speed of the transfer depends on whether other merchants are occupying the transfer channel. Transfers are queued in order of initiation time. Under normal circumstances, the transfer can be completed within the day, but if there is congestion, it may take until the next day (T+1).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763619186854/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
