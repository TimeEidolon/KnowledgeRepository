* What is AI Templates?

### What is AI Templates?

AI Templates is an automatic design application that relies on a large number of Coohom users’ excellent designs. With the templates + algorithm, designers and even sales who don't understand the design are able to quickly come up with wonderful designs.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843808835/E940226C016068BE2552AD351C142B92.png)

* Characteristic

### Characteristic

**1. Upgraded algorithm capacity**  
with better effect, the lower failure rate  
​**2. The more flexible application**  
supports to apply soft decoration, construction, single room, and whole-house separately  
​**3. Perfect supporting capabilities**  
supports templates evaluation, data dashboard

* How to access AI Templates?

### How to access AI Templates?

In the design tool, there is a button in the left navigation bar. AI Template-Style, AI Template-Ceiling are for AI Templates.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843812895/705763743F7765938ABB2B090E3A0D0A.png)  

* Step-by-step illustration on How to use AI Template

### Step-by-step illustration on How to use AI Template

1. Create a floor plan, then click AI Templates on the left navigation bar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843816328/29E2AEDE507FFF4C4805C6100721B67A.png)

2. Select Types for AI Template: AI Template-Style, AI Template-Ceiling

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843820585/6812F241B4E5259AA04B335EE6C053A2.png)  

3. Select a library, enterprise catalog (only for enterprise account),public library, or personal library. Filtering tags may help you to find a more suitable template.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843822607/8EBB4B3BC8D99D9F273B59B041C3D4B8.png)

***Meaning of different tags.***

**Room Type**: More than 10 kinds of room types are allowed to select, and you can choose the room type you want to use AI template for.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843825485/13C979C873D3431E320F10E071A3AABD.png)

**Style**: More than 20 kinds of styles can be selected. You can just choose one that fits your selected room best.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843828406/3BD612981C49C1957F824D6BD08ECC0B.png)

**Area**: There are 6 area measurement for you to choose to match your selected room.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843830135/B868C51E1151D0F686F2AEE5A011A560.png)

4. You can set layout before applying or you can just click Apply button to proceed with AI template directly. (PS: Currently Kitchen is not suppporting layout setting. )

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843832068/5D3319B4A1CE72E0F89BE1BE2EB638A0.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733843833949/9EC2FDBBAE07FF85199DC7B6F88C1B53.png)
