If you are an enterprise user, you can export quotation lists for Kitchen & Bath, Custom furniture items of a project in just a few clicks.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

### Step 1
Enter the project that you want to work on. In the left-side navigation panel, click Advanced Tool. In the Advanced Tool panel, click Kitchen & Bath or Closet. No matter what you click, you can export quotations for any of these 3 item types as long as they exist in your project.

# For Users

**Enterprise users** (the solution includes production capabilities). Only the main account can set a quotation list template. Sub-accounts can use the function of exporting quotation list. If you need custom configuration, you can contact CSC for consultation.

# Entrance

1. Enter the project that you want to work on. In the left-side navigation panel, click Advanced Tool. In the Advanced Tool panel, click Kitchen & Bath or Closet.

No matter what you click, you can export quotations for any of these 3 item types as long as they exist in your project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749725225607/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Then, select among the 2 options, which are Kitchen & Bath and Custom Furniture, for the quotation you want accordingly.

You can search for any info on the quotation by typing in a keyword in the text searching box.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749697227241/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

You can adjust the View Mode to display quotation details on multiple pages pieced together with a long scroll bar or on individual pages that can be switched to and fro.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749725613997/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

7. You can add extra details such as Installation fees directly on this page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749697314163/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

8. If you want to edit or fill in fields, such as Customer, Contact Info, Sales Representatives, etc, **you can click Save on the top-left corner of the page and export the quotation in PDF, Word, or Excel format to your local device.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749697332529/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
