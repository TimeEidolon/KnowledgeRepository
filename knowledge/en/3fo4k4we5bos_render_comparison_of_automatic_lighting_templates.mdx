### Comparison of Automatic Lighting Templates: Interior, Ultra-Fast, and Real-Time Series

Automatic lighting templates are categorized into three series based on effect, speed, parameters, and materials. The specific advantages and differences are summarized in the table below:

Note: Each automatic lighting template has its unique advantages and differences. Users can choose the most suitable one based on their needs.

Apart from these three series, all other lighting templates will soon be discontinued.
