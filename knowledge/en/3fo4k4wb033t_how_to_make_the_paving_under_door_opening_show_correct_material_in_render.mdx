Recently, our user comes to service team to report that their floor texture under door opening is not showing correctly. 

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744021323212/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Target users: All Users**

**Reason:** This is a historic logic by floor plan. Coohom product team is in the process of optimizing this feature. 

**Resolution**: There are two alternative options to resolve this. 


**Option one**:

* Select the floor patch to go to floor editor
* Set the extrusion value as 1.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744021902774/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Option two:**

* Select the floor patch to set as threshold stone
* Then apply the material again

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744022070566/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Here is the final result.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744022802491/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
