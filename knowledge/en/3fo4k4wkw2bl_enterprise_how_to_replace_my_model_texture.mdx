To replace the texture of a model, you may:

1. Select **Enterprise Library** ＞**Products**  in the left panel.

2. Click one model in the library and go to its product info page.

3. Click **Component Texture > Click here.**

4. In the **Component Texture**window, **Click here to enter.**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392412354/15F55356E27547A0D1827C1650309037.png)

5. Click **New Component** to create a new component for editing.

6. **Edit** or **Delete** your newly-added component in the right panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392416404/CBE3FB0820EAFCDB76347B7846601A5E.png)

7. Click **Upload** to import textures for this component.

8. Choose files via **Select from Local** or **Select from Library**.

9. Tick the target texture and click **Save**.

10. Or delete textures by clicking the "**x**" at the top right corner of every texture.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392422427/B07404D450F9B427067C07D8D1C84DE9.png)
