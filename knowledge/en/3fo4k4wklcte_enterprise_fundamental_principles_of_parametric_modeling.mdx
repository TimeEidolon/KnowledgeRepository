### Step 1
Parametric modeling ​ Parametric modeling is a modeling method that changes the model by setting parameters during modeling and quoting the parameters to the model to change the parameters. The parametric model has three dimension parameter variables W, D, and H, corresponding to the width, depth, and height of the model, and a material parameter variable CZ. The size of the model can be changed by changing the parameters W, D, and H; the material of the model can be changed by changing the parameter CZ. ​
