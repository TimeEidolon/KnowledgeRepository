In the Material & Component tool, you can create custom ceiling templates and categorize them according to room types. Different rooms can apply the custom ceiling templates with one click, helping you design ceilings more efficiently and quickly. This article will explain in detail how to create custom ceiling templates.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0MpV1N-Dq1Q)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

Enter the Ceiling Editor tool, after creating the ceiling, select **"My Account - My Styling"**, click **Save as Ceiling Template.**

# For Users

# Entrance

**Entrance One:** Enter the interior design interface and select **"Advanced Tool - Material & Component"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746771661772/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

After entering the Material & Component tool, click on Ceiling, select **Ceiling Editor.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1755236612007/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

**Entrance Two:** Switch to 3D Mode, click on Ceiling, select **Ceiling Editor.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755229078003/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Create Custom Ceiling Templates

1. Enter the Ceiling Editor tool, after creating the ceiling, select **"My Account - My Styling"**, click **Save as Ceiling Template.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1755236611839/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. Choose the **Room Type** for saving the ceilingtemplate, and **name** the template. After finishing the setup, click **Done.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755229411056/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1755236611809/A3F9CDCCE78228F3C7C3CE2B91179E78.gif)

3. The created ceiling template will appear in your styling list.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755230245110/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Apply Custom Ceiling Templates

1. Choose another room, enter the Ceiling Editor tool, select **"My Account - My Styling"**, click **Apply.**

* **Note: The room size applied with the ceiling template needs to be greater than or equal to the ceiling template size.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1755236611825/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. After clicking apply, you can adjust the ceiling according to the room size. After adjustment, click **Done.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1755236406593/1FB30339B9E9B73A46F714445B61C709.gif)
