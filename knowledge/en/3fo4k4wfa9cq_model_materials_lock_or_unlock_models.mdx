In the design tool, users can lock, unlock, and lock the ground clearance of finished models to ensure that models are not accidentally moved or deleted during editing, while maintaining the flexibility for adjustments.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LRYhN807EVw)

# For Users

All Users

# Lock Models

# 

1. Select the model and click the floating window's **Lock** button.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774506565675/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Once locked, the model cannot be moved or deleted, effectively preventing accidental operations. However, users can still modify basic parameters (such as **Size** and **Angle)** through the property panel on the right.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774506629273/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774506798220/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Unlock Models

# 

Select the locked model and click the floating window's **Unlock** button. Once unlocked, other operations can be performed as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774506830876/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Note: Lock and unlock tools can be applied to individual models, furniture combinations from the models, and multiple selected models.

# Lock Model Distance to Slab

Select a model, and click the **Locking** button next to**Distance to Slab** in the property panel on the right. After locking the distance to slab, dragging the model with the mouse will only move it horizontally, the height will remain unchanged.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774506982034/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1774508230183/51844A4D0040326E06973E3DD4ACE8B9.gif)

* When not locked, the distance to slab changes during movement, as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1774508254909/A5103715D99933353788DA96625BF3FF.gif)
