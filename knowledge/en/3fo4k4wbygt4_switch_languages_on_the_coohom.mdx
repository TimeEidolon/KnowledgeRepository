Are you looking to use Coohom's website in your preferred language? Switching the website and workspace language can help you browse more comfortably and access information more easily. In this guide, you'll learn how to quickly switch the language on the Coohom website and workspace by following simple steps.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

### Step 1
If you wish to switch the language of the Coohom website, you can go to **the bottom of the page** and click on the corresponding **language icon** to switch.

### Step 3
For certain countries or regions, Coohom also provides localized languages and content. * If you choose the language which belongs to the localized sites such as English (India), not only the language display will be switched, but the overall layout and content recommendation of the page will also be adapted according to user habits. * If you choose the language which belongs to the global site, only the language display will be switched, and the overall layout and core function modules of the page will maintain the unified style of the global site and will not change. In the future, Coohom will gradually offer localized content for more countries and regions. Stay tuned!

# Switch Languages on the Coohom Website

1. If you wish to switch the language of the Coohom website, you can go to **the bottom of the page** and click on the corresponding **language icon** to switch.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749807712049/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After clicking the icon, you can select the corresponding language to switch. 

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1749807574504/E07D6B82BDEE1A9A9C9D6F2D4E58BE1F.png)

3. For certain countries or regions, Coohom also provides localized languages and content.

* If you choose the language which belongs to the localized sites such as English (India), not only the language display will be switched, but the overall layout and content recommendation of the page will also be adapted according to user habits.
* If you choose the language which belongs to the global site, only the language display will be switched, and the overall layout and core function modules of the page will maintain the unified style of the global site and will not change.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750042705834/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

In the future, Coohom will gradually offer localized content for more countries and regions. Stay tuned!

# Switch Languages on the Workspace

1. On the workspace page, hover the mouse over the avatar in the upper right corner, and then click **Account Settings**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765440073931/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the **Account Settings** page, click **Edit** next to **Preferred Language**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1767949247950/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. In the popup panel, select the language you want to switch to, then click **Save**, after the setting is completed, the tool interface will switch to the language just set.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765440281525/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
