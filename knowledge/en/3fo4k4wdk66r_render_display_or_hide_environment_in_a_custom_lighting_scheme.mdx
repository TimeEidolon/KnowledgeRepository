When creating custom lighting schemes in render tools, you can set environment to display or hide. This article will explain in detail how to display or hide environment in a custom lighting scheme.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CULNn-tC1uQ)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Enter interior design, and select **Render** from the left toolbar.

# For Users

All users

# Prerequisites

You must first create a custom lighting scheme and enter the lighting scheme to set the display and hide for environment.

# Display and Hide Environment

1. Enter interior design, and select **Render** from the left toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761292948304/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After entering the render page, click **Add to Custom Lighting** from the **Basic Settings** column on the left, select a basic lighting template, and click confirm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761647792183/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Or click **Edit** in an existing custom lighting scheme to enter the custom lighting scheme.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761647935667/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click the **Eyeball** icon below, check **Environment** to display it, uncheck to hide it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761648252417/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1761648476394/61511D92028114EB19FDF68CD9B0D329.gif)
