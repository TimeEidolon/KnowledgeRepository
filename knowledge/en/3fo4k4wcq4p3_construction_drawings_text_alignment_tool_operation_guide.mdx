In construction drawings, you can use the text alignment tool to quickly and automatically align text, improving drawing editing efficiency, as well as the aesthetics and readability of text information in drawings.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qugJdKIaz\_k)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Entrance Enter the 3D design, select **Drawings - Construction Drawings**, and click **Edit Views**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750748257782/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 2
Align Text Annotations Enter the **Edit Views** page, click on any drawing with **text annotations,** hold down **Shift/⇧** to **multi-select text annotations** in the drawing. At this time, the **text alignment tool** will appear in both the right-side properties panel and the floating toolbar, and you can choose according to your preference.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754549021745/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 4
There are six modes of alignment: **right align, center horizontally, left align, top align, center vertically, bottom align.** After clicking and selecting the alignment mode you need, the text will automatically align.
