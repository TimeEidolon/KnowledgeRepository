When designing a project, you can quickly access commonly used models through shortcuts, reducing the time spent searching through the model catalog on the left, and improving design efficiency. This article will explain in detail how to use the quick material tool to quickly access models.

* This quick material template distinguishes between public templates and custom templates, allowing users to switch freely according to their needs.
* You can create multiple quick material templates, allowing users to add up to 5 sets of templates to correspond to different quick material access scenarios.

# Quick Material Templates

## Public Templates

The public catalog is the default template and does not require individual creation or modification of quick models. Users with customize configuration needs for quick models can use custom templates, detailed configurations are provided below.

**Usage Method One: Directly drag out a model**

Drag the quick model directly from the quick material panel into the design scene.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753869347062/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Usage Method Two: Use shortcuts to bring out the corresponding model**

Press the **shortcut****s (F1, F2, F3...)** marked in the quick material panel to bring out the corresponding quick model and place it in the design scene.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753869502193/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Custom Templates

You can customize configure the quick models in the template, adding up to 5 sets to correspond to different quick material access scenarios.

**1. Customize Configure Quick Models**

**a. Select Quick Models**

Click on the shortcut blank area, choose the catalog where the model you want to add is located, and then select the corresponding quick model from the catalog.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753869685601/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753869714440/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* An operation demonstration is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1753869771738/911B2F84F2A7DD0A7E968EF54EE80656.gif)

**b. Modify Quick Models**

Hover the mouse over the quick model you want to replace, click the replace button, and select the new quick model.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753869899652/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**c. Delete Quick Models**

Hover the mouse over the quick model you want to delete, click the delete button, and the selected quick model will be removed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753869940036/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**2. Create Multiple Custom Templates**

Hover the mouse over the avatar symbol and click **Add a New Template**. Up to **5 custom templates** can be added.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753870108302/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The name of the custom template can be modified. An operation demonstration is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1753870303936/F3A55462E391FB3B677A6605673BA0D1.gif)

**3. Switch between Custom Templates**

You can switch between multiple templates by clicking in the template bar or using the shortcuts **(Win: Alt+Q / Mac: ⌥+Q)** for a quick cycle switch. An operation demonstration is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1753870542261/4263A48A0FA1620C8589AC4C36D74B5D.gif)

## Switch Quick Panel

The quick panel can switch between **public templates** and **custom templates**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1753870607395/5B32E20F65297C4F44BEDB76A48E643A.gif)

## Collapse Quick Panel

If the quick panel is not needed, you can click the **collapse** button to collapse the quick panel. To use it again, click the **expand** button to open the quick panel for use.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753870691483/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1753870734429/80A747969FC7951AC387350650958D60.gif)

# Notes

1. The default template in the toolbar will remember the user's last selection. If you are accustomed to using the custom template, the default template will be the custom template the next time you open the toolbar.

2. If enterprise users close the entrance to the public catalog on the left sidebar, the public catalog template in the templates will not be displayed.
