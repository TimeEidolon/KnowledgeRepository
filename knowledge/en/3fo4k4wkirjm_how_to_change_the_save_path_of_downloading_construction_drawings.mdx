**Step 1:** Go to Google Chrome browser.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855280606/3C2FDBDB429E7B74A85C903517F34201.png)

**Step 2:** Go to three dots on top of right hand side corner > click **Downloads**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855281651/5B472DEE8F259E400A6189DB18E4BF30.png)

**Step 3:** Construction drawing can be found from download history.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855283795/43F04D98598D5FA85AEC67CBF6FB8DE0.png)

**Extra: If** to change the default download path, follow below steps. Go to browser settings.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855285749/875E72672D9E079428C8C77B5FF3B3CF.png)

**Step 5:** Click **Change**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855288203/24A447152102065C5556CAB73C38B394.png)
