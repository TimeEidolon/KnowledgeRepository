The Doors＆Windows material library includes **Whole wood doors and windows, Aluminum door and window library and Materials**. Users can select the required Doors＆Windows materials and add them to the design plan. You can adjust the size, material and style of the Doors＆Windows models.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zWekMycqJwo)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

1. Enter the **Workspace** and select **Create** **New Project** in the upper right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779343695360/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After entering the interior design page, click **Advanced Tool**.

3. Select **Doors ＆ Windows** to enter the customization interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763015900597/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Filter Materials

In the **Public Catalog**, there are Whole wood doors and windows, Aluminum door and window library and Materials. You can choose the Doors＆Windows materials that meet your design needs.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763015963148/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Set up Doors ＆ Windows and Sizing

Take the **whole wood doors and windows** as an example, After selecting the Doors＆Windows model in the **Public Catalog** and dragging it out, **the parameter panel** can adjust the model parameters.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763016319304/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Appearance Modification

## Style

1. Click on the doors and windows whose DoorsStyle needs to be changed.

2. Select **Style** in the Parameters panel to replace the material style of doors, moldings, panels and handles.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757494690508/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The demonstration of setting the door is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757494231937/236A5CC2D59C26F8C234148336354658.gif)

* The demonstration of setting the window is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757494392407/B326EF594ADFCE6AC83056D7D3816C69.gif)

## Global Style

1. In the top toolbar, switch to **Select Cabinet**.

2. Select **Global Style** in **Global Edit**. 

3. Click on the doors and windows, and select the material style of the Doors＆Windows to be replaced in the parameter panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757494614297/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
