# Solution

Therefore, to effectively reduce our color differences, we must perform the following actions:

1. Ensure that the processed tiling material is color corrected against the real product on the same computer.

2. To upload, choose new material upload (batch upload selects new material category, individual creation defaults to new material effect), rendering light should be chosen as natural realistic under realistic conditions. If manual adjustment of lighting is needed, it must use natural realistic under realistic conditions as a base template to add or delete lights.
