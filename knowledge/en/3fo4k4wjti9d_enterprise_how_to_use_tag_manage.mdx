Tags help users to sort and filter their products easily.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Tags help users to sort and filter their products easily. Customizes your tags and adds them to your products in the detailed information page. ​**Merchant Platform** > **Enterprise Library** > **Product Info** > **Tag Manager**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842261555/9731996D9B447FBE57F4EC53C63841C9.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842261555/9731996D9B447FBE57F4EC53C63841C9.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
**Create Category** > **select Type** > **add Tags > Confirm** ​ *Single Type* means only one tag in this category can be selected for products. ​ *Multiple Type* means you can select some of the tags for one product.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842264870/72868EBFBAF326B588F781BB787D13A0.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842264870/72868EBFBAF326B588F781BB787D13A0.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
Go to Enterprise Library > Products > Furniture/Finishes Select one product to edit its information. Select Tags as you created before.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842269156/61DE593FCC0E474A0A6FF705EBE43389.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842269156/61DE593FCC0E474A0A6FF705EBE43389.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
Go to Floor Planner, you can filter the products with Tags.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842273334/BC02BED3767FF2E4790D5F264CE990B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842273334/BC02BED3767FF2E4790D5F264CE990B5.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />
