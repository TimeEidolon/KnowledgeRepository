Account Management only appears in ***enterprise-level*** accounts. Manager and admin accounts can create new accounts for the teams.

​

### Create a new enterprise account

1. Select **Account Management** in the left panel.

2. Click **New Account** to create a new account.

3. Fill in all required information.

4. Click **Create**.

### Manage enterprise accounts

1. Select **Account Management** in the left panel.

2. Click **Edit**, **Reset Password,** or **Delete Account** in the drop-down menu under **Action** to manage the accounts listed on this page.
