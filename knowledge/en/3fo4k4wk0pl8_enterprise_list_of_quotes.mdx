Introduction: Different enterprise users may have different quotation systems, and thus need different quotation templates. Therefore, Coohom helps enterprises to customize and configurate your own templates.

### Steps:

### 1. Make a quotation template

A template is made by special devices and then imported into Coohom system. It needs training, so please consult engineers and custom services for more details.

### **2. How to configurate quotation templates**

* Go to Merchant Platform ＞ Enterprise Catalog ＞ Custom Furniture ＞ Advanced
