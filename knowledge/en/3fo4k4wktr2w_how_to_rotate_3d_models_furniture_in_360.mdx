### Overview

You can rotate custom cabinets and decorations in 360°.

When you work on a project, you often need to place a model in the scenario at a specific angle. You may also need to rotate the model to turn it into another material. In these cases, rotation around axes is needed.

### Steps

1. Enter the project that you want to work on. In the left-side navigation panel, click Custom Design. In the Custom Design panel , click Closet.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907474320/D9D9F58A1F38B2ED589E7AD5733D7A11.png)

2. Select a piece of Closet and drag it into the 3D scenario. You can adjust the basic parameters in the right-side pane or simply use the default values. A custom wardrobe is used here as an example.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907475889/BE2C81E06E48270CA4D1D40EE79C0ED0.png)

3. In the popped tool bar, hover the cursor over the Switch Control icon on the left. From the drop-down menu, select Rotate.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907477187/7BB062C6E6CB21B5589309FD79F3985D.png)

4. Then, three colored arrows appear. The arrows stand for the 3 axes around which you can rotate the Closet 360°.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907478638/8B83AB718E43D05A5E9C870476F706B8.png)

**Rotate around the X axis**

As you rotate, some parameter value changes. In this example, the distance from the floor is affected.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907480256/98D5A501827B8FE1784EA438F964A98A.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907481905/F1AD2091C95A264DEB372DACDFFEB7C0.png)
