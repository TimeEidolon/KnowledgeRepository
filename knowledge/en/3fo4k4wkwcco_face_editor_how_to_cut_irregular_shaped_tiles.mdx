1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

### Step 2
Click the face you want to edit.

### Step 3
Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

### Step 4
Drag the target tile under **Public Library** ＞ **Tiles** from the left panel to your floor plan.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899119151/923BB56214449E02DE0B48447F7C5DB2.jpeg)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899119151/923BB56214449E02DE0B48447F7C5DB2.jpeg" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
Click the tile.

### Step 6
Click **Tile Processing**＞ **Cutting** ＞**Irregular shapes** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899121804/6BEAA16D4301A0287AD9F2E2478E1C18.jpeg)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899121804/6BEAA16D4301A0287AD9F2E2478E1C18.jpeg" alt="Step 6" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 7
Edit its shape, angle and edge lengths.

### Step 8
Click **Confirm**. Note: You can add this shape to Favorites. Find it in **Tile Processing** ＞ **Cutting** ＞ **Irregular shapes** ＞**Shape** ＞ **My Account** next time.


2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

4. Drag the target tile under **Public Library** ＞ **Tiles** from the left panel to your floor plan.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899119151/923BB56214449E02DE0B48447F7C5DB2.jpeg)

5. Click the tile.

6. Click **Tile Processing**＞ **Cutting** ＞**Irregular shapes** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899121804/6BEAA16D4301A0287AD9F2E2478E1C18.jpeg)

7. Edit its shape, angle and edge lengths.

8. Click **Confirm**.

Note: You can add this shape to Favorites. Find it in **Tile Processing** ＞ **Cutting** ＞ **Irregular shapes** ＞**Shape** ＞ **My Account** next time.
