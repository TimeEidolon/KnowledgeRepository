---

1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

4. Drag the target tile under **Public Library** ＞ **Tiles** from the left panel to your floor plan.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899119151/923BB56214449E02DE0B48447F7C5DB2.jpeg)

5. Click the tile.

6. Click **Tile Processing**＞ **Cutting** ＞**Irregular shapes** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733899121804/6BEAA16D4301A0287AD9F2E2478E1C18.jpeg)

7. Edit its shape, angle and edge lengths.

8. Click **Confirm**.

Note: You can add this shape to Favorites. Find it in **Tile Processing** ＞ **Cutting** ＞ **Irregular shapes** ＞**Shape** ＞ **My Account** next time.
