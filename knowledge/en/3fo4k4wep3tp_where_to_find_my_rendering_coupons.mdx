Some Coohom users may wonder where they can see the rendering coupons peculiar to their paid plan after subscription. In this article, we will show two methods to check the coupons.

## Users: All individual users


**Method one**: Go to **Render** interface to check the coupons specific to each render type.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766044392325/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766044438990/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Method two**: Go to **My subscription** button via the Profile icon to check the coupons. (Please be noted that the unlimited coupons will not show here)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766044550809/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766044636937/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

For any further concerns or queries, please feel free to reach Coohom Customer Service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
