Some Coohom users may wonder where they can see the rendering coupons peculiar to their paid plan after subscription. In this article, we will show two methods to check the coupons.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Users: All individual users **Method one**: Go to **Render** interface to check the coupons specific to each render type.
