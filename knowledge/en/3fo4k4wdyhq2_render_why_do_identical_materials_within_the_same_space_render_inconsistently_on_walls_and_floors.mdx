Reason This issue mostly occurs in small enclosed spaces like bathrooms. These spaces have certain characteristics: they are small, have no windows or small windows, and in such scenarios, spotlights or additional lighting is needed for illumination. The characteristic of such light sources is that their impact is greater directly below than to the sides. Therefore, the impact on the floor is greater than on the walls, resulting in the floor material appearing lighter than the wall material, causing a visual color inconsistency.
