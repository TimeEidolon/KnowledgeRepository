1. **The columns participate in the enclosure of the room**

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
**The columns participate in the enclosure of the room** In Cloud Design 4.0, there is no fusion relationship between columns and walls, and they do not participate in the enclosure of rooms; In Cloud Design 5.0, the columns and the walls have a fusion relationship and participate in the enclosing relationship of the room.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841462845/D6E263D7838A5569FBF87E5E7B449131.png)

### Step 2
**Low walls no longer divide rooms.** In Cloud Design 4.0, the low wall will divide the room. In Cloud Design 5.0, the low wall no longer acts as a partition in the room; the room partition line can be used to partition the room.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841465565/83CA906CCCCA1C4BB5CDDC0E6898533C.png)

### Step 3
**The beam divides the top surface of the room.** In Cloud Design 4.0, the beam will not have any influence on the top surface; In Cloud Design 5.0, the beam has parameters and will affect the top surface area of the room, as shown in the figure, the top surface is divided into areas.
