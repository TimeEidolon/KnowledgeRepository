1. **The columns participate in the enclosure of the room**

In Cloud Design 4.0, there is no fusion relationship between columns and walls, and they do not participate in the enclosure of rooms;

In Cloud Design 5.0, the columns and the walls have a fusion relationship and participate in the enclosing relationship of the room.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841462845/D6E263D7838A5569FBF87E5E7B449131.png)

2. **Low walls no longer divide rooms.**

In Cloud Design 4.0, the low wall will divide the room.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841465565/83CA906CCCCA1C4BB5CDDC0E6898533C.png)

In Cloud Design 5.0, the low wall no longer acts as a partition in the room; the room partition line can be used to partition the room.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841468069/D969AD65B1D4FA8C230CB04B0DD83B75.png)

3. **The beam divides the top surface of the room.**

In Cloud Design 4.0, the beam will not have any influence on the top surface;

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841471966/C9DCE808542583092AAE7CC3164CD517.png)

In Cloud Design 5.0, the beam has parameters and will affect the top surface area of the room, as shown in the figure, the top surface is divided into areas.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841474410/E332A478737E84F2703632C3800D75F6.png)

4. **Wall connection relationship**

As shown in the figure, 4.0 and 5.0 have different ways of wall connection in 2D interface and wall editing in 3D interface.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841477371/FFC9482D018504C0A2963034E908F3F7.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841480551/D165F3E27833A922E80CBF22E039E207.png)
