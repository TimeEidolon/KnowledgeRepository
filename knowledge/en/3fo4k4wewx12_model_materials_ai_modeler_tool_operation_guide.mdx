In the models, select the AI modeler. You can use Lux3D/Tripo 3D/HY 3D tools to upload images and generate models, or use Tripo 3D/HY 3D tools to upload multiple view images or text to generate models. You can upload materials based on your needs. This article will explain in detail how to use the Lux3D/Tripo 3D/HY 3D tools.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

### Step 1
When choosing different tools and methods to generate models, you can click **Parameter** and adjust settings as needed. * **PBR:** Enabling **PBR** makes materials more realistic, with more natural lighting and shading effects and richer details. * **HD Texture:** Enabling **HD Texture** makes the model's surface texture finer, clearer, and closer to the real effect of the original image. * **High- Poly:** Enabling **High- Poly** ensures smoother model edges and more precise restoration of detail contours.

# Notes

1. Both **Tripo 3D** and **HY 3D** tools support uploading images/multiple view images/text to generate models.

2. The **Lux3D** tool supports only uploading images to generate models.

3. Each tool consumes different credits to generate models, and the generated results vary accordingly.

4. When choosing different tools and methods to generate models, you can click **Parameter** and adjust settings as needed.

* **PBR:** Enabling **PBR** makes materials more realistic, with more natural lighting and shading effects and richer details.
* **HD Texture:** Enabling **HD Texture** makes the model's surface texture finer, clearer, and closer to the real effect of the original image.
* **High- Poly:** Enabling **High- Poly** ensures smoother model edges and more precise restoration of detail contours.
