**Product Collections** can help to collect products with different styles. Once users collect products in enterprise library, they can select different styles of products in tool easily.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733832292026/E96F5B4C9B91578000477F96E630851A.gif)

Go to **Enterprise Library**, select the models or finishes you want to collect together.  
​**Add to Collections**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733832295025/2717E3906EBECD7E443E575F0C07F4BF.png)

Create a **new collection** or add to the **existed collection**, confirm.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733832297900/751CA1D1DD69BC3640E3580191CC79F1.png)

Collections list at the top of models.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733832300541/952F9E1C1AA58ECB2EE9B63F55989D68.png)

You can change the starred model, or remove model from collection with selecting.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733832301569/66870B6DFB07188D85774C5D1354AC1E.png)

Once you upgroup the collection, all the models in the collection will go back to the original positon in enterprise library.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733832304894/6ED414155173CAFCA762122197878439.png)

After collecting, users can select different stypes of models easily in floor planner.
