1. Drag two or above models into your floor plan from the left panel.

2. Hold down Shift, select these models and click **Group** to group your models.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851460064/4C2674EFAC4663E38880554B008DDF40.png)

3. **Ungroup** will replace **Group** in the floating toolbar once Group was chosen.

4. Click **Edit** to modify every single model seperately in this group.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851461377/C56FE4A8BD8C8D39734CF54DFCFDEC76.png)

5. Select the model group and click **Ungroup** to ungroup your models if needed.
