1. Drag two or above models into your floor plan from the left panel.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Drag two or above models into your floor plan from the left panel.

### Step 2
Hold down Shift, select these models and click **Group** to group your models.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851460064/4C2674EFAC4663E38880554B008DDF40.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851460064/4C2674EFAC4663E38880554B008DDF40.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
**Ungroup** will replace **Group** in the floating toolbar once Group was chosen.

### Step 4
Click **Edit** to modify every single model seperately in this group.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851461377/C56FE4A8BD8C8D39734CF54DFCFDEC76.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851461377/C56FE4A8BD8C8D39734CF54DFCFDEC76.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
Select the model group and click **Ungroup** to ungroup your models if needed.
