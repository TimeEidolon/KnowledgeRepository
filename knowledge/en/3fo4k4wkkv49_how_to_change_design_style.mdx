### Introduction

By using Design Style, you can edit the style of all custom furniture with one click.

### Steps

1. Custom Design ＞ Kitchen and Bath/ Closet

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855181331/47055F9F314B4C7962B4E2EA2378A5E2.png)

2. Click **Design Style.**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855183470/2676F65FBC14C3263ECF803F6CEA421D.png)

3.Choose a template and click **Apply,** and the style of all cabinets will be replaced**.**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855186021/1A41C923A4FEFEE39889653BDD21B378.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855187577/4E1A177A2E8F5533CF60DBD7E3C31E50.png)

**Note: you can also apply a style before designing. When you add a new model to the drawing, the style will be applied automatically.**
