1. Go to Coohom Website: [www.coohom.com](http://www.coohom.com) to find the **Sign Up** button.

Go to Coohom Website: [www.coohom.com](http://www.coohom.com) to find the **Sign Up** button.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854316075/421D3FEA3259AF5D80672ECFFCD219D8.png)

2. After clicking **Sign Up** button, you have three ways to create a Coohom Account.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854317767/A9EC34F7F7836DC60E3807E7F771C3A6.png)

3. If you choose **Continue with Email**, you need to input your email to proceed and then input the password.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854321040/EF67D33E40EF6851A35FD0E36DC115EB.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854324024/24FAB61EFD9B22D766DD6FED4A12580C.png)

Note: When registering, be sure that your password is meeting the below rules.

1) Passwords may only consist of uppercase or lowercase characters (A-Z/a-z), numbers (0-9), or any of the following special characters ~!@#$%^&\*()\_+

2) Passwords should have a combination of at least two of the character types mentioned above (uppercase/lowercase characters, numbers, or special characters).

3) Passwords should be 8-16 characters long.

4. After setting the password, please click **Continue** button, you will receive a 4-digit code to verify the email.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854325993/B82A7799E344057134FA71EB43F1289F.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854328274/DF033364B4A1F15EFF4ED027C235EF14.png)

5. After the code is input, you can just click **Verify** to finish account creation.
