### Step 1
After the room is completed, switch to **3D mode** and hold the **Ctrl/⌘ key** to select the wall. * **Shortcut for Windows system: Ctrl** * **Shortcut for macOS system: ⌘**

### Step 2
Modify the wall **height** in the **Basic** section of the parameter panel on the right (the default wall height matches the floor height, which is set to 2800 by default).

### Step 3

### Step 5
To set a wall height greater than the room height, set the **Height** in the **Basic** on the right panel to a value greater than the floor height, then click **“Confirm”** in the pop-up panel; the floor height and all wall heights will be updated accordingly.

# Entrance

1. After the room is completed, switch to **3D mode** and hold the **Ctrl/⌘ key** to select the wall.

* **Shortcut for Windows system: Ctrl**
* **Shortcut for macOS system: ⌘**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762913990834/30CB840C33E3128D0D8C0A93B976E517.png)

2. Modify the wall **height** in the **Basic** section of the parameter panel on the right (the default wall height matches the floor height, which is set to 2800 by default).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843764162/85ED56FEE3C9980341FB6FFC7E7B2A62.png)

# Modify Wall Height

1. To change a wall to a low wall, set the **Height** in the **Basic** on the right panel to a value lower than the floor height; after modification, a **Regular** will automatically switch to a **Custom**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843795602/42F26F0927ED462008890C1259A93CCC.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1762914110889/105BDBA4D70924487197EFF18F5604F1.gif)

2. To make a low wall the same height as the room, set the **Height** in the **Basic** on the right panel to 2800; after modification, the low wall will automatically switch to a **Regular**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843807264/8076A270E35CA2DB52A7E141425CD0D6.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1762914170317/69E6F9A16F66B10355A5FBF739EDCDF5.gif)

3. To set a wall height greater than the room height, set the **Height** in the **Basic** on the right panel to a value greater than the floor height, then click **“Confirm”** in the pop-up panel; the floor height and all wall heights will be updated accordingly.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843818269/1D29C271EC1F976C57B9EEA920690E47.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762914224330/3F7840083D7F14E8F4673A8A7F8EDDBE.png)
