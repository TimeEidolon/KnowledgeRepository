After the room is drawn, you can modify the wall height in the basic section of the parameter panel on the right as needed. This article will explain in detail how to adjust individual wall height.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BxDwfyasWoE)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
After the room is completed, switch to **3D mode** and hold the **Ctrl/⌘ key** to select the wall. * **Shortcut for Windows system: Ctrl** * **Shortcut for macOS system: ⌘**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762913990834/30CB840C33E3128D0D8C0A93B976E517.png)

### Step 2
Modify the wall **height** in the **Basic** section of the parameter panel on the right (the default wall height matches the floor height, which is set to 2800 by default).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843764162/85ED56FEE3C9980341FB6FFC7E7B2A62.png)

### Step 1
To change a wall to a low wall, set the **Height** in the **Basic** on the right panel to a value lower than the floor height; after modification, a **Regular** will automatically switch to a **Custom**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843795602/42F26F0927ED462008890C1259A93CCC.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843795602/42F26F0927ED462008890C1259A93CCC.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1762914110889/105BDBA4D70924487197EFF18F5604F1.gif)

### Step 2
To make a low wall the same height as the room, set the **Height** in the **Basic** on the right panel to 2800; after modification, the low wall will automatically switch to a **Regular**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843807264/8076A270E35CA2DB52A7E141425CD0D6.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762843807264/8076A270E35CA2DB52A7E141425CD0D6.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
To set a wall height greater than the room height, set the **Height** in the **Basic** on the right panel to a value greater than the floor height, then click **“Confirm”** in the pop-up panel; the floor height and all wall heights will be updated accordingly.
