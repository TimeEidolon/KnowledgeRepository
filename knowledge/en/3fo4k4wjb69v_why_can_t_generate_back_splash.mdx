### **Overview**

When generating a countertop, the back splash cannot be generated because the cabinet is not against the wall. By placing the cabinet against the wall, it can be generated normally.

### **Step**

Step 1: Move the cabinet against the wall.

Step 2: Click on 'Countertop’ to generate it.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733830131587/AFC056C281F9B2E75228D0491ED2F1A1.png)

Step 3: Set the front and backs plash, materials, and related parameters.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733830135858/9F95029D0E17080A6FC5B9B964532A21.png)

Step 4: Complete the countertop generation

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733830138268/5323DEE7BFAEE9065653CF3F780A876F.png)
