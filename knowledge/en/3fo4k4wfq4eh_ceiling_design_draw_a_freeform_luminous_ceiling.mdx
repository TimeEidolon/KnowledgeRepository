In the ceiling editor tool, utilizing the curve and offset tools allows you to easily construct unique ceiling shapes. Then, by combining these designs with luminous material, you can quickly create distinctive and textured luminous ceilings.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

# Entrance

Select the ceiling surface and click to enter **Ceiling Editor**.

# Draw a Freeform Luminous Ceiling

1. In the ceiling editor tool, click the **Curve** tool at the top to draw unique structures.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777019038487/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

2. After drawing, you can adjust the design as needed. Select the line to adjust the curve’s angle. Repeat adjustments until you achieve a satisfactory result.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777019579063/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

3. Once adjustments are complete, click the **Offset** tool at the top to offset layers, then refine the structure according to your design needs.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777020786180/8B92EAD58843012015ACE668614C773A.gif)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777021076174/CD8FC63C91BC49078853BAFD719BE561.gif)

4. Next, set different **Extrusion** values on various surfaces to create an intricate spatial hierarchy.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777021593969/BA3CF5EA969D0747CC1A36BC94ED33E3.gif)

5. Click **Wall Decor** on the left menu. At the top, choose **Paints & Coatings**, then click appropriate materials for the ceiling. Next, click **Paving Brush** at the top to pick target textures and apply them to other areas.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777022394996/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)

6. Select the modeling line, and click **Section Edit**. Place appropriate materials in the desired region.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777022800454/6AF38499B9E5D3FBFDF01E6FA177365F.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777023020304/8D279F09BF8E1777AE3C80ED8403A599.png)

7. Modify the dimensions in the profile editing popup window as needed. Once adjustments are completed, click **Done**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777023112080/D223EF61135483A3D9A152A05325A89B.png)

8. Click the **Molding Brush** at the top, pick a ceiling material, and apply it to decoration lines.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777023328322/BDD03DBD7B143CFD44CBE3156FF860F8.gif)

9. Select the set modeling line, click **Duplicate** in the floating panel, then **Paste into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1777023481238/5BE693C2A037151FD75670ED2B8C2A1C.gif)

10. Click **Wall Decor** on the left menu. At the top, search for luminous material, then choose and place them in the desired areas.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777023575261/0D675C6566608F1414F9C3E6C9674159.png)

11. After completing the settings, you can drag and enlarge the view window at the top right to inspect the effect and determine any adjustments.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777023714803/29FF7A53205D766ADC2FBD3C2EEB50DE.png)

12. If no adjustments are needed, click**Exit** **with Save** at the top right. After rendering, a satisfactory rendering can be generated.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1777023745687/B8C69D804944DAF2AF246DFFA0A0156C.png)

* Final effect is as shown:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1777268425778/9B8FB4EA4AC939286F1813B6B98AEB65.jpg)
