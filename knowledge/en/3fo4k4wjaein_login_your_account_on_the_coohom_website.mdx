Before designing with Coohom, successfully login your account is an important first step. This article will provide a detailed guide on how to login your account on the Coohom. If you encounter issues like forgetting your password or need to change it, relevant tutorials are also provided in the text. Follow the instructions below to quickly start your Coohom design journey!


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

### Step 1
Go to the [Coohom](https://www.coohom.com?kjl_source=hc) and click the **Login** button at the top right corner of the homepage.

# Login Your Account

1. Go to the [Coohom](https://www.coohom.com?kjl_source=hc) and click the **Login** button at the top right corner of the homepage.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753426586731/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Before logging in your account, you need to first **check** the authorization of the  [Privacy Policy](https://www.coohom.com/pub/market/portal/c/privacy-policy) and [Terms of Use](https://www.coohom.com/pub/market/portal/c/user-agreement?kjl_source=hc).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1753427518053/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. After checking authorization, enter your registered email and password, or you can log in using Facebook, Google, or Twitter accounts.

* If you forget your password or want to change your login password, see the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/change-your-account-password-in-coohom?hl=en&kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753426993999/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1753427518019/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

4. After entering your account and password, click **Continue** to successfully enter the workspace page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753427074442/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
