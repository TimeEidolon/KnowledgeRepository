In the floor plan tool, you can select a single structure or batch select multiple structures to lock or unlock them. Once locked, they cannot be moved or deleted, preventing accidental deletion or movement of walls during planning that could change the floor plan. This article will explain in detail how to lock and unlock floor plan structures.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

### Step 1
Select the **single/multiple** floor plan structures you want to lock, and click **Lock** on the popup window (**Press "shift/⇧" when selecting multiple structures**). * Multiple selected structures must be of the same type. Example: Walls can be locked together with structures, but walls cannot be locked together with doors and windows. * When selecting multiple floor plan structures, if the lock status is consistent, they can be locked or unlocked simultaneously.

# Types of Lock

**1. Windows**: models of windows and doors.

**2. Floor Plan**: walls, structure, room seperation line, angle lock.

**3. Text and Labels**: room name and area, marking.

# Lock or Unlock Single/Multiple Structures

## Lock Floor Plan Structures

1. Select the **single/multiple** floor plan structures you want to lock, and click **Lock** on the popup window (**Press "shift/⇧" when selecting multiple structures**).

* Multiple selected structures must be of the same type. Example: Walls can be locked together with structures, but walls cannot be locked together with doors and windows.
* When selecting multiple floor plan structures, if the lock status is consistent, they can be locked or unlocked simultaneously.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754646922008/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1754647657818/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. After locking, actions such as **dragging, deleting, and hiding** cannot be performed (the delete and hide icons are black and cannot be selected).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754647130778/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Unlock Floor Plan Structures

To move them, unlock first by selecting single/multiple structures and **click unlock.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1754647657834/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Batch Lock and Unlock

## Batch Lock

Click the **bottom toolbar lock icon** to **check** the type you need to lock. Once set, all corresponding types in the floor plan will be locked.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754647337931/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1754647657926/76C0ACD641C790ABF81E71A1BC910E67.gif)

## Batch Unlock

Click the **bottom toolbar lock icon**, **uncheck** the type you need to unlock. Once set, all corresponding types in the floor plan will be unlocked (Example for unchecking the wall).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754647481807/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1754647657945/E04FF26551C3E2258763D17FB89F2C8B.gif)
