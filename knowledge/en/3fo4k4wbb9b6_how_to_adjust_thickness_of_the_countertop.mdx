some users unable to adjust the thickness of countertop in advanced tools page. This article will illustrate several easy ways of adjusting thickness of the countertop from Coohom. 

## ****Target Users: All individual users****


step1: click on the countertop that you want to adjust

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745062773739/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

step 2: if the thickness can't be changed go to preferences tool

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745062833614/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

step 3: set the unit in preferences tool from meters to Millimeter

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745062969006/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

step 4: go to advanced tool page again and click on the countertop to adjust thickness normally.
