### Step 1
PRODUCT UPDATE-Big news from Coohom! We're thrilled to announce the launch of our multi-language Public Model Library, featuring models in Indonesian, Spanish, French, Italian, Vietnamese, Korean, and Japanese, in addition to English. With over **350K Models** already in the library, you can now search for and use models in your native language, making it easier than ever to create your dream space. Coohom is committed to making our platform accessible to everyone, no matter where you are in the world. Two ways try out our new multi-language Public Model Library today and see the difference for yourself! **Method 1:** In your Coohom Workspace, first switch the language to Indonesian, Spanish, Vietnamese, Korean, or Japanese, and then enter your Project or create a New Project.
