**Before**

Previously, if a company wanted to use another company's product library within a design tool, they could only contact the manager, obtain authorization offline, and perform cross-account copying of the models. This process led to three main issues:

Firstly, the operation process was cumbersome and very inconvenient.

Secondly, many companies were not willing to directly expose their model data. They preferred that other companies only have user rights while shielding their administrative rights.

Lastly, for some custom products, the pricing rules behind the parameters are considered trade secrets that cannot be disclosed through offline methods.

**After**

Now, enterprise can allow their products to be used by other companies in 3D design tools through product sharing. In this process, the company sharing the product externally retains full administrative control over the product, while the company using the shared product only has user rights.

**How to use**

How to share with others

Access the Coohom workspace, locate "Product Sharing" in the company library, and then click "Share Products" to initiate product sharing.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245131553/8E10D7ACC2A3F3649EB4A4A33EFA51CF.png)

Select the product folder you wish to share.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245135471/8CC13649DA4B7F0FFD7E39CD7B2E7761.png)

Select the account and validity period for sharing.  
You can also choose to share the files with the entire company.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245136231/C5C010414364B7BADBC52553AD435F78.png)

**How to accept shared products**

Navigate to the Coohom workspace, locate "Product Sharing" in the company library, click on "Share Products," switch to "Shared with Me," and accept the shared items from the other party.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245138003/49588C065BD6E9A3284F17CBB0387DB7.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245140393/BD1F06112A302AE91D52D994CC479F5D.png)

**How to use shared products**

In the design tool, navigate to the Share library to access the relevant folders where the shared files can be found.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245142858/AEC3F97240FFD26E409C48EDAC49A4BF.png)
