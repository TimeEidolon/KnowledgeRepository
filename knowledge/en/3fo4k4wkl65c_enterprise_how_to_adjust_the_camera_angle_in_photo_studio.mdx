Coohom provides multiple scenarios in Photo Studio. Users can not only adjust the scenarios but also adjust the camera angle for rendering for a personalized design.

# Function Description

1. Camera adjustment UI optimization  
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855030098/702A0119D3AEBBDEE6EF453FE7806878.png)

The toolbar on the right is mainly composed of five functions: [Lens Movement Description], [Picture Scale Selection], [Camera Pitch Angle Setting], [Effect Preview] and [Confirm Shooting], which are clearer and easier for users to operate.

2. New image ratio selection  
Support to choose the camera lens ratio, provide 4 kinds of ratios besides the preset lens ratio for selection: 16:9 9:16 4:3 3:4 1:1

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855031542/AF4BE4978588CB208F2895171655D78C.png)

3. Added camera pitch angle setting  
The user can directly drag the corresponding picture on the right to adjust the pitch angle and camera height, and support manual parameter adjustment

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855034296/018A71B3084C688CC22E8268C2350752.png)

4. Added camera lens reset  
After adjusting the lens, the user can select the reset button to restore the camera's preset lens angle

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855037135/57B92431E56F118AD367751638774E88.png)

# Steps

1. Go to Photo Studio. Enter the studio shooting list and click **Edit** to enter the editing mode of the studio.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457477709/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457451554/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Find the switch view in the lower left corner and enter the camera angle list.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457414653/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Press **WSAD** to adjust left and right, QE to adjust up and down, space is to reset. Click "**save camera position**" to save the adjusted angle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457388140/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

When adjusting the angle, if the following prompt appears, that means you need to use the **s** key to pull the angle away.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457358751/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. After adjustment, click **shoot**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855044937/7A6BFCEA6FCECD98D22D34DF903B75AA.png)

Animation of the whole shooting process.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733855047683/2756C7ED9E1003D8353F8C33D0507E17.gif)
