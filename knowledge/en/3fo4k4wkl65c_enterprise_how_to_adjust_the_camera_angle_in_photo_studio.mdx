# Steps

1. Go to Photo Studio. Enter the studio shooting list and click **Edit** to enter the editing mode of the studio.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457477709/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457451554/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Find the switch view in the lower left corner and enter the camera angle list.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457414653/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Press **WSAD** to adjust left and right, QE to adjust up and down, space is to reset. Click "**save camera position**" to save the adjusted angle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457388140/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

When adjusting the angle, if the following prompt appears, that means you need to use the **s** key to pull the angle away.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749457358751/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. After adjustment, click **shoot**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733855044937/7A6BFCEA6FCECD98D22D34DF903B75AA.png)
