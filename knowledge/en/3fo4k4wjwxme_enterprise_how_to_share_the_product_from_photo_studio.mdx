How to share the product:

1. Go to Photo Studio in Coohom.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839673878/262AF4B5DA8B617C093723438FA46744.png)

2.Choose the specific product and click the **Share items** button to share.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839676815/E2A82B7C59672CAF29FAF13A4AC11B0B.png)

3. Choose one of your Sub-accounts to share the item. Tick the role name and **Confirm** it.

\*\*Note: Only able to share to sub-accounts which those roles **MUST BE** admin, head designer, and manager.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839678953/1174FC8F47C29004EF38FBD7D5B983D9.png)

4. If to share the products by **bulk**, add the necessary products first. Then click on **Share the product**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839682361/8B16512D4A95AB42C195E51845EF537D.png)

5. Click on **Bulk sharing.**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839685156/6EF9E114ABD6CFE7C0B651416EC42807.png)

6. Tick the items that needed to be shared, then click on the **Next** button.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839687655/75F9071D2C1585C0F9544F02B1A3FC18.png)

7. The next page would be the same as step 3.

### Apply for the permission of photo studio & quota usage

Live date: July 20th, 2021

* Original scenario —— Among the accounts under the enterprise, if account A adds a studio shot product to his photo studio section, account B cannot shoot the photos/video for this product.

Original scenario —— Among the accounts under the enterprise, if account A adds a studio shot product to his photo studio section, account B cannot shoot the photos/video for this product.

#「New function 1」  
The accounts who have the permission of the photo studio can share their photo studio products with each other for the shooting.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839690050/66E15AD3D1E86A3406CFD224F22DF58C.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839691476/B499247A82D61262DD4C681C8614EDA7.png)

* Original scenario —— The quota will be only assigned to the admin account after purchase. There is no photo studio permission and photo studio quota for sub-accounts.

Original scenario —— The quota will be only assigned to the admin account after purchase. There is no photo studio permission and photo studio quota for sub-accounts.

#「New function 2」

Photo Studio Permission and Quota could apply for accounts.  
If the client wants his sub-account to have the ability to shoot photos/videos in a photo studio module. The client or the corresponding CSC needs to apply the photo studio permission and the quota needed for the designated accounts.
