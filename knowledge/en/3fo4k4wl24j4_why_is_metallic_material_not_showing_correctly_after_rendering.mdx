Dear Coohomers,

When you are using the metallic material like golden material on the handle etc., you may encounter the issue that they will show wrong colors like black color.

Here are screenshots for your reference.

3D view before rendering

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974257182/7A7AFA5EEFF0AA267F2C691222D8E95B.png)

Rendered image

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974259238/611BD94344CFFE66FDA84168CCCCDFFA.png)

What is the reason of this?

This is because of that the handle is made of metal, which has the physical property of reflecting light. At a certain camera angle, it will reflect other objects. This is a normal physical phenomenon and cannot be eliminated, but it can be avoided by slightly adjusting the camera angle. It can be avoided by changing to non-metallic reflective materials too.

The effect of adjusting the camera angle.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974261096/D21EEDD762C9B158D8DEDC2CD19ECC9F.png)
