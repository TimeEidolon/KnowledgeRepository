### Step 3
What is the reason of this? This is because of that the handle is made of metal, which has the physical property of reflecting light. At a certain camera angle, it will reflect other objects. This is a normal physical phenomenon and cannot be eliminated, but it can be avoided by slightly adjusting the camera angle. It can be avoided by changing to non-metallic reflective materials too. The effect of adjusting the camera angle.
