In the room tools, there are three types of walls: straight wall, curved wall, and rect wall. All types of apartment structures can be restored.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Enter the kitchen＆closet design and choose the **Room**.

### Step 2
Select a **wall**, there are three types of walls: **straight wall, curved wall, and rect wall**. **Method One:** In the parameter panel, you can set the **Inputs-Location line**, and there are three types of Location lines to choose from: **internal, center, and external**. **Method Two:** **Press the space bar** to switch the **internal, center and external** Location lines of the wall to quickly adjust the wall drawing position. During the drawing of the wall, **press the Shift** to quickly check the **enable**. At this time, the wall can only be drawn along the **X/Y axis** direction. * **Win System Shortcut：Shift** * **mac System Shortcut：⇧**

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen＆closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

1. Enter the kitchen＆closet design and choose the **Room**.

2. Select a **wall**, there are three types of walls: **straight wall, curved wall, and rect wall**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741087989378/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Set Location Lines

**Method One:** In the parameter panel, you can set the **Inputs-Location line**, and there are three types of Location lines to choose from: **internal, center, and external**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741146100531/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741146182576/3A9EAED2E99247AEE5EFAC5D0C4C68CE.gif)

**Method Two:** **Press the space bar** to switch the **internal, center and external** Location lines of the wall to quickly adjust the wall drawing position.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741146373411/DF158D151008495B88F5D1630CA76704.gif)

# Set Wall Angle

During the drawing of the wall, **press the Shift** to quickly check the **enable**. At this time, the wall can only be drawn along the **X/Y axis** direction.

* **Win System Shortcut：Shift**
* **mac System Shortcut：⇧**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741147205257/270674710C96E0F3663F8D3534E1A557.gif)

# Lock Wall Angle

1. Click **Lock** in the lower right corner, select the **floor plan**, and check the **angle lock**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741147407924/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After checking the **angle lock**, when moving the endpoint of the wall, the wall can only move in the original direction.

* Shortcut: **Hold down the Shift / ⇧** to move the endpoint of the wall, and can also lock wall angle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741147640485/9698FEA71CD143A25EE73C24C2FB6CFB.gif)

# Draw Curved Walls

## Draw by Radius

Draw in the order of **start→end→radius**, the curved size is determined by the radius size of the circle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741153860555/8400D5144CE81076B2CDAC030675B78E.gif)

## Draw by Chord Height

Draw in the order of **start→end→chord height**, the curved size is determined by the chord height size of the circle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741153753230/29E1AF935B6D7A26B1AB01467F65C3F3.gif)
