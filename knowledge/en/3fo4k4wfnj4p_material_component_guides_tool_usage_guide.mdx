## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

### Step 1
**Entrance** In the design tool, select the **Advanced Tool** and click to enter **Material & Component**.

### Step 3
There are three ways to **Add Guides** (**On Edge, On Face**, and **End Point**) * **On Edge:** Drag guideline from the edge of the wall to the desired position and left-click, or enter a specific value in the **Distance To Edge** at the bottom left for accurate distance positioning. * **On Face:** Click any two points on the wall surface to generate a guideline. * **End Point:** Click two **End Points** of walls or models to **Add Guides**.

# Add Guides

1. Click the guidelines tool in the top toolbar, and select **Add Guides**. Two parallel or intersecting guidelines can assist in spatial design positioning, improving design efficiency.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778749419309/12C7D8CC73FF419767BD84F288D275EE.png)

2. There are three ways to **Add Guides** (**On Edge, On Face**, and **End Point**)

* **On Edge:** Drag guideline from the edge of the wall to the desired position and left-click, or enter a specific value in the **Distance To Edge** at the bottom left for accurate distance positioning.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778749419425/7D727521A073818C752123EB3E170FDF.png)

* **On Face:** Click any two points on the wall surface to generate a guideline.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778749419375/A44518F2BCD09BACEFA8B1755225141C.png)

* **End Point:** Click two **End Points** of walls or models to **Add Guides**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778749419331/54F12043045AA7EC3489ECE248E568A5.png)

3. Once the guidelines are added, you can use the **Straight Line/Rectangle** tool to accurately draw based on the intersection points of the guidelines.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778749419376/0CBA2AC816658E6420BB50667DDA1842.png)

# Clear Guides

After the design is complete, you can click the **Clear Guides** tool in the top toolbar to clear all guidelines from the canvas in one click, keeping the design view tidy.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778749419471/5620AA3AC6166A262D6EEF3B098803C4.png)
