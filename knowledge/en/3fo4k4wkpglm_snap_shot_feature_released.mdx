What is Snap Shot(Lite Rendering)?

Lite Rendering is the convenient version of the rendering video function of Coohom. With many video templates, you can shoot high-quality short videos easily. Click and experience the rendering with fewer steps.

How to generate a video via [SnapShot](https://app.intercom.com/)?

Step 1:

1. Select the video templates in the Lite rendering.

   1. You can filter templates by compositions and styles.
   2. You can also preview the templates video by hovering over the templates.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745244732361/53966750F4DFC77A1C5A35B237A36E8C.png)

Step 2:

Adjust the configuration of the template. You can select the video's aspect ratio and the room type you want to show.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745244734579/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 3:

Choose one project, or select several projects.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745244745763/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 4:

Choose the resolution of the video. Then you can render now!

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745244747311/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
