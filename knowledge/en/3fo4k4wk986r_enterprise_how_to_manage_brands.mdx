Create new brand

1. Select **Enterprise Library** ＞**Product Info** ＞**Brand Manager** in the left panel.

2. Click **New Brand** to create a new brand.

3. Fill in all required information.

4. Click **Confirm**.

​

### Manage brands

1. Hover your mouse over one brand.

2. Click **Edit** and **Delete** to modify.
