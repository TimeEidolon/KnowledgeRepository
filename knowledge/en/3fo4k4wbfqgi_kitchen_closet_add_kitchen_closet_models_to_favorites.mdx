Kitchen & Closet models and materials support add to favorites, and can be classified and add to custom named folders and can be used at any time.

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen & closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Add Kitchen & Closet Models to Favorites

## Method One

1. Select the **Kitchen/Closet** to enter the customization interface.

2. Click the Kitchen & Closet model, click **Add to Favorites** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744785665514/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Method Two

Select the Kitchen & Closet model in the **Public Catalog**, click the upper right corner ☆, One-click **add to favorites**.

# Add Materials to Favorites

## **Entrance One**

In the **style** of the parameter panel, select the Kitchen & Closet material and **add to favorites**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744785984137/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1745226797346/8D801B6A2694C56BA363E4B51936E874.gif)

## **Entrance Two**

Select the Kitchen & Closet material in the **Public Catalog**, click the upper right corner ☆, One-click **add to favorites**.

# Favorites Category

1. Click **Add to Favorites** in the floating toolbar to **create folder** and customize the name to classify favorites.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745227136601/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the corresponding tool, switch to **Personal Catalog**, under the **overall** bar is the Kitchen & Closet models favorites, under the **parts** bar is the Kitchen & Closet parts favorites, under the **material** bar is the Kitchen & Closet materials favorites, select the materials under the folder and drag them out to use.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744786358430/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1744786459943/DD2E1D9E113B11A5BEF25CADE20F4AA2.gif)
