Want to efficiently get inspiration for your desired space and easily reuse favorite models and interior finish? This guide will help you complete everything in one go! From filtering inspiration spaces to various ways of flexibly picking and dragging out models, and then to one-click favorites of preferred designs, every step is clear and easy to understand. Follow the steps below to quickly unlock practical operations in the inspiration library~


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.
