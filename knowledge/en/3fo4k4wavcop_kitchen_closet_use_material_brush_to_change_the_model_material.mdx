The material brush can copy the model material and assign it to another model to achieve the effect of quickly adjusting the cabinet material.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

### Step 1
Select the **Toolkit-Material brush** from the top toolbar

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen & closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

1. Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **kitchen/Closet**.

2. Select the **Toolkit-Material brush** from the top toolbar

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1744340814007/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Material Brush

Place the **Material brush** on the model to absorb the material style, find the model part that needs to replace the material, click the brush, and the material will be applied with one click.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1744342698947/800C0EF917DF818A941E2F90615289FE.gif)

# Material Brush Supports Cross-Tool Brushing

In the design tool, common material styles can be brushed together. The following model materials can be brushed together:

* custom furniture, custom cabinets, custom doors and windows, soft decoration, and other accessories (hardware decoration is not supported at the moment)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1744342780834/F94655DC3EAA9C60728B7F4624B9F9DC.gif)
