Introduction: **Style Brush** enables you to uplicate a part's style and apply it to others.

### Case: replace B with A style.

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QxGcadEn3qk )

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Click **Toolkit ＞ Style Brush**

### Step 2
Click the panel to extract its style. Click the blister door and its style will be replaced.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886159611/B38A8FBACE8023A2D1ACAE6EBDBCB762.png)

### Step 3
Do the same to another door, and the whole house's style is unified.
