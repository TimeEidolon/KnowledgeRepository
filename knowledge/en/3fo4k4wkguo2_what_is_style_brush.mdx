Introduction: **Style Brush** enables you to uplicate a part's style and apply it to others.

### Case: replace B with A style.

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QxGcadEn3qk )

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886156460/C2F1F34632B12927D4ADC435A32E5193.png)

1. Click **Toolkit ＞ Style Brush**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886158040/5999BECB6BF957DF17D429EEFBF3DF92.png)

2. Click the panel to extract its style. Click the blister door and its style will be replaced.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886159611/B38A8FBACE8023A2D1ACAE6EBDBCB762.png)

3. Do the same to another door, and the whole house's style is unified.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886163011/152151E1DEB8174A6362872FBA0F5267.png)
