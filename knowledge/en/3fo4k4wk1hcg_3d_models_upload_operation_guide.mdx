Here is a quick guide on how to upload your own 3D modelsto Coohom.(excluding the model types: the whole floor plan with interior designs, the whole ceiling etc. )

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started\~

<br />

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rgfR6lHe988)

<br />

## For Users

All individual users  

## Entrance

Enter the **workspace** , select the **Models** , click **Upload Models**.

* To better render the model, please make sure your model includes materials. Before uploading the model, please refer to the model requirements: [Click to](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/coohom-3ds-max-sketchup-model-upload-requirements?kjl_source=hc)[view.](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/coohom-3ds-max-sketchup-model-upload-requirements?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778136603564/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Select Models

On the model upload page, click **Local Upload**, or directly drag and drop model files here for upload. (You can drag and drop multiple model files at once). Please ensure your files meet the requirements as described below.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1778138202956/16191F156D795B9DE6A2C686BAA8E785.gif)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778136638739/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Edit Information

Please fill out all required fields and name your model accurately, for example: "Eames Lounge Chair - Black Leather", this will make it easier to search for the model.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762332021465/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**1. Edit model information separately**

You can edit model information separately.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762332565748/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**2. Edit all model information**

Click **Edit All** to edit all model information at a time.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762331957701/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1762332429534/45E9DEB925C03BEDDE87FF547C60CEF6.gif)

## Submit Models

1. After uploading models, a blue border indicates that the corresponding model is valid. Then click to **Submit Models**, submit the model to the models library.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762332673389/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After successful uploading, you can view the model in the personal library.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778139392374/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## View Uploaded Models in the Project

Go to Interior Design, select **Models** , click **Uploads**, you can see the model you just uploaded in the uploads field, drag it out and use it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778140528895/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1778140474270/542F8B725441419D89B288BFF4C99B97.gif)
