M1: In the upper right corner of the model image, select **Add to photo studio**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886600117/86CE50C95E88CBD607EF339D1C327A68.png)

M2: Select one or more models and click **Add to Photo Studio** in advanced.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886602399/D4AD9C4E7262B77B9FCE7EB615F48C23.png)

M3: Click the model to enter the model details page, **Photostudio Album** > **Click here** to add the product to Photo Studio.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886603813/B5F51714EB997F2CE3FCA9F1DB3E4D59.png)

M4: Go to the Photo Studio on the left, click **Add Product** to enter the furniture list, and use the above 1-3 method to add the model to the photo studio.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886605021/67513EFB7B299066CFC398792EF491B8.png)
