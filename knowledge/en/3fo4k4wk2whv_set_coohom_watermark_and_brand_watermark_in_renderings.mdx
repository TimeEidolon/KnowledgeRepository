In the renderings, Coohom watermark and brand watermark can be optionally removed, or you can re-upload the image to update the watermark. This article will provide a detailed explanation on how to set Coohom watermark and brand watermark.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j\_rdBhThjog)

# For Users

Users with Pro and above permissions

# Coohom Watermark

## Disable Watermark

Users with Pro and above permissions can choose not to display the Coohom watermark in images (turn off the watermark switch before rendering).

Enter **Workspace**, choose **"Settings - Customization - General Settings"** and **uncheck the Watermark**. After unchecking the watermark, watermarks in renderings, 360° walkthrough, and 720º VR tours will no longer be displayed.

* **Note:** If you want to hide the Coohom watermark, please uncheck this option before rendering .

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754042557998/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1754043364552/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

## Upload Watermark

If you need to upload your own watermark, please click **Update,** then click **Change Image**, and then **Save**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754042777788/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1754043364387/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Brand Watermark

The brand watermark is another watermark function displayed in renderings (excluding 360° walkthrough and 720º VR tours). Brand watermarks can be turned off and can only be edited in account settings.

## Disable Watermark

1. Open the **Rendered Gallery**, and select **"Edit - Picture Settings"** under the rendered image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754042906101/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. The **brand watermark** switch can be turned off. After turning off the watermark, watermarks in renderings will no longer be displayed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1754043364581/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

## Edit Watermark

1. In the upper right corner of the Workspace interface, you will find the **Profile Icon**. This icon typically resembles a person or an avatar and serves as a gateway to access your account settings.

2. Click the profile icon to open a dropdown menu. Select **Account Settings** from the menu.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1752822087738/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. In **Profile**, select **Name**, click **Edit,** enter the new name, and **Save**.

* **Note:** The edited name must be **unique**, because if another user has previously used the same name, it will not be effective in the image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1754043364467/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754042267871/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
