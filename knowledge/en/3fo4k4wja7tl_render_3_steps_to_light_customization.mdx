# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Enter the 3D design and choose the **Render**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746699570832/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Select Base Templates

1. Click **add to customize**.

2. **Name** the lighting scheme, select a **basic template** and customly adjust it based on the template.

* **Tips:** It is recommended to understand the functional effects of the basic lighting template before using it, [Click to view.](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/default-light-settings?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746699757012/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Adjusting Sunlight

Enter the custom lighting interface, the left panel can adjust the **color temperature, brightness and position** of the sunlight.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746700067207/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**The following buttons related to sunlight parameters do their specific roles:**

* **Status:** Turn on/off the sunlight effect.
* **Color Temperature:** Determine whether the overall color temperature is warm or cool.
* **Shadow Softness:** Adjusting the softness of sunlight shadows.
* **Brightness**: A property of visual perception that represents the effect of a light reflection, with a recommended brightness of no more than 50%. To change the luminance units, change them in **Preferences - Renderings - Brightness Measurement Unit**, as shown in the following image:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746703917525/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Sunlight projects to every room:** Turn on auto adjustment, the sunlight position will change automatically, so that each room has a sun cast effect when rendering.
* **Custom sunlight position****:** The azimuth and pitch angles of the sun can be customized.

# Add Lighting Effects

1. In the lights toolbar, you can select the **basic lights, IES lights, glow and volumetric lights** that you want to add, and drag it out to complete the addition.

* **Basic Lights**  
  + **Rectangle Light:** area light source/surface light source.
  + **Omni Light:** spotlight/sphere light.
* **IES Lights:** IES represents a file format in the lighting industry that describes the distribution of lights.
  + Many manufacturers provide IES files for their lights, and if you need a specified brand of light, you can upload your own IES lights.
* **Volumetric Lights:** It enhances the realism of the scene and makes the light look more three-dimensional and deep.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772086741164/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click on the lights in the base template to adjust the **color, brightness and height** of the lights in the right panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746703243963/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After the lighting project is set up, click **Save**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746703382667/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
