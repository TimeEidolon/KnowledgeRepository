All users

# Applied occasions

* When you are using a decoration mirror in your design, you need to tick this option in order to make the mirror show the reflected designs correctly.
* When you finish the rendering to find that some designs are missing in certain unexpected angles, you may tick this option to avoid this.
* When you need to render the design part that is inside the glass windows/doors, you will need to tick this option to avoid incomplete display of all designs.
