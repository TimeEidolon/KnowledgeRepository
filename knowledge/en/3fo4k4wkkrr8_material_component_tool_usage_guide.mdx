# Function Features

**⭐****[Create your own ceiling templates](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/material-component-tool-how-to-create-your-own-ceiling-template?kjl_source=hc)**

In the Ceiling Editor, you can save your current ceiling as a template for your next use.

**⭐****[Add light strips](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/material-component-tool-how-to-create-light-strips-in-ceiling?kjl_source=hc)**

Light Strip in Material & Component tool helps you to add lights anywhere you want, such as on ceilings, background walls, grounds, etc.

**⭐****[Draw sloped ceiling and attic/loft studio](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/material-component-draw-sloped-ceiling?kjl_source=hc)**

If you want to design an inclined/sloped roof for your project, we recommend you create it with our Material & Component tool.

**⭐Others - decorative wall panels, ceilings, and flooring moldings**

With Material & Component tool, you can model freely for more complex forms of objects, partitions, and elements. Watch the video tutorial for further learning:

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x9qVUXIoYXA)

# Entrance

1. Enter the **workspace** and select the **Creat New Project** in the upper right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779343695360/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the 3D design, select **Advanced Tool - Material & Component**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750995510363/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Steps

1. In the Material & Component tool, you can use **wall editor, ceiling editor, floor editor,** **edit paving, and modeling tools.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750996639808/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click a wall, a floor, or a ceiling, and **Wall Editor**, **Floor Editor**, or **Ceiling Editor** appears correspondingly.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750996893354/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750996927293/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750997003286/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click **Edit Paving** to perform advanced paving. You can design paving patterns as shown in the following figure.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1751005501959/3BD6B5F88E66952DCA16A7BFACF08B59.gif)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1751004671539/067457CD7A7124F4EC7CA7A266252B12.gif)

* **Note:** After you enter wall editor, floor editor, or ceiling editor from the DIY page, you can no longer enter the corresponding edit paving by clicking the pencil icon in the floating toolbar on the DIY page. To enter edit paving again, you need to first enter wall editor, floor editor, or ceiling editor, and click **Edit Paving** in the floating toolbar that appears.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751005215922/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1751004464306/3425EF7D49E1CF0C57E69AE04C40ECB1.gif)

4. You can use **modeling tools** to design custom shapes and build custom objects.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1751005839860/E48DC93DD0475A4BAB9CA8A120FE32F6.gif)
