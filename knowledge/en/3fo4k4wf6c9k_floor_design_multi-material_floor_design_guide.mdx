In the floor editor tool, you can divide the ground into different areas based on functional zones and then choose different materials for tiling, achieving personalized floor design.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=APKWq0UgJXk)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Select the ground and click to enter **Floor Editor.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775787151884/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Multi-material Floor Design

1. Place the mouse on the top **Toolkit**, select **Dimension**, and measure the dimensions needed to divide the ground.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775787519662/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775787704221/8B92EAD58843012015ACE668614C773A.gif)

2. Select the ground and click **Floor Editor** in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775787737942/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After entering the floor editor, use the top **Straight Line** tool to draw the required dividing lines (the size entered in the input box should be larger than the measured dividing distance). After drawing, you can see the ground has been divided into two areas.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775787806433/CD8FC63C91BC49078853BAFD719BE561.gif)

4. Click **Tiles** on the left-hand sidebar, select suitable material and apply it to the left area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775786891136/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Click **Flooring** on the left-hand sidebar, select suitable material and apply it to the right area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775786967885/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Select the wooden floor and adjust the **Angle** to the desired value in the control in the left column.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775818517410/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

7. After finishing the settings, click **Exit** **with** **Save** in the top-right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory effect image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775788755566/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
