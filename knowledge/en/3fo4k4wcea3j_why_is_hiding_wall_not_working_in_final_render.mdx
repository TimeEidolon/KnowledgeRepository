Some Coohom users report that they have put the camera behind the wall but hidden the wall in order to only render the interior part. However, they still only see the wall blocking the interior design by preview or final render. This article will help clear out this issue.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Some Coohom users report that they have put the camera behind the wall but hidden the wall in order to only render the interior part. However, they still only see the wall blocking the interior design by preview or final render. This article will help clear out this issue.

### Step 2
Reason: This hiding wall function is only for viewing the interior design easily which cannot erase the existence of the wall.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751617954307/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 3
Solution: To resolve this, you can use clipping function to make the camera shoot only the inside part while keeping the camera behind the wall.
