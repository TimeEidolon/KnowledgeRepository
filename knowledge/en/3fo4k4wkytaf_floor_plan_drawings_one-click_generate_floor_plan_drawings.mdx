Want to easily generate floor drawings in interior design? This operation guide will explain in detail to you: from adjusting the color style, setting the size and proportion, adding text annotations to downloading drawings, helping you to efficiently edit and export floor plan drawings.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GXCCEt3CUlU)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Enter the 3D design, select **Drawings - Floor Plan Drawings**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750917425461/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Adjust the Drawing Color Style

The toolbar on the right can adjust the style and color style of the floor plan drawings. For specific operations, please see the tutorial document: [Modify the Color Styles of the Floor Plan Drawings](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/modify-the-color-scheme-of-the-floor-plan-drawings?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750918042336/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Set the Drawing Size

Click the **adjust the size** tool in the lower right corner to adjust the drawing size and proportion.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750918375238/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Add Text Annotations

1. The toolbar above can add text, pictures and annotations to the drawings.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750918508459/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the label mode, you can use the drawing edit tools above to mark the floor plan again.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750918885927/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Download Floor Plan Drawings

After the adjustment is completed, click **Download** in the upper right corner to choose to download the image or PDF format.

* Note: The download feature is available to Elite only.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750919100641/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750919287687/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
