To provide a faster and better rendering experience, we will upgrade our lighting templates and phase out the old versions:

1. **OfflineTime**: September 15, 2025
2. **Templates to be Deprecated**: [Ultra-Fast 1.0], [Ultra-Fast 2.0], [Ultra-Fast 2.1]
3. **Replacement**: These will be automatically switched to [Ultra-Fast 3.0] or [Ultra-Fast 3.1]
4. **New "One-Click Upgrade" Feature**: Both the rendering page and album page will support an **Upgrade** button. With one click, old templates will be quickly converted to the new version. All lighting parameters will remain the same, so you won't need to reset them.

# Template Offline Details

## 1. "Offline Soon" Label

**① Description**: A “Offline Soon” label will appear when you use or view [Ultra-Fast 1.0], [Ultra-Fast 2.0], [Ultra-Fast 2.1], or existing Custom Lighting templates.

**② Where It Appears**:

* Lighting template list & template version selection page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1758005797210/BC45E84F73FE8540D34F431C2D45A3E0.png)

* Custom Lighting creation pop-up & Custom Lighting templates in existing projects.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1758005796885/D9ABBA3C9F738290CAF872EE2510ED42.jpg)

**③** **Post-deprecation behavior:**

After deprecation, [Ultra-Fast 1.0], [Ultra-Fast 2.0], and [Ultra-Fast 2.1] lighting templates will no longer appear in the rendering pop-up and will be automatically replaced with [Ultra-Fast 3.0] or [Ultra-Fast 3.1].  
Custom Lighting templates in existing projects will no longer support rendering, and high-resolution upgrades in the album will be unavailable.

## 2. One-Click Upgrade

****①** Description**: The  "One-Click Upgrade" feature will fully inherit old template lighting parameters (e.g., virtual lights, sunlight settings). However, due to engine differences, certain effects (such as material performance) may slightly vary and require manual adjustments.

****②** Where It Appears**:

* **Rendering Page**:**Custom Lighting templates in existing projects will provide an "Upgrade" button.**  
  After clicking, the old template will be copied into a new template, with all lighting parameters preserved. No manual adjustments are required, and users can continue editing and rendering directly on the new template.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1758012960346/CB50F3383C2860630D874F533DAD4EF1.png)

* **Album Page**:**When enlarging the resolution,** if a deprecated template is in use, a **"One-Click Upgrade"** option will also be provided. The upgraded template will be automatically saved to the Custom Lighting list.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1758013732922/94B742EF19F1EF86E2F66835FC9867D5.jpg)

## 3. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What templates can I use after old ones are deprecated?**  
**A:** The system will recommend [Ultra-Fast3.0] and [Ultra-Fast3.1]. These new templates retain the strengths of previous versions while further improving rendering speed and image quality.
