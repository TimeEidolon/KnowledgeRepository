Designers can customize the size of the internal zone and quickly implement the design of the cabinet's internal structure. The design internal zone can be used to add false doors, add decorative door panels to the side of the cabinet, and accurately divide the cabinet's internal space to meet the personalized design needs of different users.

## Entrance Two

1、Select **Kitchen＆Bath** / **Closet** to enter the customization interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1737454894562/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Adjust the Size of the **Internal Zone**

## Use the Parameter Panel to Adjust the **Internal Zone** Size

After entering the **Design Internal Zone** interface, **Select Internal Zone-Custom inner space** in the upper toolbar, and adjust the **Width and Height values** ​​in the parameter panel on the right **(you can directly enter +- values ​​in the parameter panel without manual calculation)**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1737514542347/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737514688186/200B287673455C51A202544A3D8E42F8.gif)

## **Resize the Internal Zone with Scale**

**Win system:** Press and hold the **Ctrl** key, select the direction arrow and drag it to any position to stop.

**Mac system:** Hold down the **Command** key and then select the direction arrow and drag it to any position to stop.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737515111127/48D2282AA218BDBC56A5E52BBC19CD36.gif)

# Place Parts

After setting the internal zone size, drag the parts into the cabinet space, and the panel size will automatically adapt to the internal zone size.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737515356156/A31EDEA83F1B1F91F3E2CFF90D05F3D4.gif)

# Add Doors

## **Internal Zone** **Direction Switch**

In the angle column of the parameter panel on the right, you can choose to switch the internal zone direction, which determines the direction of adding doors.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1737516090447/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* If you need to add decorative doors on the side of the cabinet, select the internal zone direction as the direction to add the doors.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737517724449/090B1C6078A23695875A17EF4731C624.gif)

## Drag Doors

Drag the doors from the **Parts** and place it on the cabinet with the internal zone direction set.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1737518033959/AFE97FA21D3336E38F764B381351E336.gif)
