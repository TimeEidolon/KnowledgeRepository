When moving the canvas or adjusting material and lighting parameters, real-time preview allows you to observe the design effects in real-time. The split screen preview function enables bi-directional collaboration —— you can focus on adjusting rendering parameters, moving the canvas, or optimizing materials and lighting on one computer screen, while another screen displays the rendering effect in real-time, allowing design adjustments and effect preview without interference, greatly enhancing operational efficiency~

### Step 5
Split screen preview provides a **Lock** function. When a split screen window needs to be fixed at a specific view, click **"Lock Current Perspective"** under the current view to fix the split screen window view. When the split screen window needs to follow the canvas changes, click **"Unlock Current Perspective".**

# Split Screen Preview

1. After entering the real-time rendering tool, click on **"Split Screen Preview"** in the upper toolbar or **"Switch to Split Screen Preview"** in the preview box in the upper right corner to switch to another screen for real-time preview.
