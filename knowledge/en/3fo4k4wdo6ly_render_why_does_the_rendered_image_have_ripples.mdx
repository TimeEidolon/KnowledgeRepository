# Problem

Why do ripples appear in rendering images?

# Solution

1. Eliminate lighting issues.

2. Caused by insufficient color depth, compression distortion, or the monitor's color range being inadequate. Reducing the contrast between light and dark might help but cannot completely eliminate the issue. Subsequent technical evaluation may be required to optimize the possibility: consider saving the image as a higher color depth to resolve (e.g., render output as 16-bit or 32-bit images).
