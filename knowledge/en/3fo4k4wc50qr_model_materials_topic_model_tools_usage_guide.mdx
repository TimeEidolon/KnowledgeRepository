Curated model spotlights from top websites, all within the tool! It is a super convenient favorites folder, featuring high-quality models neatly categorized.

# For Users

All users

# Select Models from Model Topic

## Method One

Step 1: Go to the Coohom project interface and click on **Models** - **Popular**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300097432/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 2: Click on **Trend**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768299500908/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 3: Past model topics are all here. You can use the filters above to find the model topics you want.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768299572873/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 4: Click to enter the model spotlight you want, and start selecting models.

Step 5: Click on the model you are interested in to begin your exciting design journey.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300249947/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Move your mouse cursor over the model you are interested in, then click the star icon at the top-left corner of the model to add it to your favorites.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300388924/CEC16BFAFB1969BFB0F096CE77E011B5.png)

## Method Two

Step 1: Go to the Coohom homepage and click on **[3D Models]**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300743505/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 2: Click on **Featured Model** at the top right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300789630/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 3: Past model topics are all here. You can scroll up and down to browse the model topics that interest you and build your high-quality model library.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300828571/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 4: Click to enter the model spotlight you want, and start selecting your favorite models.

Move your mouse cursor over the model you are interested in, then click the star icon at the top-left corner of the model to add it to your favorites.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300861557/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 5: Click a model to open its detail page, where you can view detailed information, add it to your favorites, or jump to the design workspace to start designing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768301035062/7E324956620C81D698178626807583AF.png)
