Step 2: Click on **Trend**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768299500908/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 3: Past model topics are all here. You can use the filters above to find the model topics you want.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768299572873/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 4: Click to enter the model spotlight you want, and start selecting models.

Step 5: Click on the model you are interested in to begin your exciting design journey.

## Method Two

Step 1: Go to the Coohom homepage and click on **[3D Models]**.

Step 2: Click on **Featured Model** at the top right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1768300789630/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Step 3: Past model topics are all here. You can scroll up and down to browse the model topics that interest you and build your high-quality model library.
