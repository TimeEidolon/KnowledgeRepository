### After the texture is uploaded, the color difference is obvious after the Image or Video is generated

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851029889/A6D0C7303A57777F6E97EF501AE96977.png)

You can see the color difference between the Texture uploaded and after generating the image/video.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851032281/9D8607A49174961CF4E3A1271E93F9F7.png)

The reason why it has a huge color difference is that the Texture's Color Mode is CMYK, it needs to be converted into RGB.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733851034474/89FA4FA6311747CB367A08C3E39474B2.jpg)

After changing into RGB Color mode, the color difference is almost the same as the one you uploaded.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851036699/09F89B0D5DC41992DF789026659FC12A.png)

There is another reason is the texture you uploaded hasn't been color-corrected.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851037847/3788B928E6EAA9EEBB66432116338FDC.png)

The color of the texture uploaded is different from the color you want.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851038459/23D86594C3A7703C20B0586323761EF6.png)

After the color-correct.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851039053/D11846D51C7B3F08DA97ADF4CB71D32C.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851040263/835D3904C2EA658F689810A596D6C954.png)

And it can also be the lightning settings.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851042290/E010941633D8B16370A116C90F3EB04F.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851043623/7834EC402FC7B3D5D91371969C29254B.png)

After changing the color of the light. The effect will be better.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851046529/A89E87AE2C4351FB1CE959830A730552.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851048935/B4FB0F3A4A96F6CB6038B1C83BE6BC79.png)
