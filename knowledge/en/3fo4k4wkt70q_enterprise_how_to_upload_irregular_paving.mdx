Coohom only have three default shapes to choose. Users are able to customize shapes via AUTOCAD as only supported for CAD file.

Step 1: Click on Irregular Paving> Upload Irregular Paving

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907854698/A1FF5EFFD95A98CC99FFD373BAE2BEBB.png)

Step 2: Click on **upload from computer**, select texture from computer to upload.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907857283/164ADC0C937562148F6AC9E67630FD76.png)

Step 3: Key in the measurement accordingly and click **Save**. Click on Edit Shape.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907858155/F55787F4ACB74CFD2C1B49B38D43A9E7.png)

Step 4: Choose one of the default shape (three types of shape) as shown below. **Customize** button is to upload user own designed shape.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907860509/0E908388879EA0EFF65C01797838C4B7.png)

Step 5: After click **Save**, back to **Edit All** page. **Click to Refresh** to preview the shape.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907862121/D19B22A3B78A5D2B55153DB5E81C7899.png)

Step 6: Click **Save**. Next page, click **Submit**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907865130/4CA7FF62DDF2A1ECD5DBF3BFBC5C2633.png)

Step 7: Upload paving is located in library as shown below:

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907867730/E7D6CD11B678C465E4D524110468776E.png)
