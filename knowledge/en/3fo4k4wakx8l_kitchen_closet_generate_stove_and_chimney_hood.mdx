# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen & closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **kitchen**.

**Entrance One:** Select the **Generate-Stove** from the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742281400145/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Entrance Two:** Select the cabinet, and select **Generate-Stove** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742281433454/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Generate Stove

1. After entering the generate stove interface, select the cabinet that needs to be generated. In the basic parameters on the right panel, select add **Stove Style and Chimney Hood Style**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742281607514/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After selecting the appropriate style, determine the **Installation Type** **and** **Alignment**, modify the stove margin at the **Spacing Mode**, and then click **Generate**.

**Note: If the Generate button turns gray, you need to check whether the cabinet that needs to be generated is selected!**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742281669560/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After generating the stove and chimney hood, click **Finish** to exit the generate stove interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742281718899/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Q&A

**Q：**How to modify the stove size?

**A：**Exit the generation environment and click to select the **stove** to modify the **size and position** of the stove in the parameter panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742281770910/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
