In the wall editor tool, you can use the rectangle tool to divide the wall into multiple areas and paint different colors of wall paint in each area to achieve a personalized multi-color wall effect.

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Select the wall surface and click to enter **Wall Editor**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776325629534/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Multi-color Wall Design

1. Enter the wall editor page, select the **Rectangle** tool at the top to draw. First, determine the rectangle's endpoints, then input the corresponding values in the length and height text boxes as needed, and press the **Enter** key to confirm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776325883183/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

2. Click on **Wall Decor** in the left sidebar, select **Paints & Coatings** at the top, choose **Pink** in the **Color...**, and then select an appropriate material to place in the bottom area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776326742192/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327165764/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Next, choose **White** in the **Color...**, and then select an appropriate material to place in the top area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327366811/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. After that, you can drag to enlarge the top-right viewport to view the effect and observe whether adjustments are needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327629715/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. If no adjustments are needed, click on **Exit with Save** in the top-right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory rendering image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776327516049/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The final effect diagram is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1776329944278/04B0D2AD1B62D8534A2FD8C3A12782E5.jpg)
