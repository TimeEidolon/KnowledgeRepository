# Porcelain Tile Floor Design

1. On the floor editor page, drag the upper right corner window to enlarge it as needed to allow real-time preview during subsequent operations.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775726045505/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Tiles** in the left sidebar, Select the **Porcelain Tile** at the top, then click the desired material to apply it to the floor.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775727300153/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click the floor and set the desired laying method in the **Paving** section of the left panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775727323661/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. In the **Control** section, you can adjust properties such as the laying position**,** angle, and gap value of the floor.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775727394399/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775727469109/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

5. In the **Pattern** section, you can directly modify the dimensions. If you need to change the floor style, click the replace button to select suitable material for replacement.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775727525647/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. After finishing the settings, click **Exit with Save** in the upper-right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory render image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775727547144/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The final effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1775788852740/840FF58C6C44E3F9839713499E0F1BFD.jpg)
