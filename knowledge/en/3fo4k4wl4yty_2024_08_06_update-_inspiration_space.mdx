**Description**

Users can export all product quotes to PDF, not just select individual products.

**Before**

Previously, when consumers enter the inspiration space to complete the matching, they can only export all product quotes to PDF by default, rather than selecting individual products

**After**

Now, after consumers complete the matching in the inspiration space, they can select specific products to export the quotes and generate a PDF.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745245054156/56621F57053CFF6003ED033E15AF6B3F.png)

How to use  
When you create an Inspiration space template room, you need to turn on the switch to display prices for this template room, and then wait for the template room to be generated to see this feature.  
Note that you need to set the prices for the products in the template room separately in order to see the corresponding quotations for the products.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733932166678/59E507E6D2099D97E5FCACB724FF1523.png)
