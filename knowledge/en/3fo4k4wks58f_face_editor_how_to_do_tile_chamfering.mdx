## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

### Step 2
Click the face you want to edit.

### Step 3
Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

### Step 4
Drag the target tile under **Public Library** from the left panel to the face.

### Step 5
Select the tile.

### Step 6
Choose **Chamfer**.
