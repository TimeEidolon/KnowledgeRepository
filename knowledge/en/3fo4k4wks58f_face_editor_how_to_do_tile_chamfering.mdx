1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

4. Drag the target tile under **Public Library** from the left panel to the face.

5. Select the tile.

6. Choose **Chamfer**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733908452667/EC3BB01011E0F4B6C44985DC956FB671.jpeg)

7. Select edges and enter grinding value.

Note: Hold down Shift to select multiple tiles at one time.
