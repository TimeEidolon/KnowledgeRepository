After drawing the wall, the thickness and height of the wall can be adjusted to make the wall conform to the actual apartment type.

# For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen＆closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

# Entrance

1. Enter the kitchen＆closet design and choose the **Room**.

2. Select a **wall**, there are three types of walls: **straight wall, curved wall, and rect wall**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741087989378/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Adjust the Wall Thickness

**Method One:** In the parameter panel, you can set the **Basic-Thickness**, and can be selected in the value column or directly entered in the value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741159222304/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741159694937/C5741C720B10E1CFBF0F0282946CFED9.gif)

**Method Two:**Press the **Alt** to switch the thickness of the wall, which is convenient and fast.

* **Win System Shortcut：Alt**
* **mac System Shortcut：⌥**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741160268336/7BFA07DD76F576302DB2BE5FA32E4996.gif)

**Method Three:** Drag the wall anchor point to quickly adjust the wall thickness.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741160956722/721EE6C89CAC664871E570D45AEF5477.gif)

# Adjust the Wall Height

1. Switch to **3D mode**, press and hold the **Ctrl** to select the wall.

* **Win System Shortcut：Ctrl**
* **mac System Shortcut：⌘**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741161385807/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Switch to **Custom** in the parameter panel and adjust the **Height** in the **Basic**.

* The default drawing height of the wall is the same as the floor height.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741162321059/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741162473659/AEB8E732ABF0E8B0E1043DA9CDC423BE.gif)
