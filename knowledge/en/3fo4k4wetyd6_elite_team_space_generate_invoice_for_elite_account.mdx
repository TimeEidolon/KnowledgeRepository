Downloading invoice is a self-service, which Coohom does provide any manual invoices for our individual users. The payment invoices can be generated from the Coohom account.

# For Users

All Elite users

# Generate Invoice

1. **[Login](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)**with your Coohom account.

2. Click the**Profile** icon in the upper right corner to locate **My Subscription**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695286277/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click on **Purchase Record** in Subscription page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695339369/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Click the **Create Invoice** button, enter the required information in the three listed fields, check **Save as Invoice Template**, then click **Create**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695457020/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695495022/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Click the **Download Invoice** button to generate the invoice in your computer.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772695548294/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1772682184914/F1233AF29A34A75E376985C42A1078AD.png)

For any further help, please feel free to reach Coohom Customer Service team via: support@coohom.com.
