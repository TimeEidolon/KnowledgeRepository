1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849444306/5AD3F878257EA7AEC56510015EBAEB4C.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849444306/5AD3F878257EA7AEC56510015EBAEB4C.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
Click the face you want to edit.

### Step 3
Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

### Step 4
Drag the target textures under **Public Library** from the left panel to your floor plan.

### Step 5
Click the texture.

### Step 6
Click **Drag to Pave** in the floating toolbar.

### Step 7
Choose **Grid**, (**Mixing**), **Brickwork,** or **Checkerboard** paving method.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849445977/9094C451C9479E2050EEC9F053E5D727.jpeg)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849445977/9094C451C9479E2050EEC9F053E5D727.jpeg" alt="Step 7" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 8
Drag your mouse to pave your floor plan.

### Step 9
Left click to confirm.
