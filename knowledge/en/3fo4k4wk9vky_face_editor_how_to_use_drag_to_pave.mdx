1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849444306/5AD3F878257EA7AEC56510015EBAEB4C.png)

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

4. Drag the target textures under **Public Library** from the left panel to your floor plan.

5. Click the texture.

6. Click **Drag to Pave** in the floating toolbar.

7. Choose **Grid**, (**Mixing**), **Brickwork,** or **Checkerboard** paving method.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849445977/9094C451C9479E2050EEC9F053E5D727.jpeg)

8. Drag your mouse to pave your floor plan.

9. Left click to confirm.
