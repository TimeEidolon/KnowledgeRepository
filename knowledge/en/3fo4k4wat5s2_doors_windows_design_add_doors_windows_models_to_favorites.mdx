Doors＆Windows models and materials support add to favorites, and can be classified and add to custom named folders and can be used at any time.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=66IXzRhBDv0)

# For Users

All users

# Add Doors＆Windows Models to Favorites

## Entrance One

1. Select **Doors＆Windows** to enter the customization interface.

2. Click the Doors＆Windows model, click **Add to Favorites** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740387168180/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Entrance Two

Select the Doors＆Windows model in the **Public Catalog**, click the upper left corner ☆, One-click **add to favorites**.

# Add Materials to Favorites

## Entrance One

In the **style** of the parameter panel, select the Doors＆Windows material and **add to favorites**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740387456759/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Entrance Two

Select the Doors＆Windows material in the **Public Catalog**, click the upper left corner ☆, One-click **add to favorites**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740387625058/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Favorites Category

1. Click **Add to Favorites** in the floating toolbar to **create folder** and customize the name to classify favorites.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1740133127243/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Personal Catalog**, under the **overall** bar is the Doors＆Windows favorites, under the **material** bar is the Doors＆Windows materials favorites, select the materials under the folder and drag them out to use.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1739957395366/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1739957465753/21CD7E179214935F1C7A8EBB848FBF95.gif)
