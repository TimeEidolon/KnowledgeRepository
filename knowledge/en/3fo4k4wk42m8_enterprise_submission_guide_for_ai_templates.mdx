Enterprise users can submit AI templates in the workspace. After uploading, they can be accessed in the design tool’s inspiration library/ai, making it convenient to directly use them for future designs and reduce repetitive work. This article will introduce how to submit AI templates and use them in the design tool.

# For Users

All enterprise users

# Entrance

Enter the workspace and click **AI Templates** to **Submit**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764127542780/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Submit AI Templates

1. After clicking **Submit**, select **Enterprise Catalog**, then click **Submit** again.

* Note**:** Submitted designs must be fully completed and accurately named for each space.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061709/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. Choose the design to upload and click **Next** at the bottom.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764128332049/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Set **Project Name, Style, Description**, etc., then click **Next** at the bottom.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061685/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

4. Select the spaces to upload, then set **Title, Scene, Showmap, and Configure the Ceilings，Placement Products** as prompted (if selecting all spaces, all selected spaces need setting).

* Uploaded model rooms can be used in both the **Smart Assistant** and **Inspiration Library**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764139666128/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Configure the Ceiling**: Click **Select the Associated Ceiling**, choose the required ceiling in the popup panel, then **OK**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061723/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764139814698/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Placement Products**: Select **Configure Single Product/Configure Product Packages**, click **Finished Product**, then click **Add Finished Product** on the right. In the pop-up panel, select the product and click **Confirm**. (only one configuration method is supported, and it only works with Cloud Design 5.0 tools).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061707/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764139884285/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Once completed, click **Next** to view the space quality score, then click **Submit & Publish**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061713/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764141848037/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Note:** If the score is low, click **View** to see specific issues, then return to the **Prev** step for adjustments. Once adjusted, click **Submit & Publish**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061740/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* The enterprise main account directly shelves submissions, while sub-account users need approval from the main account under **Template Manager** before shelving.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764223907292/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061727/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Using in Tools

After uploading, enter the design tool to find and use the model rooms in **AI** and **Inspiration Library**.

## AI **Style**

1. Select **AI** in the left sidebar and go to **Style**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764146243936/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the top **Enterprise Catalog**, choose uploaded materials from the left, then select application type at the bottom and click **Apply**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061747/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

## Inspiration Library

Select **Inspiration Library** in the left sidebar, then click **Enterprise Library** at the top, choose materials, and in the popup panel, click **Pick them All**. Afterward, hard and soft decorations will appear in the tool for further adjustments.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764215813573/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1764228061809/9F78812AAB2F6E08258F152F8BDD823D.gif)

# Important notes

1. Successfully submitted model rooms by the main account will be directly shelved in the **Enterprise Library**.

2. Sub-account submissions require approval from the main account or administrator before being shelved in the **Enterprise Library**.

3. After completing the upload, there will be a delay before the model room appears in the **Inspiration Library** tool for use, typically becoming available the next day.

4. If **Select the Associated Ceiling** is disabled in **Configure the** **Ceiling**, follow these steps:

* Step 1: In the design tool, go to **Ceiling Editor**, draw the ceiling, and **Save as Ceiling Template**. Detailed operations can be seen in the online documentation: [Click to View](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/ceiling-design-create-your-own-ceiling-template?preview=1&kjl_source=hc)
* Step 2: In the workspace page, click **AI Templates - Config Management** in the left sidebar, then choose **Manage** **Ceilings** at the top and click **Add to**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764143236209/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* Step 3: In the popup panel, set name, room type, then click **OK**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228061701/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* Step 4: **Click** **Configure**. Choose the ceiling style in the popup configuration page, then click **OK**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764143638229/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1764228067339/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* Step 5: Click **OK** for the corresponding ceiling in the configuration management page to complete the configuration.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764143720730/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
