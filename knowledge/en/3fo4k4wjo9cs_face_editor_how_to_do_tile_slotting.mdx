## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848822211/84A3C50E8269515D7F7C1F175FCFF37B.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848822211/84A3C50E8269515D7F7C1F175FCFF37B.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
Click the face you want to edit.

### Step 3
Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848824044/7434B074B10D72AEBB45B04BAF9E99DC.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848824044/7434B074B10D72AEBB45B04BAF9E99DC.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
Drag the target tile under **Public Library** from the left panel to the face.

### Step 5
Select the tile.

### Step 6
Choose the **Slotting** tab to edit. Note: Hold down Shift to slot multiple tiles at one time.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733848824765/7641F9EEBEE92A2BD939FA68E8D8ED0F.jpeg)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733848824765/7641F9EEBEE92A2BD939FA68E8D8ED0F.jpeg" alt="Step 6" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />


1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848822211/84A3C50E8269515D7F7C1F175FCFF37B.png)

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848824044/7434B074B10D72AEBB45B04BAF9E99DC.png)

4. Drag the target tile under **Public Library** from the left panel to the face.

5. Select the tile.

6. Choose the **Slotting** tab to edit.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733848824765/7641F9EEBEE92A2BD939FA68E8D8ED0F.jpeg)

Note: Hold down Shift to slot multiple tiles at one time.
