1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848822211/84A3C50E8269515D7F7C1F175FCFF37B.png)

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848824044/7434B074B10D72AEBB45B04BAF9E99DC.png)

4. Drag the target tile under **Public Library** from the left panel to the face.

5. Select the tile.

6. Choose the **Slotting** tab to edit.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733848824765/7641F9EEBEE92A2BD939FA68E8D8ED0F.jpeg)

Note: Hold down Shift to slot multiple tiles at one time.
