### **Function Introduction**

Do you dislike diamond-shaped shower rooms? Do you want to see what style of shower room suits your bathroom space better? One-click replacement of shower room styles allows you to see the difference instantly!

### **Operation Steps**

Step 1: Select a shower room model and choose the "Style Replacement" from the right dashboard.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848834037/C721C8DC4365F257101C8AE1CF4F0E21.png)

Step 2: Click on the "Shower Room Style", choose another style of the shower room, and replace it.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848835458/3E6EC06EA16B716463FD2A54D7E1D4DC.png)
