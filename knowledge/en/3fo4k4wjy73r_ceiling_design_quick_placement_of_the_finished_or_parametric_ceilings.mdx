In the design tool, two types of ceiling models can be directly placed: finished ceilings and parametric ceilings. Parametric ceilings support flexible editing, allowing you to adjust dimensions, materials, lighting, and other attributes as needed. Finished ceilings, on the other hand, only support dimension modifications. You can select the appropriate ceiling type as needed to quickly complete ceiling design plans and improve work efficiency.

# Placing Parametric Ceilings

## Entrance

Click **Models** in the left sidebar, select **Construction—Parametric Ceilings**, you can view a wealth of ceiling materials.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773986556414/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Placing Ceilings

Click on the desired ceiling and drag it to the target location. The system will automatically match the room dimensions.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1773988720803/EE4FB18CCEB62F9F3B337D3653EE3E6D.gif)

## Modifying Parameters

1. After selecting the ceiling, you can adjust the dimensions in the **Basic Properties** on the right as needed. By modifying the model parameters, you can quickly achieve customized ceiling effects required for your project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773987727298/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. You can also adjust the **Light Strip** **Width, Length**, **Brightness**, and **Color**, as well as the **Downlight** style and **Spacing** in advanced parameters.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1773988720910/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. Additionally, you can adjust the **Corner Line** style and position in advanced parameters.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773988062313/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Double-click the ceiling to enter edit mode, then click the replace button for materials on the right to select the desired material. After applying the material, click a blank area to exit the edit mode and view the final effect.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1773988721060/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773988874515/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1773988245176/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)
