# Rename Your Projects

## Method One

1. Go to **Workspace - Projects**, select the project you want to rename, click **Rename**, and you can rename the project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752568544570/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752568584572/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Method Two

1. Select the project you want to rename, click **Project Details.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752568632189/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click the ... symbol in the upper right corner, select **Edit Project Information**, and you can rename the project.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752568674065/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1752568816484/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
