1. Find the Tile Texture in your Enterprise Libray.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930745051/9856F5562A686D7665333674EFC579E1.png)

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930747694/8EB28F88DB79977642EC5CDA5B80B806.png)
3. Change the Value of Hue.  
   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930750274/6E499982FE1D3FB57201316D80D3C0EC.png)
