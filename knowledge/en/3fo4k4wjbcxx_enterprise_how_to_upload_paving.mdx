### Material Upload Requirements:

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733829727743/ADDAA354E1287733B1D46113EF19B49E.gif)

4. The color mode of the texture file is RGB color;

5. Upload up to 200 paving at a time.

6. JPG and PNG formats are allowed
