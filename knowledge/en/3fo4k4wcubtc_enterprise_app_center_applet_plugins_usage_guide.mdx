The Coohom App Center provides designers and enterprise users with a more efficient and convenient work experience. Through simple steps, you can easily install and use various applet plugins to enrich the functionality of design tools and improve work efficiency. This article will detail how to install and enable applet plugins in the Coohom App Center and provide related consultation and purchase service contact information to help you quickly get started and fully leverage the value of applet plugin tools~


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

Entrance Enter the **Workspace** and select **App Center** in the **Application**.

### Step 2
Install Applet Plugins Enter the app center and select the applet plugin you want to install. * Currently, we have listed two applet plugins in the app center. Please stay tuned as we will continue to list more applet plugins, offering you a richer and more diverse selection.

### Step 3
