The Coohom App Center provides designers and enterprise users with a more efficient and convenient work experience. Through simple steps, you can easily install and use various applet plugins to enrich the functionality of design tools and improve work efficiency. This article will detail how to install and enable applet plugins in the Coohom App Center and provide related consultation and purchase service contact information to help you quickly get started and fully leverage the value of applet plugin tools~

# Install Applet Plugins

Enter the app center and select the applet plugin you want to install.

* Currently, we have listed two applet plugins in the app center. Please stay tuned as we will continue to list more applet plugins, offering you a richer and more diverse selection.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753435869199/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Enter the applet plugin details page and click **Install**.

**Note:**

* **Trial version applet plugins can be installed directly. After the trial period, please contact the applet plugin developer for purchase.**
* **Official version applet plugins require contacting the applet plugin developer for purchase. Specific contact information can be found below.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753435920620/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

After successful installation, click **Get** **Started**, and it will automatically redirect to the design tool interface.

# Use Applet Plugins

After successful installation, use it on the applet page in the tool.

# One-click Inquiry

If you are interested in the applet plugin and want to inquire about related information, there are two ways to contact the service provider.

**Method One:** The applet plugin details page contains contact information for third-party service providers. You can contact them to inquire about applet plugin information and purchase methods.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753436760158/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Method Two:** Click **Contact Us**, fill in your **Name, Mail**, and **Consultation Content**. The third-party service provider will contact you upon receiving your inquiry.
