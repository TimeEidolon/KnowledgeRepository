Dear Coohomers,

You can always check your account status in Profile Icon of upper right corner-**My subscription**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547832067/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547932524/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

If you are having an auto-renewal plan, you can check the renewal information in the **Billing Info** where you can [*cancel subscription*](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/how-to-cancel-your-subscription?kjl_source=hc) or [*update the card information*](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/how-to-update-payment-information?kjl_source=hc).

You can also [*download the invoice*](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/how-to-generate-invoice-for-pro-account?kjl_source=hc) from **Purchase Record**.

If you still have any concerns, please feel free to contact Coohom Customer Serice team via **[*Live Chat*](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/how-to-contact-live-chat?kjl_source=hc)** or send email directly to **support@coohom.com**.
