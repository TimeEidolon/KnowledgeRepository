### Overview

In Coohom, you can generate sinks for the cabinets that you select. You can also configure the sink that you want to generate.

### **Steps**

1. Enter the project that you want to work on. In the left-side navigation pane, click Custom Design. In the Custom Design panel, click Closet.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835890614/E27E0F20C5A5377A49B4CAD2BD6E7844.png)

2. In the tool bar that appears, hover your cursor over the Generate icon. From the drop-down menu, select Sink.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835893363/E44AFDD5EC710F2B672A5293DF69D0DA.png)

3. Then, select the cabinet for which you want to generate a sink.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835896573/A1ADBD8E0CBC6606B72D80607D79D234.png)

4. In the Sink pane on the right, click on the + icon to choose a sink style from Public Catalog or Brand Zone. Then, a thumbnail of the sink replaces the + icon.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835901226/D3F2B300AD3DB366C268CA197EE01D2E.png)

5. Next, expand the Sink size menu to set the width, depth, and height parameters for the sink.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835903684/5F63F9EEA991C70476DEEFA7AC8751CB.png)

6. If the style of sink comes with a faucet hole, you can configure a tap. In this case, you need to choose its installation type.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835906008/87A54987F558794DFBA5034856F11921.png)

7. At the lower part of the Sink panel, you can set the spacing parameters for the sink. These include left, right, front, and back edge distance.

A top view sketch of the sink and the cabinet, indicated with the default width and depth figures, is shown for your reference.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835908355/99F9573347F0DF234C8DF3A5BBE139A0.png)

8. After you configure the sink, click the Generate button at the bottom of the right-side panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835911809/CA7EC78EEB7699968C8089D6BE0DB585.png)

9. The sink is then generated based on your configurations. You can click the Finish button on the top right corner of the page to exit the feature.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733835914726/17B64475B3476C2D0E4A00701D84B123.png)
