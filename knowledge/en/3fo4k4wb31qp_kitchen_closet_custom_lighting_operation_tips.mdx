In the images tools, custom lighting has a variety of lighting options to meet the lighting needs of different scenes. You can also select lights in batches and adjust lighting parameters with one click to greatly improve design efficiency.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Click **add to customize**.

### Step 2
**Name** the lighting scheme, select a **basic template** and customly adjust it based on the template.

### Step 3
Enter the custom lighting interface, the left panel can adjust the color temperature, brightness and position of the sunlight.

# Add Custom Lights

1. Click **add to customize**.

2. **Name** the lighting scheme, select a **basic template** and customly adjust it based on the template.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1743154663513/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. Enter the custom lighting interface, the left panel can adjust the color temperature, brightness and position of the sunlight.

4. Click on the lights in the base template to adjust the color, brightness and height of the lights in the right panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743154515219/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. In the lights toolbar, you can select the **basic lights, IES lights, glow and volumetric lights** that you want to add, and drag it out to complete the addition.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743665831123/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1743666074436/D6141E74A31017CF00934AE72BBD133C.gif)

6. After the lighting project is set up, click **Save**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743667339124/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Process Lights in Batches

## Method One: Press **Shift / ⇧** Multiple Select Lights + Filter

When there are many downlights and spotlights in large spaces, this method can better improve efficiency.

* Hold down the **Shift / ⇧** to box or click multiple lights to make batch adjustments; you can also filter the light type in the toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743668720407/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1743669175975/A6CFC3CF4488FCF48C4D865F7085510D.gif)

## Method Two: Apply the Parameters to the Same Lights

You can quickly apply the parameters to all lights of the same type.

* After selecting a light, adjusting the color temperature, brightness and other properties, click **Apply the parameters to the same lights**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743670326555/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Method Three: Duplicate Property

You can quickly apply the parameters to certain specified lights of the same type.

* After selecting a light, click **Duplicate Property**, then click on other lights of the same type to paste the properties.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1743670546138/BE1B246B68B8B52834378D55FC49A798.gif)

# Replace Lights

Select the **IES light**, click **Replace** in the floating toolbar to replace other styles of IES lights.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1743670880035/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Three-axis Directional Replication Lights

When placing cabinet panels lights, it helps to improve efficiency.

* After selecting a light,**hold on the Alt / ⌥** and drag the three-axis control to copy along the axis direction.
