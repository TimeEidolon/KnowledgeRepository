# Problem

How to solve the color discrepancy after model upload

# Solution

Enter the rendering-material resource manager and find the non-gamma 2.2 map, then enter the material editor, or directly enter the material editor-slate material editor, find the non-gamma 2.2 map, open it, change to "Override-2.2" and save (overwrite the original map)
