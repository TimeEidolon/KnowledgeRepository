In the Original Material Library, you only need to upload a material image to publish a newly created material simultaneously across various industries, including furniture, paving, Kitchen & Bath and Closet, significantly saving time and effort for material maintenance. This document will give you a detailed explanation of how to create a material in the Original Material Library.

# For Users

Enterprise users with the "Original Material Library" function enabled.

# Prerequisites

You need to contact your CSC to try or purchase services related to the "Original Material Library".

# Create a New Material and Publish

Once the function is enabled, you can directly create a new original material.

## 1. Create an Original Material

Enter the **Workspace**, select **"Enterprise Catalog - Original Material Library"**, click **[Create an Original Material]**, and enter the material creation tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753771253600/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## 2. Replace Material Textures

First, select the type of material from the left panel, then adjust the texture and color, and effect properties of the material on the right panel according to specific needs.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753771543105/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## 3. Save Original Materials

After completing **material property editing**, click **Save** to supplement the remaining material information.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753771788755/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## 4. Publish Original Materials

Once the original material is created, it cannot be directly used in the tool. You need to select these original materials and publish them to the required industry.

1. Select the **original material** needed for use in the tool, then click **Material Publishing**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753771969951/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Set the **Merchandise Library and Folder** where the published material is located, then click **Next** **Step** after setting.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753772322065/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Set the **Rendering Classification** of the published material, then click **Release** after setting.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753772555756/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Extract from Existing Materials

You can also extract materials from existing materials into the **Original****Material Library** and send them to other industries.

## 1. Select a Non-Original Material

From any industry in the **Enterprise Catalog**, select one or more existing materials, click **Bulk Action/Edit**, then select **Material Extraction**.

As shown below, it demonstrates the material extraction methods for **Textures, Paving, and Custom Product Catalog**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753772764221/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753773069376/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753773168068/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## 2. Publish Original Materials

After material extraction is complete, you can enter the **Original Material Library**, find the corresponding original material, and publish it to the required industry.

* The operation steps are the same as above, for detailed steps, refer to the previous content: **"****Create a New Material and Publish — 4. Publish Original Materials".**
