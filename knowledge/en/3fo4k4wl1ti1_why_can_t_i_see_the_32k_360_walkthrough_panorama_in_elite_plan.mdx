Dear Coohomer,

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Dear Coohomer, While trying to render the 360 panorama, you may find that the 32K resolution is missing. In this case, kindly check whether you are in the **Fast** light template. If so, that's correct as Coohom currently is only supporting the maximum resolution of 16K for **Fast** light template: Ultra Fast Daylight, Ultra Fast Nightlight to ensure the fast rendered input.

### Step 2
If you want to render the 32K resolution, please make sure that you select other light templates than Fast template such as Realistic ones.
