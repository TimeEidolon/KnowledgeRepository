Dear Coohomer,

While trying to render the 360 panorama, you may find that the 32K resolution is missing. In this case, kindly check whether you are in the **Fast** light template. If so, that's correct as Coohom currently is only supporting the maximum resolution of 16K for **Fast** light template: Ultra Fast Daylight, Ultra Fast Nightlight to ensure the fast rendered input.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974279702/C3A6697D0579FE9E2367C49AC1367618.png)

If you want to render the 32K resolution, please make sure that you select other light templates than Fast template such as Realistic ones.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974281150/73181CB2C6A8795E6C1DC482487FEAED.png)

For any further queries or concerns, please feel free to reach Live chat or directly send email to support@coohom.com.
