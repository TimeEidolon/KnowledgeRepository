Some users will wonder where to change the floor area in Coohom. This article will show the guide on how to do that.

## Users: All Users


## Operation Steps

**Step one**: Enter the project created or create a new project

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1767865545698/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Step two**: Switch to 2D view and find the interior area where you can change the floor plan area

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1767865628822/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Important note**:

1) Here the interior area is for the whole floor plan. Changing the floor area of a certain room is not supported this way. You can change the wall length and width to change the area for a certain room.

2) External area is not calculated here either.
