Admins and above can upload non-parametric 3D assets such as models, materials, and hard furnishings to the team library, and after successful uploading, all team members can use them during the design process.

# For Users

Team admins and above, gradually opening up~

# Entrance

In the enterprise backend, click **Team Library - Products.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772608262260/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Upload Team Model Materials

1. Select the required product category in **Team Library - Products** (e.g., uploading a Textures).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772608286724/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Then click **New Material** in the upper right corner, and choose **Upload from Computer**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772608307473/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772608357092/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. In the pop-up panel, choose the texture image you want to upload and click **Open**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772608430804/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Next, provide the required information for the texture upload (e.g., name, material category, texture category, etc.). After completing the details, click **Upload Texture** at the bottom.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772608508998/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Upon successful upload, a relevant notification will appear. You can choose to **Continue  to Upload** or **Check Textures**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772608700889/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Application on Tools

All team members can access the uploaded assets in the design tools page by clicking the left-side **Team Library**. The assets can be found in the corresponding folder and directly dragged onto the layout for application.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1772609302205/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
