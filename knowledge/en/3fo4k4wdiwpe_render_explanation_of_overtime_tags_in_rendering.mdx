### Step 2
Cause of the Problem Rendering detection introduced rendering task overtime prompts and labels; providing a clearer indication of the reasons for longer rendering times. When overtime prompts/labels appear, it means that there are models or materials in the rendering that affect the rendering speed, and the corresponding optimization options were not clicked before rendering. Or ultra-realistic rendering and other parameters were enabled in the panorama (these parameters require more rendering resources to provide better rendering results, thus taking relatively longer). ps: This prompt is only for reminder purposes and does not increase the rendering time. # Cause of the Problem

Rendering detection introduced rendering task overtime prompts and labels; providing a clearer indication of the reasons for longer rendering times.

When overtime prompts/labels appear, it means that there are models or materials in the rendering that affect the rendering speed, and the corresponding optimization options were not clicked before rendering. Or ultra-realistic rendering and other parameters were enabled in the panorama (these parameters require more rendering resources to provide better rendering results, thus taking relatively longer).

ps: This prompt is only for reminder purposes and does not increase the rendering time. The rendering duration mainly depends on the complexity of the design, model materials, and overtime parameters.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1761548730802/521C2C96DB04405C47056425665B249A.png)
