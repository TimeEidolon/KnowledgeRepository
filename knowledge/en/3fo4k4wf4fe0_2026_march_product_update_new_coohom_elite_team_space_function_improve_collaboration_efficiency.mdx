# 1. Functions for All Users

## Key Update Details

**[Elite Team Space] Newly added Coohom Elite team function** **(Available to newly upgraded Elite users)**, **[Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/coohom-elite-plan-team-function-overview?kjl_source=hc)**

Coohom Elite is tailored for professional designers and small to medium teams, combining high-quality rendering, automated construction drawing exports, and a cloud-based team collaboration space to help teams shorten delivery cycles and improve efficiency.

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfyZOgMpnLc)

**[Floor Plan Design] After importing drawings, you can use the pre-import processing tool to efficiently correct the copying drawings**, **[Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/floor-plan-efficiently-correct-imported-floor-plan-drawings?kjl_source=hc)**

In the floor plan tool, use the pre-import processing feature to solve pre-processing issues such as distortion of floor plan images and messy visuals in one step. No complex operations are needed—complete perspective correction and image cleanup with one click, combined with accurate scale settings to easily restore the real dimensions of the floor plan, quickly completing intelligent floor plan recognition for more efficient.

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=\_LtTVgDxCOc)

# 2. Functions for Enterprise Users

For details on enabling and using enterprise functions, please consult your corresponding CSC.

## Key Update Details

**[Custom Tools] Boolean modeling production docking supports enterprises to open configurations independently (Available to some users), [Click here for details and learn how to use](https://www.coohom.com/helpcenter/enterprise-boolean-modeling-production-docking-configuration-instructions?kjl_source=hc)**

The boolean modeling function can be used for tool front-end hardware, panel hole punching, and slotting. If you wish to obtain the results of Boolean modeling during production docking, you need to proactively enable the configuration and apply it.
