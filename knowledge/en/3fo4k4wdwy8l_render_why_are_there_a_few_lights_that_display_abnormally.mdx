# Problem

Two recessed lights are not shining, while other lights are displaying normally.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135712628/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Solution

The recessed lights are embedded into the ceiling; adjust the position of the recessed lights to fix the issue.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135712590/8930A5C65CDA4F41382139D3E47C1E18.png)
