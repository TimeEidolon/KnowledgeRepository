# Problem

The edge of the shadow is too sharp and the darkness is too high, causing the spatial level to be abrupt.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135959103/A45F5AEAA47C6969E56239E26A2157F2.png)

# Solution

1. Due to the shadow created by sunlight, you can adjust the shadow softness; the higher the value, the softer the shadow edge.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135959114/852DB08411120674B0649707C1F842DF.png)

2. In the lighting version, using a larger light source (such as an area light) will make the shadows softer and more natural.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135959110/299064F71AF542EFBE52BB11D1DDB87E.png)

3. It is recommended to use the latest lighting version, which can produce more natural lighting.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135959138/7BD1BF14468054DCDC3CE455D4E2E4F3.png)
