**Tutorial**

1. Go to the Custom design> Closet.

Go to the Custom design> Closet.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853612588/92C317FB2AA858B3D1F8BA014F0A29B0.png)

2. Find the Cabinet

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853614827/9923FDFAEA8C175E657DCBEEB7DFE4EC.png)

3. Drag Staircase(Cabinet) into floorplan and adjust size.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853616228/0AA49E8E40CEC1A6A843909ACE34F515.png)

4. Detailed parameters can be adjusted, such as the number of doors, the size of each door, and so on.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733853617538/728EA96E8CB44948A0B0EDB2CFAB811A.png)
