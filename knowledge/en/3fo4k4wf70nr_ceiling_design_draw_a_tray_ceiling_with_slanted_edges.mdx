In the ceiling editor tool, using operations such as guide line positioning, offset modeling, section editing, and adding oblique angle of line, you can quickly draw a tray ceiling with slanted edges and achieve an ideal design effect.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yW1ZJmovv08)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Select the ceiling surface and click to enter **Ceiling Editor.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775112284052/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Drawing a Tray Ceiling with Slanted Edges

1. On the **Ceiling Editor** page, Add guides first, then use the **Straight Line** tool to draw out the curtain box area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775117908395/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

2. Click the **Offset** tool in the top toolbar, then click anywhere on the ceiling surface, input the offset value, and press the **Enter** key to confirm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775117954562/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

3. Click the rectangular area generated after offsetting, and set the **Extrusion** value in the lower-right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775117984063/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Next select the modeling line and click to enter **Section Editing**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775119005488/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. At the top of the left material bar, search for **the Oblique Angle of Line**, then click and drag the desired line to the target position. Adjust the chamfer line size as needed, and click **Done** after adjusting.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775119145362/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775119180708/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Select the configured modeling line, click **Duplicate**, and then **Paste Into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775119377854/8B92EAD58843012015ACE668614C773A.gif)

7. After completing the settings, you can drag and enlarge the top-right viewport to check the effect and see if adjustments are needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775119532640/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)8. If no adjustments are needed, click **Exit** **with** **Save** at the top-right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory rendering.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775119638620/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The final effect is shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1775206567644/780100DB193D87DB52BCD9BE20926573.jpg)
