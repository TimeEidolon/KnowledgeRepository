**Material Brush** helps to absorb a kind of material you select and attach it to other surfaces.

Select **Material Brush** in top toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733839528424/6B991F36FCDFFBF033BF507CC034B813.png)

Absorb the material and attach to other faces.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733839532502/C54897738E0EC877C21ECEEBE6431595.gif)
