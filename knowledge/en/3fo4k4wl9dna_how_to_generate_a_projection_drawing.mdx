#### **Function Introduction**

#### Users can generate the axonometric drawing automatically or manually.

#### **Operation steps**

**Path：Click on [generate of product]-[Get Construction Drawings]**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747813981172/DC4702FA3042FDF6D09EFC4E3F0F91D3.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814042913/F7E24763CB5BF096D6B1BDC1C6D19B29.png)

**1.Generate projection drawing automatically**

**Path:Click [Auto-projection]-[Generate]-[Auto-projection views]**

Select the corresponding cabinet then enter the construction drawing; you can find the generated drawing in the left side view list;

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814074656/DB53C8B8AFAC171D631B226A4F0F4E61.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814097763/38BB8945F782B22B32CC3B836E28B0F8.png)

**2.Generate projection drawing manually**

**Path:Click [manu-projection]-[Generate]-[Manu-projection views]**

Click on the corresponding model then select the desired direction of the projection drawing next to the cabinet.
