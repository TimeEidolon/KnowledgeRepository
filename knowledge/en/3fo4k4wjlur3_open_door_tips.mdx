Hi Coohomers,

We have updated new **open door** models rencently. And this article will show how to use the **Open Door** to do a rendering as below:

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849210525/72000B20893D2FF544E67E6595950C7F.png)

First, you need to find **Open Door** models as per this route: Public Moldel Library - Construction - Doors - Open Door.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849212447/47E3B2E17988B81730DA8D9F19115306.png)

Second, you can drag the **Open Door** model over to the existing door model on the wall in the project.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849215496/7091E815D05653C72B66A124F6D2CE82.png)

Third, kindly use **Flip** to change the door's opening direction.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849216818/9D4AEC9DF38DE59E6E1A6E104842A6E3.png)

Last but not least, please don't forget to adjust **Thickness** to about 2200 MM until it goes just a little beyond the wall.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849219862/3AA926B7486AE0ADB1A8AD06355CA93B.png)

Here is a short video for your reference:

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aCU-RD0-WSs )
