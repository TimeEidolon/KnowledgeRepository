### Step 1
Overview Sometimes we found that the depth of the cabinet is exactly the same, but there is always one cabinet that protrudes after the countertop is generated. This is because the contours of different custom cabinets may be different. Some cabinet models include the thickness of the door panel in the profile restrictions, while others do not, which causes errors to occur when generating the countertop. In other words, this is caused by inconsistent modeling methods.

### **Steps**

It is possible to adjust the depth of the cabinet when generating the countertop correctly and then change it back to its original value. The protrusion value that needs to be modified is usually the thickness of the door panel.
