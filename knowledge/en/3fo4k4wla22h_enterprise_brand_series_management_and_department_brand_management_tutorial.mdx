The functions of brand & series management and department brand management provide an efficient solution for large companies with multiple brands and series of products to effectively manage them and display different products to various sub-accounts. Target users as follows:

* Large companies with multiple brands and series of products.
* Customers who wish to display different series of products for different sub-accounts.

# For Users

Admin account or manager account

# Brand＆Series Management

## Entrance

Enter the **workspace**, and select **"Enterprise catalog - Product info - Brand＆Series management"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750132262743/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Brand Management

1. On the Brand Management page, click **Add Brand**, you can create brands, name the brands, and upload logos.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750132546824/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After filling in the brand information, click **Save**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750132609589/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Series Management

1. On the Series Management page, click **Add Series**, you can create serieses, each of which must belong to a brand and serves as the second level of the brand.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750139233396/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After filling in the series information, click **Save**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750139318486/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Product Details Page

1. Enter the **workspace**, and select **"Enterprise catalog - Products - Furniture"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750140158224/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. On the product details page, you can select the **Brand＆Series** to which each product belongs.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750140482212/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750140530837/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Department Brand Management

1. Enter the **workspace**, and select **"Enterprise catalog - Product info - Department brand management"**. In the department brand management module, you can select the departments to which you want to allocate products.

* **Note: Brands and series of all products copied to your enterprise through authorization will also appear in this list. Please pay attention to distinguishing them.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750141117488/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the Brand＆Series management, by using the buttons **"Add Brand"** or **"Remove Brand"**, you can assign brands and series to these departments to ensure that these users only see the brand series that you want them to see.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750141165995/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
