In the parameter panel, all parameters are displayed in the form of groups, and the basic parameters and advanced parameters are separated, which makes it more convenient to view and modify the corresponding parameters in the process of design and review, and improves the efficiency of modifying model parameters. It also has a search function, which can filter out the parameter column with one click, and adjust the parameters more quickly and efficiently.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Wrge7953GD8)
