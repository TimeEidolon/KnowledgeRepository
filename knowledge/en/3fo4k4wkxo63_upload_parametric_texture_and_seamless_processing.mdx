**1-1. Shooting**

Use a professional SLR camera to shoot wood board samples in an environment with uniform light.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733900412061/E3B4B0AB1324CAECC7BD0B39B87FF297.png)

**Advantages:** Strong three-dimensional texture is needed. SLR camera can shoot large surfaces, and texture details can be obvious.

**Disadvantages:** High technical requirements, high light requirements, high center sharpness in the picture, blurring at the edges, and difficult post-processing.

**Shooting – Sample Requirements**

1. Board size should be at least 600mm\*600mm with complete texture.
2. The board should have no defects or dirt.
3. The sample texture should be beautiful and represent the product’s texture.
