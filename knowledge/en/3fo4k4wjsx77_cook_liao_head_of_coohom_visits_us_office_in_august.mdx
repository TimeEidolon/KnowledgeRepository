### Coohom at Summer Las Vegas Trade Show

Earlier on, in the Summer Las Vegas Trade Show, Cook Liao and the U.S VP, William Avedon, met with [Ashley Furniture Industries](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ashley-furniture-industries/), the phenomenal home furniture & decor company, which is also Coohom's great partner in U.S.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733844092746/442E2A0E4F253A6575D7EC1FFBF44E8C.png)

They discussed the future of the home industry and how 3D visualization could propel the market to grow. It's an honor to have this meeting with Ashley Furniture in person, and we are looking forward to bringing more values to the home decor and furniture industries to the world.
