\*This service is only available for enterprises.

### Application: room paritition & division designs.

### Example: create a Room Divider Closet

1. Click "Select Parts"

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886048634/34E95253F01A0D5C98CD8EE4628E5E81.png)

2. Double click a door panel and click open. (do the same to the other panel)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886051081/A801E9CEC28AFB5DDFB5CF660E3D2BC7.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886053614/C9EF1F22BF387D6728F90FA6ED71AB92.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886054606/60C9B87C52C9C3A76092EA61FD5EB6E0.png)

3. Double click the back board.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886055612/5A2FDE141C2A3CFD6955DE62D7999631.png)

4. Click

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886056199/D2C6FEDD107D53283BB85E6FCEF418B4.png)

to delete the back board.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886057243/DE56D4026F4C70360433A8113691F3EB.png)

5. Click "Design Internal Zone"

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886058226/BBEA992350A7B5C1FA9D01730F349BEE.png)

6. Choose

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886058811/16E680CC4D414829B814CFBE631EBAB7.png)

. Select the internal zone. Choose

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886059353/FDB0BBAC187056C8E53D4FA39C5941FB.png)

to make the zone rotate 180°.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886060345/C90BB733BB09111B6A8783A1A9B23FE1.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886061316/2B13FEBA6D54265E04012F6BAFB2F7C8.png)

7. Drag a door panel from **Parts**, and the board will fit it automatically.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886062369/D08506FFB77FBC0430F7D00DD462D474.png) ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886063396/E9709054B8F681FBF948D97DC500FD86.png)

8. Divide the door into two.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886064489/D3AEAC6D28326DE273B05C8D716CAF50.png)

9. A room divider closet is made.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733886065625/D03DE6D4EF17147AEDA5F15213DF7B52.png)
