When you open the Material & Component tool, you will default to 3D mode, with view and walk views available to meet different viewing needs. Next, let us understand the specific operations of the two views in 3D mode, and how to switch to 2D mode, helping you use the tool more flexibly and efficiently to view space details.

# Entrance

Enter the 3D design interface and select **"Advanced Tool - Material & Component"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746771661772/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# 3D Mode

Open the Material & Component tool, **default page is 3D mode**. In 3D mode, there are **Top View** and **Walk** views available.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755166673130/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Top View

**1. Camera Settings**

In the camera settings, you can set the values of **Height** and **FOV.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755167206563/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**2. Zoom In and Out**

**Method One:** Click the zoom buttons to zoom in and out, **minus** button for **zoom out**, **plus** button for **zoom in.**

**Method Two:** Move the small dot on the zoom bar to zoom in and out.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1755167869066/9C36EAF0C8EA9176BDED243088FC57BD.jpg)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1755167985849/77D998668A911CF5963FA67DFB3652E5.gif)

**3. Box Zoom in**

Select the area you want to zoom in on, allowing you to zoom in on parts to view space details more conveniently.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1755168071679/2C59B2F1A17F820A96DBAC068F392459.jpg)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1755168188100/BA3CF5EA969D0747CC1A36BC94ED33E3.gif)

**4. Zoom Extents**

After zooming in, click **Zoom Extents** to return to the default view, with a default height of 1300mm, a default field of view is 60 degrees, and canvas size is 100%.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1755168264136/B1E5CA24A92BEAD3FD13F1EEE5C775E7.jpg)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1755168403111/BDD03DBD7B143CFD44CBE3156FF860F8.gif)

## Walk View

Switch to walk view and use shortcuts to operate the movement of the view. The relationship between shortcuts and movement directions is as follows:

* Forward — W, Backward — S, Move Left — A, Move Right — D, Move Up — Q, Move Down — E

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/jpeg/1755168614871/5EE6FB0380D92D345E20F7192E4715D5.jpg)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1755168707288/E621DA89292319CD0682934CAE911ACF.gif)

Hold down the left mouse button, and a rotation icon will appear, allowing you to change the perspective of the space view 360°.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1755168831446/224070BBD0FEA231C555DBA16061712F.gif)

# 2D Mode

Click the 2D icon in the **Floor Plan Navigator** box at the top right to switch to **2D mode**. The zoom in and out view and zoom extents operations in 2D mode are consistent with those in 3D mode. See the above text for detailed operations.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755168954975/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
