Some users may wonder where to find the uploaded models/materials to use in the design project after finish uploading. This article illustrates the clear route here. 

## **Target Users: All individual users**


Step 1: Go to **Models** icon in the left navigation bar

Step 2: Click **Uploads** button to see all the uploaded 3D models and materials/textures.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750239251468/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Note**: For textures user upload, they are for wall/ceiling/floor or models except for custom cabinets in the advanced tool: kitchen&bath, closet tool.

If users still have any doubt, please contact customer service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
