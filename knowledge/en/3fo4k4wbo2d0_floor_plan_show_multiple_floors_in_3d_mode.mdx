In the floor plan tool, you can show all floors or a single floor of a villa on multiple floors by setting floor navigation, and this article explains in detail how to show multiple floors in 3D mode.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=22Dpa83A8O4)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Switch to **3D mode**.

### Step 2
Click the **floor navigation**, select **show all floors**. * **Note: Show and hide a single or all floors, and other floors except the current floor only support preview, not edit.** Set the **other floors opacity** to view effect, you can preview all floors as a whole, or you can click the small eye button to preview two floors individually.

# Show All Floors

1. Switch to **3D mode**.

2. Click the **floor navigation**, select **show all floors**.

* **Note: Show and hide a single or all floors, and other floors except the current floor only support preview, not edit.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747893823632/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1747894357368/10CB6F18489D13C410D507C1CA06CD49.gif)

# Show a Single Floor

Set the **other floors opacity** to view effect, you can preview all floors as a whole, or you can click the small eye button to preview two floors individually.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747894515994/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1747894721571/3514A4A5553E6385693D4BEF1825C107.gif)
