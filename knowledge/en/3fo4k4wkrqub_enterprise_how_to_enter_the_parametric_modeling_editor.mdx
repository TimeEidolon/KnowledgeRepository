1. After you log into the Coohom Merchant Platform, move your cursor over the crown icon.

In the sidebar menu that appears, select the second option Custom Products. Then, choose **Product Catalog**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908663540/BD4F944DCE7843F9576ED1984772265C.png)

2. In the Custom Product Library page, click the + Create Asset button near the top-right corner.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908665137/E972EB7C6FC22BBE3618EB9132F5DEF5.png)

3. In the Create Asset pop-up window, click the Parametric Model Library near the top-left corner. You can choose Kitchen & Bath, Custom Furniture and Doors & Windows.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908666164/4033AE0590B63F633B339C2205A094E5.png)

4. In the Select Model Category page, chose the category of models that you want to edit in the Parametric Modeling Editor before clicking the OK button.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908667337/C9C3FB607D8F240A1D8B0187ACA9BA83.png)

5. Now, you are in the Parametric Modeling Editor.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908669338/51D6AA05208C84A083772459CFCE0C7F.png)

If you have any questions about the content above, please consult \{LiveChat Customer Service\}Coohom Website. We will provide you with one-to-one service during working hours!

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908671596/303F20DCB4B37D07F82300664C052177.png)
