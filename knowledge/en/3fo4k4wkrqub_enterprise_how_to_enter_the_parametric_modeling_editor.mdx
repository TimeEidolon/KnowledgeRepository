1. After you log into the Coohom Merchant Platform, move your cursor over the crown icon.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
After you log into the Coohom Merchant Platform, move your cursor over the crown icon. In the sidebar menu that appears, select the second option Custom Products. Then, choose **Product Catalog**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908663540/BD4F944DCE7843F9576ED1984772265C.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908663540/BD4F944DCE7843F9576ED1984772265C.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
In the Custom Product Library page, click the + Create Asset button near the top-right corner.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908665137/E972EB7C6FC22BBE3618EB9132F5DEF5.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908665137/E972EB7C6FC22BBE3618EB9132F5DEF5.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
In the Create Asset pop-up window, click the Parametric Model Library near the top-left corner. You can choose Kitchen & Bath, Custom Furniture and Doors & Windows.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908666164/4033AE0590B63F633B339C2205A094E5.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908666164/4033AE0590B63F633B339C2205A094E5.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
In the Select Model Category page, chose the category of models that you want to edit in the Parametric Modeling Editor before clicking the OK button.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908667337/C9C3FB607D8F240A1D8B0187ACA9BA83.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733908667337/C9C3FB607D8F240A1D8B0187ACA9BA83.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
Now, you are in the Parametric Modeling Editor. If you have any questions about the content above, please consult \{LiveChat Customer Service\}Coohom Website. We will provide you with one-to-one service during working hours!
