### 【3D Viewer】3D Viewer Configurator Launched on Coohom

#### **Introduction**

3D Viewer Configurator provides a more immersive and dynamic way to showcase products. It belongs to a subcategory within 3D Viewer, where model variations are controlled by a parameter editor.

Users can display the product's effects on the frontend page, allowing them to replace and showcase different product parameters, materials, and styles. It also supports generating quotations and integrating them with production processes.
