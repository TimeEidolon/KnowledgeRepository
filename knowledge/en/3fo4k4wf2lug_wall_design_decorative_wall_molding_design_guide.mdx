In the wall edito tool, you can use decorative lines on the wall to create a wall shape with a sense of hierarchy and exquisite details, effectively improving the texture of the space.

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Select the wall surface and click to enter **Wall Editor.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776393603594/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Decorative Wall Molding Design

1. Enter the wall editor page, click the **Rectangle** tool in the top toolbar, and draw the ceiling area on the wall.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776408605583/27E6FF860C19EBC5C760E7F17B8C7DFE.gif)

2. Use the **Add** **Guides** tool in the top toolbar to evenly divide the wall into 5 parts, then add corresponding auxiliary lines according to the wall design.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776408912058/2F0477DC8162AE5F7BDC4B2126DB28E9.gif)

[![
](//qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/newt/1144/image/png/1549879747859/B0556275535F7BF4FEF2DCC4F752A054.png)](https://qhstaticva-cos.kujiale.com/media/yun/help/video/UID_77c1df97_9f52_4ca1_1776421989291.mp4)

3. Click the **Rectangle** tool at the top to draw the outer contour of the wall design, and then use the **Offset** tool to create the inner contour of the wall design.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776409205689/81AF50308F3EF395607AA52BF65C9FF7.gif)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776409248856/E0CD53D6A78F3944EF85F8C087584D08.gif)

4. Click **Moldings** in the left sidebar. At the top, select **Decorative Moldings**, then drag and drop the appropriate material into the target area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776409364027/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Select the outer contour decorative line, click to enter **Section Edit**, adjust the size as needed in the pop-up panel, and then click **Done.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776410081231/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776409886423/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Select the outer contour decorative line, then click the material replacement button in the left panel, and choose the appropriate material for replacement in the pop-up panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776419970736/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

7. Next, select the ready outer contour decorative line, click **Duplicate** in the floating window, and then **Paste Into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776420080320/621E30309BA9866E04FA15CBEBC98179.gif)

8. Select the inner contour decorative line, use the same method to replace the material, and **Paste Into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776420202318/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776420254097/1C1CFA57FF5BE869192969C86E86EE48.gif)

9. Click **Wall Decor** in the left sidebar. At the top, select **Paints & Coatings**, choose **Gray** from the **Color...**, then place the needed material in the corresponding area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776420779095/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776420825541/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

10. Once completed, hold the **Shift** key to frame the designed elements, click **Duplicate** in the floating window, choose **Accurate Copy**, and copy the design to the corresponding position.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776421098553/B9DCDDC4477899D7A9DD015BABEB6890.gif)

11. Next, you can drag and enlarge the upper right corner of the viewer to check the effect and see if adjustments are necessary.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776421660494/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

12. If no adjustments are needed, click **Exit with Save** in the upper right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfying effect image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776421679402/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
