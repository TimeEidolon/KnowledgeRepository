Dear Coohomers,

If you are having a PRO plan which has not ended and will end in a certain period in the future, it will be good news that you can freeze the remaining PRO plan's time by days once you want to upgrade to the higher Elite plan to use it immediately. Here is the step by step guide.

First, Proceed to **Profile Icon-My subscription**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745738541477/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Then, Check the **ratio** button to see the pop-up window where you can click **confirm** button to make the Elite plan effective.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745738997152/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Once the Elite plan is effective, your PRO's remaining days will be frozen first. Later when the Elite plan is not renewed and gets expired, your PRO will then take effect.

For any further concerns, please feel free to contact Customer Service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
