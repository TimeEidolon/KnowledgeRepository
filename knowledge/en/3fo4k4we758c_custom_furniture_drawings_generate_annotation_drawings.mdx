The annotation drawing feature integrates the dimensions, styles, and scenes of customized cabinets, allowing for a more intuitive and clear presentation of solutions to customers through screenshots and integrated drawings. This article will provide you with a detailed explanation of how to generate annotation drawings.

# View Switching

## Global Quick View Switching

1. The bottom-left **2D** button allows for quick selection of five views: **Front View, Rack View, Left View, Right View, Top View**, which are relative to the overall room layout.

2. Switching to a specific view automatically locks into the orthogonal perspective, allowing movement within that plane only.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763005528199/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Single Model Quick View Switching

1. After selecting a model, click **Quick View** in the floating window to quickly access five views for this object. The selected model will be centered on the screen.

2. Switching to the top view allows the canvas to be rotated for the best display.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1763005647898/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
