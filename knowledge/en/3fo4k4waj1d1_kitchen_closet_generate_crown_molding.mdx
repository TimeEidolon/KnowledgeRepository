Use the crown molding generation tool to complete the layout of the cabinet crown molding, and you can select multiple cabinets to design the cabinet crown molding efficiently.

Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **kitchen**.

**Entrance One:** Select the **Generate-Crown molding** from the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742372050815/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Entrance Two:**Select the cabinet, and select **Generate-Crown molding** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742372096244/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Generate Crown Molding

1. After entering the generate crown molding interface, select the cabinet, the tool will automatically identify the cabinets that need to generate countertops. You can also **press and hold shift to select multiple cabinets** for generation. If you need to identify all cabinets, you can click **Auto-select**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742372131077/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the basic parameters on the right panel, select add **crown molding style and material**. After adjusting the specific parameters, click **Generate**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742372294557/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Generate Wrap:** Fit for cabinets with crown moldings. Adds wraps for the cabinets with crown moldings.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1739859501611/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. If you need to freely adjust the range of the crown molding generation, click **manual edit** in the top bar to enter the manual mode.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742372332503/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. In the manual interface, use the drawing tools on the top bar in 2D to edit edge and add rectangle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742372525736/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1742374694776/0C8891BE35EB6500E3C68BEDD231F9E2.gif)

5. You can manually edit points and lines, or enter values ​​for precise adjustments. After the adjustments are completed, click **Generate**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1742376652445/0AA021B226DA85F7675168603F9A50CE.gif)

6. After generating the crown molding, click **Finish** to exit the generate crown molding interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742376770987/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The generate crown molding effect is as follows：

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742376790969/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
