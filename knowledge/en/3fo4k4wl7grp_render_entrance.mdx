This article explains the location of the render tool entrance, so that you can quickly enter the render interface.

# For Users

All users

# Render Entrance

Enter the **workspace** and select the **Create New Project** in the upper right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779343695360/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

After entering the interior design interface, click **Render**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750323386552/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

The render tool can produce a variety of renderings: **perspective view, 360° walkthrough, top view, video and real-time rendering**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750323459839/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Directly enter the render interface, or select the type of rendering you need, then you can start rendering~

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750324523449/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
