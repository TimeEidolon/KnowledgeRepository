Designing interiors can be a meticulous process, especially when it comes to replicating wall, floor, and ceiling designs across different levels of a building. Fortunately, most modern wall/floor editors offer a user-friendly way to streamline this task by allowing users to save their designs as templates. Here’s how you can efficiently save and apply your wall/floor/ceiling designs using the template feature.

## Steps with screenshots

**Open the Wall/Ceiling/ FloorEditor**: Launch your editor and navigate to the specific floor where your desired design resides.

### Step 1: Save Your Design as a Template

Before you can replicate your design on another floor, you’ll need to create a template. This is done by saving the current design you have worked on—be it intricate wall modeling or unique flooring patterns.

1. **Open the Wall/Ceiling/ FloorEditor**: Launch your editor and navigate to the specific floor where your desired design resides.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392766987/380DEDB986D9A0A872E99C3016D0D5A7.png)
2. **Select Your Design**: Click on the wall, floor, or ceiling that you wish to copy. Ensure all elements are highlighted for inclusion in the template.
3. **Save as Template**: Look for an option labeled “Save as Template” or something similar in the menu bar or context menu (right-click). Provide a descriptive name for your template that will help you easily identify it later.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392770507/EE3060B9563A2FFDA1E4945D3AD4E19B.png)

### Step 2: Apply the Saved Template

Once you’ve saved your design as a template, applying it to other walls, floors, or ceilings is just as straightforward:

1. **Navigate to Another Floor**: Move to the new floor where you want to replicate your design.
2. **Access Templates**: Locate the “Templates” in My account>My Styling

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392774237/0161615D39AB8C85068F61733A26B3D1.png)
3. **Select and Apply**: Choose your saved template from the list and select it for application on your new space. The design will automatically populate onto the selected wall/floor/ceiling area.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392776628/83DEF0416CD09BFACAAFFCFEDF446B3F.png)
