In the design tool, you can perform quick grouping and ungrouping operations on finished models. Grouping facilitates batch adjustments, edits, and management of models, improving design efficiency.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-zVA5KgCLzk)

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Hold the **shift/⇧**key, select multiple models, and then click **Group** in the floating window. * Furniture can also be merged and grouped with wall decorations, lights, and other models.

### Step 2
After grouping, you can adjust the overall **Size** and **Distance to Slab** of the group in the **Properties** bar on the right. * If you want to edit a model within the group without ungrouping, you can double-click the model to be edited within the group, and then modify the size in the property bar on the right.

### Step 3
You can also select the group, click **Add to** **Favorites** in the floating window, making it convenient for future use. Select the grouped model and click **Ungroup** in the floating window. After ungrouping, you can independently replace, favorite, or delete models. * If you use models within **Model Sets**, ungrouping is also supported, but only certain combinations support ungrouping. If you can't find the ungroup button, then ungrouping is not possible.

# Model Grouping

1. Hold the **shift/⇧**key, select multiple models, and then click **Group** in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1773991643483/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

* Furniture can also be merged and grouped with wall decorations, lights, and other models.

2. After grouping, you can adjust the overall **Size** and **Distance to Slab** of the group in the **Properties** bar on the right.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1773991807619/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

# Model Ungrouping

Select the grouped model and click **Ungroup** in the floating window. After ungrouping, you can independently replace, favorite, or delete models.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773993145225/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* If you use models within **Model Sets**, ungrouping is also supported, but only certain combinations support ungrouping. If you can't find the ungroup button, then ungrouping is not possible.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773994877651/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
