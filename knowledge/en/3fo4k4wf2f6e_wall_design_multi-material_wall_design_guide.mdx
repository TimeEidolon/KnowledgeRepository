In the wall editortool, you can quickly complete a background wall composed of grille panel, marble, and light strip by using tools such as add guides zoning, shape drawing, material mapping, and array, effectively enhancing the spatial texture.

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

Select the wall surface, then click to enter **Wall Editor**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726875/FAF5A8BC0B65F24D0020FD029204F038.png)

# Multi-material Wall Design

1. Enter the **Wall Editor** page, and use the **Guides** tool from the top toolbar to add auxiliary lines as needed. Then, choose the **Rectangle** tool from the top to divide the wall into three separate regions.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726720/BFDEEEAA95BE60F47369A7390EFCC8B3.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726839/7C99097CFABCF98689E3E05368A907A3.png)

2. Next, use the **Rectangle** tool to draw the grille outline in the left region. After drawing, select the target area and set the **Extrusion** value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726684/648FCA5E7FD8C1D251EFFBAA9F62304B.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726623/56B269750E4249E4FAC002FC68C2718F.png)

3. Click on **Wall Decor** from the left sidebar. At the top, select **Wood Veneers,** then choose a suitable material and place it in the left target area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726669/C789D7A2A0A740D84040812D07C35767.png)

4. Select the modeling line, click to enter **Edit Elevation,** and choose the desired material to place on the facade. Then, click **Done**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726952/3B0F54C15CE9D98E5ED9257E5DE18969.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726821/25714D7A93E9621AD33BED2E51C65DE8.png)

5. Next, select the configured modeling line, click **Duplicate** from the floating window, and then **Paste into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726720/D871286146D0C5C4564B02BC31C23ABC.png)

6. Hold the **Shift** key to frame-select the configured grille design. Click **Array** from the floating window, select **Rectangular Array,** and then move the mouse to the target location. Click the left mouse button to confirm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726981/D0C200A4E2C1CA1CAB66033998516EE8.png)

7. Click on **Wall Panel** from the left sidebar, select a suitable material for the middle region, and set its **Extrusion** value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726949/33A31D86C2C20BCB0563FCE50B2C6815.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726678/ED8BFD13D672A4CB928B6F999F46E1C9.png)

8. Click **Stone** from the left sidebar, select **Marble** from the top, then choose a suitable material for the right region and set its **Extrusion** value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726885/E66BE2A2A8661B3CF2883F97D508AC3C.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726730/0C281844C336B37EBC21C6DB75A64E74.png)

9. Select the modeling line of the marble region, enter **Edit Elevation,** choose the desired material to place on the facade, then click **Done**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668727111/7F9457F9DB80D94B7D54B68D20D15A56.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726715/6908DA123A8B0AFCF9CE32D7B7D0F573.png)

10. Select the configured modeling line, click **Duplicate** from the floating window, and then **Paste into Current Styling Area**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668727105/02A26AFCEEAD8C246B309029B7EAD2A6.png)

11. Next, select the modeling line where the light strip needs to be added. Check **The Indented Lamp Belt** in the panel on the left, then set the **Brightness** and **Color** of the light strip according to the design requirements.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726797/51E1916523CED2398CB219026D1C2E30.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726875/62A7AC0D2D7BD405FCF98884F7C26D2C.png)

12. After finishing the setup, you can drag and enlarge the view window in the upper right corner to check the effect and confirm whether adjustments are required.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668727105/7F0DD801B1E31D17B6226490571B77AF.png)

13. If no adjustments are needed, you can click **Exit with** **Save** in the upper right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory effect image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668726878/6CB588FDDB58684D91DF7DF7A6776DD1.png)

* The final effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1776668727072/0EE05DFD51BD0389153BDF9D6109B659.png)
