In the wall editortool, you can quickly complete a background wall composed of grille panel, marble, and light strip by using tools such as add guides zoning, shape drawing, material mapping, and array, effectively enhancing the spatial texture.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Enter the **Wall Editor** page, and use the **Guides** tool from the top toolbar to add auxiliary lines as needed. Then, choose the **Rectangle** tool from the top to divide the wall into three separate regions.

### Step 2

### Step 3

### Step 5
Next, select the configured modeling line, click **Duplicate** from the floating window, and then **Paste into Current Styling Area**.

### Step 6
