1. **How does Basic, Plus or Pro Coohom user get to access for Version 5.0?**

   Please send email to Coohom Customer Service team via **support@coohom.com** and the access will be granted within 24-hours during Mondays to Fridays.
2. **Why the floorplan image is disappeared in "My Project"?**

   It was due to the specific project was saved without floorplan,therefore it is unvisible in "My Project".

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243835765/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
3. **Why the project still in V4.0?**

   Please hook the mouse to here and click Upgrade to V5.0.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243836940/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**4. Why cannot see the CAD image in the 2D floorplan?**

You need to adjust the Floor Transparency at the left-bottom. Adjust the number 100 to 10.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742391010239/FE5DCB2EBC0950A9F0A5BC38E2E09787.png)

**5. Why the furnitures added in the layout of V5.0 project are disappeared in the render page?**

Select the option: Direct model display.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742391014166/61909781E6CA4F6553ED2AEC82C3DD4B.png)
