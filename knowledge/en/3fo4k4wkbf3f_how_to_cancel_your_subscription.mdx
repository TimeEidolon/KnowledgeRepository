Here is the procedure for you to cancel the subscription

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
**[Login](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)** with your Coohom account.

### Step 2
Click the **Profile** icon in the upper right corner to locate "**My subscription"**.

### Step 3
Click on **Billing Info** in Subscription page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547298054/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 3
Click on the **Cancel Subscription**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547423145/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547423145/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
A confirmation window will show on your screen, click "**Cancel Subscription**" to continue for cancellation.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547494963/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547494963/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547476483/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547476483/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
Your subscription is now discontinued for next renewal if you manage to see **Activate Subscription** button. You will no longer be charged on the next billing date. Please make sure you cancel the subscription before the due date. For example: If your subscription is due on 15th May, please cancel before 15th May and do not worry about it as it will not affect your current subscription.

### Step 6
**Please take** **note** that once the money is deducted, it is non-refundable.
