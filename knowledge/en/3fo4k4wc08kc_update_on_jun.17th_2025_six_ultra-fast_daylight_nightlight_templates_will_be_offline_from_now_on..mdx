## Replacement for offline templates

For 5.0 project, it will be applied with **3.1 version** once selecting the ultra-fast Daylight/Nightlight template.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750147905509/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750148019928/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

For 4.0 project, it will be applied with **3.0 version** once selecting the ultra-fast Daylight/Nightlight template.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750148106472/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

Please be aware that users who have used the old version of 1.0,2.0,2.1 for the Ultra-fast Daylight/Nightlight template can copy the same template but cannot create new ones. Please use the 3.0 version or above for new creation of lighting templates to avoid any potential issues.

At last, we want to inform our existing users that the offline process will be gradually done and expected to go offline completely by the end of July,2025. When you see the notice in the tool, please do not be suprised.

For any further concerns, please feel free to contact Coohom Support Team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
