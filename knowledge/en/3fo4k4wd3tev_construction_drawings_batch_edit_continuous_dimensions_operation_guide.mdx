When marking dimensions, you can enable the batch edit function, allowing these continuous dimensions to be edited as a whole, supporting overall movement, style modification, deletion, and other functions. This article will explain in detail how to batch edit continuous dimensions.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r-VHB5VKihE)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

1. Enter interior design, select **Drawings - Construction Drawings.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750748257782/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the **Edit Views** page, click **Settings** in the upper right corner, and select **Preferences.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757577267483/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. In the Preferences panel, switch the **Dimension Chain Editing** option to **Batch Edit.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757577772910/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Batch Edit Operation

After a successful switch, the newly generated continuous dimensions will be a whole and support overall editing.

**1. Regenerate annotations, click continuous dimensions to move them as a whole**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757578292862/98A5A91214FD3C231EFA2C336CAA6528.gif)

**2. Click continuous dimensions to modify the style as a whole in the right panel** (including font size, color, background, border, dimension lines, ext line length, etc.)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757578350583/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757578454461/1DE9D756273E67B511D1D1CA08F2C5AA.gif)

**3.** **When the annotated object is hidden, continuous dimensions will not break.**

* After selecting the legend and hiding it, the continuous dimensions as a whole will not break and will maintain a continuous state.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757578741812/6412D7DB091A5968392CC98DC684FDC4.gif)

**4. You can split the overall dimensions into independent dimensions with one click.**

* Select the overall continuous dimensions, click split in the floating window, and after splitting, the dimensions will return to their independent states.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757579204137/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757579257525/669B16C6F6F39FE108D840B282105B8A.gif)

**5. You can split the overall dimensions into multiple overall dimensions.**

* Select the overall continuous dimensions, click the red dot or line on the annotated object, then click split in the floating window, and the overall dimensions will break from the point or line position of the selected annotated object into two independent overall dimensions.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757581194612/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757581289451/EDD669ADFA0DD147CB2AC9043A4E70E2.gif)
