### **Decorate a few objects**

1. Select an object, or multiple objects with pressing and holding Shift.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848861650/7B7ACA6414D83E3B1DB14753BF8C999F.png)

2. Choose the decor style you want and click **Apply**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848863235/5E83CFF5170F05C58A94DD2832840385.png)

3. It will add some decorations to your furniture automatically.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848864418/5DE4E0813379A4BCA540A4288E21B4C9.png)

* **Decorate all eligible furniture**

### **Decorate all eligible furniture**

1. After you enter Smart Decors, click the button to select all eligible furniture.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848866643/792D1E4EAA7834E6651A0E9B7E218FC8.png)

2. Select a category. Choose a template you like and click **Apply**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848867863/AF3BB0CF6957757B5512A28AE7DA0F6B.png)

3. Decoration has been applied successfully.
