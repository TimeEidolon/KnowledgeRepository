#### **Function introduction**

#### We support the ability to generate a detail view.

#### **Operation steps**

**Path:[Construction Drawings]**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814534037/86D90C55C6C6C94FA658AEC93048E9A0.png)

**Path:click on[Generate views]-[Callout]**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814562507/705C1EFA358F7891874447D6C37728C5.png)

Click the mouse to select the area where you want to generate detailed drawings.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814593250/BFF534295F3A08FDD5A023B89A85BB8D.png)

After selecting, the generated detail view will be seen in the left side views list

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814625891/CE88B342BEEA26A5679604CAB4D15171.png)
