Coohom workspace is a place where users can see and manage various functions such as language change, subscription cancellation etc.

However, some users may have difficulty finding the workspace from their interface. This article will show clear routes to workspace.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742981534374/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Target Users

**Individual Users**

# Several Routes To Workspace


**Route One:** Click **Workspac**e in [Coohom website](https://www.coohom.com?kjl_source=hc) to enter

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742981378734/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Route Two**: Click the **Profile** icon in 2D/3D design tool from upper right corner to **exit the tool** and then click **Home** button to enter

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742981707684/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742981846178/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

If you are in other tools such as custom modeling, material editor, wall/ceiling/floor editor etc, you can exit the tool first to enter 2D/3D design tool in order to go back to workspace.

For any further queries, please feel free to reach Coohom Customer Service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
