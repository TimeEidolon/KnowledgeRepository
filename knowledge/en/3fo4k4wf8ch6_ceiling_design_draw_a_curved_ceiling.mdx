### Step 2

Select the ceiling surface and click to enter **Ceiling Editor**.

# Draw a Curved Ceiling

1. Click the **Offset** tool in the top toolbar, then click anywhere on the ceiling surface, enter the offset value, and press the **Enter** key to confirm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775123148764/CD8FC63C91BC49078853BAFD719BE561.gif)

2. Select the area, click the **Chamfer** tool in the floating window, choose the needed chamfer mode, enter the **Radius**, and then press the **Enter** key to confirm. Next, set the **Extrusion** value for the design surface in the bottom-right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775123345140/BA3CF5EA969D0747CC1A36BC94ED33E3.gif)

3. Use the **Offset** tool to create two additional stepped contour lines and set the **Extrusion** value for each design surface in the bottom-right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1775123827844/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775123948148/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. After completing the settings, you can drag and enlarge the top-right viewport to check the effect and see if adjustments are needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775123986665/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. If no adjustments are required, click **Exit** **with** **Save** in the top-right corner. After rendering, you will get a satisfactory effect image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775124086127/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The final effect is shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1775206495329/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
