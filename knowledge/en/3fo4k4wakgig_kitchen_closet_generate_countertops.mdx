The countertop generation function allows efficient and flexible countertop design, which can be automatically identified or freely drawn.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
After entering the generate countertop interface, select the cabinet, the tool will automatically identify the cabinets that need to generate countertops. You can also **press and hold shift/⇧ to select multiple cabinets** for generation. If you need to identify all cabinets, you can click **Auto-select**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741920934832/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741920934832/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
In the basic parameters on the right panel, select add **front edge,** **back splash and countertop material**. After adjusting the specific parameters, click **Generate**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741921629494/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741921629494/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
If you need to freely adjust the range of the countertop generation, click **manual edit** in the top bar to enter the manual mode.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741921702297/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

### Step 4
In the manual interface, use the drawing tools on the top bar in **2D mode** to **edit edge and add rectangle**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741922870712/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741923669118/3A6C058AE60B7E609172437AC2CA5F49.gif)

### Step 5
You can manually edit points, lines, and surfaces, or enter values ​​for precise adjustments. After the adjustments are completed, click **Generate**.

# Entrance

Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **kitchen**.

**Entrance One:** Select the **Generate-Countertop** from the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741920648575/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Entrance Two**: Select the cabinet, and select **Generate-Countertop** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741920812349/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Generate Countertop

1. After entering the generate countertop interface, select the cabinet, the tool will automatically identify the cabinets that need to generate countertops. You can also **press and hold shift/⇧ to select multiple cabinets** for generation. If you need to identify all cabinets, you can click **Auto-select**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741920934832/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. In the basic parameters on the right panel, select add **front edge,** **back splash and countertop material**. After adjusting the specific parameters, click **Generate**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741921629494/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. If you need to freely adjust the range of the countertop generation, click **manual edit** in the top bar to enter the manual mode.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741921702297/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. In the manual interface, use the drawing tools on the top bar in **2D mode** to **edit edge and add rectangle**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741922870712/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741923669118/3A6C058AE60B7E609172437AC2CA5F49.gif)

5. You can manually edit points, lines, and surfaces, or enter values ​​for precise adjustments. After the adjustments are completed, click **Generate**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1741924371957/8806D0D4E90FEF3C15CEC9EFE72491E4.gif)

6. After generating the countertop, click **Finish** to exit the generate countertop interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741924741997/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The generate countertop effect is as follows：

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1741927964432/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
