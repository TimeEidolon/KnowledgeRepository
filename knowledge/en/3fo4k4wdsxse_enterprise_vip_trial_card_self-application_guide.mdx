Enterprises can independently apply for a 7-day VIP trial card in training and other scenarios, used for training account experience VIP functions. This article will explain in detail how to independently apply for a VIP trial card.

# For Users

Coohom enterprise account administrators

# Prerequisites

Only the enterprise primary administrator can apply for this account

# Entrance

Enter the **Workspace**, select **"Account Management - Employee Management"**, go to the **Employee****Management** page, click on the upper right corner **"Account Toolbox - Apply for a Trial Card"**, to open the VIP trial card management function.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762252264734/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Trial Card Application Process

1. After entering the trial card page, you can view the details of historically applied trial cards and use filters to quickly narrow down the records.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762252709014/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Apply for a Trial Card**, open the application pop-up window for the trial card.

* **Note:** If the enterprise's VIP purchases are less than 5, or the application quota for trial cards has been used up, **the ‚Äã‚Äã"Apply for a Trial Card"‚Äã‚Äã entry will be hidden**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762252892783/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. In the application trial card page, set **Experience Card Type** and **Number of** **Application**, the following matters need attention:

* **Available Application Quantity (as shown in the picture 204):** [Total application quantity] minus [Already applied quantity]
* **Total Application Quantity (as shown in the picture 205):** Calculated as 20% of the total number of VIPs purchased in the version. For example, if the enterprise currently has 5 official VIPs, it can apply for one 7-day trial VIP.
* ‚Äã**VIP Type**: Generally includes the VIP types available in the current version.
* ‚Äã**Trial Card Description**‚Äã: The duration is fixed at 7 days. The latest redemption deadline is the expiration date of the current solution version.
* **Account Binding Rules**‚Äã: An account can be bound to a trial card for a maximum of 14 days within one renewal version cycle.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762253149708/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# VIP Trial Card Allocation

1. After setting is completed, click **Are you sure**, the system will automatically allocate VIP.

* **Note:** The VIP applied needs to be assigned to the designer account by the primary account to use it, need to click **Direct Allocation.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762253274747/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After clicking **Direct Allocation**, a person selection interface appears, you can quickly select people through **Organizational Structure** or **import Email Addresses to batch search**¬Ýand then select

* **Note:**
  + The number of selected personnel must correspond to the number of VIP trial cards available, otherwise the system will display an error.
  + Selected individuals must have no existing VIP status in order to ‚Äã**‚Äãassign trial and directly activate the trial card binding.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1762253459509/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. After selecting the account, it will be assigned to the sub-account. The sub-account will automatically complete the ‚Äã**redemption and binding process**, thereby gaining 7-day VIP privileges.
