In the Custom Modeling tool, the powerful toolbar enhances your modeling capabilities, and the vast asset library sparks your creativity. With features like view switching and perspective adjustment, you can fully control your models from every angle. Let's quickly unlock these feature modules together! We recommend watching the tutorial video before reading this article to help you get familiar with the tool quickly.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

# ② Asset Library Introduction

The asset library in the Custom Modeling tool mainly consists of two categories: Model Library and Material Library.  
Models from the Model Library can be imported directly onto the canvas, or into the SU (SketchUp) model. Once imported, these models can be customized and edited.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751424056175/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751424088790/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# ③ View Switching and Display/Hide Control

## View Switching

We provide the ability to switch between 2D and 3D views, as well as display/hide control:

* 2D views include Top View (shortcut 1), Bottom View (shortcut 5), Left View (shortcut 3), Right View (shortcut 4), Front View (shortcut 2), and Back View (shortcut key 6).
* 3D views include Overlook (shortcut 9) and Roaming (shortcut 9).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751424263418/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Display/Hide Control

* For your convenience, you can control the display/hide of "Household Reference (Walls)" and "Furniture".
* Models created using the Custom Modeling tool can also be hidden. Additionally, we provide the ability to view hidden objects, so you can quickly locate hidden custom models.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751424901652/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# ④ Canvas Camera View Switching

## Camera Settings

During the modeling process, you can adjust camera parameters to change the view.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751425349902/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Default View

Quickly switch to the default view angle to help you avoid losing your orientation in 3D space.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751425420081/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Adapt to the Canvas

Adapt to the canvas perspective for the model as a whole.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751425491078/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Zoom the Window

Select and zoom into specific areas of the model for detailed editing, making it easier to handle finer details.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751425618098/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# ⑤Room Selection

Manage models from the perspective of individual rooms. Quickly select a room to enter and display the corresponding models within that room.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751427125006/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
