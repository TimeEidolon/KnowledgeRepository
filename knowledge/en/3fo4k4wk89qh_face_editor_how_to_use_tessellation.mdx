1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849984341/3D9F12B3F92D90922BF63F12AAF9B675.png)

4. Drag the target finish under **Public Library** from the left panel to your floor plan.

5. Click the finish.

6. Click **Tessellation** in the floating toolbar.

7. Choose **Grid**, (**Mixing**), **Brickwork,** or **Checkerboard** paving method.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849986092/2F312F4BE15D0AB35D26D10DCCB09FF4.jpeg)
