1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

### Step 2
Click the face you want to edit.

### Step 3
Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849984341/3D9F12B3F92D90922BF63F12AAF9B675.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849984341/3D9F12B3F92D90922BF63F12AAF9B675.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
Drag the target finish under **Public Library** from the left panel to your floor plan.

### Step 5
Click the finish.

### Step 6
Click **Tessellation** in the floating toolbar.

### Step 7
Choose **Grid**, (**Mixing**), **Brickwork,** or **Checkerboard** paving method.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849986092/2F312F4BE15D0AB35D26D10DCCB09FF4.jpeg)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733849986092/2F312F4BE15D0AB35D26D10DCCB09FF4.jpeg" alt="Step 7" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />
