#### **Function introduction**

#### Drawing of room, after generating the drawings, you can use the elevation tool to complete the generation of elevation drawings.

#### **Operation steps**

**1.Auto-place markers to generate elevations**

**Path:[Generate Per Room]-[Base Unit Plan View]-[Auto-Place]**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814281651/7855A0F6136675FEDF3E4A3EB53739E4.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814305898/D73ACAE558E7B06B20F2584A3FF09A9B.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814330639/492A3160C2AFC414C5EEB18F840A959F.png)

**2.Manu-place markers to generate elevations**

**Path:[Generate Per Room]-[Base Unit Plan View]-[Manu-Place]**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814353959/1A68ED638CD5C183A7086187413A81AD.png)

Move the mouse over one of the elevation markers and click on the icon that appears to quickly generate elevation markers in the other three directions using that marker as a reference.

**3.Empty Marker**

If you are not satisfied with the current elevation, click “Clear” to clear all the elevation markers in the current plan and the corresponding elevation on the left side.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814378749/F3B5DA7524884CCE27C9E8506431768F.png)

**4.Elevation Box Adjustment**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747814408579/485A59F305A607BFB80778319C92882E.png)

Select the elevation markers, you can display and adjust the elevation box, the elevation box corresponds to the display range of the elevation, the model needs to be in the range box before it will appear in the elevation.
