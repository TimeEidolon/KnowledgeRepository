# Pre-import Processing

1. Click to **Import 2D Drawing** in the floor plan tool. After the drawings are loaded successfully, the AI will automatically identify whether the image needs to be processed, and click **Confirm** to process it automatically.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778224459023/9A5EA73C16A8ED9BB761A7BC754F9BA3.png)

* You can also click the pre-import processing buttontoremoving watermals, backgrounds, wrinkles, and fixing perspective distortionwith one click. Processing will start automatically after clicking.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773131721761/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Wait for the processing to complete. On the left side is the original image before processing, and on the right side is the processed image. Both images can be zoomed in or out with the mouse wheel and moved with the mouse scroll button. Then, select the image you want to use.

* When choosing **Before**, the unprocessed floor plan image will be utilized; when choosing **After**, the processed floor plan image will be applied.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773130898787/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Move the scale to the wall and enter the actual length.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773130993312/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Click **Import** at the bottom right corner, wait for a moment, and the floor plan will be successfully generated.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773131360411/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773131643549/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
