Based on material replacement, we have launched the material editing function in this phase.

It supports editing public library materials and texture uploading, enriching material effects.

Currently, among the eleven categories of materials in the public library, the editable parameter items are basically consistent, while some materials do not support uploading some texture items.

Let's take stone as an example to understand the editable items of materials.

# Basic Parameters

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1769566588049/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* **Base Color**

Color: Controls the surface color of the material.

Texture: Click the plus sign to upload a texture, controlling the surface color and texture of the material.

* **Reflection Color**

Color: Reflects the corresponding color. Black is completely non-reflective, while white is completely reflective.

Texture: Reflection texture color.

* **Refraction Color**

Controls the refraction color and transparency of the object. The closer the color is to white, the more transparent the object.

* **Normal**

Normal maps can be understood as bump texture maps, giving the model a variety of three-dimensional visual effects.

* **Reflection Glossiness, Refraction Glossiness**

Value: The larger the value, the clearer the reflection and refraction.

Texture: Usually a black and white channel map.

* **Self-illumination**
* **Opacity**

Value: The lower the value, the more transparent.

Texture: The darker the color, the higher the transparency.
