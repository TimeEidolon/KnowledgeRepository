### Step 3
**Operation Steps** Step 1: Use the straight wall or rectangle wall tool to draw the wall first. Step 2: Select one of the wall segments and set the automatic connection to "No". Step 3: Move the wall to separate the originally intersecting two walls and modify it to a staggered connection, as shown in the figure. Step 4: Click the arc wall tool to draw the wall again, and modify the thickness of the wall to 239. Here, you need to subtract 1 from the original wall thickness, and the logic is that the radius cannot be equal to the wall thickness. For example: If the original wall thickness is 260, the wall thickness needs to be set to 259 when drawing an arc wall, and so on. Step 5: Click the arc wall tool and use it to connect the missing corner position. Finally, you will get the effect shown in the figure.
