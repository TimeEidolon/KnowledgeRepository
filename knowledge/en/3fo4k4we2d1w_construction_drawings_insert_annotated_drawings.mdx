Users can display the annotated drawings generated for customized furniture within the construction drawings and output them together with the complete set of drawings. This increases the completeness of the drawings and allows construction drawings to better express the design. This article explains how to insert annotated drawing.

# For Users

All Users

# Entrance

Click **Drawings** in the left sidebar, then select **Construction Drawings.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764230181426/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Insert Annotated Drawings

1. First, prepare a project that has already generated customized annotated drawings.

* For detailed steps to generate annotated drawings, refer to the online documentation: [Click here](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/custom-furniture-drawings-generate-annotation-drawings?kjl_source=hc)

2. Go to design tool, select **Drawings** in the left sidebar, and click to enter the **Construction Drawings**page.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764230154675/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click **Drawings Layout** at the top, then select the desired drawings from the sheets list in the left sidebar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764230306220/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Click **Insert - Picture** on the right, then select the suitable drawings and drag them to a proper position within the layout. After completing the addition, you can **Download** it as needed.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764230477183/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1764230575018/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
