**Operation steps**

Open the SU file, click on the "Window" menu, select "Model Info" - "Statistics".

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749118386943/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

In the Model Information dialog box, click "Purge Unused Items" and "Fix Problems".

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1749118401972/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

That's it.
