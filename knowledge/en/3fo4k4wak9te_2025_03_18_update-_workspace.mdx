# **【Workspace】Supports 3D Preview of Design Project**

**Target users:**

All Coohom Enterprise Customer

**Before:**

Previously, if you wanted to share your design concepts with your clients, you had to render the design project into rendered images first before you could share them with your clients.  
If you just wanted to showcase your ideas in the initial design stage, this greatly increased the communication cost.

**After:**

Now, with the 3D preview function of the design project, you can send the design project to your clients in 3D form.

![image2025-3-17_11-31-32.png](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1742200138952/EE765A07986E598E272BEA158378E7F4.png)

**How to use:**

You can enter the Coohom Workbench, select a design scheme of version 5.0 from "My Projects", and then find the 3D preview function in the "More Options".  
Note: This function currently only supports 3D design schemes of version 5.0.

![image2025-3-17_11-22-16.png](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1742200222935/48A9261C5F3707E9AF948FE4AD42A43E.png)

After entering the preview mode, you can click “Comment”to add annotations, and then share the link with any client by clicking “Share”.  
As a client, he can also quickly add comments or annotations to the scheme and communicate and reply with you in the comment section.

![image2025-3-17_11-38-57.png](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1742200258953/BB253151F99EE3D8438EEFE3E1A8AE88.png)

In the preview mode, you can freely switch the display and hiding of elements in the canvas, making it convenient to showcase your scheme from various angles.

![image2025-3-17_11-41-56.png](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1742200285254/266EB31AEDE5A4B4A517DF14FC4251D3.png)
