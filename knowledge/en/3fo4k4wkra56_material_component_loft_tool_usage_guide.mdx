In the materials & components tools, after drawing the lofting path and section, click the loft tool at the top to quickly generate a three-dimensional shape and improve design efficiency.

# **For Users**

All users

# **Entrance**

In the design tool, select **Advanced Tool**, and click to enter **Materials & Component**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778495293493/21049075DFBA281AC19B6ED78D1C98BD.png)

# **Using the Loft Tool**

1. Click the rectangle tool in the top toolbar, first draw a model surface as a **Path**, and then draw a lofting section.

[![
](//qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/newt/1144/image/png/1549879747859/B0556275535F7BF4FEF2DCC4F752A054.png)](https://qhstaticva-cos.kujiale.com/media/yun/help/video/UID_0c30a33c_92f7_4f2f_1778495322311.mp4)

2. **Select Face or Continuous Path**, then click the **Loft** tool in the top toolbar, and then click the section to perform lofting.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1779090374471/FC4BD0A36618EB0CB2B4C5B7B5883E50.png)

* Select the model surface to use the **Loft** tool as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1778495293558/DB850C6448F7B67567E9EB86B81F18AD.png)

* Hold down **Shift** key to select continuous path, then use the **Loft** tool, as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1779090374911/1C655E878793F1A1CF334BC1EC0CC7BD.png)
