**Global style** supports one-click replacement of materials, styles, hardware, etc. for all custom models in the scene, and also supports continuous replacement operations.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Enter the kitchen & closet design and choose the **kitchen/Closet**.

### Step 2
Select the **Global Edit -** **Global Style** from the top toolbar. Select the cabinet, and select the material and hardware to be replaced in the parameter panel on the right to complete the global style. * **Detailed instructions on how to quickly search for models in the model catalog panel can be found in the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/kitchen-closet-use-style-panel-to-replace-the-material-and-style-of-models?hl=en&kjl_source=hc)** * The operation demonstration is as follows:
