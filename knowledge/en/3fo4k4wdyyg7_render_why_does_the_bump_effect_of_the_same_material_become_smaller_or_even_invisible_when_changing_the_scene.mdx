# Reason

1. The bump effect changes with the lighting, and it may disappear when the lighting is weak because the bump effect is sensitive to changes in light. As shown in the image below.
2. The effects within the green box and the red box in the image are completely different, but it is indeed the same material. This is because different lighting conditions produce different effects.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1595988779909/BE1C91BDE5E7808DA7DB4118C508129F.png?x-oss-process=image/resize,m_mfit,w_488/format,webp)

# Solution

When dealing with a bump texture (such as diatom mud), use spotlights or downlights to enhance the bump effect as much as possible, or increase the light and shadow contrast. This will make the light and dark sides of the bump effect more three-dimensional.
