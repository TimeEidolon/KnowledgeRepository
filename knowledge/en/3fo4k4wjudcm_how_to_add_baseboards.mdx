To add baseboards for your project, please:

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Public library** ＞**Construction** ＞ **Baseboards** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842771947/2CBDE23B6CA31C592208DA1F67348105.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842771947/2CBDE23B6CA31C592208DA1F67348105.png" alt="Step 1" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 2
Choose one baseboard and drag it into your floor plan.

### Step 3
The baseboard will be applied to the entire room. Hold down Ctrl to tile one wall only. ​

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842778002/EF66BCD8A603E3161D902F2899F9C318.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842778002/EF66BCD8A603E3161D902F2899F9C318.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
Left-click the baseboard you added to use the **Duplicate** or **Delete** functionality in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842782003/66C336A280B7958F78432B651EFE9BB9.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842782003/66C336A280B7958F78432B651EFE9BB9.png" alt="Step 4" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 5
Click on the replace button if want to replace other type materials.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842785293/D59FCCE472856C8C39424F6174485A55.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842785293/D59FCCE472856C8C39424F6174485A55.png" alt="Step 5" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />


1. Select **Public library** ＞**Construction** ＞ **Baseboards** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842771947/2CBDE23B6CA31C592208DA1F67348105.png)

2. Choose one baseboard and drag it into your floor plan.  
3. The baseboard will be applied to the entire room. Hold down Ctrl to tile one wall only.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842778002/EF66BCD8A603E3161D902F2899F9C318.png)

​  
4. Left-click the baseboard you added to use the **Duplicate** or **Delete** functionality in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842782003/66C336A280B7958F78432B651EFE9BB9.png)

5. Click on the replace button if want to replace other type materials.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733842785293/D59FCCE472856C8C39424F6174485A55.png)
