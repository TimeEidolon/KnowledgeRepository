1. Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

2. Click the face you want to edit.

3. Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

4. Click the face.

5. Choose **Waistline** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733842794914/F2A1E2A3ABCAEC05C8A5D2ACFE5AABB6.jpeg)

6. Click the newly-added waistline.

7. Edit in the floating toolbar or under **Waistline Settings** in the left panel.

8. Click **Done** to confirm.
