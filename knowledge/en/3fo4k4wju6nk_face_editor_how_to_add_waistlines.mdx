## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **Material&Component** in the left panel.

### Step 2
Click the face you want to edit.

### Step 3
Click **Face Editor** in the floating toolbar.

### Step 4
Click the face.

### Step 5
Choose **Waistline** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733842794914/F2A1E2A3ABCAEC05C8A5D2ACFE5AABB6.jpeg)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733842794914/F2A1E2A3ABCAEC05C8A5D2ACFE5AABB6.jpeg" alt="Step 5" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 6
Click the newly-added waistline.

### Step 7
Edit in the floating toolbar or under **Waistline Settings** in the left panel.

### Step 8
Click **Done** to confirm.


2. Click the face you want to edit.

5. Choose **Waistline** in the floating toolbar.
