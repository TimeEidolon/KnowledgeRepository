Select **Closet, Kitchen＆Bath, or Doors＆Windows** according to your business needs, then you can enter the customization interface.

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

Click **Advanced Tool**.

### Step 2

# Kitchen＆Bath Entrance

1. Click **Advanced Tool**.

2. Select **Kitchen＆Bath** to enter the customization interface.

* **Win shortcut key：AIt+4**
* **Mac shortcut key：⌥+4**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1735808958986/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Closet Entrance

1. Click **Advanced Tool**.

2. Select **Closet** to enter the customization interface.

* **Win shortcut key：AIt+5**
* **Mac shortcut key：⌥+5**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1735809627596/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Doors＆Windows Entrance

## Entrance One

1. Click **Floor Plan**.

2. Drag out the doors and windows from the **Doors＆Windows**.

3. Click on the Doors＆Windows model, and click **Customize** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1739935178586/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Entrance Two

1. Click **Advanced Tool**.

2. Select **Doors＆Windows** to enter the customization interface.

* **Win shortcut key：AIt+6**
* **Mac shortcut key：⌥+6**
