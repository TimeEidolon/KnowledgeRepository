### Step 3
Click **Download** in the upper right corner, **select the drawing you want to download** on the left side of the pop-up panel, **set the format of the exported drawing** on the right, and click **Download** after the setting is complete. * **Note:** There are four export formats to choose from: **CAD, PDF, JPG, and Mix**. Select Mix to export CAD, JPG, and PDF at the same time. * For beginner operations related to construction drawings, you can see the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/construction-drawings-basics?kjl_source=hc)

# Enter Your Project

1. **[Log in](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin?kjl_source=hc)** to your Coohom account.

2. On the **[Projects](https://www.coohom.com/pub/saas/apps/project/list?kjl_source=hc)** page, click **Enter the Project.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1757497283689/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Export Construction Drawings

1. Enter the interior design, select **Drawings - Construction Drawings**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750748257782/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Download** in the upper right corner, **select the drawing you want to download** on the left side of the pop-up panel, **set the format of the exported drawing** on the right, and click **Download** after the setting is complete.

* **Note:** There are four export formats to choose from: **CAD, PDF, JPG, and Mix**. Select Mix to export CAD, JPG, and PDF at the same time.
* For beginner operations related to construction drawings, you can see the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/construction-drawings-basics?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750752845086/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1757497625305/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Export Floor Plan Drawings

1. Enter the interior design, select **Drawings - Floor Plan Drawings**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750917425461/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After the adjustment is completed, click **Download** in the upper right corner to choose to download the image or PDF format.

* For beginner operations related to floor plan drawings, you can see the tutorial document: [Click to view](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/floor-plan-drawings-basics?kjl_source=hc)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1750919100641/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
