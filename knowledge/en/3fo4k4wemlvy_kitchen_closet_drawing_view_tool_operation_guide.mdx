By using the Drawing View Tool, you can perform actions like designing floor plans and customizing while observing the automatically generated styles and annotation effects in real time. Additionally, you can switch between different view nodes for thorough inspection and effects evaluation, allowing for design adjustments tailored to drawing outputs at any time. This article explains how to use the Drawing View Tool.

## For Users

**All users**.If some enterprise accounts do not have the kitchen & closet design entrance, please contact the CSC to apply.

## Entrance

After entering the design tool, click the third tab in the small view window at the top right corner: **Drawing**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766547523775/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Adjusting the Size of Drawing View

If you find the current view window too small, you can click **View Arrangement** to change the arrangement between the small window and the main canvas.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766547602155/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* When selecting **Vertical Sliding Window**, it appears as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766548048951/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* When selecting **Left and Right Window**, it appears as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766548015022/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* When selecting **Window Size Default**, you can restore the default layout, as shown below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766548102816/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1766548199755/8B92EAD58843012015ACE668614C773A.gif)

## Switching Views

By clicking the view node entry, you can switch between different view nodes, making it convenient to view various visual effects.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766548288332/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Automatic Annotation

Click the **Auto - Annotate** button at the top right corner to immediately trigger the generation of default annotation effects in the **Drawing**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766548458646/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Quick Access to Drawing Editing

1. Click **Editing Drawing Views** to instantly switch tools and enter the drawing tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766548490887/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After entering the drawing tool, click **Back to Design** to go back to the design tool.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1766548573971/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
