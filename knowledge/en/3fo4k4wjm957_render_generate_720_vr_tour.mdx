### Step 4
After generating **720° VR Tour**, click **View** **Light 720° VR Tour.** * Make sure every room is closure by doors. * You can [rename Panoramas](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/how-to-rename-panorama?kjl_source=hc) to make it correctly shown on that tour. * Only one 720° VR tour rendering can be categorized into your project. Be careful in case it was covered by a new one.

# Generate 720° VR Tour

1. Generate a lot of panorama renderings for your project.  
​![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748421002334/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the **project**, click **project details**, select **720° VR Tour**, and click **Generate Light 720° VR Tour**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748421044134/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748421154406/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Select **panoramas** and click **Generate.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748421414043/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

 4. After generating **720° VR Tour**, click **View** **Light 720° VR Tour.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748421496922/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Tips

* Make sure every room is closure by doors.
* You can [rename Panoramas](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/how-to-rename-panorama?kjl_source=hc) to make it correctly shown on that tour.
* Only one 720° VR tour rendering can be categorized into your project. Be careful in case it was covered by a new one.
