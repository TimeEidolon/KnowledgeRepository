# Problem

Mirror effect appears after rendering glass material

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1555308922666/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp)

# Reason

The model's face normals are reversed and need to be flipped. Some glass is single-sided, and the back shows a mirror effect; adding a face will show the glass effect.

# Solution

 1. Click monochrome display, white is the front, and blue-gray is the back (default sketch color, can be changed).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1555308934713/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp)

2. Double-click to enter the group, select all faces, right-click to reverse the plane.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1555308950965/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp)

3. After reversing the plane, everything appears white, indicating the model is facing the right way. Then, click material mapping display, reapply the material to the model and save/upload, and it's OK.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1555308969651/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp)
