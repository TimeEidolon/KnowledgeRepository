1. User-oriented  
All users  
  
2. Function introduction  
Note: This function only applies to the raised shapes set in the background wall design. The shapes made by stretching/lofting in the material&component  tool are no longer within the scope.  
  
After the background wall is raised, the sun corner of the room will appear as a "missing corner". The sun corner closing tool launched this time supports closing the sun corners of the whole house with one click, and automatically connects shapes, paving, light strips, and lines.
