1.Go to Merchant platform > Enterprise Catalog > Custom Products > Custom Furniture > Create

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851344996/03F9A391704E5ABBE8C74252AE72D503.png)

2.Go to 3D model.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851345965/543BD7482D6BCF5FB371E21BEC495303.png)

3.Click or just drag and drop files to add models. Only 3Ds Max and SketchUp models can be uploaded.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851347092/A88EEDB2E7A3E24DAF9E5E08CCF9DC0A.png)

Categorize the 3D model after uploading. Handles, sinks, and faucets require X&Y coordinates.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733851347654/D92D0F7102B11230F41BB0345052BC59.png)

4.Select a category and click to save the changes.
