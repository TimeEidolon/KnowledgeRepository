### **Overall**

Multiple lighting templates are available for a one-click application, making it easy to switch between day and night lighting with just one click.

### **Steps**

Step 1: Select "Render" from the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733920335108/3F744B8DC664BAF0BB4486A239C5FB86.png)

Step 2: Choose the "Aspect Ratios" or "Resolution" settings, select the desired lighting template, and choose an exterior view.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733920337612/9DEB12D2697FBB030C257241D1D14A7C.png)

Step 3: Adjust the "Camera Parameters", switch between camera views, and use "Camera Clipping" for rendering through walls. You can also save the current camera angle for later adjustments.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733920339719/5C4A2C478E39ADC2C4C19C3AA6868D7B.png)

Step 4: Click "Render" and wait for the rendering to complete.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733920341242/2DB5C9DC45A5148ADDB5D0800F6F1A3F.png)

Step 5: Once rendering is complete, you can view and adjust the final result.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733920342883/BF2A4DA24F7C281CFFEAFE226ED7FF97.png)

Step 6: Those who pursue excellence can also add custom lighting to the lighting template

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733920344280/0F3557E04E30EC864CEE092F4846FEDA.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733920345979/59ED6DB1D91725DFFF05CDCD4A22EA4A.png)
