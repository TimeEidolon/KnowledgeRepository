Removing walls in Coohom can significantly enhance your design process, allowing for greater flexibility and creativity in your projects. Whether you’re looking to change the layout of a room or create an open space, here’s a quick guide on how to delete or remove walls effectively.

#### Step 1: Access 2D View

The first step in deleting a wall is to **switch to 2D view** within your project. This view provides a clear perspective on the layout, making it easier to identify and select the wall you wish to remove.

#### Step 2: Click the Wall

Once you are in 2D view, simply **click on the wall** that you want to delete. After clicking, wait for the **toolbar** to appear. This toolbar contains various editing options, including the delete function.

#### Step 3: Click the Delete Button

After selecting the wall and ensuring that the toolbar is visible, locate and **click the delete button**. This action will remove the selected wall from your design.

#### Conclusion

Deleting walls in Coohom is a straightforward process that enhances your ability to customize spaces effectively. By accessing 2D view, selecting the wall, and using the delete button, you can easily modify your designs according to your vision. Happy designing!
