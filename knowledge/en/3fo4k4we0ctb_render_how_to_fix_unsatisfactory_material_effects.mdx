# Problem

Not satisfied with the material of the furniture model itself, want to replace the material.

# Solution

1. Click to enter the material editor interface of the model.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135013801/C02A77B2ABDF2B91AED8CA2EB49B353C.png)

2. If the existing material library does not have satisfactory material, you can enter the Real-time Material Creation.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135013634/07C24744A08DF7C00B4A5B3A847387F8.png)

3. You can upload satisfactory textures yourself.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135013833/582158EAE9C4FC745BEAA84A49DD51B6.png)

4. If the texture feels too repetitive, you can choose seamless stitching.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135013664/EF2EBF250FEC12DF0A3B461E9BD9652F.png)

5. You can adjust the material in real-time rendering.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762135013665/D81093C10DA8B7E7E62AE491DE2EB9E5.png)
