**Overall**

**【Photo Studio1.0】Supports to build 【Own Shed】**

**Overall**

Users can now upload their projects to their own shed for use.

**Aim User**

Enterprise

(For this function, you need to purchase own shed coupons, and can only be added to the Photo Studio package. For purchase, please contact CSC.)

**Steps**

Click to【Build your shed】, then select a project

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1747798174567/14A05FF0CBA11DC0D42B92882C28A8FE.png)

**【Tips】**

* Only the project from the V4.0 Design Tool can be selected
* Currently, deleting own shed is not supported.
* You can view the usage data of the self-built studio vouchers in 【My Assets】
