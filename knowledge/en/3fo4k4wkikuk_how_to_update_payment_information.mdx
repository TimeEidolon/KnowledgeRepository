Payment Information Update

## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
**[Login](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)**with your Coohom account.

### Step 2
Click the**Profile** icon in the upper right corner to locate "**My subscription"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547145278/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp)

### Step 3
Select **Billing Info** to check the Payment Info and then click **Edit** to update your payment information.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745736266454/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745736266454/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png" alt="Step 3" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 4
Click **UPDATE** to confirm.
