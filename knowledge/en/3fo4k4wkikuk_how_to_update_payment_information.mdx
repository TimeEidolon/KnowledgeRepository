Payment Information Update

1. **[Login](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)**with your Coohom account.

2. Click the**Profile** icon in the upper right corner to locate "**My subscription"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745547145278/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png?x-oss-process=image/format,webp)

3. Select **Billing Info** to check the Payment Info and then click **Edit** to update your payment information.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1745736266454/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Click **UPDATE** to confirm.
