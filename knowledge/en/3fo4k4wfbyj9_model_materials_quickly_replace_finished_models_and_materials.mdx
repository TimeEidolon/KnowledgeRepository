In the design tool, you can use the replacement tool to quickly replace the style and material of finished models, helping users efficiently complete personalized designs.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fH7L-AYE5cg)

# One-Click Model Replacement

1. Select a furniture model and click on **Replace** in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774233797601/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Select the model you want to replace in the pop-up panel, then click to successfully replace it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774233982808/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Material Replacement

1. Select the model and click **Material Editor** in the floating window**.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774234041349/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Enter the **Material Editor** page, click on the part you want to replace, and then select the new material on the left panel to replace.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1774234845842/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

3. After replacing, you can adjust the location and size in the right property panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1774234907399/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

4. You can also click **Ultra-fast Rendering** to preview the effect in advance.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1774235005625/8B92EAD58843012015ACE668614C773A.gif)

5. Once the settings are completed, you can click **Complete** in the upper right corner to exit the material editing page and view the final effect.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774235418345/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1774235468876/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
