Hi Coohomers,

It is recommended to use realistic and fast series lighting templates, which are closer to the actual environment and material effects while customizing the light before generating Images and videos.

Here is a detailed instruction regarding the various lighting templates.

**Realistic---**

**&lt;Indoor Daytime&gt;**

gamma=2.2

Ambient light: Yes

Based on the Nature daylight template, the impact of ambient light on the interior is optimized, reducing the total indoor light energy, making the daytime feel more obvious, and the depth of field of the picture enhanced.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930282280/9F206837B821661644093506779CF5D0.png)

**&lt;Indoor Nighttime&gt;**

gamma=2.2

Ambient light: Yes

It is a nighttime indoor lighting optimized and upgraded based on the Nature nightlight template. It is mainly warm-colored indoor light, combined with cool-colored outdoor ambient light. The picture has clear lighting levels, and the warm and cold combination is warm and comfortable.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930285451/3E130757CAE8A9FFC7EEC5D3814D5AA2.png)

**Balance---**

**&lt;Natural Daylight&gt;**

gamma=1.0

Ambient light: No

The light feels natural and warm white, suitable for living rooms or open workspaces. The effect is better when combined with floor-to-ceiling windows.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930287484/C53624CE88EA496049125B8949F8029A.png)

**&lt;Natural Nightlight&gt;**

gamma=1.0

Ambient light: No

The light is warm and yellow, suitable for creating the atmosphere of quiet spaces such as bedrooms and restaurants.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930290480/610FA980C3C04AA23C6C8444F22130EB.png)

**Fast---**

**&lt;Ultra-Fast daylight&gt;**

Ambient light: Yes

It is suitable for various indoor scenes during the day, achieving extremely fast rendering, bright and transparent, and clear layers. The effect is similar to Indoor Daytime.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930294782/A3D97882C0606064A010E36B142BAD5F.png)

**&lt;Ultra-Fast night&gt;**

Ambient light: Yes

It is suitable for various indoor scenes at night, achieving extremely fast rendering, warm color tone, and clear layers. The effect is similar to Indoor Nighttime.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733930300072/3ADA99C74DD7FD9B20A1ACDDD836D457.png)
