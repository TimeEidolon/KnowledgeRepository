To meet the diverse needs of floor plan design and enrich outdoor spaces, you can use the external area tool to quickly create courtyards, terraces, and other areas outside existing rooms on the floor plan. These areas will be presented as independent slabs without ceilings or walls, while retaining basic room attributes. You can modify the room names as needed. This article will explain how to draw external areas.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cweYHgyEBzg)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

On the **Floor Plan** page, click **External Area** in the material panel on the left.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1769582525032/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Drawing External Areas

1. Enter the external area drawing page. Use the tools in the top toolbar to create the desired area. Once completed, click **Finish** in the top right corner.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1769582879207/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/gif/1769583129006/D0F6A7934A629582CB972C4F55C2A615.gif)

2. Switch to **3D** view to check the effect.

* Note: The external area feature involves using the top tools to draw a closed area, forming an independent slab without ceilings or walls as an external area.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/gif/1769583257823/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

3. Click on the external area to modify the **Room Name** in the property panel on the right.

* Note: Essentially, the external area is treated as a room with room attributes. You can set the room name, but the room type as outdoor cannot be changed.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/image/png/1769583443822/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Notes

1. The outline of the external area can only be drawn outside existing rooms and cannot intersect with them.

2. Walls of rooms cannot intersect with the outline of the external area; you can only draw a new room within the external area.
