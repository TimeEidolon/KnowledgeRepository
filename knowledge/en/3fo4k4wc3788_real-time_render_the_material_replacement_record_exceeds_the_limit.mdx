In fact, this messages appears due to that there is a maxium quota of 400 times of material replacement from backend. Once exceeding this quota, it will cause some potential technical issue such as lagging or glitch etc. Hence, to prevent these issues, there is such a pop-up message to warn users to hold on the action.

If the user still wants to replace the materials for this project, **there are two options here**.

**First**, Go back to 3D view to change the material there

**Second**, Add a new lighting template under the same project and then change materials.
