# Recover the Deleted Project

1. If you want to recover deleted project you can send your coohom account email and the project name to customer support **live chat** or send email to **support@coohom.com** to raise a recovery process.

2. After customer support team receive recover project request it usually takes 1-2 working days to be recovred.
