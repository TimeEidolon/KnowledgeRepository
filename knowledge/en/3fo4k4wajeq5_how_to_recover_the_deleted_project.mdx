## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
If you want to recover deleted project you can send your coohom account email and the project name to customer support **live chat** or send email to **support@coohom.com** to raise a recovery process.

### Step 2
After customer support team receive recover project request it usually takes 1-2 working days to be recovred.
