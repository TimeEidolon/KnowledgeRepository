Some users will meet the issue that their furnitures in the 3D floor plan are well displayed but they will disappear in the construction drawing's views. What could be the reason and how to resolve them?

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974304674/5D6C5030349A5C98A391FE468F1829F1.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974306965/4E6EFD1A3B1B21E5E2D535109D3B8915.png)

As for the reason, it's probably because of that the user's own uploaded furniture's categories are not configurated from backend tool or due to other issues.

Then how to resolve it from users' side temporarily? Kindly use this **Detection** function to detect the missing models first and then click one by one to make them display.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733974308767/0D226A0C81E74866BA6DB1AA9B5FA8A8.png)

Please be noted that our construction team keeps optimizing this function to reduce the occurency frequency of undisplayed model so that you will not need to do this manually. But this can be a temporary method for emmergency use. 

If you need any further help, please feel free to contact **Coohom Customer Service** team via [**Live Chat**](https://www.coohom.com/en_US/helpcenter/article/how-to-contact-live-chat?kjl_source=hc) or send email directly to **support@coohom.com**.
