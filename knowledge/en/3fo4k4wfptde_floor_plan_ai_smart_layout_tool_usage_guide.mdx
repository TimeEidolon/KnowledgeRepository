Want to say goodbye to the hassle of space planning and unlock your ideal home with one click? The AI design feature brings you an unprecedented efficient design experience. Whether you need to plan the full layout or focus on renovating a specific rooms, AI can quickly generate a personalized solution based on your preferences and support one-click switching to a 3D view for an immersive preview. Come try it out~
