Want to say goodbye to the hassle of space planning and unlock your ideal home with one click? The AI design feature brings you an unprecedented efficient design experience. Whether you need to plan the full layout or focus on renovating a specific rooms, AI can quickly generate a personalized solution based on your preferences and support one-click switching to a 3D view for an immersive preview. Come try it out~

# For Users

All users, gradually opening up~

# Entrance

In the design tool, click on the top right corner's **AI Design.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776843276922/41074E674438B51A6B9B9D82AF571CDE.png)

# AI Smart Layout

1. In the pop-up panel, choose **Full Layout** or **Specific Rooms** in the top right corner .(Example: full layout).

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776848252776/007A7A68B6F3B5B44EFB6236267EB13D.png)

* If **Specific Rooms** is selected, you need to first choose the room (multiple selections allowed) before proceeding with other operations.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776848437384/BA3CF5EA969D0747CC1A36BC94ED33E3.gif)

2. Place the mouse over the corresponding space to **Change Room Type** in the pop-up window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1776848612991/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)

3. Next, at the bottom, **Choose Your Preferred Style** and then click **Generate AI Design.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776932183240/2A781F3DBE004189376501EE586342F4.png)

4. Once the generation is successful, you can switch to **3D** view to see the effect.

* When **Full Layout** is selected, the generated effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776848147673/5548F3BC780CC181CD9954FF9BC9B2E5.png)

* When **Specific Rooms** (Living Room) is selected, the generated effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776849737812/9515661B7ACF1849AB0D78AC39816F36.png)

# Notes

1. In the **AI Design** tool, only the room types listed in the pop-up window can be selected and used to generate smart layouts.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776910587947/4E9B70BBC32F5D5A98FDF443C3150C2B.png)

2. If a room type is not within the supported range as shown in the pop-up, it cannot be selected or used to generate a smart layout, as shown below:

* When the room type is set to **Customized**, the AI Design tool will prompt "Change the room type for greyed-out rooms, or they cannot qenerate Al layouts"**.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776853518887/0E4DE1C335B1BC44D23FE5C2A45C7C72.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1776910630272/9019040D5E6C5B4DAEB6E545FBA8E39F.png)

* At this point, it is necessary to replace the room type with a supported one in the pop-up window. After the replacement is completed, smart layouts can be generated normally.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1776911259498/B3C88C7D33C1A76482B99FC61D4EEE51.png)
