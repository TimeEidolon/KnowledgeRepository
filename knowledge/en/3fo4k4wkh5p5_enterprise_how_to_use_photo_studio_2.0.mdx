Overview

Photo Studio 2.0 is a product display rendering tool launched by Coohom. It not only contains a large number of studio templates and material public libraries, but also supports real-time preview of rendering effects, which greatly improves the efficiency of drawing, and saves more than 95% of the production cost of product marketing materials.

At present, Photo Studio 2.0 is only open to Coohom enterprise users, and enterprise users can contact the exclusive customer service to obtain a quotation.
