## Steps with screenshots

Each step below includes the screenshot used in this article.

### Step 1
Select **IMAGES/VIDEOS** in the header of our 3D floor planner.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841176421/BD362FDADD1ED2F1EC446355FC8AE687.png)

### Step 2
Choose **Front View**, **Panorama,** or **Top View** in the header of the render page. You can also choose the Default view (2D or 3D)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841179000/C32780AF481D31A523B8CDA59CCCE9B5.png)
<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841179000/C32780AF481D31A523B8CDA59CCCE9B5.png" alt="Step 2" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 3
Select proper options for **Aspect Ratios**, **Resolutions**, **Ambient Lights**, **Environment**, **Wall Modes,** and **Backgrounds** in the left panel to complete render settings.

### Step 4
Modify your camera settings in the right panel.

### Step 5
Decide the scenario you want to capture.

### Step 6
Drag the camera icon in the canvas to change the camera's position and angle.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733841187520/C58656F14F6E75A201DDC0D98891CF8C.jpeg)

### Step 7
Or directly left-click to rotate and right-click to move the camera in 3D window on the right side.

<img src="https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841190787/7170B6E8785251763578DD75F7B9227F.png" alt="Step 7" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;" />

### Step 8
Click **Render** to get your stunning images.

### Step 9
Click **View more** under **Gallery** to check renderings.

2. Choose **Front View**, **Panorama,** or
