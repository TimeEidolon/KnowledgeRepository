### Step 5
You can switch display modes by clicking the "Sphere model" button 6.Parameter settings: * Diffuse: Upload a diffuse map. A diffuse map is a texture that defines the overall color and shape of an object. * Reflection: keep the reflection by default. * Reflection Glossiness: choose Map or Color mode to adjust the reflection glossiness. The lighter the color, the higher the highlight, the darker the color, and the more matte it is. * Bump/Normal: Upload bump/normal map and adjust the scale value. The larger the value, the more pronounced the surface texture.
