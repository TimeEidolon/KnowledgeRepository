The open / close doors tool allows you to open and close doors with one click. Using shortcuts makes it faster and more convenient, and the internal structure of the cabinet is clearly displayed. This article will explain in detail how to open and close doors with one click.

⭐Before reading the article, it is recommended to watch the video tutorial first for easy getting started~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZCcNU6PMNAk)

# For Users

All Users

# Entrance

1. Enter the design tool and select **"Advanced Tool - Kitchen＆Bath / Closet / Doors＆Windows".**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756196843011/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Select **"Toolkit - Open / Close Doors"** in the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756199151843/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# One Click Open / Close Doors

**Method One:** Select **"Toolkit - Open / Close Doors"** in the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1756197795360/61FDE4853ABB3640523A136FBC057384.gif)

**Method Two:** Use the **shortcut "Ctrl+O"** to switch the door with one click, the cabinet door opens with one click, is displayed in real-time, and the internal structure is clear and distinct.

* **Win system shortcut: Ctrl+O**
* **Mac system shortcut: Command+O**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1756199151583/C5DFE369B6AA905ACF1176148F71CD78.gif)
