# One Click Open / Close Doors

**Method One:** Select **"Toolkit - Open / Close Doors"** in the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1756197795360/61FDE4853ABB3640523A136FBC057384.gif)

**Method Two:** Use the **shortcut "Ctrl+O"** to switch the door with one click, the cabinet door opens with one click, is displayed in real-time, and the internal structure is clear and distinct.

* **Win system shortcut: Ctrl+O**
* **Mac system shortcut: Command+O**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1756199151583/C5DFE369B6AA905ACF1176148F71CD78.gif)
