We are thrilled to announce that the highly anticipated **Coohom 5.0 design tool** is now accessible to all Coohom users! This update marks a significant enhancement in our platform, empowering both existing and new users with cutting-edge features and capabilities designed to elevate their design experience.

#### Access for Existing Users

For our loyal users who have been utilizing the Coohom v4 tool, we have great news!

### Transition to v5 Access

* **Automatic Upgrade**: All existing users with only v4 access will automatically receive access to the Coohom 5.0 design tool.
* **Version Selection**: When creating new projects, you will have the option to select which version—v4 or v5—you want to use. This flexibility allows you to choose the best fit for your project requirements, ensuring a seamless transition while still being able to utilize familiar features if needed.

#### Access for New Registered Users

In addition to providing enhanced access for existing users, we have also streamlined the onboarding process for newcomers.

### Exclusive Use of v5 Design Tool

* **New User Registration**: All new users registering on our platform will exclusively gain access to the Coohom 5.0 design tool from the moment they sign up.
* **Enhanced Features from Day One**: By using v5 right away, new users can take full advantage of all the latest tools and functionalities that enhance project creation and collaboration from their very first experience with Coohom.

#### Why Choose Coohom 5.0?

The launch of Coohom 5.0 introduces a host of exciting features designed with user experience in mind:

* **Advanced Design Capabilities**: With improved rendering speeds and more intuitive controls, designing your space has never been easier.
* **Expanded Asset Library**: Gain access to an even larger selection of furniture, decor items, and materials that help bring your creative visions to life.
* **Improved Collaboration Tools**: Work together seamlessly with clients or team members by utilizing enhanced sharing options and real-time feedback capabilities.

#### Conclusion

We are committed to continuously improving your design experience on our platform. The introduction of Coohom 5.0 is a testament to this commitment, offering both existing and new users robust tools tailored for innovative design work.

Whether you’re an established user exploring new possibilities or a newcomer eager to dive into modern design practices, we’re excited about what you can create with Coohom 5.0!

Thank you for being part of our community! If you have any questions about accessing these tools or need assistance as you transition between versions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team. Happy designing!
