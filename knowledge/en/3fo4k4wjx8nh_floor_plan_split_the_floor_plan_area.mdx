In the floor plan tool, the split area function supports dividing the floor plan area and naming the room, this article explains in detail how to divide the floor plan area and name the room.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JOUTwvHuM1c)

# For Users

All users

# Split Area

Enter the **floor plan** tool and select the **split area** in the top toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747724546068/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Split Room

1. The split room tool can select **floor only** or **ceiling, wall and floor**, then click to enter the drawing interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756882214672/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. After entering the drawing interface, use the tools in the top toolbar to draw, and click **Finish** after drawing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747726526982/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Split Floor

1. The split floor tool selects the **floor** or **ceiling** and clicks to enter the drawing interface.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756882214677/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. After entering the drawing interface, use the tools in the top toolbar to draw, and click **Finish** after drawing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747728666877/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Only the floor or ceiling area is split, and the split area cannot be named.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1756882214741/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Name the Room

In **2D mode**, click on the drawing area and set the **room type and room name** in the right panel.

* **Note: The room name can only accept a maximum of 60 alphabetic characters.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747726993778/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
