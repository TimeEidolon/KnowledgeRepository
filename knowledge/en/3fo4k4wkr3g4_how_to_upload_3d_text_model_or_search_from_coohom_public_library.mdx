### **How to search 3D text in Coohom public library:**

1.Go to **Public model library,** look for **Home Decor,** next **3D Text.**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911876647/ED7EC813A897F07FDDAEE837A2710DE5.png)

2. Drag the 3D text into floorplan and adjust the size of 3D text.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733911878075/78F859DAA9DD570D04DA833BE937D9D4.png)
