# Add Light Strips in Materials & Components

## Entrance

Enter the design tool, and click **Advanced Tool—Material and Component.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770256416311/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Add Light Strips

1. After entering the tool page, click **Light Strips** at the top, set up in the pop-up panel, and click **Confirm** after completing the settings.

* When the **Light Trough** option is selected, you can set the brightness, color, length, and width of the light strip.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770270980625/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* When the **Light Trough** option is not selected, you can set the brightness, color and position of the light strip on the ceiling.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770256946519/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Hold the **Shift** key to select multiple paths, release Shift after selection, and click the blue surface where you want to place the light strip. The light strip will be automatically placed around the path.

* When the **Light Trough** option is selected, the effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1770270980710/9F78812AAB2F6E08258F152F8BDD823D.gif)

* When the **Light Trough** option is not selected, the effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1770257555786/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

3. Hold **Shift** and double-click the **Light Strip** to set the **Connect Faces** and **Group** in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770270980815/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

4. Click **Light Strips** to configure the **Color** and **Brightness** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770257764779/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. After completing the setup, click **Back to Floor Planner** in the top right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770270980684/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

# Add Light Strips in Ceiling Editor

## Entrance

Select the ceiling surface, and click **Ceiling Editor** in the floating window.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770258206724/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Add Light Strips

1. After entering the tool page, you can click the tools like **Line/Curve/Rectangle/****Circle**. In the toolbar, select **Light Strip** and draw your desired shape.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1770270980680/6FB51597E71C9383060A677E01A2CC95.gif)

2. Once finished, select the required shape surface and set the **Extrusion** value. The shaped surface and the original ceiling need to have a height difference to see the effect. Then, adjust the top-right window to preview.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770258732515/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. You can also draw the shape first, then select the shape surface and configure the **Extrusion** value.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770270980770/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

4. Click the edge of the shape. In the left profile panel, **Set Light Trough and Light Strip**.

* When **THE** **Indented Lamp Belt** is selected, you can set the brightness, color, width, and thickness of the light strip. Once configured, adjust the top-right window to preview.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770259572370/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* When **External Expansion Belt** is selected, you can also configure the brightness, color, width, and thickness of the light strip. Once configured, adjust the top-right window to preview.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770270980657/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

5. After finishing, click **Exit with Save** in the top right corner.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770259964214/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
