When import 2D drawing, you can enter the page of checking floor plan. Here you can perform operations such as re-recognize, align the wall, delete components, etc., effectively improving the accuracy of house type recognition generation.

## For Users

All users, grgadually opening up\~

## Entrance

**Import 2D Drawing** and click **Advanced Import** to enter the **Check Floor Plan** page.

* Note: If you do not need to adjust the floor plan, you can directly click the **Import** button to produce the layout.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778308830183/B0B3B750BB3506EF6BE93C4FE60AB6FA.png)

## Adjusting the Imported Floor Plan

### Regional Re-recognition

On the **Check Floor Plan** page, click **Re-recognize in Selected Region** , then draw a selection box over unsatisfactory regions on the canvas. In the list, confirm the required components (walls are included by default), with options to delete or add more. Next, click **Re-recognition** to precisely re-recognize that area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778308903440/E60AA00EEA0A7D875E2FADB2FE22D69C.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1778309331657/8B92EAD58843012015ACE668614C773A.gif)

### Align Walls

Click **Smart Wall Alignment** to automatically identify positions in the current plan that may need alignment. Identified positions can be marked as **Ignore Current** or **Focus On** . Click **Focus On** to center the canvas on the position needing alignment. Two walls requiring alignment will be highlighted. You can then manually click on the wall that needs adjustment to complete the alignment.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778309384708/C1EC1965362A71618F8F8DAE3BA8E432.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1778310029921/CD8FC63C91BC49078853BAFD719BE561.gif)

### Delete Component

Click **Delete Component**, and then click on the unwanted component to delete it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778310103194/96B45CCD339DBD22D5680BB9052E0E42.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1778310149230/BA3CF5EA969D0747CC1A36BC94ED33E3.gif)

### Undo and Redo

All operations support undo and redo. If an error occurs, click the **Undo/Redo** button in the top-right corner to correct it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778310176076/DAEDB5A53445D93C384FCC6103EBD498.png)

### Opacity Adjustment

While performing operations, hold down the shortcut key **Y** to adjust opacity, or click **Settings** to adjust it directly using the slider in the opacity pop-up.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1778310641617/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)

### Generate Floor Plan

Once local adjustments are completed, directly click **Generate**to finalize.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778310724682/2DE787103875984F16E02A61CB13CC4E.png)
