When import 2D drawing, you can enter the page of review and adjust results. Here you can perform operations such as  re-recognize, align the wall, delete components, etc., effectively improving the accuracy of house type recognition generation.

# For Users

All users, gradually opening up~

# Entrance

**Import 2D Drawing** and click **Review Recognition** to enter the review and adjust results page.

* Note: If you do not need to adjust the floor plan, you can directly click the **Import** button to produce the layout.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779258465890/CFD84F283EF8818D0AFDEA4DC9254DAE.png)

# Adjusting the Imported Floor Plan

## Regional Re-recognition

On the review and adjust results page, click **Refine Area Recognition**, then draw a selection box over unsatisfactory regions on the canvas. In the list, confirm the required components (walls are included by default), with options to delete or add more. Next, click **Re-recognition** to precisely re-recognize that area.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779262616156/D9ABE9D7058F13FB068E8B8BC2B6B629.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1779262750285/BA3CF5EA969D0747CC1A36BC94ED33E3.gif)

## Align Walls

Click **Smart Wall Alignment** to automatically identify positions in the current plan that may need alignment. Identified positions can be marked as **Ignore Current** or **Focus On**. Click **Focus On** to center the canvas on the position needing alignment. Two walls requiring alignment will be highlighted. You can then manually click on the wall that needs adjustment to complete the alignment.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779262793139/BFB8E36B5427C9EFBD7894B0C75BEFBE.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1779262855441/DCE71965D19C1438840BBBDE410C6B53.gif)

## Delete Component

Click **Delete Component**, and then click on the unwanted component to delete it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779262881839/3C43B8238F07EA7ABA4AF3EB6C4FFF7E.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1779262918111/BDD03DBD7B143CFD44CBE3156FF860F8.gif)

## Undo and Redo

All operations support undo and redo. If an error occurs, click the **Undo/Redo** button in the top-right corner to correct it.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779262938202/44290D3B00219795D5661204CC137FF3.png)

## Opacity Adjustment

While performing operations, hold down the shortcut key **Y** to adjust opacity, or click **Settings** to adjust it directly using the slider in the opacity pop-up.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1779262984698/5BE693C2A037151FD75670ED2B8C2A1C.gif)

## Generate Floor Plan

Once local adjustments are completed, directly click **Generate**to finalize.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1779263008225/75C5B97A48302B2F648BEF87218A4A40.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1778310800038/75CA28965AA7DCC7180395ACBCC01626.png)
