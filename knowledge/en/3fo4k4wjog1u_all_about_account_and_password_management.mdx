* How to Reset Password?

### How to Reset Password?

If you wish to update your password for security reasons, please follow these steps to set a new password:

1. **[Login](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)** with your Coohom account.

2. Click your avatar at the top right corner of the merchant platform.

3. Select **Account Settings** in the drop-down menu.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848337467/B492865BB617FFEF68BE88B63644E169.png)

4. Go through **Account Settgs** ＞ **Password** and fill in **Old Password**, **New Password,** and **Confirm Password** fields.**in**

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848338553/EE0CBF022395A1D7BF877027E08777F8.png)

5. Click ***SAVE*** to confirm.

* What if I forgot the password?

### What if I forgot the password?

1. Click the [link](https://www.coohom.com/user/forgetpassword) and visit the page: [https://www.coohom.com/user/forgetpassword](https://www.coohom.com/user/forgetpassword)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848342007/DA2DAFE3E8851242EA2D708F747AF849.png)

2. Check the email in your email Inbox or Junk box which the email looks like below template:

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733848343799/EA69089C63C7888A5926ED3558A5EFF4.png)

3. Click on **RESET PASSWORD**, then you can reset the password.

### How do I log in with a social media account?

To simplify your login process, Coohom supports signing in with social media such as Facebook, Google, or Twitter. Here are the steps:
