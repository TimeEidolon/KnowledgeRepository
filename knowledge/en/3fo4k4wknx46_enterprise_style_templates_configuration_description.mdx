After the merchant configures the overall style template, designers can select the appropriate template in the design tool, and after the one-click setting is successful, the model dragged into the scene will automatically replace the selected style, which greatly improves the design efficiency.

# For Users

All enterprise users

# Configure Style Templates

1. Enter the **Workspace**,select **"Enterprise Catalog ＞ Custom Products ＞Product Catalog ＞Advanced".**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748509289810/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Style Templates**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748509593111/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. Click **New Template**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748509654889/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. Describe the new style template, including the **style name, introduction, cover image, and display image**. When you are done entering the information, click **Next.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748509753611/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. Edit the materials and style of **cabinets, door panel and drawer front**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748510093606/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

6. Click to select, filter the category, select the desired material/style and click **Confirm.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748510205245/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

7. After you have edited the style item, please click **Next** to select the tag. The step is optional, you can skip it or choose any tags that fit the template, and click **Complete** to save the changes.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748510572595/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748512512325/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

8. After the setup is completed, style items can be added or deleted by **Edit Style Item**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748511300427/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* the tags can be added, deleted and renamed in **Edit Tag**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748511323851/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Design Tool Interface

After you have configurated the templates, designers can see it in the design tool.

* **Location:** Go to Tools and click on the top toolbar **Style Preset**, select the **configured style template**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1748511467083/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
