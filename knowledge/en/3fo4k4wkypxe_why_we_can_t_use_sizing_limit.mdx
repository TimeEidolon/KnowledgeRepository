Because there exists the risk of production docking after turning off sizing limit, some enterprise versions will uniformly turn off this switch, which may cause some designers to be unable to use this function!

Friendly reminder: After turning off sizing limit, designers can freely adjust the size, but they need to undertake the risks of adjusting modeling results, which may include risks related to dock installation or production.
