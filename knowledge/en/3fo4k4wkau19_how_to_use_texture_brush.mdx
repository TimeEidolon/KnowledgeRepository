Introduction: **Random Texture Brush** makes wood grains look more natural.

### What problem does it solve:

Wood grain is too neat to look authentic, because natural texture is somewhat random and irregular.

### How to use it?

1. Go to Toolkit＞Random Texture Brush

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896434866/66A7421AD61C4AC94625218ACB18873C.png)

2. Click a board, and its texture will be changed randomly.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896437703/3BC3F3703B8E55B981068C9E776D2122.png)
