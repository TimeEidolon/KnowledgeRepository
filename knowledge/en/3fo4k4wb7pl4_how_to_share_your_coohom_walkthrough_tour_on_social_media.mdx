Sharing your stunning 360/720 walkthrough tours created in Coohom is a fantastic way to showcase your design projects. Here's how you can effectively share your creations on social media and the options available for doing so.

#### Direct Sharing from Coohom

Users can easily share their rendered walkthrough tours directly from Coohom to popular social media platforms such as **Twitter, Facebook, and WhatsApp**. This feature allows you to promote your work effortlessly and engage with your audience without any hassle.

#### Downloading for Other Uploads

If you prefer to upload content manually or want to share it on platforms not directly supported by Coohom, you'll need to download the images or video of your walkthrough tour first. Once downloaded, you can upload these files to any social media platform of your choice.

#### Two Ways to Share Walkthroughs

There are two convenient methods for sharing rendered walkthroughs:

**Access Project Details in Workspace**: You can navigate directly to the project details within your workspace. From there, simply select the option to share the rendered walkthrough, making it easy to connect with others immediately.

**Access Gallery**: Alternatively, you can visit the Gallery section of Coohom. This allows you to browse through your saved projects and choose which rendered walkthroughs you'd like to share with your audience.

#### Conclusion

With these straightforward options---direct sharing on major social media platforms and downloading content for other uploads---you can effectively showcase your impressive designs created in Coohom. Whether you're sharing via project details or the Gallery, promoting your work has never been easier!
