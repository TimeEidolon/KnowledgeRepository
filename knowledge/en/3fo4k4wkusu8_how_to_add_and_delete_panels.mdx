### Add Panels

Method 1

Step one: Choose a panel and add it to the closet.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733907143847/385BE0D8262DD4E96D9BD46DD5C000E0.png)

Step two:

First, choose Select Part, then Single-click the panel, Choose duplicate to add more panels.

Or choose Panel array.
