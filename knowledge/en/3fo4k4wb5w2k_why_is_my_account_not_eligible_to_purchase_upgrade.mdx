Sometimes, our Coohom users will meet error and cannot upgrade to or purchase the plan they want, which can be really frustrated. This article will illustrate most situations of eligible upgrade/purchase and ineligible upgrade/purchase.  

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1742888882506/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## **Targeted Users: Individual Users**

## **Eligible Purchase**


**Auto-renewal Plan**

* Basic account: Basic account means free account so users can purchase any auto-renewal plan they want.
* One-Month/One-Year PRO Account**:** Users of this type can purchase any auto-renewal plan they want.
* Monthly PRO account: Users of Monthly PRO can purchase Yearly PRO, Monthly Elite and Yearly/Annual Elite.
* Yearly PRO account: Users of Yearly PRO can only purchase Yearly Elite
* Monthly Elite Account: Users of Monthly Elite can only purchase Yearly/Annual Elite

**One-time Plan**

* Users who subscribe to **auto-renewal plan** by paypal, local payment methods(Dana etc.) can purchase one-time plan only after they cancel the auto-renewal function.

## **Ineligible Purchase**


**Auto-renewal plan**

* Yearly PRO account: Users of Yearly PRO account cannot purchase Monthly PRO, Yearly PRO and Monthly Elite account.
* Yearly/Annual PRO account: Users of this type cannot purchase Monthly Elite

**One-time Plan**

* Auto-renewal plan paid by card: Visa/Master/American Express cannot purchase any one-time plan: one-month/one-year Pro or Elite.
* If you have any further concerns, please feel free to contact Coohom Customer Service team via: [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
