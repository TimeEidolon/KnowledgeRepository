# Reason

If you go to the hard material editing, you will see a message like this, Unable to save, The face containing cylinders does not support the integrated panel.

The curved wall is considered a cylindrical surface, so the curved surface currently does not support placing integrated panels of this special material.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1762134822826/DE81FE9FC608B45DD783CA2B957EBED8.png)

# Solution

Clear the integrated wall panel material, replace it with a regular wood veneer material or other texture materials, and then it will render normally.
