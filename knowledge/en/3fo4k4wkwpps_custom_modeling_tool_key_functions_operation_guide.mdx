The series of key functions in the Custom Modeling tool will become your powerful assistants! Whether it's generating 3D text with one click, flexibly adjusting the size and position of models, using the Mirror and Array functions for creative layouts, or adding unique details to your models with Bevel and Boolean functions—these practical and powerful functions are here to help. Next, let's take a closer look at these functions and discover how they can enhance your modeling experience~

## Modeling Function

1. Click on the **Line** or **Arc** tool in the top toolbar to draw the shape you want.

* The drawn pattern must be in a closed state, so that it will become a surface after drawing.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1770185305561/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Then you can select this surface and click on **Grouping** in the floating window. After grouping, you can **Move**, **Rotate** and edit more easily.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1770187005004/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. If you want it to have thickness, you can double-click to enter the group, then click the **Push****/Pull** button at the top, Next, select the surface to be stretched, then set the **Stretch Distance** in the right panel, After setting is complete, click **Enter** to confirm.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1770185630512/435B495D2CC0DB3743BE6523FD081D5D.gif)

## Sweep Function

The **Sweep** Function is one of the most creative modeling functions in the custom modeling tool. It allows you to quickly transform simple shapes into complex 3D models. By defining a path and selecting a cross-sectional shape, the **Sweep** Function extrudes the section along the path, automatically generating a 3D model with a specific form.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751435487369/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1770187324993/CC8B0470F7CF537A9606879660610D27.gif)

## BevelFunction

The bevel tool includes **Bevel 2D** and **Bevel 3D**, which can be used to round the corners of both 2D and 3D models.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1751432916449/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

You can use the bevel 2D to round the corners of straight lines. If you select an entire plane, you can round all the corners at once.
