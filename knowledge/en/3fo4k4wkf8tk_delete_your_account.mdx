# Navigate to Your Workspace

Start by [logging into](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin?kjl_source=hc) your Coohom account. Once you're in, head over to your **Workspace** where all of your projects are displayed. This is the central hub for managing and accessing everything related to your designs.

# Delete Your Account

1. In the upper right corner of the Workspace interface, you will find the **Profile Icon**. This icon typically resembles a person or an avatar and serves as a gateway to access your account settings.

2. Click the profile icon to open a dropdown menu. Select **Account Settings** from the menu.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1760585490271/C709A4E4F6A47B36A86D49088F73D677.png)

3. Select **Profile** and click **Delete My Account**. Here, you can delete your account.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760585886997/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4.After the account is deleted, your design projects, render images, model texture data, and any unused rendering coupons will be permanently deleted. Please read and understand the consequences of deleting your account, then enter your account email to permanently delete the account.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760587027231/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

5. As long as there are no VIP services under any subscriptions for the current account, it will be deleted immediately.

6. Users with VIP services cannot be deleted and must wait until the account expires and becomes free. If you have any questions, please contact customer service.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1760587669522/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
