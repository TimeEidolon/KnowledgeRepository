In the Material & Component tool, you can freely move the modeling surfaces or bodies. Select a starting point and move it to any position you desire. This article will explain in detail how to move a modeling surface and body.

⭐ Before reading the article, it is recommended that you watch the video tutorial first for ease of getting started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K6F9zOV9kgo)

After entering the Materials and Components Tool, **"Shift/⇧+Double Click"** to select the modeling body and click the **Group** button in the floating toolbar. The modeling body needs to be grouped as a whole before it can be moved.

# Entrance

Enter the interior design interface and select **"Advanced Tool - Material & Component"**.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1746771661772/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Move a Modeling Surface

After entering the Material & Component tool, select a modeling surface and click the **Move** button in the floating toolbar. Choose a starting point and then move the modeling surface to your desired position.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1755243949355/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1755237552620/31F61D60C42B4AFA24A25454D9F4BB4B.gif)

# Move a Modeling Body

1. After entering the Materials and Components Tool, **"Shift/⇧+Double Click"** to select the modeling body and click the **Group** button in the floating toolbar. The modeling body needs to be grouped as a whole before it can be moved.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1755243949316/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. After grouping is complete, the **Move** button will appear in the floating toolbar. Click **Move**, then select a starting point and move the modeling surface to your desired position.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1755243313682/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1755243949713/0958E9E13D3DD4FDAD27D63A6EE94C4F.gif)
