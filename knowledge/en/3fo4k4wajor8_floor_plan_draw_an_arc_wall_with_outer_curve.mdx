Want to add a streamlined feel to your space? This guide will step-by-step you through using the Arc Wall tool to elegantly transform the original straight wall into a curve. Prepare your floor plan, follow the steps, and create a naturally smooth arc wall.

⭐Before reading the article, it's recommended to watch the video tutorial first to get started quickly~

🎥 [Video URL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D75qPsclkhI)

# For Users

All users

# Entrance

After completing the floor plan drawing, select **"Floor Plan - Arc Wall".**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757991700565/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Draw an Arc Wall

## Add an Arc Wall

1. After clicking the Arc Wall tool, select the starting point of the curve and set the **radius value.**

2. When the angle is **45°**, click to set the endpoint of the curve.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003245523/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1757993686993/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. At this point, the wall will become curved, and when the angle is **90°**, click again.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003245553/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* Animated demonstration as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757994489380/D203CD6805C93A1A82016D7A2D269EFB.gif)

## Split and Delete the Straight Wall

1. After adding the arc wall, click the walls adjacent to the arc wall and click **Split** in the floating toolbar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003245856/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757994834009/D71F20B876E2532EE3569B220C895344.gif)

2. After splitting, delete the two split straight walls to complete, and you can switch to **3D mode** to view the arc wall effects.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1758003245574/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1757995029509/AA0F7CB9FE88CD7A94BD5016A8F28F8A.gif)
