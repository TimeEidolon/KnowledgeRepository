Whether you want AI to boldly reshape the space, or just optimize lighting while retaining furniture, this AI Image Generator Guide will get you started quickly~

# AI Image Generator Guide

## Choose a Drawing Mode

There are two modes: **Inspiration** and **AI Lighting**. The **Inspiration** mode will use AI to recreate space and furniture based on your description, while the **AI Lighting** mode retains your furniture and adjusts the lighting in the space based on your description

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761213355922/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* The effect of Inspiration mode is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761207014531/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

* The effect of AI Lighting mode is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761213355957/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

## Select a Style

There are multiple styles for you to choose from, click **More** to view, and try selecting a style you like to see the effect.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761207100792/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1761207250226/3DBDF431AC2256D0DBA44A47FBF8702B.gif)

## Describe the Scene

Enter keywords or a paragraph that describes the scene you want to generate in the description box. You can also click the **Text** tool, which contains descriptive words related to space types, home decoration, finishing, materials, design styles, color matching, etc. Click to select, making it more convenient and quick.

* **Note: The number of descriptive words in the text tool is limited, up to 8 can be added.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761207833575/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761213355943/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1761208383652/935DA4C824E7DAB969282D5B7F43C887.gif)

## Adjust Camera Parameters

In the right-side camera parameter panel, set **aspect ratios, camera height, and camera angle**, etc.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761213355902/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1761209015716/821F3DFC76BB27718798D6859BCED333.gif)

## Generate AI Images

After setting all configurations, click **Generate Now.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761213356033/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* The generated effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761209334539/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## View AI Images

Click **AI Gallery** to view the generated images.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1761213355952/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

The left side shows historically generated images, the right side shows different lighting or accessory effects for the current image.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1761209531590/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1761209594183/5879F3D667769113C59E4FE73253914C.gif)
