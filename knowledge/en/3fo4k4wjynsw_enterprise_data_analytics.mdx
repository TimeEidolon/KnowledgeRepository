### Data Analytics is useful for enterprise users. Admin and managers can get information about how products use with all enterprise users.

**Data Analytics** is useful for enterprise users. Admin and managers can get information about how products use with all enterprise users.  
There are three sections under Data Analytics:

*Account Data- You can check Individual and Enterprise's projects created, Rendering made.*

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854146322/479A2CA06536F6F8036F5751ACDCB33E.png)

Overall activity - You can view the activity status of all accounts, including the creation of plans, rendering, and the duration of tool usage for each account.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854148419/A103AF51D52DFD5D5BE5598485639F39.png)  

Account Ranking- Check each designer's data under your enterprise.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854149172/EBB88A7FE0A6CAB9EA0E991272E7A4AE.png)

In Account Ranking, you can download accounts usage details.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854149825/76CBC83E96E080D75646E2973575D0D7.png)

You will see how much time your team members use in Coohom.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854151335/A9B41F4A8A0B48BCDD209952C87DC718.png)

Hot Products: See the popularity of the model

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733854153108/D79F4771CA5A6D8B6EF57CF5BEA07249.png)
