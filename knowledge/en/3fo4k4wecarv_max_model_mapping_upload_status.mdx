# Realistic Lighting Materials and Overall Maps Support

The materials and textures referenced in this document are all standard 3ds Max materials and V-Ray textures, with the V-Ray version being 4.1.

* **V-Ray material**

Not supported：VRayMDLMtI、VRayFastsss2、VRayGLSMtl、VRayPointParticleMtl 、VRayScatterVolume 、VRayVRmatMtl、VRayVectorDisplBake、VRaySwitchedMtl

* **V-Ray map**

Not supported：VRayGLSLTex、VRaysky

* **General material**

Not supported：Physical Materia、Double Sided、Ink 'n Paint、Matte/Shadow、Morpher、MultiSub-object、PBR Material、Shell Materia、Top/Bottom、XRef Material

* **General map**

Not supported：Vertex Color

* Green indicates supported materials and maps.
