Building efficient scenes often hinges on quickly obtaining and managing model resources. Want to access high-quality public models quickly but don't know where to start? Don't worry, the comprehensive Model Library is here to solve these challenges for you. With clear sections covering personal resource management, premium model recommendations, and precise classification navigation, every model search and use will become effortless and efficient. This article will explain in detail how to use the model library.

# For Users

All users, function gradually opening.

# Entrance

Enter the design tool, click on the **Models** in the left sidebar.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765423765295/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Model Library Introduction

The **Models** is divided into **Personal**, **Explore**, and **Catalog** sections. Below, we'll explain each in detail.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765445213967/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

## Personal

The "**Personal**" section acts as a personal model storage space for viewing all models a user has **Favorites/Uploads/Recently Used**, facilitating quick use.

1. Click **Favorites** to quickly view previously favorited models in the corresponding folder and drag them directly into the floor plan.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765432427656/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1765445214199/9F78812AAB2F6E08258F152F8BDD823D.gif)

* In **Favorites**, you can search by name, type, creation time to quickly locate necessary models.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765432821145/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Uploads** to view all uploaded models in the list and drag them directly to the floor plan.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765445214083/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. Click **Recently Used** to view recently used models and drag them directly into the floor plan.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765433254866/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Explore

A curated collection of public models recommended by the platform, featuring high-quality model resources certified by the platform and uploaded by users. Divided into **Popular, Brands**, and **Recommend** sections for easy user selection.

1. Click **Popular**, select **Mostly Used/Trending** at the top, then pick the desired model from the materials to use.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765445214070/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765433956509/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765445214106/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

2. Click **Brands**, search by category for the desired brand, then choose suitable material within the brand to use.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765434398681/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765445214011/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

3. Click **Recommend**, select **Editor's Picks/Latest Models** at the top, then pick the desired material to use.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765434878904/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Catalog

The classification navigation center for model resources, allowing users to precisely locate needed models through hierarchical directories, avoiding blind searches.

1. Click **Catalog**, and the current directory is **By Rooms**. Users can filter as needed (e.g., kitchen-appliances) and select required materials accordingly.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765445214018/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

* Note: After clicking Directory, the **Grouping Options** can be switched to **By Categories**. The directory will update to display categories upon switching.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765442582975/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. When selecting materials, you can filter by **Style** at the top or click **FilterLayout.More.Filters** icon at the top right. In the pop-up window, refine filters further (e.g., door,form), making it easier and faster to find needed models.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/png/1765445214102/2D37C2734C50A6837B78ACB4959B66FF.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765442446872/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Quick Search

Method 1: In the **Models**, click thesearch bar at the top, input relevant keywords, and pick required materials from the search results.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/yuntai/image/gif/1765445213994/1CA83DEA6D0928946F2BD389D15284CF.gif)

Method 2: Use **Search by Image**, upload or paste an image to find models similar to the image, improving search efficiency.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1765438290746/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
