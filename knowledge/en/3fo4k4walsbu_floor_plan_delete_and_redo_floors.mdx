In the floor plan tool, floor deletion and redo are flexibly supported, making it convenient for users to manage the floor structures of villas, duplexes, commercial spaces, and other multi-story buildings. This effectively prevents data loss caused by accidental operations and ensures the reversibility and undoability of design operations.

### Step 4
Redo a Floor If you want to restore a deleted floor, click **Redo** in the floor attribute panel on the right. Then, click **Confirm** in the pop-up panel to recover the most recently deleted floor. * When deleting floors, only one floor can be restored at a time, and floors can be recovered one by one. * Floors will be inserted back into their original positions in the order of deletion. The corresponding floors at those positions will not be replaced. Floors above ground will move upward, and floors below ground will move downward.

# Deleting a Floor

1. Click the dropdown arrow of **Current Floor** in the attribute panel on the right, move the mouse to the desired floor, and click the **Delete** button.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773628806634/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. Click **Confirm** in the pop-up panel to delete the floor.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773628836535/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

3. The 1F floor serves as the base floor and cannot be deleted. However, you can switch 1F to the current floor, then move the mouse to the top of the screen and click **Clear**, followed by **All**. You will find all contents in the tool have been cleared.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773628863579/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773628889855/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Redo a Floor

If you want to restore a deleted floor, click **Redo** in the floor attribute panel on the right. Then, click **Confirm** in the pop-up panel to recover the most recently deleted floor.

* When deleting floors, only one floor can be restored at a time, and floors can be recovered one by one.
* Floors will be inserted back into their original positions in the order of deletion. The corresponding floors at those positions will not be replaced. Floors above ground will move upward, and floors below ground will move downward.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1773628922084/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
