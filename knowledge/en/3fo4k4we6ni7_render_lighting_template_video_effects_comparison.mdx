# Comparison conclusion

* Indoor Daytime 3.1 features fast rendering, supports a wide range of animations, and offers relatively stable effects. It is recommended for use
* Indoor Daytime 2.1 renders extremely slowly. To ensure that images are produced on the same day, 480P only supports low frame rate rendering.
