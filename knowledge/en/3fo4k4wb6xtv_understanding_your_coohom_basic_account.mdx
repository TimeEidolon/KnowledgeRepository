At Coohom, we believe in making design accessible to everyone. That’s why we offer a **Basic Account**, which is completely free and designed to help you get started on your design journey without any barriers.

#### What Is a Basic Account?

When you create a Coohom account using your email address, you automatically receive a **Basic Account**. This means that right from the start, you have access to a range of features that allow you to explore the platform and begin designing your spaces without any financial commitment.

#### No Cancellations Required

One of the best aspects of the Basic Account is its simplicity. Since it’s free, there are no subscription fees or charges involved, meaning there’s nothing for you to cancel. You can freely use the account as long as you wish without worrying about hidden costs or obligations.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742387607999/415C22147F0F2DF6D5871974EACA83AD.png)

#### Start Designing Today!

Creating your Coohom Basic Account is quick and easy! Simply sign up with your email, and you’ll gain immediate access to essential tools and resources that empower you to unleash your creativity.

In summary, a Basic Account with Coohom is an excellent way for users to dive into design without any hassle. Get started today and see what amazing projects you can bring to life!
