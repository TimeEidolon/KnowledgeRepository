I.Background of the Function

Currently, the merchant's master account can grant sub-accounts permission for parametric modeling. As a result, there may be multiple sub-accounts that can perform modeling and modify models. To avoid the inability to trace which account performed an operation on the model, the editor supports recording the "Modifier" in historical versions.

### **II.Introduction of the Function**

When the model is initialized, the operator who performs the "initialize" operation is recorded, including the merchant account name and email address. The specific record is shown in Figure 1-1.
