Let’s go to Construction tool in Coohom tool.

2. Draw rectangles with Guidelines and Rect tool.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896588769/28A6BEEC8BD69D37A2D302FEE839AE3F.png)

3. Use Chamfer tool to do a filleted corner.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896590462/3085918DE6DA438E57944AEEEDD5BC69.png)

4. Draw the other rectangles in the same way.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896592666/353A4D599964A94D5DB0C58FDC7129A7.png)

5. Make thickness with Push/Pull button.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896594279/2B3CE8F944440134DF778BD6655CF5A0.png)

6. Group them together.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896596631/35FAEEF52CD7CD2EC23186CE091081B1.png)

7. Add material to wall niches.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896598144/BC54C433E4A5C48A9CF3EE531EB84531.png)

Then, it comes to the last step of final rendering.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733896600124/074D98D66267CDAA3B93080E3D0F6D6B.png)
