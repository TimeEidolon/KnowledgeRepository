Hi Coohomers, it's easy to create with Coohom curved window and glass like this. (*Youtube link at the bottom*)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733830456243/7E794BA5A08515C43576F25C513ACF9E.png)![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733830458870/F4876D898307223121B0D917926E9269.png)

**Create Curved Window:**

1. If you have a curved window 3D model, you can upload it into your coohom personal library.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243304890/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
2. Of course, you can also search for an Curved window in the Coohom public model library.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243320155/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
3. Adjust wall to curve and set it as Dwarf with a Height:200.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243327769/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
4. Drag the curved window on the dwarf wall.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243333791/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

   ​
5. Adjust the size so that you can get an curved window now.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243345031/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

   **Create Curved Glass:**

   1. First, adjust the thickness of the wall to 10mm, then set the wall as Curve and adjust the size.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243359165/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

   b. Search for the Glass material, then drag the Glass material onto each wall.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243399472/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

   c. After rendering, the Curved glass wall that you just added Glass material to becomes transparent.

   ![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1745243403741/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)
