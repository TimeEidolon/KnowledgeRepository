---

1. Click **Model Library** in the left panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733910828016/DB8BE65B0425C4D33A8172427214B44E.png)

2. Select or drag your target model to your floor plan.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/gif/1733910829571/F39529819CF413A66EBFFD01FB8E7DEC.gif)

3. Once the model is placed, the floating toolbar appears. Flip, duplicate, rotate and delete the model you selected right here.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733910832885/53AE8086A5444AE82077F1E81A9CA7E5.png)

4. Modify model properties in the right panel.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733910834251/8A3C793D08E0AD1DDA62E34235F80BD4.png)
