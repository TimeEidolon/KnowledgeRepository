Users can choose and replace the material and style of models in the style panel, quickly replacing the material and style of the cabinet. This article will explain in detail how to use style to change model material and style.


## Steps with screenshots

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

Entrance Click the model to enter the **Style** panel, divided into the categories of **cabinet, doors, and handles**, where you can choose to replace **styles, materials, etc.** under the corresponding category.

# Introduction to the Style Panel

## Switch Model Catalogs

Click the switch catalogs icon in the upper right corner, and you can switch between different catalogs in the dropdown menu.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753345178000/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## View Model Catalogs

1. Click the dropdown icon at the model location to view the model catalog nodes in the dropdown, and you can choose to switch to different model catalogs.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
|  |  |

2. Click the arrangement icon to switch the different display forms of the models.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
|  |  |

## Expand Model Catalogs

1. Click the expand/collapse icon in the upper right corner to **expand/collapse the model catalog** with one click.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753347431908/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

2. After expanding, the model panel becomes a moveable split view, with nodes of different catalogs in the vertical bar and specific model classifications under the corresponding catalog, with secondary catalog models tiled on the right.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753348379099/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Support modifying the model bar width and moving the view panel**

* You can manually drag the middle divider to modify the width of the vertical bar.
* Drag the lower right corner of the model bar to change the width of the model bar and the number of columns displaying models.
* Freely move the view panel, placing the model bar panel anywhere on the tool interface, as shown in the operation demonstration below:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1753348642093/4FBE7164E4D390A1935E45374135C2FC.gif)
