**Target Users**: All individual users with Pro plan or above.


Since **May.7th,2025**, the **Bill of Materials** button is formally changed to **Quotation** and moved to **Drawings** icon on the left navigation bar.

Quotation is not available for **Basic accounts**. If you need it, please upgrade to Pro or Elite account: [https://www.coohom.com/pricing](https://www.coohom.com/pricing).

1. **[Log in](https://www.coohom.com/user/signin)**with your Coohom account.
2. Click on **Enter Tool**.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733887733963/6F88A948D046DF69D0F3F93E8863DE9F.png)

3. Click on **Drawings** > **Quotation**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1747220025103/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

4. The quotation will be downloaded as zip file. Each of the furniture applied in the floorplan will have own separate bill of material.

5. Please check out the overview bill of material which named "Bill of Material" which consists of all furniture applied in floorplan.  
​

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733887736409/19945D5C0FC23F414729A90E53141E06.png)
