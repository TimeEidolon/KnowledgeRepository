This article will provide a detailed understanding of the key functions of the Kitchen & Closet advanced 2D design tool —— Auto-refresh annotate, convenient multi-view layout, and one-click generation of elevation and callout views. With these practical and powerful functions, you can precisely focus on every detail and preview modifications in real-time, making complex spatial design simple and orderly!

# For Users

Users with relevant trial permissions. To apply for other trial accounts, please contact your CSC.

# Configuration Entrance

1. Log in to [Coohom](https://Coohom.com?kjl_source=hc).

2. Enter the **Workspace**, select **"****Settings - Customization - Usage Preferences - Kitchen & Closet Design Tool Version"**.

Two options are provided:

* Currently Selected: **"Kitchen & Closet"**(current stable version)
* Alternative Option: **"Kitchen & Closet Beta"** (2D design version)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754470365198/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Usage Notes

When switching to the Kitchen & Closet 2D design version:

* All your Kitchen & Closet tool items will automatically open in the 2D design environment.
* Version switching can be done at any time (changes take effect immediately).

⭐**Tips:** It is recommended to first use the Kitchen & Closet 2D design version for a comparison with previous version functions.

# Key Functional Features

## Display Style

You can switch the **Display Style** by clicking the icon at the lower-right corner of the tool. For the best view, it is recommended to choose **Fill and Line Style** as **Color Mode.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754471305122/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754471333961/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Auto-refresh Annotate

**1. Entrance**

Select the **Annotate** tool from the top toolbar, and click **Auto-refresh Settings.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754471592125/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**2. Enable the Auto-refresh Function**

In the auto-refresh settings popup, you can set the corresponding switch to control which annotations automatically generate on your canvas.

* **Note: Annotations update instantly with design. Deletion or modification of annotated objects can't be made with auto refresh turned on.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754472153030/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**3. Real-time Preview**

The selected annotations will appear in real-time during your design.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754448920971/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Generate Elevation and Callout Views

**1. Entrance**

Select the **Add Views** tool from the top toolbar, and select **Elevation or Callout.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754473533127/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**2. Generate Elevation Views**

After clicking the **Elevation** tool, select any wall direction on the canvas and click to generate the corresponding elevation view.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1754473616375/4CEF8F44819EF3F8D04A7F7875E51EDB.gif)

The design interface will automatically divide into upside and downside panels:

* **Downside Panel****:** Original floor plan view.
* **Upside Panel****:** New elevation view generated in the direction of the wall you selected.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754473743765/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**3. Cross-view Editing**

Drag and drop models onto either panel, and changes will be synchronized across all views.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1754473862883/963D24A04680CA3F15B74D9C12D8722B.gif)

**4. Generate Callout Views**

After clicking the **Callout** tool, draw a boundary box around the area you want to callout to automatically generate the corresponding callout view.

**⭐Key Functional Features:**

* **Precision Focus** —— Isolate and enlarge complex design areas.
* **Live Updates** —— Edits in main/callout views sync in real-time.
* **Multi-Callout Support** —— Create multiple callouts for different details.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1754474209922/E0AFE31488EE57CFBEDE573546815747.gif)

## View Switching Instructions

**1. View Switching**

The view options are displayed at the lower-left corner of the canvas, click the corresponding view option to switch views.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754474303592/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1754474376516/885F41F138ECFDE7D1F7D9F9A10867F6.gif)

**2. Edit View Options**

Right-click the view options, and select the corresponding option in the expanded menu to edit, including the following:

* Modify the position of the view option within the window.
* Rename the view.
* Duplicate views to compare side by side.
* Delete unused views.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754474497769/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**3. Rearranging View Locations**

**a. Drag and Drop to Adjust View Locations**

Click and hold a view option, then drag it to reposition between view areas.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1754474623071/11421134A88105A71CDCD495C62C92C5.gif)

**b. Switch Window Layouts**

Click the **Window Layout** option at the lower-left corner to freely switch view layouts.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1754474750267/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1754474899287/49C5E993D1B73ABF4D9ED18132E2F278.gif)
