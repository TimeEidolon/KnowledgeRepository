### Step 3：

**Layout ambient light**

Use the rectangular light source to simulate the effect of ambient light. The external surface light source selects a cool color light source, the value of the light source is controlled at 300-500. The internal surface light source has a light source increment, the color of the light source selects white, the value of the light source is slightly higher than the external surface light source, the size of the light source is slender and in the middle of space.
