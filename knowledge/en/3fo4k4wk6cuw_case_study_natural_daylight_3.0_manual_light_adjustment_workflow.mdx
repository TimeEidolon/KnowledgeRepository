**Problem：** The whole surrounding is too dark, lack of detail lights, those who rely on the wall shoot the lamp height to have a bit low. **Strategy：** - brighten up the entire space by raising the base lighting - especially the areas highlighted in the red box - add a ball to the chandelier - adjust the type and height of the spotlights on the right side **Step 1: Strengthen the “Skylight”** “Skylight” is can be understood as rectangle light in the window. ### Step 3
After adding up the "skylight", the whole space is brighter. **Step 2: Adjust down lamp spotlights** Add downlights in important areas, such as below the chandelier. Adjust the spot lamp height and distance against the wall. The brightness of downlight and spotlight reference system default value, generally floating between 200-300, spotlight height in 2400 meters or so, the living room can use warm white light during the day.

### Step 4
Regarding some cabinets without lamp belts, you could add a vertical surface light. Set brightness around 150-200, with warm white light. To increase the brightness of the furniture, add a downlight and adjust the light angle to 30-45 degrees. Set brightness between 100 and 150, with warm white light. **Step 3: Add detailed ambient light** Finally, add some detailed lights, such as chandeliers, wall lamps, and some hidden light strips to increase the effect of a certain atmosphere.

- brighten up the entire space by raising the base lighting

**Step 1: Strengthen the “Skylight”**

“Skylight” is can be understood as rectangle light in the window. The size can be close to the window size, and the color is light blue during the day and darker blue at night.

**Step 2: Adjust down lamp spotlights**
