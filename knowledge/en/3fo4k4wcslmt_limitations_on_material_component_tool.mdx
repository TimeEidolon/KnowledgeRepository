You may have met some issues while using the Material & Component tool. This article will list some common issues, the possible reasons and the resolutions.

**Users: All Users**


**Common Issues:**

1) The modelings made here are only visible in this tool and invisible in 3D view.

2) The modelings made here are visible in this tool, 3D view tool but invisible in construction drawing/floor plan drawing.

3) The modelings made here can cause the failure of rendering or the missing of modeling in the final rendered result.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753700710797/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1753700734207/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**Reason**:

1）This tool is no longer being updated in backend as it's outdated and has been gradually replaced by new tool.

2) Complicated modelings like cabinetry or window shape are consisting of large data.

**Solution**:

1) For cabinnetry, please proceed to advanced tool: kitchen&bath, closet.

2) For other complex shapes, please proceed to the new custom modeling tool.

For any further help, please contact [support@coohom.com](mailto:support@coohom.com).
