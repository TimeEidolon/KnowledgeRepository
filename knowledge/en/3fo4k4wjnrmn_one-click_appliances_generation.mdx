### **Function Introduction**

One-click generation of sliding doors, countertops, furniture leg, footline, crown modeling, knob, stoves, sinks, etc.

**Operation Steps**

【Step 1】Select "Generate - Sliding Doors" from the toolbar.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849077812/4BA270BCBCC193E8D3DDCEFA218E6B6A.png)

【Step 2】Choose the cabinet with top lines to generate and adjust the generated area and number

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849079138/41217E2F630F4392CB5513AF52CCBD7B.png)

【Step 3】Select the style of sliding doors and click "Generate"

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849081970/8CD6B142A65C830EC36F4EA23A5C1F96.png)

【Step 4】The sliding doors will be generated with one click.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733849084736/1184F1A973B339C1BEB9226A8880430A.png)
