Through the straight cut function of the cabinet, it is possible to cut out the expected size of corner cutting shapes in different parts of the cabinet, meeting design scenarios such as cutting beams and columns. This article will give you a detailed explanation of how to use the straight cut function of the cabinet.

Each step includes plain MEDIA_* URLs (for AI tools) plus rendered images.

Entrance Select **"Advanced Tool - Kitchen＆Bath / Closet"**to enter the customized tool page. * **Individual Users:** The cabinet corner cutting entrance is open by default. * **Enterprise Users: It is necessary to configure whether the cabinet corner cutting entrance is open. # Straight Cut Function Instructions

## Straight Cut Direction and Position

The straight cut can be set in different cutting directions, which mainly manifest as **Column** **Avoidance,  Avoid Beams, Avoid the Stringers.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756179088822/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Straight Cut Types

Straight cuts are divided into different types, mainly manifesting as **Cut Corners, Cut the Middle Corner**. Different types of the corner cutting correspond to different selection positions.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756179177840/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**1. Cut Corners.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756179280760/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

The effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756179400400/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

**2. Cut the Middle Corner.**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756179504756/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

The effect is as follows:

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756179617594/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Fillout Settings

You can change the patch board thickness and different patch board processes.

**1. Whether to Generate Patch Board.**

a. If unchecked, only the cabinet will be cut, and no patch board will be applied.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756179790110/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

b. If checked, you can further configure the patch board process.

* **Patch Methods** provide three options: **Full Cover, Half Cover, Embed.**
* **Patch Board Thickness** can be set to specific values.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756180008479/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

## Retain Part of the Board After Cutting

In **Plate Retention** check the boards that need to be retained (not cut) and click **"Are you sure".**

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756189015385/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756189372070/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

After setting is completed, **the cutting effect is generated as follows**, in the example the bottom shelf was selected for retention, so the bottom shelf will not be cut.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/gif/1756189298697/5AA0D772771706F46412A0C58B6364A0.gif)

## Multiple Cuts

You can generate different types of the corner cutting multiple times in different positions of the cabinet.

![](https://qhstaticssl.kujiale.com/image/png/1756189785867/A8CEBABD0663ECBAEEE7516DF7A7D2B5.png)

# Precautions

1. Corner cutting cabinets support generating crown moldings, toekick moldings, and countertops, moldings will be generated according to the corner cutting shape.

2. Cabinets with doors also support corner cutting.
