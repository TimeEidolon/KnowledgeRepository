The 360 tour cannot be downloaded as it can only be opened by link to see the walk-through effect.

But you can download the panorama image package.

For Users
=========

Pro/Elite/Enterprise users

Download 360 Walk-through Photos
================================

Go to "My Projects", click "Project Details" and you will be relocated to the render gallery. Click the three dots on the panorama render to download the image package.

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392478931/F4C5F260F2D844D3649085B02725EBCB.png)

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392487206/6157930715E229CBD648C2A9721E56DB.png)

Then unzip the package, you'll get the panorama view image with the filename "sphere.jpg".

![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1742392488747/A1DDDD4F9C282FEE6ABBF6A493112A66.png)
