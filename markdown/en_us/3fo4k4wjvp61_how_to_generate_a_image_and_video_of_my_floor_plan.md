1. Select **IMAGES/VIDEOS** in the header of our 3D floor planner.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841176421/BD362FDADD1ED2F1EC446355FC8AE687.png)  

2. Choose **Front View** , **Panorama,** or
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841179000/C32780AF481D31A523B8CDA59CCCE9B5.png)

**Top View** in the header of the render page. You can also choose the Default view (2D or 3D)
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841185256/430DE83CC496C3F49B4EB05697E46110.png)

3. Select proper options for **Aspect Ratios** , **Resolutions** , **Ambient Lights** , **Environment** , **Wall Modes,** and **Backgrounds** in the left panel to complete render settings.

4. Modify your camera settings in the right panel.

5. Decide the scenario you want to capture.

6. Drag the camera icon in the canvas to change the camera's position and angle.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/jpeg/1733841187520/C58656F14F6E75A201DDC0D98891CF8C.jpeg)

7. Or directly left-click to rotate and right-click to move the camera in 3D window on the right side.
![](https://qhstatic-us-s3.coohom.com/yuntai/image/png/1733841190787/7170B6E8785251763578DD75F7B9227F.png)

8. Click **Render** to get your stunning images.

9. Click **View more** under **Gallery** to check renderings.
